# Phil's Swing Trading Academy — Wiki Index

## Part 1: Non-Negotiable Rules

The following rules appear across virtually every tutorial Phil teaches. These are the constants — the things he reinforces in every session, every coaching call, and every walkthrough. Breaking these is not a stylistic choice; it undermines the entire system.

### Chart & Analysis Rules
- Always use the **monthly timeframe** for trend and structure analysis
- Always use **logarithmic scale** — never linear
- Use a **naked chart** — no indicators, no RSI, no MACD, no moving averages
- Zoom out to see at least **10–20 years of history** before analysing
- Only trade stocks with **$200B+ market cap** (minimum $100B)
- Only trade **uptrends** — downtrends are a hard skip

### Entry Rules
- **Only buy at support** — never between support and resistance (that is gambling)
- Wait for a **Bullish Break of Structure (BBOS)** on the daily chart before entering
- BBOS requires **two consecutive daily candle closes** above the previous lower high
- The lower high level is defined by the **wick**, not the candle body
- Entry is via breakout or break-and-retest (retest is preferred)
- If the retest fails (price slices through), **do not enter** — wait for a new setup

### Stop Loss Rules
- Stop loss is always placed **below monthly support with room to breathe**
- Monthly support is only invalidated by **monthly candle closes** below — daily noise does not count
- **Never move the stop loss against yourself** — once placed, it stays
- When horizontal and trend line support are both nearby, the stop goes below the **horizontal level**

### Risk Rules
- Maximum **5% risk per trade**
- Maximum **~5 concurrent trades** (~20% total open risk)
- Minimum **1:1 RRR** before entering any trade — below 1:1, skip it
- Max **2% risk per day trade** (day trading only)
- **No revenge trading** — two session losses and you walk away

### Options Rules
- LEAPs: strike = **ATM (at the money)**
- LEAPs: expiration = **today + 1.5 to 2 years minimum**
- No stop loss needed on LEAPs — **premium paid = total risk**
- Take profit on single contracts at **+100%** (double the premium)
- Macro research = **watchlist input only** — never a trade trigger

### Psychology Rules
- Place alerts, then **do nothing** until the next alert fires
- After entry: **do not monitor the account** between alerts
- **Never close winners early** out of fear
- After two losses in a day trading session: **walk away**
