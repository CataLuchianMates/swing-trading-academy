USB at 12x PE vs JPM at 16x with better growth potential relative to valuation. Private credit exposure is 3.6% of assets — manageable. Post-Liberation Day added as new position.

### ITUB — Itau Unibanco (Multiple Articles, 2024-2026)
Brazil's largest bank. Most extensive EM individual stock coverage. Single-digit PE throughout most of the period. Thesis: 2003-2007 EM bull cycle saw ITUB 8x its price. A similar cycle could produce similar returns. July 2025 Trump tariff threat (50% on Brazil) dismissed: Brazil exports only ~$41B/year to US on $2.2T GDP; commodities have alternative buyers. Result: +81% in dollar terms in 2025 alone.

STRC specifically described as competing with money market funds at 9% variable yield.

### Adobe — ADBE (Multiple Articles, 2024-2026)
Original 2019 entry at ~$230. Watched it go to $650, fall to $250, rise to $630, fall below 2022 lows to ~$260. Bull case: still dominant at professional level; Firefly AI consumption growing 45% QoQ; fortress balance sheet ($15B cash ≈ $15B debt); aggressive share buybacks. Lyn is "dabbling" and waiting for a technical bottom before pounding the table.
