## Selling & Exit Rules

**Q: What are your sell guidelines?**
> "For the 2026 Portfolio, I manage each name individually using my Stage Analysis framework. As long as the stock remains firmly in its Stage 2 uptrend, I will hold. If I start seeing signs of topping behavior or a Stage 4 downtrend, that's my signal to exit. So no hard stop losses on these positions, but I exit when I believe the uptrend is breaking/a downtrend is beginning."

**Q: Some people say holding through drawdowns is conviction. Disagree?**
> "I wouldn't call holding stocks through stage 4 downtrends/bear cycles 'conviction'. I would call it poor risk management. Holding stocks through huge drawdowns, hoping and praying it'll recover, and calling it 'conviction'? That's just not how I do things."

**Q: When reducing a position, how much do you typically trim?**
> "I'm reducing each position by 70%." (SMID Cap 6 portfolio reduction, Oct 2025)

7. **Celebrates subscriber wins** — genuine interest in subscriber outcomes, not just content publishing
