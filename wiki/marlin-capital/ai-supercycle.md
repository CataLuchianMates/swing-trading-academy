## What Marlin Avoids (Late-Stage Speculative Shorts)

In the late stage of the AI Bubble, Marlin has identified the "greater fool theory" stocks that have already topped:
- **QUBT (Quantum Computing)** — No product, no revenue, -989% gross margins, -16,689% EBITDA margins. Short candidate.
- **NNE (Nano Nuclear Energy)** — No product, no revenue, just a story. Short candidate.
- **OKLO, IONQ** — Put in "climax top" patterns in October 2025. Down 60%+ from highs.

Pattern: When pre-revenue companies with no product begin topping, the broader speculative bust is beginning. The quality AI names follow later and present buying opportunities on the way down.
