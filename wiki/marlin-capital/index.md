## About David Marlin

David Marlin is a professional investor and swing trader running Marlin Capital on Substack. His approach combines **Stage Analysis** (Weinstein) with **fundamental catalysts** — the "golden formula" of fundamentals + technicals. He runs three concurrent portfolios: a long-term AI portfolio, an active swing trading portfolio, and periodic thematic SMID Cap 6 portfolios. His 4 closed long-term portfolios have averaged +72% in ~9 months (~140% annualized), all outperforming the SPX.

His central thesis for 2025-2026: the **AI Supercycle** is the biggest technological transformation since the internet — bigger, faster, and broader.
