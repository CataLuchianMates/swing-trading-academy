## What Makes a Great SMID Cap Pick

From Marlin's June 2025 SMID Cap 6 analysis:

### The Selection Criteria
1. **Important/leading company in an industry being transformed by AI** — not a tangential AI play, but a company at the center of a structural shift
2. **Fundamental inflection point** — bookings inflecting, book-to-bill > 1.0, revenue accelerating, margins expanding
3. **Clear path to multi-year secular growth** — not a one-quarter wonder
4. **Technical setup aligning with fundamentals** — either breaking out of a base or setting up for one

### The Types of SMID Plays Marlin Likes

**AI Infrastructure Components:** Companies making the components that AI data centers run on (optical, power, connectivity, test equipment). These are "picks and shovels" at the SMID level.

**Episodic Pivots:** SMID caps that just had a major deal announcement or earnings inflection that the market hasn't fully priced yet.

**AI-Adjacent Industrial Re-ratings:** Older industrial companies re-rating as the market discovers their AI exposure (example: VPG going from a slow industrial to an AI robotics sensing story).
