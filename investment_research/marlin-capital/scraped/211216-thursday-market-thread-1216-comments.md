# Thursday Market Thread 12.16 — David Marlin Q&A

## David Marlin Q&A (Comments)

**Q:** In yesterday's post, you mentioned after Fed meeting, the first market reaction is usually fake. At current time, it looks like yesterday's relief rally is more like a bull trap. May I ask is that what you mean ----- the first reaction is yesterday's 2PM-4PM market?

**David Marlin:** Yes exactly, the initial reaction from Fed day is often the wrong move.  You’ll usually get a better feeling of how the market feels the next few days.

---

**Q:** I moved some of my Roth to XLP and XLU few days ago, of course it's the only green component there now :) however, I don't have much experience with these ETFs. How much does the overbought condition matter to these ETFs? Is there such a thing as a crowded trade for XLP/XLU at this time?

**David Marlin:** Like any stock or ETF, it will get overbought, pull back and rest, etc.  But overall XLP and XLU should perform well in late cycles, which the market is now discounting and has been.  The faster the Fed moves, the more bullish it is for those sectors.

---

**Q:** May I ask what does "sweeper" mean?

**David Marlin:** Someone buying a large # of call contracts (options).  Usually an institution.

---

**Q:** Anything on afrm ?

**David Marlin:** My base case in ARKK is still a decline to $80. That’s about 12% downside from here, so likely 20%+ more downside in AFRM and other growth stocks.  The first buy point for AFRM would be $92, but I’ll likely pass there until ARKK hits $80.
