Technical Analysis:

The market rallied above some key resistance levels in yesterday’s session, with the QQQ closing above its 21 EMA and the $320 level. Things are starting to look more bullish technically, but that can change on a dime with Fed Chair Powell speaking today. It should also be noted that yesterday’s rally came on very low volume, as investors await Powell’s commentary today.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9uwy!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbc434340-e569-45c2-b267-69e1ec341c34_958x701.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9uwy!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbc434340-e569-45c2-b267-69e1ec341c34_958x701.jpeg)

Key Resistance levels to watch: $322-323, $326-327, $330, $334

Key Support levels to watch: **$316-318**, $311-$312

Switching to the weekly chart, a strong rally today to close the week would bode well for possible continuation into next week. The QQQ is forming a mini bullish “hammer” candle after a pullback to start off this week.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zqyv!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F926708d9-f4c8-4352-ad0e-21cad2a247c3_1070x817.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zqyv!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F926708d9-f4c8-4352-ad0e-21cad2a247c3_1070x817.jpeg)

There is still plenty of overhead resistance to watch out for, but it is a bullish sign that some key support levels have held up so far.
