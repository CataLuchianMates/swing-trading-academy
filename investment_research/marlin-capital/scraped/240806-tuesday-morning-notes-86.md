# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.6

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Huge sell off across the board to kick off the week yesterday with the S&P 500 and NDX finishing -3%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qB6x!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4ed3c9fe-432a-4b54-8017-b7bef0317bf5_990x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qB6x!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4ed3c9fe-432a-4b54-8017-b7bef0317bf5_990x723.jpeg)

The VIX hit 65 intraday, which has only happened twice in the last 17+ years – November 2008 and March 2020.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c22Z!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbb431555-77c3-4d6d-961f-b95bb53027e9_963x534.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c22Z!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbb431555-77c3-4d6d-961f-b95bb53027e9_963x534.jpeg)

We went 356 straight trading sessions without a -2% decline in the SPX.  It took only 8 more sessions to register the first 3% drop since September 2022 (SPX dropped -4.32% on 9/13/22 on a hot CPI reading).

We finished well off the lows of the session, however, as the 10AM ISM Services print was solid (51.4 vs 51 expected), helping to calm some of the hard landing fears after last week’s downbeat growth numbers.

There was not a real flight to safety / recession trade / hard landing theme to yesterday’s price action (as we saw on Thursday and Friday).  China ADRs were the only Goldman bucket that finished the day in the green.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!NYO6!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4d2e6da5-c64a-4430-b605-a3d49d0b315b_1190x975.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!NYO6!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4d2e6da5-c64a-4430-b605-a3d49d0b315b_1190x975.jpeg)

A lot of what needed to be washed out, appears to have been washed out yesterday. Short term washout signal > 70 now:

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!b1LN!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa03fe181-c6b9-4aac-9056-c1aae0758d23_834x511.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!b1LN!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa03fe181-c6b9-4aac-9056-c1aae0758d23_834x511.png)

This doesn’t mean the selling is necessarily over, but I do believe the VIX has likely topped for now (barring some type of black swan event).  We saw Japan’s Nikkei bounce back +10% on Tuesday.  US equity futures are indicating a higher open for Tuesday.

I am 100% cash in my trading account.  We have some possible earnings gappers to watch today (below), but be mindful of sizing with the VIX still elevated (you do not need to enter as large for the same PNL swings when the VIX is elevated).
