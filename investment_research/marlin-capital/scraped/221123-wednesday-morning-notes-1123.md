# Wednesday Morning Notes 11.23

Technical Update

The rally continued yesterday with a strong mover higher in the indices.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Nlca!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe26f5787-4d40-47b5-a6bc-017c003679da_1210x892.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Nlca!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe26f5787-4d40-47b5-a6bc-017c003679da_1210x892.jpeg)

The SPX finally closed above the key $4k level, which has provided pivotal resistance during the last week or so. Short/medium term, this is a bullish technical setup that may allow this rally to continue to the 200 day moving average ($4060).

Resistance levels to watch include $4030, $4060, and $4080.

Support levels to watch below include $3950-$3960, $3920-$3930, and $3900.
