# Danger Zone

Marlin Capital Readers,

US stocks fell once again today. The S&P 500 touched its lowest levels of 2026 before bouncing back ~100 points into the close. The QQQ finished -1.07%.

The VIX closed at its highest level of 2026, up +19% so far this week.

**Here at Marlin Capital, this is the exact type of environment we prepared for.**

We first flagged the bearish warning signs back in October in [“Risk Off”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/risk-off-a70?utm_source=publication-search). Since then, we have been systematically preparing for this type of market environment. And we have been executing that plan ever since.

How did we prepare? 👇

We closed out an entire portfolio ([Trump Trades](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/marlin-capital-live-portfolio-dashboards)).

We moved to 43% cash (from fully invested) in the [2026 Portfolio](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/marlin-capital-live-portfolio-dashboards) and entered 3 short trades. We did not wait for the damage to happen. We saw it coming and acted quickly.

Speaking of our 3 shorts, 2 of them announced earnings last night. Both sets of numbers were poor.

I know what some of you are thinking: “This isn’t the ‘real dip’?”

No. The S&P 500 is just -2.6% off ATHs. A standard market correction starts at -10%, and such selloffs occur in most years (63%). That number is even higher when you only include Midterm Election years (which 2026 is).

#### **Big Picture Market Analysis**

In [“Choppy and Sloppy”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/choppy-and-sloppy?utm_source=publication-search), I described the market action like this. 👇

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vOzI!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9cb5172f-2678-482b-83b2-448ef586ab6d_1203x681.jpeg)](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/choppy-and-sloppy?utm_source=publication-search)

This is what we continue to see here in early March.

Today was a perfect example. The SPX touched $6710 (its lowest level of 2026) this morning, and then bounced back 100 points into the close. The selling is not following through, but neither are the bounces. The market is chopping around, swinging violently in both directions, and punishing anyone who is overtrading. This is a textbook [hard penny environment](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/hard-penny).

But here is what I am most concerned about right now. 👇

The Semiconductor Index (SOX) broke below $8100 support today, which is the [level I have been monitoring closely](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/not-enough).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!E-xa!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faf1238d2-f69d-41c9-aa61-2a677f4dd25b_1013x675.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!E-xa!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faf1238d2-f69d-41c9-aa61-2a677f4dd25b_1013x675.png)

Notice how every other time Semis lost the 21 EMA (blue line above) since this uptrend began (in April 2025), they bounced back above it in short order. And they have not meaningfully broken and established below the 60 EMA (gold line above) once during this entire uptrend.

Today, we closed right at the 60 EMA (gold line below).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!dQW2!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff101df22-4c81-437d-89fc-956d072a717b_1013x675.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!dQW2!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff101df22-4c81-437d-89fc-956d072a717b_1013x675.png)

This is the danger zone. If Semis break and establish below the 60 EMA ($7750), it is a very bad sign for Tech and the rest of the market. Semis have led this entire Bull Market, and now, they are sitting right at key support.

Again, we have only closed 1 day below the 21 EMA (today), so it is too early to hit the panic button. But, SOX is definitely in the danger zone here. $7750 (the 60 EMA) is the key support level I will be watching. If $7750 breaks, the character of this market changes significantly.

For the QQQ, we are nearing the 200-day moving average. Nasdaq Futures (/NQ) came within ~1% today.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2tuk!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2f736242-04c9-4b18-9f36-6bff90f65e34_1013x675.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2tuk!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2f736242-04c9-4b18-9f36-6bff90f65e34_1013x675.png)

The S&P 500 is in a different spot. We are still ~3.8% above the 200-day MA, which gives us a little more of a cushion (as non-tech has outperformed recently). But eventually, I do expect us to test that level (~$6560) in the SPX as well.

Whether or not we bottom there, and this turns out to be the “[bear trap](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/hard-penny)” I described in [“Hard Penny”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/hard-penny) remains to be seen. But that 200-day test is the scenario I continue to see as most likely based off the current price action.
