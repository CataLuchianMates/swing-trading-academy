# Friday Morning Notes 8.4

Technical Update

The S&P 500 finished lower again on Thursday (-0.25%), but managed to close just above $4500 support.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Jfy0!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff4efa83d-dcfe-4d4a-a45f-4ebe1c26ff29_955x728.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Jfy0!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff4efa83d-dcfe-4d4a-a45f-4ebe1c26ff29_955x728.jpeg)

We are set for a gap up this morning after AMZN and AAPL earnings. The key for me today is to see if we can close above the 21 EMA resistance ($4511).

Meanwhile, key support has been established at $4485-$4500. A decisive break below opens the door for a further decline to the 60 EMA TO $4385-$4400.

Today is a critical day for determining the market direction for the rest of August.

There is some reason for caution with certain leaders beginning to struggle (NVDA, AAPL). However, I continue to like how my earnings gapper watchlist is shaping up.

CLS and COUR have held up nicely on this 2 day pullback. I plan to initiate a starter long position in CLS today.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_H8G!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1885ee23-ea00-40f1-b190-d4d936eea5cc_1185x891.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_H8G!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1885ee23-ea00-40f1-b190-d4d936eea5cc_1185x891.jpeg)

I will be waiting on COUR and ROKU to set up cleaner as well as observing the general market conditions to close the week.
