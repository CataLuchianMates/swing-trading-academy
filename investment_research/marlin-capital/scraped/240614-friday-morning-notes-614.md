# Friday Morning Notes 6.14

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Another set of all time closing highs for the SPX (+0.23%) and NDX (+0.57%) yesterday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LyUq!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F043aec0f-f91c-48c8-b4d0-18b2ab090608_1207x897.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LyUq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F043aec0f-f91c-48c8-b4d0-18b2ab090608_1207x897.jpeg)

10YR yields eased 7bps to 4.24% on the PPI miss/claims weaker.

The NDX was up +0.57% despite >70% of names in the index trading lower on the session.  NDX outperformed the NDX Equal Weight by 90bps, which is a TOP 5 day of outperformance over the last year.  NVDA, TSLA, AVGO, SMCI led the way yesterday while there was not a ton of participation in non-Mag 7/AI names.  Software continues to slide lower despite yields dropping while Payments/FinTech still can’t catch a notable bid (PYPL, SQ, TOST, etc.).  Pockets of internet names are also stuck in the mud (UBER, ABNB, SPOT, AMZN – worst performing Mag 7).

After the bell, ADBE announced a solid beat and raise quarter (positioning was 5/10 into the print) and is set for a +15% gap up this morning.

Updates on my trades –

ONON  and NVO – Still holding.  No changes to either position.

ZETA – Didn’t break above $17.59 yesterday so no long entry triggered here.  Software continues to struggle overall.

AVGO – Gave an inside 15 and inside 1 HR yesterday but broke to the downside on both.  No long entry triggered here yesterday.  This is still 1 of my top watches as I believe we will see this stock start to set up on the daily chart.

ADBE – Potential earnings gapper setup I’m watching today.  We have a gap fill to $570 on the daily chart if the stock can start to move up following today’s earnings gap.
