# Top 10 Stocks to Watch For 2024

Below are my top 10 stocks to watch for the rest of 2024.

As I talked about in ["Framing The Debate For 2024”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/framing-the-debate-for-2024), I believe the AI theme will remain hot this year.  That is why most of these stocks fall into that category in one way or another.  Please note that the below stocks are in no particular order.

The common theme in the below names is that they are each in leading Tech industries (AI, Semis, Software, FinTech) and all projected for strong EPS and revenue growth for 2024. As of now, these are my top names to watch for the rest of the year:

As we enter the Q4 earnings season (slated for the next 3-4 weeks), I am planning to wait until after each of these stocks announce their results for a cleaner trading entry.  As Marlin Capital readers know, I view [bullish earnings gaps](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-earnings-gapper-trade-setup) as some of the best times to buy/trade stocks.  Therefore, these stocks are my top 10 to watch into their earnings, and I will be looking to buy any that announce strong results.

For the next several weeks, my trading focus will remain on Q4 [earnings gaps](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-earnings-gapper-trade-setup). As always, please keep an eye on the daily morning notes for specific focus stocks each morning, where I will be calling out potential earnings gaps that I find promising.
