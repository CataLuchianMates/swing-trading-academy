# Tuesday Morning Notes 4.4

Technical Update

The market continued its move higher to kick off the week yesterday. There is not much standing in the way of the S&P 500 and YTD highs now.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9a54!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fed1a5f64-c115-4b31-8bd5-6248f1bf2271_1194x902.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9a54!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fed1a5f64-c115-4b31-8bd5-6248f1bf2271_1194x902.jpeg)

On the short-term time frame, we are reaching overbought levels after 4 straight green days. A pullback to $4050 would be healthy in the coming days. With that said, it is fully possible for this squeeze to continue to $4200 before a larger pullback. Key resistance levels ahead include $4150-$4160 and YTD highs at $4190-$4200.

Key support levels include $4100, $4075-$4080, and $4050, and the last line of technical support at $3990-$4000.
