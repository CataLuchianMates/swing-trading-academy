# Friday Morning Notes 12.16

Technical Analysis

The market broke lower yesterday, with the SPX breaking and closing below the key $3900 level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!a4tC!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3cd6ac8b-8cd6-4132-9a74-79435ae86045_1396x669.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!a4tC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3cd6ac8b-8cd6-4132-9a74-79435ae86045_1396x669.png)

We are now set for another gap down this morning. Today is triple quad witching, with $4T of options set to expire. This should add to the intraday volatility, especially towards the close.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!y0qS!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4a5ebf56-21bf-46ca-baed-bdfeb9c77fb3_1284x711.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!y0qS!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4a5ebf56-21bf-46ca-baed-bdfeb9c77fb3_1284x711.jpeg)

On a technical basis, this is a bearish setup on all time frames. Key support levels to watch include $3845-$3850 and $3800-$3810.

On a bounce, look for the $3900 level to turn into resistance. Further resistance includes the $3960 level on a larger bounce.
