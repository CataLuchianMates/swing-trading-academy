# Friday Morning Notes 10.21

Technical Analysis

Thursday was another choppy day for the market, with the QQQ once again topping out at the daily 21 EMA ($274-$275). The index has been rangebound since the beginning of the week, tracing between $268-$275.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!A0L3!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F05a5b495-26b3-4330-96eb-8dc76603ec20_953x734.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!A0L3!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F05a5b495-26b3-4330-96eb-8dc76603ec20_953x734.jpeg)

Monthly options expiration is today.

Key support remains at the $268-$269 level. If this support level breaks, the next level to watch is $264-$265.

On the upside, $274-$275 remains the key resistance level for bulls to break through.
