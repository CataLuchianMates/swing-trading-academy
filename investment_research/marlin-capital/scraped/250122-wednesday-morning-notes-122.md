# Wednesday Morning Notes 1.22

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The 1st trading day post-Trump inauguration closed with the major US indices solidly in the green with the S&P 500 rallying +0.88%, NDX +0.58%, and RTY +1.85%.

In Tech, the main theme was Small > Big with Small Caps outperforming (R2K TECH +2.5% and +9% over the last 5 days) and AAPL (-3%) once again underperforming. AAPL is (quietly?) on track to have its worst month (-11.1%) since December 2022 and is now lagging the NDX by ~10pts MTD, which is its biggest underperformance since November 2018.

All this coming while other Tech/Big Tech hasn’t missed a beat - the NDX has been above its 200-day moving average since 3/13/2023 (467 straight sessions), its 2nd longest streak ever (and has rallied +84% during this time).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MRgh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F10fcd046-be9a-4402-9d80-959d527b68db_1003x635.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MRgh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F10fcd046-be9a-4402-9d80-959d527b68db_1003x635.jpeg)

After the bell, Netflix reported strong earnings with a huge Net Adds beat (18.9M vs 9M cons) and raised FY guidance. The stock is +11% this morning, boosting the Nasdaq higher.

The other big news of the day was President Trump announcing that Softbank, OpenAI, and Oracle will form a $100B joint venture to fund AI named “Stargate” with a goal of increasing it to $500B. AI infrastructure stocks rallied big on the news with my [Top 33 AI picks for 2025](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/third-bounce) having a fantastic day collectively.
