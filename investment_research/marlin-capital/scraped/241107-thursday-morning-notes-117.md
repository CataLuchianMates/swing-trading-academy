# Thursday Morning Notes 11.7

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Global markets aggressively postured around Trump’s landslide victory in the Presidential Election yesterday (R2K +5.8% / SPX +2.5% / NDX +2.7%).

As expected, we saw tons of money rotating under the hood with Small Caps, Banks, De-regulation beneficiaries, Crypto, and TSLA (+15%) all soaring higher while tariff exposed, China, Renewables, were left behind.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MYrE!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fab5dfa8d-e119-4c06-bcbb-f9cc70bf2a9a_917x2393.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MYrE!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fab5dfa8d-e119-4c06-bcbb-f9cc70bf2a9a_917x2393.jpeg)

My broader thinking is that institutional investors remain lighter on risk/positioning and may need to add back some exposure for a potential year end rally.

Today, we have the conclusion of the FOMC meeting. The Fed is widely expected to cut interest rates by 25bps at today’s meeting. Today is as anti-climactic of a Fed meeting as I can remember as we are just coming off the election and today’s Fed decision (25bps cut) is already seen as a foregone conclusion. Still, Powell’s commentary at the press conference can and will move markets/add volatility to the trading day.

Active Portfolio -

LRN - Holding 2/3rds long heading into Thursday. Stop at $92. I will be looking to take more profits at my $100 target.

TWLO - No changes here. First target = $92. Stop below $83.

ALAB - Strong close yesterday after a pull back off the open. I am moving my stop to yesterday’s lows at $86.50

APP, BROS, FRSH, LYFT - potential earnings gappers to watch today
