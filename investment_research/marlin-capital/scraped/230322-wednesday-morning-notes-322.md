# Wednesday Morning Notes 3.22

Technical Update

The market rallied big yesterday in anticipation of today’s Fed meeting, with the SPX finishing +1.3% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pXbh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9a4d1510-a9e5-4aba-9665-3d5a703158b0_962x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pXbh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9a4d1510-a9e5-4aba-9665-3d5a703158b0_962x724.jpeg)

We are now back above all key moving averages on the daily chart as we head into today’s meeting. The technicals can change on a dime after market moving news events, but this is a bullish setup for the SPX heading into the event.

The FOMC policy statement and summary of economic projections will be released at 2 PM EST today, followed by a press conference with Chairman Jerome Powell at 2:30 EST.

The market is currently pricing in a 25 BP hike as the most likely scenario. Meanwhile, a pause in interest rate hikes is still on the table (~20% probability according to futures).
