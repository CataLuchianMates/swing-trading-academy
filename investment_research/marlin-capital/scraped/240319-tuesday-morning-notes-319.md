# Tuesday Morning Notes 3.19

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Markets rebounded to kick off the week yesterday with the SPX +0.63%, QQQ +0.95%, and R2K -0.63%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!xUMd!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F923eef83-d977-46d4-b891-501f3d807009_1220x888.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!xUMd!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F923eef83-d977-46d4-b891-501f3d807009_1220x888.jpeg)

Overall, markets closed weak after a gap up to start the day as the morning rally was largely faded.  The QQQ did pop back above its 21 EMA, but we remain in a precarious position here heading into tomorrow’s FOMC meeting.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XLbR!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1f67c8e4-e0f3-4d9b-aff2-4e316b183e57_1228x896.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XLbR!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1f67c8e4-e0f3-4d9b-aff2-4e316b183e57_1228x896.jpeg)

There was some strength in Mega Cap Tech with a lot of attention on the Bloomberg story that Apple is in talks to build Google’s Gemini AI on the iPhone.  TSLA was +6% on news Biden is set to crack down on Auto Emissions to accelerate EV sales.  NVDA was +70bps on the day after being up +5% early as the GTC conference continues.

We are set for a gap down this morning after yesterday’s weak close.  Key support levels for the QQQ remain at $436 (21 EMA) and $433-$434 (key 1-HR support).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-AXw!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0920dc4c-cee3-4ace-ab9d-c934cce79b7e_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-AXw!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0920dc4c-cee3-4ace-ab9d-c934cce79b7e_1396x653.png)

No planned trades for me today ahead of tomorrow’s critical FOMC meeting.
