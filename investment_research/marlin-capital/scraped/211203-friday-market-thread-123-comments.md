**Q:** Newer here, do you really see the gap between Chinese EV stocks like Nio/XPEV and Tesla that large? Mainly CCP and regulatory issues? Or different spots on the growth curve?

**David Marlin:** Welcome Chris.  To answer your question, it's mainly a short based off the technicals and the CCP & regulatory issues.  Right now, there is enormous risk that all Chinese stocks/VIE's will be delisted by 2024.  To NOT be delisted by the US exchanges, these companies will have to be in compliance with US auditing rules. None of them are right now, and the CCP vehemently disagrees with this rule.  If they do comply, there is the threat of punishment from the CCP.  If they don't comply, they will be delisted, which is essentially a death sentence to these stocks no matter the fundamentals.
