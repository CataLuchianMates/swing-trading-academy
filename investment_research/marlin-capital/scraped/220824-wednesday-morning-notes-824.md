# Wednesday Morning Notes 8.24

Technical Analysis:

After 2 straight big daily declines (Friday and Monday), the market chopped around yesterday, closing near flat on the day. The technical outlook remains the same as we head into Wednesday. For the QQQ, the $313 region has marked the low for 2 straight days. If this support fails, the 60 EMA at $310-311 is a key support level to watch.

Resistance is found in the $317-$318 region, which has marked the high for 2 straight days. The 21 EMA is just about $318.
