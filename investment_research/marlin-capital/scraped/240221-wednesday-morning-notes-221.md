# Wednesday Morning Notes 2.21

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The market extended losses from Friday to kick off the week yesterday with the S&P 500 finishing the day -0.6%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!kHZ5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcae668fe-5fb1-4ec5-9530-6b0a47623104_949x707.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!kHZ5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcae668fe-5fb1-4ec5-9530-6b0a47623104_949x707.jpeg)

We saw broad pressure in lower quality pockets in tandem with tech bellwethers NDX -79 bps and R2k -1.29%.  It was a fairly quiet session with investors on edge ahead of today’s FOMC Minutes (to be released at 2 PM EST) and NVDA’s earnings print after the close.  There is a lot riding on this print in terms of sentiment and setup, with options implying an ~11% directional move.  Reminder - the Tech sector was net bought for the 6th straight week last week and we saw the largest net buying in over a year.  Last week’s notional net buying ranks in the 92nd percentile versus the past 5 years, according to GS Prime.

NVDA, AMD, ARM, SMCI were all down -5%+ for most of yesterday’s session as AI names came under pressure ahead of today’s NVDA print.  My sense is that this was driven by (1)an ongoing technical break in SMCI, following a 20%+ reversal after a parabolic rally; and (2) growing concerns surrounding positioning dynamics and sustainability of the AI rally into NVDA earnings this afternoon (positioning is currently a 10 on a 1-10 positioning scale).

PLTR – Triggered my stop at $23.30 in yesterday’s session.  I am all out of this position with a +13.6% gain on the final portion of this trade.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-DRM!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7992481d-ac90-4a28-ad97-cac91368438b_1194x891.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-DRM!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7992481d-ac90-4a28-ad97-cac91368438b_1194x891.jpeg)

CLSK – This name gapped well above my entry point yesterday, so I did not take this trade.  After yesterday’s candle, this stock no longer appears ready and appears more likely to consolidate/chop here.

Overall, not seeing any reason to do anything today ahead of this afternoon’s NVDA print.
