# Tuesday Market Thread 3.8

Stay patient, avoid FOMO. There is absolutely no rush to do anything here.

ARKK briefly made new lows yesterday and closed down 3.6%. My base case remains a move to the low $40 region.

The US 2/10yr yield curve is starting to ring some alarm bells. The fixed income market is telling us that it does not have faith in the Fed’s ability to tame inflation while avoiding a recession (it shouldn’t, as I have mentioned several times).

The Yield curve inversion has preceded 6 of the last 6 recessions dating back to 1980. It has typically preceded the recession by 1-2 years.
