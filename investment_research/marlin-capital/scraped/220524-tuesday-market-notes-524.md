The indices are set to gap down today after Snap and Best Buy macro warnings.

Now, we will watch to see if the indices can hold their recent support levels and bounce. If this dip gets bought up, and the market is able to rally in the face of bad news, I believe there is a good chance Friday was a temporary bottom.

On the other hand, if sellers regain control here, this mini bounce is likely over and new lows should come soon.

Key SPY support levels: $390, $387, $385, $380

Key SPY resistance levels: $395, $397, $400
