# Thursday Morning Notes 4.20

Technical Update

The choppy market action continued on Wednesday as the S&P 500 closed -0.01% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5v3X!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff9829a91-5170-47f8-ac66-5e53332dbaf3_965x715.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5v3X!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff9829a91-5170-47f8-ac66-5e53332dbaf3_965x715.jpeg)

We are set for a gap down this morning towards $4130 support in the SPX. Gap downs and dips have been bought up relentlessly this year, but the market appears to be running out of momentum here. Bears have been unable to take control thus far, however.

Key support levels to watch below include $4130-$4135, $4100, and $4075-$4080.

Key resistance level to watch above include $4160, and $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).
