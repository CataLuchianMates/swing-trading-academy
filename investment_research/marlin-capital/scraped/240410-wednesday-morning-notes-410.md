# Wednesday Morning Notes 4.10

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks continued their choppy/rangebound action yesterday with the SPX, QQQ, and RTY each finishing slightly green.

March CPI Report:

❖ U.S CPI (MOM) (MAR) ACTUAL: 0.4% VS 0.4% PREVIOUS; EST 0.3%

❖ U.S CPI (YOY) (MAR) ACTUAL: 3.5% VS 3.2% PREVIOUS; EST 3.4%

❖ U.S CORE CPI (MOM) (MAR) ACTUAL: 0.4% VS 0.4% PREVIOUS; EST 0.3%

❖ U.S CORE CPI (YOY) (MAR) ACTUAL: 3.8% VS 3.8% PREVIOUS; EST 3.7%

CPI comes in hotter than expected for March and we are set for a sizable gap down this morning.  The headline CPI number of 3.5% is higher than any Wall Street estimate heading into the report, and a concerning acceleration from February’s 3.2% headline number.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yqkk!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2fc0fb37-7e35-4854-85aa-5c96f426c62f_1200x549.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yqkk!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2fc0fb37-7e35-4854-85aa-5c96f426c62f_1200x549.jpeg)

This was the highest headline reading (3.5%) in CPI since last September.

Following this morning’s CPI report, the odds of a rate cut in June have collapsed.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!dOnc!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2299e768-0dc3-4be8-b9f4-82e57b2a883f_1277x667.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!dOnc!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2299e768-0dc3-4be8-b9f4-82e57b2a883f_1277x667.jpeg)

There is a bigger shift in the Bond Market with Fed Funds Futures now pricing in only 2 rate cuts for 2024 with the 1st coming in September (market was pricing in 3 rate cuts with the first coming in June heading into today’s CPI).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KxSl!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0225d54b-6ee4-47a7-86dc-bdda0b73248a_1072x699.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KxSl!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0225d54b-6ee4-47a7-86dc-bdda0b73248a_1072x699.png)

We are set for a sizable gap down this morning off this news, with the QQQ set to open near ~$435.  $433-$434 remains the key support level to watch below in the QQQ.  This level has held for 6+ weeks.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G4WV!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3c7dcf33-cdf0-453f-85e8-2421782124f1_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G4WV!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3c7dcf33-cdf0-453f-85e8-2421782124f1_1396x653.png)

A move below would open the door for a drop to the daily 60 EMA at $430.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!PhSw!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fada7dcd1-e9b8-4a49-b6e2-6156977ac95b_976x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!PhSw!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fada7dcd1-e9b8-4a49-b6e2-6156977ac95b_976x726.jpeg)
