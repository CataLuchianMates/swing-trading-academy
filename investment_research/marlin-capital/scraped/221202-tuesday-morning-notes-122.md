Technical Analysis

The SPX ran out of steam at $4100 yesterday, topping out there and closing well off highs.

Now, we are set for a gap down this morning due to a hotter than expected jobs report.

Support levels to watch below include $4025-$4030, $4000, and $3940-$3950.

Key resistance levels to watch include $4050-$4060 and $4090-$4100.

Keep an eye on the weekly chart with today’s action. Full report coming this weekend.
