## David Marlin Q&A (Comments)

**Q:** I calculate a PSG for GRAL to be ~0.82.  It is still undervalued, but I wish it had strong revenue growth.  Thoughts?

**David Marlin:** I’m really focused on the future with GRAL. We are still so early in this company. They did around ~100k tests in the US last year. There are tons of potential growth catalysts in the pipeline: possible FDA approval, the REACH study potentially validating Galeri for Medicare coverage, the Quest Diagnostics and Tricare partnerships rollouts, future new partnerships (like the NHS), international expansion (GRAL is backed by Tencent, possible push into Asia?). Not to mention if they achieve any significant technological advancements. My prediction is that AI will lead to massive tech advancements in healthcare. Jensen Huang, Elon musk, and Sam Altman predict the same. I think GRAL is in a prime position to be 1 of those leading AI health companies at the forefront of it all.
