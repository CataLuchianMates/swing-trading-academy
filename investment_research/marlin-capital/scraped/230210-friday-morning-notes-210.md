# Friday Morning Notes 2.10

Technical Analysis

The market staged a big reversal yesterday following the morning gap up. The S&P 500 broke and closed below the key $4100 support level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wibS!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4e86ab15-c713-4b00-a1d3-f6bcf24d9526_965x728.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wibS!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4e86ab15-c713-4b00-a1d3-f6bcf24d9526_965x728.jpeg)

This is an ugly candle that has quickly changed the technical picture. On the daily chart (above), the SPX is nearing a bearish MACD cross. This would be the index’s first since early December, which led to one of the worst December’s in the history of the stock market.

We are set for a gap down this morning. It is time to be cautious now after a big run and some signs of a potential topping/reversal pattern.

Key support levels to watch below include $4050-$4060, $4020/$4030, and $4000.

NVDA

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!VgYf!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3e25d00f-a282-482f-a86e-414d1c59d231_987x732.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!VgYf!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3e25d00f-a282-482f-a86e-414d1c59d231_987x732.jpeg)

2 straight shooting star candles for NVDA. After a big run, this stock appears to be running out of steam. I will be taking more profits in NVDA at the open today, leaving 1/4th of my original position. My stop loss on the last portion will be moved to $215.

MELI

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HnPz!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F128dacf5-0c40-4618-a169-7d5c8922ebd0_1188x888.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HnPz!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F128dacf5-0c40-4618-a169-7d5c8922ebd0_1188x888.jpeg)

With yesterday’s candle, MELI looks to be forming a topping/reversal pattern. My stop has been moved to $1130, so I expect to be exiting the rest of my position today.
