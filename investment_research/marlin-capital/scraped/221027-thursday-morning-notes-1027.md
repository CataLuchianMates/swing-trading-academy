# Thursday Morning Notes 10.27

Technical Analysis

US stocks broke their winning streak yesterday with the QQQ closing down -2%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!SQc6!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc9bb151f-8698-4296-bcc4-f16678736f0b_1012x760.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!SQc6!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc9bb151f-8698-4296-bcc4-f16678736f0b_1012x760.jpeg)

On a technical basis, the $285/$286 level is strong resistance.

$275-$277 is key support.

The market is set to open near flat this morning despite poor results from Meta. I expect to see a choppy session today, as all eyes are on Apple (and Amazon) reporting after hours tonight. These earnings should provide a catalyst for the market into next week’s FOMC meeting.
