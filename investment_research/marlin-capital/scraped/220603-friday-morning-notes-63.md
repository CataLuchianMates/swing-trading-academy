Wedbush Dan Ives-

“Reuters reports Elon Musk sent an email to company executives June 2 titled "pause all hiring worldwide," saying that he has a super bad feeling about the economy.”

Elon Musk is the latest CEO to warn about a deteriorating economy and impending recession, although this is not his first warning. This follows Microsoft’s guidance warning yesterday, and Jamie Dimon’s warning that an [economic hurricane is coming](https://www.cnbc.com/amp/2022/06/01/jamie-dimon-says-brace-yourself-for-an-economic-hurricane-caused-by-the-fed-and-ukraine-war.html).

When you see things like this, alarm bells need to be going off in your head. Marlin Capital subscribers know I have been quite bearish on stocks since late November, which has been followed by over 60% downside in ARKK. One of the main reasons we have been on top of this sell off is because we do not ignore the facts. We do not ignore the warning signs. We do not use ad hominem attacks to try to discredit the CEOs (of some of the biggest companies in the world) who are warning about a recession.

Many CEOs are warning about the economy. CEO confidence is approaching levels only seen during recessions.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pf79!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb044d1ac-a064-4946-8171-bf2110dedbcf_1030x812.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pf79!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb044d1ac-a064-4946-8171-bf2110dedbcf_1030x812.jpeg)

Once the market decides it is time to start caring about bad news again, the rug will be promptly pulled and the Bear market rally will end. Its important not to overstay your welcome on the long side in this market. Those who ignore the warning signs in favor of a “hope” based strategy typically end up learning a painful lesson from Mr. Market.
