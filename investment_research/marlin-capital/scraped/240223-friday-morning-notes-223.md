# Friday Morning Notes 2.23

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500, Nasdaq 100, and Dow all closed at record highs yesterday following NVDA results.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5jTz!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1785dc82-f1d5-48c3-a536-895454f143f0_974x721.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5jTz!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1785dc82-f1d5-48c3-a536-895454f143f0_974x721.jpeg)

NVDA fueled the rally, surging +16.4% and adding $275B of market cap, the largest 1 day gain in market cap for any company in history.  Consensus was that NVDA would beat but expectations were impossibly high and that the stock would fade (like the last 2 reports).  In retrospect, the “beat and fade” reaction was pulled forward and priced in as investors de-risked into the print with the stock -9% over the past 4 sessions prior.  Yesterday, we saw the fast money community / big money / retail buy back what they had trimmed.

Breadth overall was better than you’d expect with 369 finishing in the green.  Yes, NVDA was ~31% of the S&P strength, but after that it dropped off to a more healthy distribution.

The NDX +301 bps had its best day in >1 year despite ~20% of the NDX in the red yesterday with some uninspiring action in Small/Mid caps (think ETSY, RIVN, FVRR, GLBE types).  These were offset by new 52 week highs from ASML, AVGO, BKNG, KLAC, META, NVDA, WDAY to name a few.

High Beta 12M winners were up +4.9% (3 sigma move) while Bitcoin Sensitive equities led the way overall up +6.4%.

As we hit new all time highs, I think it’s worth appreciating the environment we’re currently in/been in: the SPX has not had a greater than 2% down day since 2022.

CVNA – high short interest (35%) and breaking out of a long base if it can hold/accelerate this morning’s gap.  I will be looking for an [earnings gapper](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-earnings-gapper-trade-setup) entry (inside 5, 15, or 30) on CVNA this morning.
