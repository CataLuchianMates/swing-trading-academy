# Friday Morning Notes 8.19

Technical Analysis ( from 8/18 post-market):

Today was another choppy day for the indices on very low volume. It was an inside day for the QQQ. Depending on which way we break from here, we could have a topping pattern or another higher low. These are the key levels to watch for tomorrow: Above $331 is bullish. Below $326 is bearish and could lead to a larger pull back to the $319 and $310 levels. I entered SHLS on a break of $27. This renewable energy stock with strong fundamentals gave a golden cross (60 EMA > 200 SMA) and appears to be entering stage 2. There are some other setups starting to look good as well, but I do not plan to add any more long exposure until the market makes its decision.
