# Tuesday Morning Notes 2.11

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Quiet start to the week yesterday with equities finishing broadly higher (SPX +0.67%, NDX +1.24%) and holding the pre-market gap up. It was a muted weekend of newsflow outside the headline focused on Trump announcing a 25% tariff on steel/aluminum imports. Energy and Industrials led the way with strength in steel names + ROK (+13%) squeezing on better earnings. In Tech, UBER +5% was strong again on follow through post-Ackman disclosure on Friday.

For the QQQ, we closed yesterday towards the top of the 2 month trading range we have been in.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!NzYc!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1c27b48d-9b91-4ef3-90de-c57b8247862b_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!NzYc!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1c27b48d-9b91-4ef3-90de-c57b8247862b_1396x653.png)

As I talked about yesterday, I am simply not be looking to buy gap ups in a choppy market. I would ultimately expect these gaps to be filled in a market like this. We are now set for a gap down below yesterday’s lows this morning as the choppy action continues.

Gap downs, on the other hand, are a much better buying point for trades in a market like this (because again, I expect these to be filled). My top watches for potential new trades are all earnings gappers that I’m focused on:

APPS, AFRM, MNDY, NET, DOCS, AIOT
