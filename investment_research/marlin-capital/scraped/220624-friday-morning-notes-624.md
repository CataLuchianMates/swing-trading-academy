# Friday Morning Notes 6.24

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday was an impressive day for the market after the morning gap up faded. The QQQ finally broke resistance at the 8 EMA and closed at the top of this weekly 15 minute range we have been trading in.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7Qee!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd69fff29-e19a-44a6-9051-0386c6507c9d_881x422.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7Qee!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd69fff29-e19a-44a6-9051-0386c6507c9d_881x422.jpeg)

My highest probability scenario remains a failure at the 21 EMA resistance level ($291) followed by a re-test of lows ($270), as this is consistent with what we have seen throughout this Bear Market. If the re-test successfully holds, this is where I believe we may see a bigger rally like we did in March. You’ll notice that in March, we busted right through the 8 and 21 EMAs, whereas this time, the rally has not been able to pick up nearly as much steam.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!muCm!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F12521338-6a57-4e07-a5a6-050a5f7f2e8e_881x422.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!muCm!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F12521338-6a57-4e07-a5a6-050a5f7f2e8e_881x422.jpeg)

Interestingly though, we are seeing some divergences in high beta names and ARKK, etc, which have been showing relative strength versus the indices and did not break May lows on this recent wave down. I will be discussing this in my next report this weekend.
