### **The Flow Show**

**Discretionary Flows No Longer A Tailwind**

The equity market feels a bit heavy here to kick off this week. In my view, discretionary positioning and sentiment data reflect a market that finally has some length in it. The discretionary crowd is “back in the water” and levered up after being forced to chase (it should be noted, L/S Ratio is still well off Feb highs which was essentially “all in”). This by itself is not a reason to be bearish, but just a reflection that some of the asymmetries have finally shifted.

**Systematic Flows A Major Tailwind**

Systematic Buying (specifically Vol Control) will remain the dominant flow supporting the market up here. This week marks >3 months from Liberation Day, which means we will see a sharp decline in 3M realized volatility (as those early April meltdown days roll out of the sample).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G-ZD!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff8ab04ff-861d-406d-8894-415ebb101d36_1356x760.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G-ZD!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff8ab04ff-861d-406d-8894-415ebb101d36_1356x760.jpeg)

In turn, this will trigger big Vol Control buying. As mentioned on Sunday in “Big Beautiful Breakout”, **Nomura’s model projects $114.2B of Vol Control buying from June 26th - July 26th.** This is the **largest projected 1M buying in the model’s history.**

The **other 9 highest 1M Fwd Systematic buying projections from Nomura saw the S&P 500 higher 100% of the time** (9/9) 1 and 2 months out, with **average moves of +2.9% and +4.4%.**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qj9L!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F208f857a-e3a6-426b-9db7-71887bdbc73b_646x331.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qj9L!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F208f857a-e3a6-426b-9db7-71887bdbc73b_646x331.png)
