# Wednesday Morning Notes 4.24

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Big bounce back day for US stocks yesterday with the S&P 500 +1.2% and the QQQ +1.49%.  The SPX closed back above its 60 EMA after a very brief stint below the key support level.

TSLA is +13% this morning after reporting better than feared earnings yesterday afternoon.  Despite the FCF miss, the stock is likely up due to better margins + cited volume growth + comments about accelerating the launch of new models (including the more affordable models) + more color on the Robotaxi ride hailing app.

Semis also received a jolt with TXN +7% on better than expected earnings.

META earnings are the highlight after hours today along with Chipotle, ServiceNow, and Lam Research.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!d9FD!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4ce49061-6612-4af7-94ae-1fde2c2c9ccd_1290x720.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!d9FD!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4ce49061-6612-4af7-94ae-1fde2c2c9ccd_1290x720.jpeg)

We are now in the heart of earnings season and I expect stocks to go their separate ways based off individual results, which should lead to some choppiness in the major indices.  With that said, earnings gappers will be my focus in my trading account for the next few weeks.
