# Tuesday Morning Notes 11.15

Technical Analysis

We had an uneventful start to the week yesterday in the equity markets. The indices chopped around for the majority of the session before closing near lows on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!B8ZS!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faf8a2029-b987-4cc6-80c0-27656835558d_1246x937.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!B8ZS!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faf8a2029-b987-4cc6-80c0-27656835558d_1246x937.jpeg)

Now, we are set for a sizable gap higher this morning following lower than expected PPI for October (see below).

For the S&P 500, we are approaching some key technical resistance levels. None are more important than the 200 Day Moving Average at $4070-$4080. We have not closed above the 200 day moving average since the March rally, which saw a small overshoot of the resistance level.
