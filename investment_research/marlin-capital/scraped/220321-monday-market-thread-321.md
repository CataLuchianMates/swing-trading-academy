Marlin Capital Active Portfolio Strategy:

This is a crucial week for both bulls and bears. After last week’s massive rally in the Nasdaq, we have hit the 38.2% Fibonacci retracement level of the move down.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cJsr!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fef6e8d8d-ce86-436e-b5cf-b1f888004080_2047x1210.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cJsr!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fef6e8d8d-ce86-436e-b5cf-b1f888004080_2047x1210.jpeg)

Now, the question is, do we fail here or continue to rally? In either case, some backtesting should be expected here. It will be important to watch how dips are handled this week.

Last week, ARKK (my proxy for growth stocks) got its first close above the 21 EMA since this downside move began 4 months ago.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7h0e!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa56b47e2-7a28-48e7-b3b8-4f0f609376f4_1169x846.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7h0e!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa56b47e2-7a28-48e7-b3b8-4f0f609376f4_1169x846.jpeg)

This represents a short term trend change for the ETF. The first resistance level to watch above is the 50 EMA at $71.02. A second resistance level that could potentially come into play on a larger rally is the 200 EMA, currently around $94 (and dropping).

Bulls will also need to hold the 21 EMA (currently $62.94) on any backtesting. A quick break below this support level, confirmed by a close below, would point to last week’s rally being a “fakeout”, and the bounce phase likely over.

In any scenario, I am in no rush to add exposure in my Active Portfolio. We appear to be in a bounce phase, but no one can truly say how high we will bounce or how long it will last. This is why I will continue to closely monitor the price action and follow the clues that we get early in this week’s action.
