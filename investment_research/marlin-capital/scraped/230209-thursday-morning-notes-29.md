# Thursday Morning Notes 2.9

Technical Analysis

The S&P 500 has continued to trade in the 4100-$4200 consolidation range this week. Futures have popped higher following initial jobless claims.  We are set to open slightly above yesterday’s high ($4157).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vyBt!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F825cd86c-d783-4420-bfe0-d994b64ead9e_964x729.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vyBt!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F825cd86c-d783-4420-bfe0-d994b64ead9e_964x729.jpeg)

The key resistance level to watch above is $4190-$4200.

On a pullback, key support levels to watch below include $4120-$4130 and $4090-$4100.
