# Wednesday Morning Notes 4.26

Technical Update

After a seemingly endless period of chop, we saw an eventful day for stocks on Tuesday. The S&P 500 closed -1.6% and broke down below the 21 EMA.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2Q4K!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7cdee7e2-7e8d-4de0-bce5-f1498850d331_1001x769.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2Q4K!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7cdee7e2-7e8d-4de0-bce5-f1498850d331_1001x769.jpeg)

The SPX had been running out of momentum near YTD highs, but bears were previously unable to take control as the market chopped around between $4069-$4169. With yesterday’s move, the SPX closed just 2 points above the April lows.

Technically, this setup is starting to look a lot more bearish. With that said, the market is set for a gap up this morning after MSFT and GOOGL earnings. The market reaction towards today’s gap up will be very telling for what may come next.

Key support levels to watch below include $4069-$4070, $4030, and $4000.

Key resistance level to watch above include $4100, $4130-4135, $4160, and $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).
