# Tuesday Morning Notes 2.28

Technical Update

The S&P 500 continues to hover around the $4000 level and 60 EMA ($3998). The indices managed to hold on to their early morning gains yesterday to close in the green.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KcbY!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6c94a80a-9a7a-4361-a020-0cebdb865de7_875x648.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KcbY!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6c94a80a-9a7a-4361-a020-0cebdb865de7_875x648.jpeg)

In my view, the technical setup still leans bearish here. With that said, key support levels (mainly the 200 SMA at $3940) have held up so far and a larger bounce could still be in play.

We are set for a tiny gap up this morning. Key resistance levels to watch above include $3997-$4000 (60 EMA), $4020, and $$4040-$4060 (21 EMA).

Key support levels to watch below include $3970-$3975 and the critical $3940 level (200 SMA).
