**My Top Trade Idea for July**:

As Marlin Capital readers know, I believe that the largest stock moves usually stem from **upwards financial revisions**, triggered by **positive news catalysts** (earnings beats + guidance raises, partnerships, deals, new products, etc.) that shift future estimates higher. By combining these with high quality, high volume technical setups, we are able to position ourselves for outsized gains.

**July’s Top Trade Idea: OSCR**

Oscar Health (**OSCR**) is my top pick for July, showing a similar setup to **AEVA**’s [pre-June setup.](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/heading-into-june)

It starts with **MASSIVE** monthly buying volume. OSCR stock traded over 450M shares in June, its **highest volume month EVER** (by far).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mLH-!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7b6a9ab9-7597-484f-b9ee-cd1ae7208b10_1116x652.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mLH-!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7b6a9ab9-7597-484f-b9ee-cd1ae7208b10_1116x652.jpeg)

In fact, prior to May (its **2nd highest volume month ever**), the stock had never even seen >200M shares traded in a month (in its 4+ year history). OSCR’s average monthly volume (in its 4+ year history) was ~60M shares traded per month, so June represents **7x average monthly volume**. All this trading volume came in a month where OSCR stock gained +55% (its **biggest monthly gain ever**). This is a clear sign of **huge buying demand.**

On the weekly chart, the accumulation volume is apparent.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gG-Y!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe46972be-563b-4b53-a737-658811f27ab3_1112x654.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gG-Y!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe46972be-563b-4b53-a737-658811f27ab3_1112x654.jpeg)

2 weeks ago was OSCR’s **highest volume week ever** (by far) with >130M shares traded.

Last week was its **3rd highest volume week ever**.

And notice the collection of **high volume weeks** OSCR has been stacking up since this rally off May lows.

Prior to May, the stock had just 1 week in its history with >50M shares traded. Now, 5 out of the last 8 weeks have had >50M shares traded.

But this **massive buying volume** is not random (it never is). Oscar Health had a couple of **positive news catalysts** to help spark this accumulation volume since May, starting with a fantastic Q1 earnings report.

Then, on June 18th-19th, OSCR staged a >30% two day rally following news that lawmakers are proposing a new measure that would allow individuals and employers to voluntarily enroll in a new version of Medicare. Oscar Health is among the American insurance companies offering government-backed health insurance services, including Medicare and Medicaid.

This positive news has since led to **positive upwards future revisions** on FY EPS and FY Revenue of +9% and +5%, respectively.

We entered long **OSCR** on Friday at $21.03. The **positive momentum**, **upwards EPS and revenue revisions**, and **super bullish pattern** across all time frames (**+ HUGE accumulation volume**) all point to a potential run to $30+. This is my big picture target for OSCR.
