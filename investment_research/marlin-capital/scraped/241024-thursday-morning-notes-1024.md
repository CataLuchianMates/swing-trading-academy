Mega Cap Tech weighed on the tape yesterday following AAPL analyst stating iPhone 16 orders cut by around 10M units for Q4-1H ‘25. AAPL, NVDA, AMZN, META all traded down >2% yesterday.

There was also carnage in consumer with MCD -5% following the E. Coli outbreak news. SBUX held in well (closed flat) considering the negative pre-announcement and guidance cut.

After the bell, the big news was TSLA with a big gross margin beat. This is a big change from last quarter’s 200bps gross margin miss. The market is applauding TSLA’s report this morning with the stock +14% in the pre-market. This has the NDX set to open +1% higher overall.
