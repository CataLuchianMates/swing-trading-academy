# Thursday Morning Notes 8.18

Technical Analysis

We had a much needed rest day for the market yesterday with the indices pulling back in orderly fashion.  The post-Fed minutes pump was essentially erased, and we closed towards the bottom of yesterday’s range.

After 3 days this week, the indices are essentially right where they started the week. The question now is, which way do we break from here? The key support levels below to watch is the $325-$326 region (yesterday’s low). On the upside, there is resistance in the $332-$334 area (recent highs).
