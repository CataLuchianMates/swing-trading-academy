# The Week Ahead 

Hope everyone had a great Memorial Day weekend!

Last week, we saw a nice bounce in US stocks after 7 straight weeks in the red. This rally has the potential continue into this week after clearing some hurdles on Friday.

The QQQ closed above its 21 EMA and looks like it wants to test the 50 SMA at $329.

On the weekly, the SPY looks to be setting up for a potential test of the $430 resistance area.

Big picture, I believe we are in the middle of a Bear Market rally before another leg lower. These types of rallies are hallmarks of all Bear markets. Below is an analysis of the Bear market rallies we saw during the Dot Com Bust.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jlts!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb5e4786-6375-47c4-a3d7-891f70cf2244_823x392.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jlts!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb5e4786-6375-47c4-a3d7-891f70cf2244_823x392.png)

Our current rally in the Nasdaq stands at 9.9% off the lows. An 18-23% rally, in line with the types we saw during the Dot Com Bust, would take us to the $13k-$13.5k region in the Nasdaq composite. This would represent another 9%+ upside from here.
