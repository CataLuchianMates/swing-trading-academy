**Active Portfolio Strategy and Watchlist**

Here at Marlin Capital, we have fully embraced the [Small Cap Winter theme](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/small-cap-winter). Our 2 biggest swing trades on the year have come from this cohort: OSS ($260mm mkt cap) and MRAM ($320mm mkt cap).

But now, I believe we may be looking at some mean reversion after a record streak of Small Cap outperformance (14 straight days, longest since 1996). By late last week, we saw some potential signs of exhaustion (and a reversal) in Small Caps. We felt that in our Small Cap holdings pulling back sharply.

Right now, I am not looking to be overly aggressive heading into this week (at least, not at the start). If we see strong earnings results from the Mega Caps reporting this week (META, TSLA, MSFT, AAPL), we could see a further rotation out of Small Caps and back into Mega Caps.

On the other hand, if we see disappointing results from the Mega Caps, a broad market selloff would likely ensue (and while Small Caps would likely outperform, it would still be a general “risk off” event).

Additionally, I am not seeing many clean long setups heading into the week of 1/26. I will talk about the top names I’m watching this week below, but I do not see any of them as currently actionable at this moment. For these reasons, I will not looking to add any more long exposure at the beginning of this week.
