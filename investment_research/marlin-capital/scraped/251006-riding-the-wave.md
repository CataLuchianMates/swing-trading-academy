**Big Picture Thoughts**

There’s a lot of chatter that we must be nearing the top of this Bull Market

I want to reiterate my view that we are currently in an AI Bubble, fueled by widespread optimism about the transformative potential of AI (and massive CapEx boom).

History shows that massive technological advancements (like railroads, the internet, etc.) often lead to asset bubbles. This is because massive technological advancements create unprecedented opportunities for individual companies (and the economy).

In times of great optimism and exuberance, Bubbles will form. Then, at some point, for some reason, a switch will flip, and the Bubble will burst (the bust phase). This is a fundamental truth of market cycles, which have been occurring since the Dutch Tulip Mania of 1634 (the first widely recognized asset bubble).

A great trader/investor embraces the market cycle. George Soros once said: “When I see a bubble forming, I rush in to buy, adding fuel to the fire.”

This is why the title of tonight’s report is “Riding the Wave”. We are embracing the Bubble. We are riding the wave. We will embrace the Bust phase, too, when that time comes.

But that time is not today, in my view. History shows that our current Bull Market is still fairly young in length. This Bull Market started in April, and is now just ~6 months old. The shortest Bull Market since 1960 was ~15 months long (Dec ‘18 - Mar ‘20 Covid Crash).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iKTt!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6fde7e54-5d3a-4f84-8210-45aec9e8e1cb_750x758.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iKTt!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6fde7e54-5d3a-4f84-8210-45aec9e8e1cb_750x758.png)

Meanwhile, the Fed is cutting rates in a roaring Bull Market. That’s not a recipe for a topping market. In fact, it’s a recipe for a continued market rally.

The Fed has cut rates with the S&P 500 within 1% of ATHs 16 times in its history. **The S&P 500 was higher a year later all 16 times for an average return of +15%.**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Efy5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb7bcdf6-2450-49b8-9311-03006b95b973_898x578.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Efy5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb7bcdf6-2450-49b8-9311-03006b95b973_898x578.jpeg)

Positioning is neutral, and far from euphoric. Again, not a recipe for a topping market.

**-0.3z total positioning is not consistent with where prior Bull Markets have peaked.** In fact, every Bull Market since 2015 saw Goldman’s Positioning Indicator reach at least +1.8z before peaking. I do not expect this cycle to be any different.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!es5q!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4878b50c-91c7-4a65-8f1f-2691f93e5083_1267x1209.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!es5q!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4878b50c-91c7-4a65-8f1f-2691f93e5083_1267x1209.png)

And as [I’ve talked about before](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/bubbling-up?utm_source=publication-search), market tops are typically a long process. Historically, it begins with the most speculative stocks topping first. We are not seeing that right now with unprofitable companies actually outperforming (unprofitable tech +8% last week). The Russell 2000 Index of Small Caps is breaking out of a 4+ year base. This is a “risk on” indicator.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c9VA!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7bd053e9-1e66-4b27-a789-3a1b017bdf13_1135x612.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c9VA!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7bd053e9-1e66-4b27-a789-3a1b017bdf13_1135x612.jpeg)
