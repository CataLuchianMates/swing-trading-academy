# Friday Morning Notes 11.18

Technical Analysis

The market pulled back yesterday, but was still able to hold key support levels. The low of the day in the SPX was $3906, as bears fumbled near the key $3900 level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9FUM!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F88a9965e-e3f5-42a6-a75c-5951fe186f08_1173x881.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9FUM!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F88a9965e-e3f5-42a6-a75c-5951fe186f08_1173x881.jpeg)

Now, we are set for a gap up this morning towards the $3970 level. Key resistance levels to watch ahead include $4000, $4030 (high of week), and $4060-$4070 (200 SMA).

Key support levels below include $3900, $3850-$3860 (21 and 60 EMAs), and $3800.
