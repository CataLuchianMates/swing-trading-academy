# Thursday Morning Notes 6.1

Technical Update

The S&P 500 pulled back -0.61% on Wednesday, as the index has (so far) failed to meaningfully break above key $4200 resistance.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2KL2!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F659ee755-b9f8-408c-9dc2-b9f095e3820d_964x720.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2KL2!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F659ee755-b9f8-408c-9dc2-b9f095e3820d_964x720.jpeg)

Despite the big May rally in tech/semis/AI, the S&P 500 finished the month flat (+0.3%). 7 S&P 500 sectors finished the month down more than 4%, while the Dow Jones rose just 5 times in May (the fewest gains in a month since 2012).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KeX_!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa5a2c932-edb5-4aa3-a6ea-89f67f8a724a_1170x861.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KeX_!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa5a2c932-edb5-4aa3-a6ea-89f67f8a724a_1170x861.jpeg)

As tech/AI/semis have pulled back off severely overbought conditions to start this week, we have not seen a meaningful rotation to the rest of the market and recent laggards. Breadth is exceedingly narrow for a healthy uptrend.

Meanwhile, the QQQ got extended as much as 7% above its 21 EMA before beginning the recent 2 day pullback.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zvFR!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2877660d-2074-4a0c-a08f-59f78e7a7e03_958x718.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zvFR!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2877660d-2074-4a0c-a08f-59f78e7a7e03_958x718.jpeg)

The recent action here looks like a short term top and a pullback to $340 could be in the cards.

For the SPX, $4200 remains the key resistance level to watch on the upside.

Key support levels to watch include $4150-$4160, $4130, and $4090-$4100.
