# Thursday Morning Notes 4.13

Technical Update

Yesterday’s gap up open was met with heavy selling pressure as the S&P 500 failed to break out above key resistance.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MQ1P!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa2f72b9c-f281-47a4-91c2-55d87332de61_968x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MQ1P!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa2f72b9c-f281-47a4-91c2-55d87332de61_968x726.jpeg)

In yesterday’s morning notes, I wrote about how it was critical for the index to decisively break out above $4135. Yesterday’s high was $4134.37 as sellers showed up right at key resistance. We also closed below the key support level in $4100.

We are set for a small gap up this morning. Technically, this set up can break either way now. We have the beginnings of a potential topping pattern forming over the last week, but it is not yet confirmed.

Key support levels to watch below include $4075-$4080, $4050, and $3990-$4000.

Key resistance levels above include $4100, $4130-$4135, $4150-$4160 and YTD highs at $4190-$4200.
