Technical Analysis

What a difference a day makes! At yesterday’s close, things were looking very bearish and I was prepared to cover my favorite short setups in this morning’s notes. As I have discussed before, though, technicals mean next to nothing in the face of market moving news events.

After today’s lower than expected CPI number, the indices are set to gap up between +3-4%. We have not seen such a downside surprise in CPI recently, as the data has typically been surprising to the upside. My point is, this squeeze has the potential to be a violent one (and is already beginning with today’s monster gap).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ougS!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8ebb11b2-3d95-4b56-8900-117f220b202d_955x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ougS!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8ebb11b2-3d95-4b56-8900-117f220b202d_955x723.jpeg)

The QQQ is set to gap above yesterday’s high. The key now for bulls will be holding today’s gap over the next few days.

Key resistance levels ahead include $278-$281. On the downside, $270-$271 are the key support levels to watch below.
