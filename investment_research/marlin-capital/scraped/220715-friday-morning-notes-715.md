# Friday Morning Notes 7.15

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday, another morning gap down was bought up after some dovish Fed speaker comments (Waller & Bullard) on 75/100 BPs for July. We came into the day with 100 BPs nearly 100% priced in, and ended it as a 50/50 toss up. In the QQQ, $279.8 marked the low of the day as the $280 support level once again held up. This time, we closed near highs. $288-$289 is the key resistance level to watch today.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nZEo!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb765db3f-b0fa-47a9-a2b6-19febf36047d_1013x619.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nZEo!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb765db3f-b0fa-47a9-a2b6-19febf36047d_1013x619.jpeg)

If the $288-$289 resistance level breaks, I plan to potentially enter TQQQ for a long trade (above $25.85). On the 1 hour chart, it becomes a lot more clear why I am more interested in this rally attempt than the last. On this attempt, the TQQQ was able to close above the 60 EMA, which is a key indicator I like to use to assess the medium term price trend.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!o-MD!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F400058f9-10d0-4a71-ac8d-fc97b47f39fc_1041x645.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!o-MD!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F400058f9-10d0-4a71-ac8d-fc97b47f39fc_1041x645.jpeg)

We are still below the key EMAs (8, 21, etc.) on the daily chart, so I will continue to tread lightly on these counter trend trades.
