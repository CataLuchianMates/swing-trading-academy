# Friday Morning Notes 5.26

Technical Update

The S&P 500 rallied yesterday on the backs of NVDA earnings, finishing the day +0.88%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4iVl!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb654370e-a8f1-4740-868b-24c090e4000a_1191x892.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4iVl!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb654370e-a8f1-4740-868b-24c090e4000a_1191x892.jpeg)

Mega Cap tech continues to drive essentially all the SPX gains in 2023. In fact, the S&P 493 (excluding the magnificent 7) is up 1% YTD.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!o0i0!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3098bd02-7d2e-428e-b803-f57f030df396_1510x1124.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!o0i0!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3098bd02-7d2e-428e-b803-f57f030df396_1510x1124.jpeg)

The AI theme continues to drive basically all stock gains this year, as the number of stocks outperforming the SPX has dropped to new lows.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hy2U!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb6403378-70cd-41a9-b19f-9e7e7b3e87f9_1144x640.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hy2U!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb6403378-70cd-41a9-b19f-9e7e7b3e87f9_1144x640.jpeg)

For the SPX, the technical setup remains bullish as we are back above 21 EMA support after yesterday’s move. The key resistance level to watch above remains the $4200 level and YTD highs.

Key support levels to watch below include $4130-$4140 and $4090-$4100.

Full detailed analysis coming this weekend.

The stock market is closed Monday for Memorial Day. I would like to wish all Marlin Capital readers a very happy Memorial Day Weekend!
