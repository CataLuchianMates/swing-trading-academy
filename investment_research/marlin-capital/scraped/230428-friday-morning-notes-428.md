# Friday Morning Notes 4.28

Technical Update

The market rallied big yesterday following the morning gap up, with the S&P 500 closing +1.96% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!s9pl!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F967e0bcf-b179-4239-b612-39c626bf94ad_1184x881.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!s9pl!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F967e0bcf-b179-4239-b612-39c626bf94ad_1184x881.jpeg)

The index is now back above the 21 EMA after spending just 2 days below.

As of now, this appears to be another failed breakdown attempt by bears. Buyers stepped in at key support to buy the dip. We are now right back in the middle of the range we have spent all of April in.

Key resistance level to watch above include $4150-4160 and $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).

Key support levels to watch below include $4130, $4100, $4040-$4030 and $4000.
