Ilunina still owns a 12% stake in GRAL. Google, Tencent, Bristol Myers Squibb, Johnson & Johnson, Merck, and Quest Diagnostics all own shares/are partners as well. And Jeff Bezos and Bill Gates were early investors in the company.

This is an explosive looking setup for GRAL, a name now getting recognized for its immense potential in AI Cancer Detection.
