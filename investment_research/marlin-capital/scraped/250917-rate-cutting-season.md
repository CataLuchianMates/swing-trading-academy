# Rate Cutting Season

Tomorrow, the Fed will cut interest rates with the S&P 500 at all-time highs.

#### Rate Cuts at ATHs: A Perfect Track Record

The Fed has cut rates with the S&P 500 within 1% of ATHs 16 times in its history.

**The S&P 500 was higher a year later all 16 times for an average return of +15%.**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Efy5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb7bcdf6-2450-49b8-9311-03006b95b973_898x578.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Efy5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb7bcdf6-2450-49b8-9311-03006b95b973_898x578.jpeg)

Stretch that to SPX within 2% of ATHs, and it’s happened 20 times.

**Again, all 20 times, the S&P 500 was higher a year later.**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Zq5s!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F35d0f4c2-ec02-4176-9105-c3c453eb8838_975x675.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Zq5s!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F35d0f4c2-ec02-4176-9105-c3c453eb8838_975x675.png)

*h/t Ryan Detrick*

These types of returns (+14-15% avg) are consistent with the “Fed resuming rate cuts in a non-recessionary environment” narrative.

Again, the Fed will be restarting rate cuts after a 6+ month pause. So long as we steer clear of a recession, this is a very bullish signal historically (and aligns with that +15% avg 1yr return).
