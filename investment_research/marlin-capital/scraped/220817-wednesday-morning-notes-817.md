# Wednesday Morning Notes 8.17

Technical Analysis:

Markets had a choppy day yesterday with the SPY and QQQ both closing near flat on the day. The S&P closed just below its 200 day MA ($431.5), while the QQQ remains ~3% below its 200 day ($342). Participation weakened yesterday with fewer net highs across the board. The strongest group in the market remains highly shorted stocks, as I noted in this weekend’s report (BBBY +400% last 1M). Yesterday’s action in BBBY gave some blow off top vibes with the stock up 75% at 2 PM and closing +29%.

In the past, when we have seen meme stocks start to run 75 and 80% in a day, it has been a sign of euphoria and means a pullback is usually not far away. The 2 recent instances of such market action (referenced in chart below) are Meme stock mania #1 and the March 2021 Bear Market rally. Meme stock mania #1 was followed by an immediate 5% pullback in the indices, and a 12% pullback a couple weeks later. The March 2021 Bear market rally was followed by new lows in 1 month.
