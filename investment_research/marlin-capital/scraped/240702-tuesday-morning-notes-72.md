# Tuesday Morning Notes 7.2

Daily Market Commentary:

US stocks kicked off July on strong footing with the S&P 500 up +0.27% and NDX +0.7%.

Yesterday’s rally came despite a sharp 2-day 20bps move higher in 10yr yields and lighter market volumes, matching July seasonal patterns with a bounce in Mega Cap Tech (+1.38%) and momentum winners (+0.59%).  Reminder the Nasdaq has been positive for 16 straight July’s (avg return = 4.64%) with the best days of the year July 1st (92%), July 2nd/3rd (75%), and July 5th (78%).

Breadth was narrow again yesterday (60% of the NDX was down on the day despite the index finishing +0.7%).  We saw some sharp moves higher in MDB, ROKU, RNG, and TSLA.  TSLA traded up +6% ahead of delivery numbers this morning.  The stock is set for another +6% gap higher this morning following delivery numbers (444k vehicles delivered).  Side note – we are long TSLA from $194.88 in the Marlin Quant – Core Portfolio which we are now offering to all Marlin Capital readers for $129.99 per month (real time trade alerts for TQQQ, SOXL, TNA, TSLA, and KWEB quant trading systems).

Updates on my trades –

NVO – Continuing to grind higher and in my view, we are on our way to $150 pretty soon here.  My closing stop is set below $138 to ensure no loss on this position.

MSFT – Nice day yesterday after a shakeout Friday/Monday morning with the stock closing at new ATHs.  My closing stop is set below $441.

ORCL – Pivoted at the 8 EMA and now moving its way towards ATHs.  My closing stop is set below $137.5.  First target = $150.

My Top trade setup for today –

AVGO – This is setting up nicely after a multi-day pullback to the 21 EMA post earnings.  Support was found multiple times last week in the $1560-$1570 region.
