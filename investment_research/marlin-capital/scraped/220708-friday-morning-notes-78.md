# Friday Morning Notes 7.8

Technical Analysis (from yesterday post-market):

It was a big day for the market as we broke yesterday’s highs in the morning and didn’t look back. For the QQQ, on the 15 minute chart, we rode the 8 EMA higher all day. I took profits on half my TQQQ position as we are running into some resistance here (the 50 Day MA and late June highs). We also have a possible cup and handle forming on the 15 minute, which would suggest a short term pullback.

7/8 Morning Update (post Jobs Report):

\*US Labor Jun Nonfarm Payrolls +372K; Consensus +250K

\*US Jun Unemployment Rate 3.6%; Consensus 3.6%

My thoughts:

June jobs numbers released this morning were much stronger than expected.  This is a good reminder of how far the Fed is from pivoting, which the market was temporarily ignoring.  It’s also a good reminder of the fact that any economic data point that is released can be the “rally killer”, because it can serve as a sobering reminder of what lies ahead of us.  In my upcoming report to be released this weekend, I will cover why I believe the unemployment rate will be able to tell us when inflation will meaningfully decline, and when the Fed might realistically pivot.

Let’s see if the market can rally off this gap down or we get a bigger reversal.  For now, the stop on my half TQQQ position will be moved up to $26.36, below yesterday’s lows, and ensuring no loss on the rest of this position.
