# Tuesday Morning Notes 12.10

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Mixed start to the week yesterday with the SPX finishing -0.61%. Underneath the surface, we saw some interesting moves out there.

Goldman’s US Momentum Pair (GSP1MOMO -4.6%) had one of its worst days in nearly 2 years yesterday as the big Q4/YTD winners were unwound and shorts squeezed.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KM08!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1fcd4990-18ea-4613-b2f7-5d0651493d77_509x285.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KM08!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1fcd4990-18ea-4613-b2f7-5d0651493d77_509x285.jpeg)

High Beta Momentum finished the day -7.4%, its worst session since 2/2/23 and among the worst sessions in the last 5 years.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!sj5M!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2f3d9a43-82a7-40f3-a58b-1bafb4a35d9d_1290x993.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!sj5M!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2f3d9a43-82a7-40f3-a58b-1bafb4a35d9d_1290x993.jpeg)

Both legs of the Momentum pair moved the opposite way, with Momentum Longs (12M Winners) finishing -4.5% while Momentum Shorts (12M Losers) finished +1.5%. Here’s a crazy stat to sum up yesterday’s action underneath the surface - All 26 of the Russell 1000 stocks that were up +100% on the year through last Friday were down yesterday, and they were down an average of more than -5%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!GRpL!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6cd0cc0a-f126-4d52-8e00-9d30007edd10_516x612.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!GRpL!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6cd0cc0a-f126-4d52-8e00-9d30007edd10_516x612.png)

As for the catalyst for the move, people pointed to the combo of APP (-15%) not being added to the S&P 500 (as we know, APP was a monster momentum mover); NVDA -2.5% on Chinese regulatory scrutiny; and the correction in Crypto names (COIN -9%, MSTR -8%, Ethereum -7%, etc.).
