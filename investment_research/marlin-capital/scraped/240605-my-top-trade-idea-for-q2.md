# My Top Trade Idea for Q2

I highlighted this stock as my top trade idea of the week yesterday.  With today’s strong action and close on increasing volume (as well as big buying in the Options Market and A+ fundamental & industry catalysts) , I am upgrading this trade idea to my top pick for Q2. I added to my position today.

Novo Nordisk (NVO) is my top trade idea for Q2.

In the Healthcare sector, one area has really grabbed Wall Street’s attention this year: Obesity Drugs.

The booming market and potential for massive growth for Obesity Drugs has led to big rallies in the stocks of the 2 manufacturers leading the market: Novo Nordisk (NVO), producer of Ozempic and Wegovy, and Eli Lilly (LLY), which manufactures Mounjaro and Zepbound. Both stocks are posting their biggest YTD gains (so far) since 1997.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6PGg!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F080c9622-cb8d-4dbc-902e-535e22789015_1244x689.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6PGg!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F080c9622-cb8d-4dbc-902e-535e22789015_1244x689.jpeg)

Wegovy (NVO) and Zepbound (LLY) belong to a class of drugs called GLP-1 agonists developed for type 2 diabetes that reduce food cravings and cause the stomach to empty more slowly.  The new treatments have been shown to reduce weight by as much as 20%.

Goldman Sachs expects the global sales of Weight Loss drugs to reach $130B by 2030, an increase from their prior $100B forecast. This would be a >50% CAGR.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Zbz1!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3b8b2d97-2b58-464b-9392-c89742195ca6_1284x870.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Zbz1!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3b8b2d97-2b58-464b-9392-c89742195ca6_1284x870.jpeg)

The overall market for the GLP-1s, which are also used to treat Type 2 diabetes, is seen as even larger (~$170B by 2031).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!TbzM!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F409884dc-24b7-4a10-ace3-682bc3b69399_608x662.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!TbzM!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F409884dc-24b7-4a10-ace3-682bc3b69399_608x662.png)

According to Goldman, the 2 leading commercial players, Eli Lilly and Novo Nordisk, will likely sustain their dominant share and market leadership.

Goldman estimates that the 2 companies (Novo and Lilly) currently have a combined ~80% share of the market, and the firm expects the 2 players to retain that ~ 80% share through 2030, building on their vast scientific understanding, their ability to continue capitalizing on their first-to-market advantages, and their competitive moats.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yoWy!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F95303127-f689-4fa5-8c6e-26684b697d82_823x535.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yoWy!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F95303127-f689-4fa5-8c6e-26684b697d82_823x535.png)

Conover and Andersen forecasts that over 25% of obese Americans and 15% of overweight Americans will receive treatment in 10 years, and the vast majority will receive branded GLP-1 therapies. They predict the bulk of those sales will go to Novo and Lilly.

Drugmakers are also testing these drugs for other health benefits such as lowering cardiovascular disease risk.  In particular, clinical trial data (SELECT) suggests that losing weight with Wegovy (NVO) can reduce the risk of cardiovascular events, which remain the number 1 cause of death globally, giving Novo a critical competitive edge.

As mentioned, this has been 1 of the hottest industries on Wall Street in 2024. While the SPX is consolidating/chopping around, we have already seen LLY and NVO break out to new ATHs. I believe these stocks are showing strong leadership potential on a technical basis. NVO and LLY chart below.
