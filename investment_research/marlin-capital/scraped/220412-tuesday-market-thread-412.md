Technical Analysis

The QQQ broke down yesterday, and is now firmly below all key moving averages.

In the near term, we are getting extended below these key MAs. We also have 2 unfilled gaps above.

We are set for a gap up this morning. It will be important to watch how the market reacts to this gap up. If it can hold, a bounce to the $355 area in the QQQ is possible.

This stock gave a false breakdown under $50 yesterday, followed by a low volume pump. I will take the loss here with a close above the 50 EMA. This is also why I like to start small in short trades.

Specifically for HCP, I am interested in the $60 July Puts if I can buy them under $10. The puts will be much easier to hold for several months than the stock, as you are able to define your risk at the beginning of the trade. The July puts would capture HashiCorp’s lockup expiration, which I see as a big potential catalyst for selling if the stocks remains near these prices.
