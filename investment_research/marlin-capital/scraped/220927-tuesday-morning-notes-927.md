# Tuesday Morning Notes 9.27

Technical Analysis

The market experienced choppy action yesterday to kick off the week. A weak close ensured the indices finished in negative territory. On that 15 minute time frame, we have the beginnings of a base, indicating a further bounce could be ahead.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lAs7!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F90a05839-88fd-4fc3-a845-d52e5ef64b38_1361x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lAs7!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F90a05839-88fd-4fc3-a845-d52e5ef64b38_1361x653.png)

Now, the indices are set for a large gap up this morning. This is why timing is crucial and late shorts will be punished by violent counter-trend rallies.

The first key resistance levels ahead include $280 and $284-$285 (daily 8 EMA). With positioning so lopsided to the short side (most puts bought ever on Friday), we may have the beginnings of a large, multi-day squeeze in the market.
