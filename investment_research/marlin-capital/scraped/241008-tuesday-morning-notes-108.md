# Tuesday Morning Notes 10.8

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Broader indices traded lower to kick off the week yesterday as geopolitical tensions continue to lead to a more risk off environment.  The S&P 500 closed -0.96% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!CCqC!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F93524c91-1ac2-4195-bdb2-e38f4ef804b6_964x714.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!CCqC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F93524c91-1ac2-4195-bdb2-e38f4ef804b6_964x714.jpeg)

The 10YR yield traded back above 4% yesterday while the VIX is now >22.  It was overall a quiet day with total market volumes tracking down -14% vs 20dma.  The indices appear to be rangebound into Thursday’s CPI release.

GOOGL was -2.5% on antitrust case headlines.  SMCI finished +15% following a bullish press release (+ some short covering).  NVDA +2% (sell-side roadshow this week likely contributing).

Earnings kick off on Friday.  October is also known for surprise pre-announcements so I will be keeping an eye on any that we see and flagging them in the daily morning notes.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nBv8!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fabf3239e-1c1f-48d9-b0ae-d85d44628748_989x1056.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nBv8!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fabf3239e-1c1f-48d9-b0ae-d85d44628748_989x1056.jpeg)

Street consensus for SPX EPS growth in Q3 is +4% YoY.  The bad is this is the highest bar we have seen in the past 9 quarters.  The good news is the actual has beat consensus every single quarter since Q3 2022.

WGS – Another big winner from my [top Small Caps picks](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-small-caps-with-massive-upside)!  Huge move here in the last couple weeks and the stock is trading above $56 this morning.  We are now up +70% on this stock.  Similar to SEZL, I will look to lock in some gains at +100%.

Active Portfolio –

No changes for me here.  I’m holding SN, NMM, and MU into Tuesday with stops in place.
