# Tuesday Morning Notes 6.6

Technical Update

The S&P 500 finished down -0.2% to kick off the week yesterday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!f0SE!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa5a1ed8e-49fa-44eb-b8f7-fda4b4573196_1046x786.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!f0SE!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa5a1ed8e-49fa-44eb-b8f7-fda4b4573196_1046x786.jpeg)

The index hit $4300 resistance before reversing and closing near lows. We saw some reversion of last Friday’s trade (the largest 1 day outperformance of small caps vs big tech in 2 years). The Nasdaq was the strongest of the major indices yesterday, finishing -0.09% on the day.

Some consolidation/rest was much needed for small caps after a big 2 day move. Technically, the long setup is much more attractive now heading into Tuesday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!s7Zl!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8bb23407-66b2-42cf-8dfd-1ef6ac2c2de2_959x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!s7Zl!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8bb23407-66b2-42cf-8dfd-1ef6ac2c2de2_959x726.jpeg)

Ideally, we would see small caps rest/consolidate for another day or 2. With that said, yesterday’s Inside Day candle in IWO creates a nice low risk entry.

The long setup is an entry on the break of the inside day highs (above $233.10) with a stop loss placed below the inside day lows (below $230.10). Total risk is just 1.3% on this trade. A break below $230.10 first invalidates this setup and makes it more likely we need more rest/consolidation and a potential gap fill.

ARKK - still holding long here from Friday’s entry ($41.6). Stop remains below $40.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9_qs!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F22241914-d568-46b3-a10e-0f01c517c888_962x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9_qs!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F22241914-d568-46b3-a10e-0f01c517c888_962x726.jpeg)

First target = $44-$45
