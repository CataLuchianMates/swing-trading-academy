# Wednesday Morning Notes 10.12

Technical Analysis

The market broke lower once again yesterday, with the indices both finishing in the red for the 3rd straight day. This is ordinarily where I would look for a bounce after 3 straight days of heavy selling and price getting extended below the key moving averages. With that said, we have the Fed minutes to be released today and CPI to be released tomorrow. Technical indicators matter much less in the face of big market moving news events.
