Short ARKK (3/4ths position)

---

The close of this week is of great importance for the technical setup in ARKK.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c5Ub!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5cd939f9-1ec9-4539-80cc-0267c57cefdc_967x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c5Ub!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5cd939f9-1ec9-4539-80cc-0267c57cefdc_967x726.jpeg)

This weekly chart is showing no signs of a bottom. The technical setup continues to point towards new lows in the coming weeks.

However, there are still 2 days left in the week. If ARKK closes near this week’s highs (above $90), the weekly will look much different with a strong hammer candle.

If ARKK closes out the week near its current price, or worse yet lower, there is little doubt that more downside is coming.

I will be closely watching to see how ARKK closes out the week, and if growth is still decoupled from the indices.

As for the Nasdaq, it is undergoing a bounce attempt after a fast & violent downwards move. The index is in a downtrend/consolidation phase, so I will continue to watch from the sidelines in cash rather than jump right in. There is still much work to be done in the Nasdaq to prove that what we are currently seeing is more than an oversold bounce.
