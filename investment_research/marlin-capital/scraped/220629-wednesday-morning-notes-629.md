# Wednesday Morning Notes 6.29

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday was an ugly day for the market as we broke, and closed below, the key support levels I watching (8 and 21 EMA). Originally, I was looking for a bounce to fail at the 21 EMA, followed by a re-test of lows. We overshot the 21 EMA, but didn’t last very long above it. We never got any type of bullish EMA crossover or trend change on this bounce attempt. Now, if we break $280 support, it should be followed by a re-test of lows ($270).
