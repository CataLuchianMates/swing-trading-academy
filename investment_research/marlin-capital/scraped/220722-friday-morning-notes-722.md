# Friday Morning Notes 7.22

Technical Analysis:

Another bullish day for the market on Thursday, as each and every intraday dip continues to get bought up. The QQQ closed above the key $306-$307 resistance level I have discussed. Now, the next key resistance level to watch is the $312-$314 area.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RKas!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0f97208c-6b14-4a1b-8aed-bc63fb890052_881x658.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RKas!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0f97208c-6b14-4a1b-8aed-bc63fb890052_881x658.jpeg)

Short term, it’s been a big move up for the market and it would be logical for some rest/consolidation. However, that is not always how it plays out in violent squeezes/bear market rallies. For me, the best trade setups remain on a short term basis (1-3 days). I will be focusing on TQQQ for these types of trades. These shorter term TQQQ trades will be sized heavier than an average position as there will be a tight stop within 4-5% of entry. I will be shooting for 8-15% gains on these quick trades.
