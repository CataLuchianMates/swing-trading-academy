# Friday Morning Notes 11.11

Technical Analysis

The market staged a massive rally post-CPI yesterday, with the indices closing +5% and +7% respectively. To put yesterday’s move in historical perspective, it is the 14th largest 1D rally in the S&P 500 since 1990.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!if7Z!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc359d90a-b375-4e67-bd55-54c9d8acc071_653x700.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!if7Z!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc359d90a-b375-4e67-bd55-54c9d8acc071_653x700.png)

Notice how low the VIX is currently compared to the other days on the list. Also note that the only bigger 1D gains on the list have also occurred during Bear Markets.

On a technical level, we have cleared all key short term resistance levels. A trip to the 200 Day Moving average at $4080 is looking more and more likely. This is also where the previous 2 rallies have failed.
