# Let the Games Begin

Very little movement at the index level today with the SPX +0.06%, NDX +0.5%, and RTY -0.91%.

Underneath the hood, many single stocks were under pressure including momentum (GSPRHIMO finished -5%). The AI universe struggled following the WSJ story that SoftBank and OpenAI’s $500B AI project is struggling to get off the ground.

We came into Tuesday well prepared for further momo pressure. We de-risked yesterday, **locking in wins on APH (+40%), OKLO (+8.4), and IREN (+6.7%), exiting TMC for breakeven, and taking the small loss on RGTI (-2.8%).**

The hardest part about trading is the selling part, not the buying part. “It is no trick at all to be right on the market.” - Jesse Livermore.

All that matters is how much you make when you’re right, and how much you lose when you’re wrong. I had an old boss on a the trading floor who would say “until you sell, it’s NOT your money”. This is the truth, as the market can always take those paper profits right back from you.

All eyes are on TSLA and GOOGL earnings after market close tomorrow now. These are the first of the Mag 7 names to report.
