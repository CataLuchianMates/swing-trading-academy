# Thursday Morning Notes 6.30

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday was a choppy day with a lot of back and forth action. Less is better when it comes to trading in this environment.

We are back in the 15 minute range in the QQQ, and now appear to be forming a possible bearish head and shoulders pattern (as well as a Bear flag). If we clear the top of the range ($286), things would start to look more bullish. The bottom of the range ($278-$280) is the last support before 52 week lows.
