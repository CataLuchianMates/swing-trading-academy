# Wednesday Morning Notes 4.3

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Equities were under pressure on Tuesday (SPX -0.72%, NDX -0.94%, and RTY -1.92%) as the velocity of the move higher in rates week to date is making investors slightly uncomfortable.  The 10Y yield is now up to 4.35% (I believe a move above > 4.5% could spell more immediate trouble for stocks).

Risk appetite was lower with Crypto and Non-Profitable Tech underperforming (while defensives outperformed).  Retail as a group was -2.8% and had its worst trading day of the year (think NKE, LULU, etc.).

Technically, the SPX has simply pulled back to its 21 EMA on the daily.  This is the 9th test of support during this uptrend that began last November.  The previous 8 pull ins found support at the red line (21 EMA) in the chart below.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-hQE!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff618045b-c90e-47b4-bb08-f76fc3081671_977x727.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-hQE!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff618045b-c90e-47b4-bb08-f76fc3081671_977x727.jpeg)

The key support level to watch on the SPX today is $5170-$5180.  A decisive close below $5170 opens the door for a larger pull in to the 60 EMA at $5022.

COJN and MU – still holding for now.  Moving stop on MU to breakeven ($117.10).
