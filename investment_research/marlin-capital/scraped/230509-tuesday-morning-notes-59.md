# Tuesday Morning Notes 5.9

Technical Update

The S&P 500 closed flat (+0.05%) to kick off the week yesterday. We are set for a small gap down in the index this morning.

No progress yet for either side (bulls or bears) as the chop continues.

Key resistance level to watch above include $4140-$4150 and $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).

Key support levels to watch below include $4100-$4110 (21 EMA), $4056-$4060 (60 EMA), $4040-$4030 and $4000.
