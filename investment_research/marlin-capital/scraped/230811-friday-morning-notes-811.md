# Friday Morning Notes 8.11

Technical Update

The S&P 500 failed to hold its morning gains yesterday after the post-CPI gap up. The index was rejected near 8 and 21 EMA resistance at $4500 and closed roughly flat on the day ( $4468).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8H4r!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff7abe267-f257-449a-b888-23ae1dbe04a3_978x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8H4r!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff7abe267-f257-449a-b888-23ae1dbe04a3_978x723.jpeg)

We are set for a gap down this morning. Key support levels to watch below include $4420 and $4400 (the 60 EMA). I will be observing how the index reacts at key support before making any new portfolio moves. No trades planned today.
