## David Marlin Q&A (Comments)

**Q:** Hello David, thank you for your daily update post.
1) can you explain what is "5/1 breath" and "volume is 5/1 bullish"? 
2) You mentioned "this move moves too far too quickly", do you mean it will be the second leg down or this Monday is higher chance to be the bottom?

**David Marlin:** This passage was written by Fundstrat, a research organization. 5/1 advancers to decliners means for every 1 stock that declined yesterday (were in the red), 5 stocks advanced (were in the green).  The move being too quick, according to Fundstrat, suggests there will be a re-test of Monday’s lows.  Essentially, Fundstrat suggests waiting for that re-test of Monday’s lows before buying.
