# Wednesday Morning Notes 9.6

Technical Update

The S&P 500 pulled back small to kick off the week yesterday, closing -0.42% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OvC-!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F46b6782e-5068-4519-aa28-863c60fdbb7b_1195x888.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OvC-!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F46b6782e-5068-4519-aa28-863c60fdbb7b_1195x888.jpeg)

We came into the week extended from some key EMAs after a big move to close last week, so a pullback into support is not surprising. Key support levels to watch below include the 8 EMA at $4470-$4480, $4460, and $4400-$4420.

CLS - I came into the week looking to enter long on a pullback and the stock gave us just that yesterday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Sx_K!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5669545a-a575-4277-a5f8-6df0936ad855_970x733.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Sx_K!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5669545a-a575-4277-a5f8-6df0936ad855_970x733.jpeg)

I used yesterday’s pullback to the 8 EMA at $22.85 as my opportunity to get long. With this buy, I’m now holding CLS, UPWK, ROVR, and COUR with stops in place.
