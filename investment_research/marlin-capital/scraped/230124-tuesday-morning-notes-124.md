# Tuesday Morning Notes 1.24

Technical Analysis

Another big rally for the market yesterday as we took out and closed above recent highs ($4015).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LAn3!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0c051810-60c1-4981-a500-6d68456c42dc_1201x906.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LAn3!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0c051810-60c1-4981-a500-6d68456c42dc_1201x906.jpeg)

We are set for a gap down this morning. The technical setup remains bullish for an upcoming test of December highs ($4100).

Resistance levels above include $4050, $4080, and $4100.

Key support levels below include $4000, $3970, $3920-$3930 and $3890-$3900.
