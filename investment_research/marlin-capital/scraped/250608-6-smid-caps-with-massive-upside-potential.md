Elon Musk sees AGI reducing biological intelligence to <1% of total intelligence by ~2035, while Sam Altman predicts full AGI by 2030. **The Impact:**

Autonomous vehicles will reduce traffic fatalities by 90%, AI-driven diagnostics will double cancer survival rates, and data centers will handle 80% of global compute. The $16 Trillion AI market (30% CAGR) spans every industry, but most notably:

**Transport:** Robotaxis and autonomous vehicles will reduce traffic fatalities by 90% and cut commuting costs by 50%.

**Healthcare:** AI will detect 80% of cancers at stage 1, saving millions.

**Infrastructure**: Data centers will run 90% of AI applications.

**Work:** Robots will automate 40%+ of logistics, boosting GDP.

**Top Down Analysis**

Before getting into my top 6 picks, I want to give a brief overview of the 3 main industries my companies compete in. As mentioned, my analysis begins with a top down approach.

**Robotics and Autonomous Systems**

The global robotics market is projected to grow from $45B in 2024 to $350B by 2032 (25% CAGR), with AI-driven autonomy (navigation, perception, etc.) as a key driver.

Jensen Huang predicts “everything that moves will be autonomous” by 2035, emphasizing AI-powered humanoid robots, drones, and autonomous vehicles. Elon Musk forecasts “tens of billions” of humanoid robots by 2035, where robots handle tasks like manufacturing, logistics, and caregiving. He sees robotics as a $10T+ future market, with AI enabling autonomous navigation and human interaction.

**Healthcare and Digital Biology**

The AI healthcare market is projected to grow from $20B in 2024 to $200B by 2032 (30% CAGR), driven by AI diagnostics, drug discovery, and precision medicine.

We are likely to see major AI-driven breakthroughs in healthcare/scientific discovery (drug discovery, personalized medicine, etc.). Jensen Huang sees AI revolutionizing healthcare through digital biology and drug discovery, enabling personalized medicine and faster research & development. He has also said that AI will lower barriers to scientific innovation, allowing smaller firms to compete with big pharma by 2030. This makes digital healthcare an ideal industry to target Small and Mid Cap AI plays.

**AI Infrastructure and Energy**

The AI Data Center market is projected to grow from $50B in 2024 to $400B by 2032 (30% CAGR), with nuclear and renewable energy markets expanding to support AI’s 15-20% annual energy demand growth.

Jensen Huang forecasts global data center capital expenditures will grow from $250B in 2023 to $1T in 2028, driven by AI infrastructure needs. AI hardware, data center infrastructure, and energy solutions are poised to benefit immensely from this CapEx boom.
