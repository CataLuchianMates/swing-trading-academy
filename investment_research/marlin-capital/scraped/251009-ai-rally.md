AI Healthcare is an industry we are heavily exposed to in our [SMID Cap 6 Portfolio](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-smid-caps-with-massive-upside-potential). 2 of our 6 holdings are AI Healthcare companies.

**JOBY**

JOBY was down -8% today on a common stock offering.

This is all part of the game, especially in this climate. Many companies are announcing stock offerings right now. It is a truly rare market, so I can’t blame management for striking while the iron is hot.

At the same time, management’s timing is terrible here. This stock was ready to break out. In any case, JOBY’s short-term momentum is now crushed.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MM_u!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fad67b13b-a29f-4202-906b-f0fea0101054_1132x652.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MM_u!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fad67b13b-a29f-4202-906b-f0fea0101054_1132x652.png)

JOBY is now back in its large base, and we saw big volume on an -8% down day (highest volume in last 12m). This is not ideal.

There are plenty of examples of hot stocks resuming their rallies following an offering, but it can be very hit or miss. I did not want to risk losing our gains on this trade, so I closed the rest of our JOBY position at 10:30 AM EST today.

Overall, we gained +8.4% adjusted with [our 3 sells on JOBY](https://substack.com/chat/408010/post/3d65a58d-fce3-4f48-a8ed-ff9cc56f669e).
