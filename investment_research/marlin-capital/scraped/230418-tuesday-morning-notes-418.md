# Tuesday Morning Notes 4.18

Technical Update

Mostly constructive action in the S&P 500 to kick off the week yesterday. The index closed +0.33% on the day after plenty of intraday chop.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0ykF!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd57fea65-e896-4dfa-a1ac-f99824a7c080_958x715.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0ykF!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd57fea65-e896-4dfa-a1ac-f99824a7c080_958x715.jpeg)

For the SPX, we closed right at the key $4150-$4160 resistance level. Now, we are set for a gap above the $4160 level this morning.

The key resistance level to watch now is $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).

Key support levels to watch below include $4150, $4130-$4135, $4100, and $4075-$4080.
