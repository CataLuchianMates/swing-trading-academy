# Wednesday Morning Notes 2.1

Technical Analysis

The market rallied yesterday to finish off its best January since 2001. Today is Fed day, with the committee’s decision on interest rates coming at 2 EST.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!T8nW!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff4aae585-5cfc-4c5d-9456-898c44ca57b1_1184x879.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!T8nW!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff4aae585-5cfc-4c5d-9456-898c44ca57b1_1184x879.jpeg)

We have a bullish setup technically heading into the event. Resistance wise, $4100 remains the key level to watch above. Key support levels to watch below include $4050, 4020, $4000, and the key moving averages ($3978, $3954, and $3932).

Today will be a critical day for determining the market’s path in February (and beyond). Expect plenty of volatility and wild swings to both directions this afternoon while Powell speaks.
