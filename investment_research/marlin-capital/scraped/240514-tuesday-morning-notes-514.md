# Tuesday Morning Notes 5.14

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Quiet day to start the week for the market indices with the S&P 500 finishing flat (-0.02%) and the QQQ finishing +0.23%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tV9T!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb39db56d-c6c3-4c6c-957c-6f040946aedd_1201x881.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tV9T!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb39db56d-c6c3-4c6c-957c-6f040946aedd_1201x881.jpeg)

All the action was in the “Meme Stocks” with yesterday’s best performers being the most speculative parts of the market - Liquid Most Short (+5.7%), Most Short Rolling (+5.6%), Memes (+4.0%), China ADRs (+3.8%), and Non-Profitable Tech (+3.0%).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RnWL!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F01eb7f26-be65-498d-8d71-ca3a747656b2_1087x971.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RnWL!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F01eb7f26-be65-498d-8d71-ca3a747656b2_1087x971.jpeg)

Gamestop shares soared +74% (and AMC +78%) for their biggest gain daily since March 2021 after Roaring Kitty sent a meme tweet on X.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HGgh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0a0ff9ca-61e9-43ca-b011-3738b18bdf4a_1284x1973.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HGgh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0a0ff9ca-61e9-43ca-b011-3738b18bdf4a_1284x1973.jpeg)

Hedge Funds were caught off guard by this move with their VIP Longs (consensus longs) underperforming vs Most Short (most shorted stocks) by -5.86%.  This is a 4-Sigma Day and the 2nd worst day since the original Meme Stock Mania.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qqeZ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbdcad778-0274-4d89-b12e-7109bf9259bf_1117x672.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qqeZ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbdcad778-0274-4d89-b12e-7109bf9259bf_1117x672.jpeg)

With some huge moves in the most speculative parts of the market, sentiment is back into “stretched territory”, though well off the highs we saw earlier this year.  Sentiment remained in stretched territory for most of 2023.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!u2U8!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6862cb61-6dfb-448b-bbbb-0a4f0a47dd09_622x583.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!u2U8!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6862cb61-6dfb-448b-bbbb-0a4f0a47dd09_622x583.jpeg)
