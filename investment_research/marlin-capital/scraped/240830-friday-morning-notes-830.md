# Friday Morning Notes 8.30

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Choppy action in the market indices yesterday with the SPX finishing flat on the day (down 1 pt).  Both the QQQ and SPX tried to break higher during the session, but closed weak near lows of the day.

AFRM – I called out this potential [earnings gapper](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-earnings-gapper-trade-setup) in [Thursday’s Morning Notes](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/thursday-morning-notes-829).  It gave a textbook Inside 15 Earnings Gapper setup with entry at 10:00 at $40.40.  I’m long at $40.40 with a stop at $37.51.

First target = $45.  On the weekly, this has plenty of room to run to $50.

SHOP – This came within 17 cents of my first target at $77, so we had some chances to lock in some profits here.  I didn’t get the full 1/3rd sell in because it did not break through $77.  My stop on the remainder of this position will be $71.75.

SN – Finally breaking higher after a long consolidation/bull flag.  My first target is $96-97, followed by $100.  I’ll be looking to take 1/3rd profits at $96-$97 (progressively sell near 1st target/resistance, don’t sell all at once).

AXON – This is choppy but still in the full position.  Closing stop remains below $359.

KVYO – Stopped out for a loss on this trade.  Position closed.

SPOT – Also choppy but still in the full position.  Closing stop remains below $332.

Today is the final trading day of August. September is a tricky month, as it is historically the worst month on the calendar for the SPX (with a -13.5% annualized return).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!abRX!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9c521f92-f16c-4a50-8f51-8c984fb7b114_1284x605.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!abRX!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9c521f92-f16c-4a50-8f51-8c984fb7b114_1284x605.jpeg)

With that said, much of the weakness is typically backloaded, as the 2nd half of the September is the worst 2 week stretch of the year. More on that in this weekend’s report.

The market is closed Monday for Labor Day.
