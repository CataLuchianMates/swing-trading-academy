**Watchlist (for Active Portfolio):**

Things change fast in the market and you have to be ready to adapt.

If you are a trader, long opportunities are emerging.  You can put a stop loss at yesterday’s lows and try to take advantage of the beginnings of the **potential** 20-30% bounce I mentioned.

Now, every growth chart I’m looking at is in a clear downtrend.  So keep in mind that any long would be a counter trend trade.  So what does that mean?  For me, it means smaller position sizing.  These are certainly not A+ or A setups, so keep that in mind going into it. The gap up today will also make things more tricky.

**2:19 EST:**

An acceleration of selling now.

As I have maintained for weeks, my base case in ARKK is a decline to $80. That is where I will **start** to buy the dip.

In Bull Markets, buying every dip makes investors look like geniuses. This is NOT the playbook in Bear Markets. It’s important to discern the difference. Yes I see many on Fintwit and other places who have been talking about loading up for weeks, but the fact is, many of these are newer investors who have never actually traded in a Bear Market. Managing risk is the name of the game.
