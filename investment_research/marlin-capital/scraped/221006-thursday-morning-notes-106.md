# Thursday Morning Notes 10.6

Technical Analysis

The bounce stalled out a bit in yesterday’s action, but Bears were unable to take control after the morning reversal. The indices ended up closing mostly flat on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YI2o!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd74ce9d0-c5ca-41ac-9fa4-5d477aa16b77_1144x864.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YI2o!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd74ce9d0-c5ca-41ac-9fa4-5d477aa16b77_1144x864.jpeg)

As you can see in the chart above, it has been a quick and violent 3 day rally. We are currently sitting between the 8 and 21 EMAs on the daily, which is a logical spot to see some more consolidation/choppy action before the market makes its next move.

Resistance levels ahead include $284-$285 and $288-$290. On a reversal, support levels below include $278-$280 and $274-$275.
