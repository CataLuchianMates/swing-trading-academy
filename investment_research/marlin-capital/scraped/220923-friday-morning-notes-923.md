# Friday Morning Notes 9.23

Stocks followed through to the downside yesterday after a morning gap down. The QQQ bounced right at $279-$280 support, and closed at $280.07. It has been a fast move down in a short time, so a larger oversold bounce is certainly possible in the next couple sessions. On the daily, the QQQ is >3% extended below its 8 EMA, signaling that a relief bounce is likely coming soon.
