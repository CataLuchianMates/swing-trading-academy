# Wednesday Morning Notes 8.28

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Choppy late summer action in the major indices yesterday as investors await NVDA’s earnings report after the bell today.  The S&P 500 is sitting -1-2% off ATHs, while Tech is acting a bit weaker.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9Ho7!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbd3df08f-6651-4b67-b8ac-0051d1fdc516_974x721.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9Ho7!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbd3df08f-6651-4b67-b8ac-0051d1fdc516_974x721.jpeg)

Needless to say, this is a massive report for Tech, Semis, the AI trade, and the general market.  NVDA earnings have been THE key driver of broad market performance for the past 6 earnings seasons.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!fT7T!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff0430372-a833-4e39-959e-305de371c423_794x698.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!fT7T!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff0430372-a833-4e39-959e-305de371c423_794x698.jpeg)

Positioning wise, GS Prime rates NVDA at 8.5 out of 10.  The NVDA long is not quite as crowded today at $125 as it was the last time it was here in June.

Mag 7 currently makes up 18.2% of Overall US Single Stock Net Exposure (compared to 20.5% at the end of June), ranking in the 30th percentile vs. the past year.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_Gdj!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F31ab1f4a-5873-4dba-bf02-b323556e7cc4_998x634.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_Gdj!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F31ab1f4a-5873-4dba-bf02-b323556e7cc4_998x634.jpeg)

Options are currently pricing in a near ~10% move in NVDA (equating to a $300B market cap swing).  The stock has finished down -2% (the worst) to up +25 T+1 on 9 straight prints (aka a positive skew for the last 2 years).

Tech is sitting right at 8 EMA support heading into the print, while (as mentioned in “Broad Street Rally”) many other sectors have marched ahead to new highs.  In my view, this NVDA print will determine the path of Tech/AI/Semis from here/if the rotation into other sectors will continue.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!FcO8!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6244d95c-0968-48f9-8bb7-0a1afcf68246_1201x885.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!FcO8!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6244d95c-0968-48f9-8bb7-0a1afcf68246_1201x885.jpeg)

No changes in my long positions.  Still holding all including KVYO into Wednesday.

AMBA - potential earnings gapper to watch today
