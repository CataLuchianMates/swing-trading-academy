Marlin Capital Strategy:

We remain in a headline driven market, with today’s headline helping futures to rally off an ugly start:

> KREMLIN SPOKESMAN SAYS UKRAINE MUST AMEND CONSTITUTION AND REJECT CLAIMS TO ENTER ANY BLOC
>
> UKRAINE MUST RECOGNISE CRIMEA AS RUSSIAN, AND DONETSK AND LUGANSK AS INDEPENDENT STATES
>
> IF THESE CONDITIONS ARE MET, THEN RUSSIAN MILITARY ACTION WILL ‘STOP IN A MOMENT’ - SPOKESMAN

More important than whatever headline whipsaws the market, we remain in a clear downtrend. Many growth stocks have already broken down to new lows, and many others look awful. My base case for ARKK remains another 20-30% downside with a projected move to the low $40s. With that said, it is not a time to press shorts as we are oversold, and the potential for a positive geopolitical headline could cause a sizable short squeeze. If this move happens, it will provide a time to add to shorts. For now, it is a time to be patient and wait for the right setups.
