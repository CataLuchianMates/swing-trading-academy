# Friday Morning Notes 1.31

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks pushed higher yesterday after solid results from 3 of the Mag 7 names (MSFT, META, and TSLA). The SPX finished +0.53%, NDX +0.25, and Small Caps lead the way with the RTY +1.07%.

After the bell, Apple came through with a solid quarter, beating the relatively low expectations the stock had into this print (I talked about how weak AAPL was vs the NDX in January). Investors seemingly left the call feeling like AAPL did what they needed to do, providing a slight beat on the guide and services revs. The stock is set fora +4% gap up this morning, pulling the major indices along with it.
