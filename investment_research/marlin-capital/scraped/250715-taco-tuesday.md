# TACO Tuesday

The S&P 500 hit new record highs today before fading and closing -0.4%. Tech led the way with the NDX finishing +0.13%.

**June CPI**

The big Macro event of the day was June CPI, with Core CPI coming in lighter than expected MoM.

**CPI MoM:** 0.3% vs 0.3% exp.

**CPI Core MoM:** 0.2% vs 0.3% exp.

**CPI YoY:** 2.7% vs 2.6% exp.

**CPI Core YoY:** 2.9% vs 2.9% exp.

**TACO Trade**

The market is once again taking the latest tariff drama in stride (30% tariff on imports from the EU and Mexico, effective Aug 1). We are basically now at the point where new tariff news no longer even moves the market. It’s all background noise now.

The Micro focus today was on **NVDA** +4% following news that the company is planning to resume H20 chip sales to China after assurances from Washington that shipments will be approved. After the White House banned exports in April, this news is a surprise reversal that should add billions to Nvidia’s revenue this year. The stock adjusted for this positive news today, rallying +4% on the session. This may also help ease tensions between US-China in trade negotiations.

Another example of the TACO Trade (Trump Always Chickens Out) in action.
