# Tuesday Morning Notes 11.19

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Bounce back day for the market yesterday after finishing red all 5 days last week (first time in 5 years NDX went all week without an up day). Rates/VIX/USD were all relatively stable. TSLA finished +5% vs UBER -5% after headlines were out over the weekend that the Trump team would ease self-driving vehicle regulations.

We are seeing a continuation move higher so far this morning with the indices slightly in the green. The market was not at all fazed by headlines saying Ukraine attacked Russia using US-made missiles and Russia will retaliate. This speaks to the strength and buying interest out there right now.

As I talked about yesterday, NVDA earnings tomorrow are the biggest catalyst event remaining for the market in 2024. The numbers and guidance are what will ultimately matter, but this market definitely has a feel of 1 that wants to go higher. I remain bullish and I expect to see the market continue higher following NVDA earnings. With that said, the gap risk is obviously high if NVDA fails to deliver.
