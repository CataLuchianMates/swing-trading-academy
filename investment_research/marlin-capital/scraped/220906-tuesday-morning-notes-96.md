For new readers - I send out daily notes each morning containing my chart analysis updates (Technical Analysis) and my favorite readings + any relevant commentary/info/charts.

As a hedge fund trader, a large part of my process includes extensive morning/nighttime reading & research. In the daily morning notes, I aim to share the most valuable outside research that I have digested in the last 24 hours.

Technical Analysis

Friday was a bearish reversal candle for the QQQ, with the index running into resistance near $304. The technical outlook remains bearish, although it would not surprise me to see a mini bounce/chop to begin this week (due to the many short term oversold readings flashing).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4bFY!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb3e45af1-ecd0-4a01-b7a9-e45b708ff915_1252x947.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4bFY!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb3e45af1-ecd0-4a01-b7a9-e45b708ff915_1252x947.jpeg)

We are set for a gap up to kick off this week. Key resistance levels to watch ahead include $298-$300, $304-$305, and $309-$310. On a green to red reversal, it is crucial for the indices to hold last week’s lows ($293). If this support breaks, I am targeting the $280 region on the breakdown.
