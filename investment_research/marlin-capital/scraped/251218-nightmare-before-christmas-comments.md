## David Marlin Q&A (Comments)

**Q:** Thank you for the article. Happy holidays David. With respect to the anticipated AI portfolio for 2026, do you think that you might delay that  as a result of all the bearishness in the market right now.

**David Marlin:** Yes definitely.  Ultimately, my only goal in the market is to make money.  I talk about what I am personally doing for myself (trading, investing, etc) at Marlin Capital.  So, if I do not believe it is a good time to initiate new longs (like currently), I will not release a new portfolio.  If I do not believe that a buy and hold portfolio of AI stocks is the right strategy for 2026, then I will not do that for 2026.  As I have said before, “buy and hold” is a Bull Market strategy. There is a big element of market timing for when I release new portfolios/initiate new positions.  So, this type of bearish market environment definitely affects everything.
