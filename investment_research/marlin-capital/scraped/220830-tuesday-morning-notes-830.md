# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.30

Technical Analysis

We had a choppy start to the week yesterday after a morning gap down. The QQQ opened the day at $304.62 and closed the day -0.20 cents at $304.41.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Win4!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6d3286d8-c0cb-4879-820c-da85975344d8_961x719.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Win4!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6d3286d8-c0cb-4879-820c-da85975344d8_961x719.jpeg)

We are set for a gap up this morning with the QQQ set to open near the $306 region. Key resistance levels to watch above include $307 and $310-$311. Key support levels to watch below on a green to red reversal include yesterday’s lows ($303 region) and the $298-$300 area.
