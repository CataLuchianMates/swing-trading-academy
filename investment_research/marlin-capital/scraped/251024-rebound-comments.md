**Q:** Dm can you explain what you mean by waiting for 1 hr vwap? Sorry I know it’s a technical indicator of some sort you use for your set up

**David Marlin:** Yes so this is using the 1 HR chart, which is my favorite intraday chart to use as a swing trader. 

VWAP stands for volume-weighted average price. It’s a tool to figure out the average price a stock has traded at.  Think of it like a smart average that tells you exactly what people are actually paying for the stock (considering how much they’re buying and selling and at what price).  It gives more weight to the prices where more shares were traded and calculates an average based off price and volume. 

For these post-earnings plays, I use an anchored VWAP from the 1st hour (highest volume) following earnings.  If you’re buying at the 1HR VWAP, you’re essentially buying at the 1HR average price since earnings.  In my experience, it often acts as strong support for these earnings names.
