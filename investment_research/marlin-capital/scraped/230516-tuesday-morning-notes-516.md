# Tuesday Morning Notes 5.16

Technical Update

The S&P 500 continued its low volume, rangebound action to kick off the week with a +0.3% gain on Monday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5a78!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6bf48aa0-81bf-4daa-a17e-50c29860bb1c_961x721.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5a78!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6bf48aa0-81bf-4daa-a17e-50c29860bb1c_961x721.jpeg)

$4150 remains the key resistance level to watch on the upside. Above $4150 and we can see $4200.

Key support levels to watch below include $4100-$4110 (21 EMA), $4056-$4060 (60 EMA), $4040-$4030 and $4000.

LRCX - good start to this trade idea I discussed on Friday with a high volume move above the $532 resistance level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ROJo!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa109f429-6b28-4e1f-b854-af3dbcf67a09_1198x895.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ROJo!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa109f429-6b28-4e1f-b854-af3dbcf67a09_1198x895.jpeg)

I will be moving my stop loss up to breakeven ($532) on this trade. The more these trade setups start to gain traction, the more I will be interested in adding additional exposure. Still, I will be looking to keep the risk low on any new trade ideas.
