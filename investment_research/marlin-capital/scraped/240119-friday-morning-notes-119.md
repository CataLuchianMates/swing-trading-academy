# Friday Morning Notes 1.19

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500 rallied back above its 8 EMA in Thursday’s session, closing +0.88% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!m1kj!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb62dd255-f2c6-445a-a834-994f68e565c9_970x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!m1kj!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb62dd255-f2c6-445a-a834-994f68e565c9_970x723.jpeg)

Despite some whipsawing price movement at the index level, there were a lot of tailwinds in play for tech yesterday: (1) Semis squeezed on the back of TSM +7.4% beat & capex guide above + some upgrades away in analogs/equipment, (2) AAPL +3.4% and MSFT both caught upgrades overnight with Mega Caps accounting for ~72% of SPX move higher yesterday, and (3) Software demand inflecting higher (we continue to see repeat L/O demand here.)

Central bankers continue to taper cut expectations and adopt a more neutral stance compared to what we’ve seen in Nov/Dec of last year.  Fed’s Bostic – Fed should allow events to continue to unfold, urges caution on rate cuts amid global uncertainty.

Potential Earnings Gapper Trade Setup – SMCI pre-announced earnings yesterday.  The company said it now expects sales of $3.6 billion to $3.65 billion, up from its prior guidance of $2.7 billion to $2.9 billion, for its fiscal second quarter ended Dec. 31.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!CCh2!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9f528673-6e09-43a3-8ed6-8d076e786e04_1195x889.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!CCh2!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9f528673-6e09-43a3-8ed6-8d076e786e04_1195x889.jpeg)

The stock is set for a +10% gap up this morning on the back of these results.  I will be looking for an earnings gappers setup entry in the first 30 minutes of the trading day.
