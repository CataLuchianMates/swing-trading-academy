# Friday Morning Notes 8.12

Technical Analysis

The market put in a reversal yesterday after a morning rally, with many stocks going from green to red on the day. For the QQQ, $330 marked the high of the day. Calls spiked during the morning rally as bears finally showed a bit of capitulation (see GS prime note below).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!q0h0!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb3217734-fbb7-4442-9a68-afb7178c324f_1078x838.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!q0h0!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb3217734-fbb7-4442-9a68-afb7178c324f_1078x838.jpeg)

On the daily chart, yesterday’s reversal candle indicates that we could see a potential multi-day pullback, even if this Bear market rally is not fully over. Today, we are set for a gap up. The key resistance level to watch ahead is $330 (yesterday’s high). On the downside, yesterday’s low is an important level to watch ($323), along with the $318 level (8 EMA).
