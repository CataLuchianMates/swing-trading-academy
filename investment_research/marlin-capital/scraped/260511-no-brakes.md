The AI build out is the biggest investment cycle of our lifetime. And the price action is increasingly mirroring the Dot Com Bubble of the late 1990s.

I do not say that with any negative connotation. The opposite, actually.

Bubbles are some of the most lucrative environments to be a stock trader in all of market history. The Dot Com Bubble produced legendary fortunes. The Covid Bubble produced legendary fortunes. The AI Bubble is producing the same right now.

There is only 1 way to play a bubble. Iconic money manager George Soros said it best:

> “When I see a bubble forming, I rush in to buy, adding fuel to the fire. That is not irrational.”
>
> *— George Soros*

You play a bubble by being long on the way up (and short on the way down).

Right now, we are firmly in the “long on the way up” phase. And the price action is telling us we are nowhere near done yet.
