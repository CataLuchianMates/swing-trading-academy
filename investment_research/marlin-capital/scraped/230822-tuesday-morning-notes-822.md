# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.22

Technical Update

The S&P 500 rallied to kick off the week yesterday with the index closing +0.69%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!j7su!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd8ebe5f7-ad24-40cf-a3e6-bfeb0564a595_1269x954.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!j7su!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd8ebe5f7-ad24-40cf-a3e6-bfeb0564a595_1269x954.jpeg)

The SPX closed right at the $4400 resistance level (60 EMA). We are set for a further gap up this morning towards $4410.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $4415-$4420 and $4450-$4460.

Key support levels to watch below include $4350-$4360, $4330, and $4300.
