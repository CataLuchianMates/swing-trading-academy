# Thursday Morning Notes 7.14

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday morning gap down was bought up almost immediately, as the QQQ bounced (and made its low of the day) right at the $280 support level I mentioned in the morning notes. The index struggled to keep its momentum going after filling the morning gap, however. The afternoon was filled with mostly chop, and we ended up rolling over a bit to close -0.2% on the day.

All things considered, it was not a bad day for the market after a terrible CPI report. With that said, the technical pattern still leans bearish as the QQQ was unable to break its 8 and 21 EMAs. These are key Resistance levels ahead that I am watching - $288-$289 and $293-$294. If we start to roll over, the $277-$280 support levels become key to hold again. For now, we have simply bounced back into “no man’s land”. It is an easy decision for me to sit and wait until some key levels start to break to the upside or downside.
