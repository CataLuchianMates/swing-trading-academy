# Friday Morning Notes 4.14

Technical Update

The market staged a big rally yesterday, with the S&P 500 breaking above its recent consolidation range and closing at its highest level since early-mid February.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MdTI!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7ebe5f4b-e394-4dc6-a753-85c93c70284a_964x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MdTI!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7ebe5f4b-e394-4dc6-a753-85c93c70284a_964x724.jpeg)

I remain wary of fakeout moves due to the prevailing Macro and Fundamental environment, but there is no question the technical setup is quite bullish here.

There is not much resistance now standing between the current SPX price and YTD highs at $4200. If we can break above $4150-$4160, I expect to see this rally extend to YTD highs at $4190-$4200.

Key support levels to watch below include $4130-$4135, $4100, $4075-$4080, $4050, and $3990-$4000.

Full analysis coming this weekend.
