Technical Update

The S&P 500 kicked off the week with an oversold bounce after briefly breaking Friday’s lows in the morning. Despite the mid-day bounce, the index closed weak at -0.16% for the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!b-Ts!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8ca3c192-79d1-4886-8983-01757423bcb1_961x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!b-Ts!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8ca3c192-79d1-4886-8983-01757423bcb1_961x723.jpeg)

We are set for a gap up this morning after March’s CPI report was in line with expectations.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $3900-$3905, $3930-$3940, and $3970-$3975.

Key support levels to watch below include $3850-$3855 and $3800-$3810.
