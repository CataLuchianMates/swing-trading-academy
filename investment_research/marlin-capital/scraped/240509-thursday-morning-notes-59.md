# Thursday Morning Notes 5.9

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The major market indices finished flat on a boring/choppy, low volume session on Wednesday.  Yesterday was the 3rd lightest volume session of the year with only 9.8B total shares traded across all US exchanges.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LtRp!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2e8d7c13-c177-41a6-88d1-dc42a6265ad1_1203x885.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LtRp!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2e8d7c13-c177-41a6-88d1-dc42a6265ad1_1203x885.jpeg)

The tape was under early pressure (finished flat) on a combination of bond yields (US 10YR +4bps to 4.49%), retail darlings struggling post-earnings with UBER -5.7%, SHOP -18%, RIVN (finished flat from -9%), TSLA -1.7% (fraud probe), and a trickier tech tape with INTC and QCOM weighing on semis.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BYWh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F46b9e97d-7aa0-40aa-90f4-d2570373953e_1290x2143.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BYWh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F46b9e97d-7aa0-40aa-90f4-d2570373953e_1290x2143.jpeg)

Overall, healthy action after a recent bounce off lows but the technicals are pointing towards some more chop/consolidation while the key moving averages catch up. Historically, the mid-May to mid-June seasonal period is very strong for the market during election years.
