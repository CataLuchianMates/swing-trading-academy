Another month, another upside surprise in inflation. June CPI actually showed an acceleration in CPI from May. CPI core rose 0.7% m/m in June, equivalent to nearly 9% annualized. All inflation metrics released this surprised to the upside.

**Fed swaps are now fully pricing in 150bp of hikes over the next two meetings.** US rate futures now see Fed funds at 3.56% by December, from 3.41% before CPI data.

Technical Analysis

Yesterday was another bearish day for the indices, as stocks sold off heading into this morning’s CPI report. We broke decisively lower in the afternoon on the QQQ, finally bouncing at the $285 support level and closing at $286. On the daily chart, the QQQ has been rejected by the 50 day MA and is now back below the 21 EMA.
