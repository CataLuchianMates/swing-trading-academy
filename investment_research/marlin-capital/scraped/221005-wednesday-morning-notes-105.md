# Wednesday Morning Notes 10.5

Technical Analysis

Stocks rallied big yesterday, setting off one of the biggest 2 day rallies in stocks this year.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Filt!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4c38efe4-86e5-432c-8d97-737514e88db7_1200x900.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Filt!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4c38efe4-86e5-432c-8d97-737514e88db7_1200x900.jpeg)

Previous 2 day thrusts have not led to prolonged rallies during this Bear Market. Currently, the QQQ has rallied to between its 8 and 21 EMAs.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!dO3_!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4d352b7c-2369-4250-899c-6df4738a59ca_1200x884.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!dO3_!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4d352b7c-2369-4250-899c-6df4738a59ca_1200x884.jpeg)

We may see a potential overshoot, but my highest probability scenario has this rally stalling out between $281-$286. Support levels to watch below include $274-$276 and $269-$271.
