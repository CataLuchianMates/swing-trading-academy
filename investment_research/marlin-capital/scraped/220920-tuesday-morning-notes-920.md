# Tuesday Morning Notes 9.20

Technical Analysis

The market bounced to kick off the week yesterday, with the QQQ making a low of $286 (versus $285 last week). The upside move came on low volume as traders await the Fed decision tomorrow.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cuVw!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6ca52af7-7e78-4e3a-9f9d-399982b46312_1126x853.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cuVw!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6ca52af7-7e78-4e3a-9f9d-399982b46312_1126x853.jpeg)

This morning, we are set for a gap down reversal from yesterday’s action. I expect we will likely see more chop and low volume trading action today, ahead of tomorrow’s big event.

On a technical basis, a further oversold bounce looks very possible. Key support levels to watch below include $289 and $285-$286. Key resistance levels to watch ahead include $292-$293, $295-$296, and $300-$302.

For those following my ES short trade, I plan to close out the rest (final 2/3rds) of this position today. In recent FOMC meetings, we have seen an “event clearing bounce” follow, especially when the market has sold off into the event (as it has the last 1-2 weeks). We are heading into the event “oversold”, so a further bounce higher would be unsurprising.
