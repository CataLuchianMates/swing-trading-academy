# Thursday Morning Notes 6.6

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Big day for the market yesterday with the SPX +1.18%, NDX +2.04%, and RTY 1.47%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!d0Yd!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6deee22c-ef12-42a8-8087-4f619b1084b9_1209x895.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!d0Yd!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6deee22c-ef12-42a8-8087-4f619b1084b9_1209x895.jpeg)

This melt up came on the backs of a friendly ISM services reading amidst a week of downbeat data released (ISM, JOLTS, etc.).  Despite the beat, the employment component was the weakest part of the report, wages were soft, and the labor market concerns continue to build.  This is seen as a positive for the market with Fed Funds Futures are now pricing in an 83% chance of a rate cut in September (way more favorable than the ~45% that was priced in just 7 days ago).  10 YR yields have plummeted to multi-month lows after the recent slew of data, allowing the tape to fire on all cylinders with new ATHs coming for both the SPX and NDX yesterday.

There was a clear momentum rebound with AI themes boosted by HPE +11% (beat/raise results), ASML +8.5%, and AMAT upgrade.  We saw a nice bounce in the Power Up/Electrification theme after 2 rough sessions (think BE) and a reversion/bounce in growth software (CRWD +12% on beat/raise).  Large Cap Tech continues to work with NVDA +5% (now up +11% in June already and +145% YTD).  The most short pockets squeezed 2.72% on the move lower in rates.
