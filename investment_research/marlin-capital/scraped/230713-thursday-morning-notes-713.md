# Thursday Morning Notes 7.13

Technical Update

The S&P 500 gapped up and held its gap following yesterday’s cooler than expected CPI number.

The index closed at 52 week highs and we are all set for another gap up this morning.

Potential new long ideas

Long SHOP above $66. Stop below $63.69 (3.5% risk).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Qlsx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F257095cc-fa81-4670-873c-e598df171d66_1201x892.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Qlsx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F257095cc-fa81-4670-873c-e598df171d66_1201x892.jpeg)

SHOP looks ready to breakout of this bullish base pattern. The stock is set for a gap above $67 this morning. I will not be buying the gap up and looking for an intraday test of $67 to get long.

Long AMD above $115. Stop below $111 (3.5% risk).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Atno!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff987cd61-aa2c-45a5-9723-c7486efc314f_1130x842.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Atno!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff987cd61-aa2c-45a5-9723-c7486efc314f_1130x842.jpeg)

I like the way AMD is acting here after this recent pull back as semis remain strong. I am targeting a move back to 52 week highs (130) on a move back above $115.
