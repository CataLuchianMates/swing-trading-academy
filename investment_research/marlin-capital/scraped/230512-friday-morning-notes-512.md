# Friday Morning Notes 5.12

Technical Update

The choppy market action continued yesterday with the S&P 500 finishing -0.17%.

The low of the day was $4109 as the 21 EMA support level held up once again.

$4150 remains the key resistance level to watch on the upside. A break above $4150 resistance opens the door for a test of $4200 and YTD highs.

Key support levels to watch below include $4100-$4110 (21 EMA), $4056-$4060 (60 EMA), $4040-$4030 and $4000.
