# Heading into July

In my [June Preview Report](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/heading-into-june), I highlighted **AEVA** as my top trade idea for the month. It has now surged an **incredible +116% in June**!

Can we do it **AGAIN** in July!?

Let’s dive in!

In this report, I preview July and share my **Top 4 Stocks to watch**, including **my #1 Trade Idea for the month**.

First off, let’s zoom out and look at the big picture.

We are not even 3 months into a new Bull Market.

The shortest Bull Market for the S&P 500 since 1960 was ~15 months (Dec ‘18 - Mar ‘20 Covid Crash).

[On April 9th](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/bottomed-out), just 2 days after the market bottomed, [I said](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/bottomed-out) we are likely past the point of peak fear/uncertainty around tariffs, which would allow the market bottoming process to begin.

> “I no longer see us breaking below Monday’s lows, because I saw this as more of an Event Driven Bear Market than a Systemic Bear Market.”
>

And begin it did. We have profited BIG on the way up, with all 4 of [My Top 4 Trade Ideas from Q1 Earnings](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/my-top-4-trade-ideas-from-q1-earnings) gaining, and 3/4 (still holding) up >20%, including HOOD +68%.

So where do I stand now?

My bigger picture target remains **$7300 for the SPX.** The core tenants of this rally are all still in place as we head into July:

1. Continued enthusiasm around AI.
2. Underweight positioning (HF L/S ratio still only in 19th percentile vs history; total positioning in 30th percentile).
3. Rapidly improving Macro backdrop (geopolitical conflicts easing / rate cut probabilities rising / tariff concerns on back-burner).
4. Favorable technicals (vol compressing / financial conditions easy / strong liquidity during the low vol summer grind higher).

Looking at the smaller picture, July is the strongest month of the year for US stocks historically. 17 of the last 18 July’s have finished positive for the Nasdaq for an average gain of +4.23%.

This week, specifically, starts the best seasonal period of the year for the Nasdaq. Historically, the 1st Half of July is the best 2-week stretch on the calendar as Q3 inflows tend to be frontloaded.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8kWA!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1fb02d3c-f5b0-4f38-a916-41bfd10fe7bd_988x658.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8kWA!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1fb02d3c-f5b0-4f38-a916-41bfd10fe7bd_988x658.jpeg)

In fact, the NDX has rallied from ~June 28th to ~July 12th in 31 of the last 40 years (78% hit rate), for an average gain of +2.5%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!dyOQ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F192a21a5-919e-4bc8-b3a5-fcb6de49faa9_537x264.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!dyOQ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F192a21a5-919e-4bc8-b3a5-fcb6de49faa9_537x264.jpeg)

**PL**

Planet Labs (PL) just came onto my radar last week after seeing tons of unusual call buying in the stock. June 5th was its highest volume day ever (by far) after a Q1 earnings beat, and it is now closing in on its highest volume month ever here in June (including its 2 highest volume weeks ever this month).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gziP!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb831e58e-3eff-4f9e-b7ef-4c2c9b63913a_1128x654.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gziP!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb831e58e-3eff-4f9e-b7ef-4c2c9b63913a_1128x654.jpeg)

Last week, Cantor Fitzgerald raised its PT on PL to $8.50 (from $6.30), naming the stock as its “**Top Pick in Space**”, citing its **FCF acceleration**, **data moat**, and **strategic operating model**.

On Friday, Planet Labs announced it will hold a press conference on Tues 7/1 to provide an update on recent business momentum in the Defense & Intelligence sector. Unusual call buying ensued all day. Holding a business update on the 1st day of Q3 just a few weeks after your earnings call is undeniably bullish. This could always be a “sell the news” event, but based off the big buying demand (ATH volume + unusual call buying) + the type of market we are in, I am bullish this PL setup for July (and will be looking to enter long this week).
