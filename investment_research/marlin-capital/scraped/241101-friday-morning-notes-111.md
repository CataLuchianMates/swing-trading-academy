# Friday Morning Notes 11.1

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Heavy selling pressure in US stocks yesterday driven by a combination of (1) wrong way earnings movers (sell the news reactions on MSFT & META); (2) Mutual fund tax loss selling & heavy institutional outflows (see yesterday’s BofA note); and (3) jitters ahead of AMZN & AAPL reports, this morning’s NFP’s report, and Tuesday’s election.

Yesterday’s sell off has proven to be short lived so far with the market bouncing back this morning. As I highlighted in “A Huge Week Ahead”, stocks will go their separate ways based off their earnings reports and the Mega Caps will drag the indices along with them. Yesterday = MSFT -6%; and a >1% downside day for the indices. Today = AMZN +7%; and a >1% upside day for the indices (so far). This day to day volatility will lessen after we get through this week, though, as NVDA is the only Mag 7 name now left to report.

AMZN +7% with big OI upside in qtr/guide (and AWS stable +19% YoY)…Q3 AWS also stable QoQ at +19%. Q3 OI was a big beat with $17B vs midpoint guide of $13.5B. The OI guide was for $18B at midpoint vs expectations of $16.5B.

AAPL -1.2% this morning after a small beat/small headline miss on services revs ($25B vs cons $25.3B). iPhone revs came in at $46.2B vs cons ~45B. Not an exciting report but nothing to get overly bearish about, either.

NFP Report - BIG miss

The US economy added 12k jobs in October, well below expectations of 106k

The US unemployment rate for October came in at 4.1%, in line with expectations

This marks the lowest number of US jobs since July 2021.

Active Portfolio -

PRCT - Hit my breakeven stop yesterday at $87.8. Position closed.

RBLX - Inside 1 HR long setup triggered at $51.21 yesterday. I’m long. Stop at $48 for -6.2% risk. Will look to move stop up if we break yesterday’s highs.

CVNA - Inside 1 HR long setup triggered at $251 yesterday. I’m still long. Stop at $233.27 for -7.1% risk.

TWLO - Double Inside 1 HR long setup triggered at $81.82 yesterday. I’m long. We have already broken yesterday’s highs this morning, so I am moving my stop to today’s lows at $80.02 for -2.2% risk.

No potential earnings gappers of major interest for me today.
