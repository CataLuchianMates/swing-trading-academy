# Tuesday Morning Notes 7.16

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The market continued last week’s momentum to kick off the week of 7/15 with the RTY > NDX & SPX.  The S&P and NDX finished +0.27% and +0.28% respectively, while the Russell 2000 finished +1.8% higher.

Trump trades (long infrastructure, long banks, long crypto, short China exposure, etc.) were already in motion post-debate, but the weekend’s assassination attempt on DJT accelerated these moves yesterday.  The probability of a Trump victory in November rocketed to new highs following the assassination attempt.  Yesterday, Goldman’s Republican Party Basket traded up +2.8% while the Democratic Party Basket traded down -3.5%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7vDz!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe4db8af6-eefd-42a0-8a01-6bacc407de7d_1224x334.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7vDz!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe4db8af6-eefd-42a0-8a01-6bacc407de7d_1224x334.jpeg)

 Update – Trump picked Ohio senator JD Vance as his VP running mate.  JD Vance reportedly owns up to $250k of Bitcoin.

Trump has said “I will ensure that the future of Crypto and Bitcoin will be made in the USA”, and will be speaking at the Bitcoin conference later this month.  We saw big moves in Cryptos and related stocks yesterday as the probability of a Trump victory in November reached new highs.  My favorite way to play this is COIN, one of my largest 3 positions (NVDA, CRWD) in my IRA.

In my view, COIN is setting up for a rally to $300+ by November, and can see ATHs in 2025 if the election plays out as currently priced in.  I prefer to own this name rather than to trade it (same as Bitcoin and all my crypto exposure).
