# Thursday Morning Notes 2.13

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Yesterday’s hotter than expected CPI print was taken in stride for the market with the opening gap down bought up throughout the session (SPX closed -0.27%, NDX +0.12%).

On CPI - Jan Core CPI rose +45bps MoM (vs consensus +30bps) and the YoY rate ticked up to 3.26%. The volatile used cars and airfares component boosted the core by 8bps. The car insurance component boosted the core by another 7bps. Another big contributor was egg prices, which were up 53% YoY and 13.8% MoM (unadjusted). Relative to the size of the surprise, it is a narrow beat with just a few categories contributing to the majority of the surprise. So, it is a little comforting that the number is not indicative of a broad-based reacceleration of inflation.

The market digested this news and this could be the main reason for yesterday’s dip buying. In any case, I can think of 3 notable gap downs in the past couple weeks (DeepSeek, Tariff announcement, and CPI) that were all bought up quickly. Bears have had multiple opportunities on news to take the market lower on news but buyers continue to step in. We are still within the 2 month trading range we have been in, but I believe we are getting closer and closer to a breakout to the upside here. I can’t predict when but I like the way this is setting up after yesterday’s action.
