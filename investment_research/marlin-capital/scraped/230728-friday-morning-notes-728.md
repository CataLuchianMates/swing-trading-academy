# Friday Morning Notes 7.28

Technical Update

We saw a big reversal of yesterday’s gap up with the indices forming an ugly bearish engulfing candle.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hFPH!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faaddbce9-7b88-4a9a-9439-a0a53c77720c_959x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hFPH!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faaddbce9-7b88-4a9a-9439-a0a53c77720c_959x723.jpeg)

The S&P 500 closed below its 8 EMA for the first time in nearly a month. The QQQ was knocked right back below its 8 EMA with a similarly ugly bearish engulfing candle.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Ybeq!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F32603024-04a1-4203-8056-156bda6f9643_965x720.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Ybeq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F32603024-04a1-4203-8056-156bda6f9643_965x720.jpeg)

Despite the bearish closes yesterday, we are set for a 2nd consecutive big gap up this morning. For me, these gap ups are not desirable to buy into until filled below.

Keep an eye on the weekly charts with today being the final day of the week. Coming into the day, both indices are forming bearish shooting star candles on the weekly. A strong day today to close the week would help to change the look things. I will also be watching to see if the indices can close back above their respective 8 EMAs heading into next week.
