QQQ daily - we are fairly extended below the 8 EMA (>5%). This is where I would typically look for an oversold bounce and a place to add to shorts. I believe we are starting to see some fear and panic, so it is my best guess that the selling at the index level could accelerate. As I stated in “Let’s Talk About It”, another 20-40% of multiple compression makes the most sense for the S&P 500.

With that said, I am looking for a larger bounce into the declining 8 EMA. This is where I plan to look to add to shorts.
