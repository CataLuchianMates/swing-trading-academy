# Wednesday Morning Notes 8.10

**Inflation Lower Than Expected for July**

US CPI DATA RELEASE:

- US CPI (Y/Y) **Jul: 8.5% (est 8.7%**; prev 9.1)

- US CPI (M/M) Jul: 0.0% (est 0.2%; prev 1.3)

- US CPI Core (M/M) Jul: 0.5% (est 0.5%; prev 0.7)

- US CPI Core (Y/Y) Jul: 5.9% (est 6.1%; prev 5.9)

July CPI of 8.5% came in lower than expected (8.7% consensus). This is the first downside inflation surprise we have seen in quite a while. On a month over month basis, July’s 0.0% change is the best number we have seen over the last year.

Following this morning’s CPI data release, future rate hike odds have tumbled. Sept 75bps rate hike odds have collapsed to just 35%, down from 80% pre CPI.

With such positive news out this morning, the market is set for a sizable gap up into the open. On a technical basis, the recent price action appeared to be setting up a concerning topping structure. With today’s news, the QQQ is set for a gap up to the $324 range. $325-$327 are the key resistance levels to watch ahead. If we can break through recent highs, a move to the 200 day moving average at $342 becomes the most likely scenario.
