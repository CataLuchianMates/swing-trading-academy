# Technical Analysis: Long Term Holdings

On December 2nd, I warned subscribers of 10-40% declines I saw coming in each of my long term holdings in my report [“Technical Analysis: Long Term Holdings”.](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/technical-analysis-long-term-holdings)

A little more than a month later, most of my stocks have already hit the price targets I gave on December 2nd.  At the time, I said I would consider buying at those price targets, and I did consider it, but ultimately decided to hold off.  The reason I decided to wait is simple – I still see more downside in growth.

The fact is, many of these stocks are in Stage 4 Declines after monstrous run ups.  Let’s take UPST, for example, a stock I no longer hold.

I have maintained that we are in a Bear Market, and I believe patience will prove to be the best option in the long run.  As I have said, there is no escaping the selling that will eventually be coming.

For ARKK, the probabilities have shifted and the charts are saying that a breakdown is imminent. The action in ARKK closely resembles the topping of the Nasdaq Bubble in 2000.  Look at these daily charts side by side.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hhOi!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd75d5208-a484-4c4f-8f89-00c2b4ab22c2_3242x802.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hhOi!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd75d5208-a484-4c4f-8f89-00c2b4ab22c2_3242x802.png)

At this point, the Nasdaq Bubble was in the midst of bursting. The index had another 63% of downside to come over the next 2 years. History never plays out the exact same way, but I am a fan of studying it. This is topping action in ARKK after a parabolic move.

For ARKK, we have an “h sell” setup on the weekly.  On the daily, we have another h sell.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jdNc!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7d0dc565-0206-4ac7-ba46-b58f68d6a48d_1593x959.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jdNc!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7d0dc565-0206-4ac7-ba46-b58f68d6a48d_1593x959.png)

Many growth stocks have already broken down lower than their recent bottoming attempts.  I expect ARKK to break its recent low (~$88) and head towards $78-$80.  This is where I will cover my current ARKK short hedge, and begin to buy in my IRA.
