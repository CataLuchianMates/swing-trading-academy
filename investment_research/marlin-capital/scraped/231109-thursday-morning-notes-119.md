# Thursday Morning Notes 11.9

Technical Update

US stocks finished slightly positive yesterday in another low volume/choppy session.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HLpi!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0bf4adfc-736e-4759-b4d8-bf0f3e220a6e_973x742.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HLpi!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0bf4adfc-736e-4759-b4d8-bf0f3e220a6e_973x742.jpeg)

Over the last 8 days, the NDX is up 8% and on a 9 day winning streak. The SPX is up 6.5% and on an 8 day winning streak. The RTY is up 4.7% over the same time, but currently on a 3 day losing streak.

Speaking of the RTY, options traders are betting heavily on more upside. In fact, IWM Call Volume has jumped to record highs amid the recent market rally.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!CHec!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F574ce5cc-bce2-4556-82f8-7e6a4838c83b_1290x1004.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!CHec!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F574ce5cc-bce2-4556-82f8-7e6a4838c83b_1290x1004.jpeg)

Sentiment - what a difference a week makes. According to the AAII Survey, bulls are now at 42.6% versus 24.3% last week. Bears are now at 27.2% versus 50.3% a week ago. The 1 week positive change in the Bull-Bear spread is the largest since January 2009 (2 months before the GFC Bear Market lows).
