The Russell 2000 is now sitting right below ATHs after fully recovering from the -9% November correction.

**The Big Picture**

On Wednesday, the Fed will once again be cutting rates while the S&P 500 sits within 1-2% of all-time highs.

The Fed has cut rates with the S&P 500 within 1% of ATHs 16 times in its history.

**The S&P 500 was higher a year later all 16 times for an average return of +15%.**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Efy5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb7bcdf6-2450-49b8-9311-03006b95b973_898x578.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Efy5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb7bcdf6-2450-49b8-9311-03006b95b973_898x578.jpeg)

Stretch that to SPX within 2% of ATHs, and it’s happened 20 times.

**Again, all 20 times, the S&P 500 was higher a year later.**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Zq5s!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F35d0f4c2-ec02-4176-9105-c3c453eb8838_975x675.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Zq5s!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F35d0f4c2-ec02-4176-9105-c3c453eb8838_975x675.png)

*h/t Ryan Detrick*

Historically, Bull Markets don’t end when the Fed is cutting rates (and there is no recession).

Add in the seasonal tailwinds I highlighted above. With Q3 earnings season nearly complete, the Corporate Buyback window has reopened. Citadel Securities projects +$5.3B of daily VWAP demand from buybacks into the close of the calendar year.

Q3 earnings season was strong overall. S&P 500 companies generated +12.9% YoY profit growth in Q3, with 9 of 12 sectors posting positive results (led by Financials, Materials, and Tech).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6HFl!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9bfbde44-b6fc-46b1-b4d0-2fd78ef0a397_1490x872.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6HFl!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9bfbde44-b6fc-46b1-b4d0-2fd78ef0a397_1490x872.jpeg)

This marked a 4th consecutive quarter of double-digits earnings growth. Q4 is on track to become the 5th.

Putting it all together, this is a strong backdrop for US stocks. I remain bullish into year-end. Big picture, my target remains $7300 for the SPX.
