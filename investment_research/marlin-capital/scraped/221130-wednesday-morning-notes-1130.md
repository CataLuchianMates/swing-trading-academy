# Wednesday Morning Notes 11.30

Technical Analysis

The SPX pulled back yesterday, with the index temporarily undercutting the key $3950 support level. A late afternoon rally allowed the SPX to close above the $3950 support level, however.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4UZe!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F93cfa1fc-ea6e-435c-9616-0d726c623976_1235x945.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4UZe!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F93cfa1fc-ea6e-435c-9616-0d726c623976_1235x945.jpeg)

On a technical level, there are a number of key support levels to watch below.

First is $3940-$3950, a level that held up yesterday. If this support level breaks, the index has more support near the 21 EMA ($3910-$3920). A break under $3900, and specifically $3880 (60 EMA) would be a very bearish occurrence.

Key resistance levels to watch above include the key $4000 level and the 200 day moving average at $4040-$4050.

Fed Chair Powell is scheduled to speak this afternoon at 1:30 EST.
