Technical Analysis

The market followed through to the upside on Friday after a morning gap up. The QQQ closed strong into some key resistance levels.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wU8H!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe49bac82-0889-46d1-94ef-d292bc6eed48_949x708.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wU8H!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe49bac82-0889-46d1-94ef-d292bc6eed48_949x708.jpeg)

The 21 and 60 EMAs both represent key resistance lines for the QQQ to break through. If successful, such a break above $308-$309 can lead to an upside move to $313 and potentially $319-$320.

On the other hand, if these resistance levels hold, and price is rejected, watch for support to come near $300-$302. In the context of this short term upside move, the $300 level must hold for the bullish setup to be valid.

Remember, CPI data will be released tomorrow morning, so overnight risk will be highly elevated either way.
