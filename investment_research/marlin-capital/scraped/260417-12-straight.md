**Positive Feedback Loop**

In [“Negative Feedback Loop”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/negative-feedback-loop?utm_source=publication-search), I talked about how feedback from the market is the only indicator you need. In other words, the feedback on my trades tells me all I need to know. **When my trades are making money and setups are plentiful, I place more trades. When my trades are faltering/not gaining any traction, or losing, I pull back on entering new trades.**

The feedback on my trades tells me how aggressive I should be. No predictions necessary.

Needless to say, the feedback I am getting from the market right now is extremely positive.

**CTA Update**

[On Tuesday](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/lockout-rally), I talked about the massive systematic buying wave building behind this market. The updated numbers from Goldman are even more staggering. 👇

Over the last 5 sessions, CTAs and systematic strategies have purchased +$86B of global equities. That is a top 5 all-time reading.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!o9RH!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7b2e7531-e030-44a8-8329-c86ec290c557_938x502.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!o9RH!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7b2e7531-e030-44a8-8329-c86ec290c557_938x502.png)

And they are not done yet. Goldman has this cohort modeled to purchase an additional +$70B over the next 5 sessions (in a flat tape). In other words, even if the market does nothing from here, systematic buyers are projected to pour another +$70B into stocks over the next week.

Historically, previous episodes of accelerated CTA demand like this have been followed by some short-term consolidation, but medium-term strength for the S&P 500. The average return 1 month later is +2.2%. The average return 3 months later is +8.2%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!l8MC!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F44a6ee94-89d9-4fe3-8a64-2d8d524dc09e_771x138.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!l8MC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F44a6ee94-89d9-4fe3-8a64-2d8d524dc09e_771x138.png)

Overall, systematic buying will continue to be a major tailwind for the market moving forward (specifically a major source of buying demand during dips). Don’t fight these bullish flows.
