ARKK Technical Analysis:

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ezss!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ffd851b7f-2758-471f-b72f-482c7b6e0f6a_1204x881.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ezss!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ffd851b7f-2758-471f-b72f-482c7b6e0f6a_1204x881.jpeg)

ARKK has not closed above its 21 EMA since November 9th, coming up on 3 months. We had one bounce to the EMA, but ultimately price failed there before making another leg down.

Let’s see if ARKK can finally close above its 21 EMA today. This would signal a short/intermediate term trend change in the ETF. On another note, I expect many of the components of the ETF to go their separate ways as we get into earnings announcements. I expect any high growth/high valuation company that shows any hiccups in Q4 to be punished by the market. This earnings season should set the tone for how many of these stocks trade for the next couple months.
