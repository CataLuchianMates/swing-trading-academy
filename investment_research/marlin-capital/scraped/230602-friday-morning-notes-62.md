Potential Long Trade Idea:

Long ARKK above $41.61

ARKK has been consolidating since the beginning of February with not much participation from these names. As I mentioned in Monday’s report, this bubble has already fully burst and crashed through my original Bear case.

I like this setup in ARKK for a long trade above yesterday’s highs ($41.61). We are set to see a bullish golden cross on the daily chart.

My stop will be below $39.90 (4.0% risk)
