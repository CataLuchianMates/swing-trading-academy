# Friday Morning Notes 9.1

Technical Update

The S&P 500 finished -0.16% on Thursday after reversing at $4530 resistance.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YNmk!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7a4a100e-ba14-43c2-a088-704a5d63e6bf_964x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YNmk!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7a4a100e-ba14-43c2-a088-704a5d63e6bf_964x726.jpeg)

A pullback/some more consolidation would be healthy here as we are extended from the moving averages after a big 4 day move.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $4530-$4550 and $4600.

Key support levels to watch below include $4500 and $4555-$4470.

The Friday into a 3 day weekend is typically a low volume day. The market is closed Monday for Labor Day. No planned trades for me today.

COUR, ROVR, UPWK - still long each with no changes.
