I mentioned a few times that I would not read too much into the EOY/late December market selloff we saw, as there were some one-off factors at play. Thus far, we have seen buyers step in at the key 60 EMA support levels in the SPX and QQQ.

A number of individual stocks emerged as potential leaders late last week and are already breaking out higher ahead of the market indices. There are no shortage of bullish setups heading into this week.

Below are my top long setups heading into this week:

Semis/AI Hardware stocks (NVDA, ALAB, MRVL, CRDO, COHR) led Friday’s rally. With this morning’s gap up, a number of them will need to pull in for me to be interested in buying.

While today’s big gap up certainly complicates things, there are still some good long setups to watch for today.

AVGO - Broadcom has been riding high on the AI wave, with its custom AI chips attracting a lot of attention from Wall Street. The company's recent earnings proved to be a major bullish catalyst with the stock gapping up to ATHs on a huge 2-day move on massive volume.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!S3Vv!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa9ab3185-7179-45e5-b756-ee098492d5ed_1147x860.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!S3Vv!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa9ab3185-7179-45e5-b756-ee098492d5ed_1147x860.jpeg)

The stock has consolidated beautifully and held the 8 EMA support level since its earnings gap. In my view, AVGO is setting up nicely for a move back to $250+ and beyond. I will be looking to get long AVGO early this week. Keep an eye on the live trading room for my entry on AVGO.

RBRK - Rubrik is a recent IPO cybersecurity firm known for its cloud data management and security solutions. The stock gapped up to ATHs on its highest volume day ever on Dec. 6th, but has since pulled back along with the market indices.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!JxOP!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F29e0c8c6-0626-4855-9389-48f57d4f4c7b_1194x892.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!JxOP!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F29e0c8c6-0626-4855-9389-48f57d4f4c7b_1194x892.jpeg)

This is a bullish pullback for RBRK as the stock has held 21 EMA support during a period of market turbulence. Now, with the pressure lifting off the general market, I believe RBRK is setting up nicely for a move back to $75+.

I will be looking to get long RBRK today on a break back above Friday’s highs. Keep an eye on the live trading room for my exact entry on RBRK.

BE - Demand for Data Centers is off the charts right now. On Thursday, Microsoft announced that it expects to spend $80B on AI-enabled data centers in fiscal 2025.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!R8am!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff3118bc9-a991-4a71-b331-91703e8e9277_1522x426.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!R8am!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff3118bc9-a991-4a71-b331-91703e8e9277_1522x426.jpeg)

The AI Essentials group I talked about in my [2025 Preview Report](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-roaring-20s-an-ai-revolution) looks primed for a huge 2025 (some others not on my list include GEV, VST, SRE, etc.).

Bloom Energy focuses on providing clean/reliable energy, which is particularly appealing right now given the current push towards sustainability and the data center industry's enormous energy demands.

BE stock is setting up nicely for a continued move higher out of this bull flag. I will be looking to get long this week on a move back above Friday’s highs ($25+). I am targeting $29+ on a potential breakout for BE.
