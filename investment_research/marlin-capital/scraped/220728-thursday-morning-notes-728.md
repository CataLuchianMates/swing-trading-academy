# Thursday Morning Notes 7.28

7/28 Morning Headline:

US Economy enters technical recession as GDP shrinks for a second consecutive quarter. Q2 GDP fell at a 0.9% annualized rate. It should be noted this number is not final and may be revised.

---

Technical Analysis:

The market staged a rally heading into Wednesday’s Fed decision, and then soared even higher after the announcement and presser.  The Fed delivered what was expected with a 75 BP rate hike, leading to yet another “event clearing squeeze”.  These types of rallies have been commonplace on Fed Day’s in 2022.  Interestingly, the market has struggled throughout this year in the immediate follow through of these rallies.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tvnw!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F67ea1861-f666-44f9-b73a-626cddaa6d09_660x512.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tvnw!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F67ea1861-f666-44f9-b73a-626cddaa6d09_660x512.jpeg)

I will be covering the bigger picture macro developments from today/this week and what it could mean for the market in my detailed report (to be released today).  On a technical basis, the current QQQ setup is looking quite bullish.  We broke through the 60 EMA on higher volume and gave a strong close near highs today.  An upside move above July 22nd highs ($308.60) would be a bullish follow through for the market, and where I would potentially look to enter TQQQ for a trade.  On the downside, it would be best for the QQQ to hold today’s lows ($298) for the bullish setup to remain valid.  We have gotten through a lot of the potential negative catalysts this week has had to offer, although not all of them (AAPL, AMZN earnings tomorrow).  Now, let’s see if the market can make a definitive follow through move to close out the week.
