# Tuesday Morning Notes 11.26

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Bullish start to the Holiday shortened week yesterday as trading volumes were healthy with the market digesting the implications of further nominations for Trump’s cabinet. The main focus was Trump’s pick of Scott Bessent for Treasury Secretary (he has favored lower deficits & lower inflation/has said US tarriffs should be implemented gradually and is viewed as a pro stocks/pragmatic pick).

NVDA finished -4% as some money continues to flow out of Mega Caps and into Small Caps (RTY +1.47% vs NDX +0.14%). The Retail Sector (XRT) was quite strong as optimism around Black Friday/the Holiday shopping season comes into focus. Elsewhere, Non-Profitable Tech (+1.4%), TMT 12M Losers (+2.3%) and Momentum Shorts (+3.4%) were the top gainers on the day.
