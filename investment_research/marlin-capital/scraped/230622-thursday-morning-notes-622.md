# Thursday Morning Notes 6.22

Technical Update

The SPX pull back continued on Wednesday with the index closing right at its 8 EMA support level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!az3h!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fde377405-fdf7-4e17-8523-4d10f47f4b30_970x718.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!az3h!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fde377405-fdf7-4e17-8523-4d10f47f4b30_970x718.jpeg)

So far, this pullback in the SPX has been rather orderly. Yesterday, we saw QQQ and tech lag while some YTD lagging sectors (energy, industrials, financials) led the way. For the SPX, I am continuing to look for this pullback to lead to the 21 EMA area (~$4300).

As for my longs, ARKK came within 6 cents of my stop loss at yesterday’s low. We are set for a gap down below my stop ($42.43) this morning, so this exiting sell order will fill at today’s open.

IWO and small caps outperformed in yesterday’s session. My stop loss on the rest of this position remains at $237.
