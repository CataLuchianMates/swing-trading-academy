# Friday Morning Notes 6.7

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Choppy session in the market indices yesterday with investors sitting on their hands ahead of this morning’s NFP report.  The SPX finished -0.02% on the day.

Jobs report:

-          Unemployment rises to 4.0% (vs 3.9% exp)

-          272k new jobs added vs 180k expected

-          Full-time workers: -625k

-          Part-time workers: +286k

We are set for a gap down this morning on the backs of this morning’s jobs report.  Overall, this report shows more signs of weakening in the labor market with permanent job losses on the rise and only part-time jobs seeing additions.

Updates on my trades –

ONON – No changes here.  Still holding the final 20% of my position.  I am moving my stop loss to $40.34 on the rest.

VITL – Little pullback yesterday but doing nothing wrong so far.  Still holding the final 30% of my position.  I am moving my stop loss to $41.35 on the rest.

NVO – Great start to this trade so far.  Still holding my full position here.

HIMS – Formed an inside day yesterday.  I will be moving my stop to the inside day lows at $20.82 (hard stop) as I do not want to see this profitable trade go negative on me (entry was $20.33).  This will ensure at least a 2.4% gain on this trade and likely will be stopped out today.

APP – Attempted to breakout yesterday but closed weak and near lows, forming an ugly shooting star candle.  I will be using a hard stop at $81.82 for today after yesterday’s breakout failed to stick.
