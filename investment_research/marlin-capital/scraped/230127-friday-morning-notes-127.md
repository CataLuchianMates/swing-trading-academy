# Friday Morning Notes 1.27

Technical Analysis

The market continued its strong rally yesterday, breaking through and closing above the $4050 level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!agf9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fff1bc7e0-8b02-456b-9e67-452e7e1e06fe_959x729.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!agf9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fff1bc7e0-8b02-456b-9e67-452e7e1e06fe_959x729.jpeg)

We are set for a small gap down in the indices this morning. Key support levels to watch below include $4000, $3965-$3970 (200 SMA), $3950, $3920-$3930 and $3890-$3900.

The next key upside level to watch is $4100 (Dec and Nov highs).

NVDA tagged my $200 target level before pulling back. I locked in profits at $190 and $200 and am now holding the remaining position with a stop at $185.

CLH hit my first target level ($130). Next is $140. I took some gains at $130 and moved my stop up to $124.

MELI had a great day yesterday. My first target is $1200. I am moving my stop up to breakeven ($1100).

AMAT continues to act well technically. I am moving my stop up to $112 (in the money).
