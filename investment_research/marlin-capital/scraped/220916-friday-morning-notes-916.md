For those following my ES Futures trade, I covered 1/3rd of my short after hours yesterday at $3895. For those new or unfamiliar to futures trading, I will break down this trade below.

The cost to enter 1 ES contract is typically around a $12k-$14k margin requirement, depending on your broker. In my case, the margin requirement to enter my short at $4026 was roughly $12k.

At $3895, we have profited 131 points to the short side on this futures trade. This means we have profited around $6500 **per contract** so far on this short trade ($50 x 131 points) , equating to a >50% ROI (return on investment),

I am now holding the remaining 2/3rds of my short with a 2nd target level of $3830.
