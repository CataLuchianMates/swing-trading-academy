# Tuesday Morning Notes 3.5

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The market pulled back to kick off the week yesterday with the S&P 500 finishing -0.12% on the day.

The performance gap among the Mag 7 continues to widen.  It’s as if the winners in this cohort have formed their own club and are leaving the rest behind.  GOOGL -2.76% (ongoing negative feedback around Gemini rollout, now -13% off highs), AAPL -2.5% on $2B EU fine and TSLA -7% following China shipment numbers slumping to the lowest in more than 1 year.

Updates on my trades-

PLTR - Closed out this position yesterday at the open for a +2.1% gain. On to other ideas.

UBER - Hit some resistance right around the $82 level/ATHs. Still holding here and moving my stop to $79.31.

The measured move on this Bitcoin breakout is $120k, so I expect COIN to rally back towards ATHs ($429) if this crypto breakout plays out.
