# Friday Morning Notes 12.29

**Daily Market Commentary:**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!fywf!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc2f77eaa-4792-46b6-a64d-1f2b29d374f9_493x263.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!fywf!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc2f77eaa-4792-46b6-a64d-1f2b29d374f9_493x263.jpeg)

Note - Today is the final trading day of 2023.

Stocks traded flat on Thursday as this low volume/low volatility week continues. The S&P 500 closed +0.04% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qKdL!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa018b7c6-0af2-4c9a-8dab-531cc314da68_964x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qKdL!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa018b7c6-0af2-4c9a-8dab-531cc314da68_964x723.jpeg)

SPX implied vols were up small even as we remain in a very low realized environment.  In the run up to the final closing bell of 2023, the QQQ wavered slightly (-0.05% on Thursday).  Tech is set for its best year since 1999, led by the the Mega Cap Magnificent 7.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BXYn!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc195d8b5-8fd6-4e1e-828f-ecab28fda2c2_1219x895.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BXYn!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc195d8b5-8fd6-4e1e-828f-ecab28fda2c2_1219x895.jpeg)

Small Caps finished -0.38% on the day and head into the final trading day of 2023 “only” up +28% on the year (the laggard index).  We were in on the leveraged small cap catch-up trade in December with the group outperforming, and I believe this will continue to be a theme to watch in 1H 2024.  My full list of themes to watch for next year will be included in my 2024 preview report, to be released early next week.  Stay tuned!

Happy New Year Everyone!
