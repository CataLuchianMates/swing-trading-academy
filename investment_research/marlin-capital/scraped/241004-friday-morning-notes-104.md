# Friday Morning Notes 10.4

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Light volume/choppy trading session yesterday with the SPX closing -0.17%.

The VIX closed above 20 and the yield curve steepened with 10YR yields pushing higher amidst a complicated start to October (Middle East, election looming, China policy, etc.).

This Morning’s NFP Report –

The US economy added 254k jobs in September, well above expectations of 147k.  The unemployment rate came in at 4.1%, below expectations of 4.2%.

This was a strong jobs report in a market environment where good data is definitely good for the market (and bad data = bad for the market).  Following this morning’s jobs report, the odds of a 25bps cut in November have soared to 93%.  Prior to the report, the odds of a 25bps cut were around 50%.

We are set for a sizable gap up in stocks this morning with SPX futures currently +0.84%.  I will continue managing my current long positions (SN, NMM, AVO) with stops in place.

My top trade setup for today –

MU – This one pulled back to the 8 EMA post earnings, but the selling momentum stalled in the past couple days as support held up.

We are set for a gap above $104 this morning.  I will look to get long MU this morning with a stop below yesterday’s lows at $99.39 (~5% risk).
