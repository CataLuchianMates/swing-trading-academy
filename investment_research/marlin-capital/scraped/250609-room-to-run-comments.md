## David Marlin Q&A (Comments)

**Q:** Regarding the 6 new midcap stocks....do you hold these in your personal portfolio?  Also, it looks like 4 of the 6 have gone up a lot in the last few months....would you be initiating full or partial positions at these levels?  Wait for pullbacks?  Thoughts appreciated!

**David Marlin:** These 6 are being held in a separate portfolio I created for this particular strategy. Given that the strategy here is more fundamental driven and I see multi-bagger potential returns from current valuations (as per the table in yesterday’s report), I would not necessarily be waiting for a pullback. For longer term investments, buying 5-10% lower/higher is not going to make or break the trade. The longer your outlook, the less “timing the market/stock” matters (and as mentioned, I see >100% upside potential fundamentally on all of these even after the run ups).
