**My Big Picture Market Thoughts**

Last week was an incredibly bullish week for US stocks.

Many names, including the most speculative groups, are currently breaking out of long monthly bases to ATHs. This is clearly “risk on” type of price action.

**It’s a Bull Market you know.**

Big picture, I remain bullish here for 3 key reasons:

**1.) The Fed is Easing**

The Federal Reserve is **just starting to ease again** with the SPX already at ATHs.

This has happened 16 times in history. In all 16 instances, the SPX was higher a year later (for an avg gain of +15%).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Efy5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb7bcdf6-2450-49b8-9311-03006b95b973_898x578.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Efy5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcb7bcdf6-2450-49b8-9311-03006b95b973_898x578.jpeg)

So long as we avoid a recession, this is huge tailwind for US stocks.

**3.) Positioning is NOT Euphoric, it’s Neutral**

Despite the huge post-April rally, total positioning in US equities STILL remains slightly underweight, according to Goldman’s Total Positioning Indicator.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!es5q!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4878b50c-91c7-4a65-8f1f-2691f93e5083_1267x1209.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!es5q!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4878b50c-91c7-4a65-8f1f-2691f93e5083_1267x1209.png)

This is due to Discretionary positioning, which is particularly light (in the 44th percentile).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2Yj7!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5b8f3f8c-065f-48d8-963f-81442704fc67_1129x798.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2Yj7!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5b8f3f8c-065f-48d8-963f-81442704fc67_1129x798.jpeg)

Positioning has been a reliable indicator for us all year. It helped us [turn bearish in February](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/red-flags-emerge?utm_source=publication-search) and [bullish at the April lows](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/bottomed-out).

And right now, total positioning is nowhere near euphoric or extreme. In fact, it’s neutral. To me, this signals more upside ahead.
