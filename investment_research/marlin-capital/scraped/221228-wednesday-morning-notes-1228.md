# Wednesday Morning Notes 12.28

Technical Update

Low volume chop in the S&P 500 to start the week on Tuesday. The SPX made a low of $3813 as buyers stepped up near the key $3800 support level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Cmja!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd74e7c14-6f45-4219-8dae-0fbbd8080210_1139x859.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Cmja!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd74e7c14-6f45-4219-8dae-0fbbd8080210_1139x859.jpeg)

I am expecting more low volume action this week to close out the year. $3800 remains the key support level to watch on the downside. As long as this level holds, we are likely to stay rangebound into the new year.

Resistance levels to watch above include $3850 and $3890-$3900.
