# Thursday Morning Notes 1.26

Technical Analysis

Yesterday’s gap down and morning dip was bought up as the S&P 500 retook the key $4000 level I discussed in yesterday’s morning notes.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RZLt!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0049115b-6518-4c43-93be-5df02c081239_959x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RZLt!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0049115b-6518-4c43-93be-5df02c081239_959x724.jpeg)

Now, we are set for a gap up this morning following TSLA earnings. In my view, the market looks ready for a test of Nov/Dec highs ($4100). Key resistance levels to watch above include $4050 and $4100.

Key support levels to watch below include $4000, $3965-$3970 (200 SMA), $3950, $3920-$3930 and $3890-$3900.

NVDA has hit my first target level ($190) and appears set to test the $200 level. Remember to lock in profits.

CLH has hit my first target level ($130). Next target is $140. Remember to lock in profits.

MELI and AMAT continue to act well technically. Remember to raise stops.
