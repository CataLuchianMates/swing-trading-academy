# Wednesday Morning Notes 3.1

Technical Update

The S&P 500 once again faltered around the $4000/60 EMA level yesterday, closing out the day -0.3%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!uymN!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4e10c775-0a0a-494b-b620-9f700a2eca07_1100x841.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!uymN!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4e10c775-0a0a-494b-b620-9f700a2eca07_1100x841.jpeg)

The SPX has been bearishly consolidating between the 60 EMA and the 200 SMA. In my view, the index looks poised to breakdown from this Bear flag setup in March.

We are set for a gap down today to start the month of March. Key support levels to watch below include $3970-$3975 and the critical $3940 level (200 SMA).

On an upside reversal, key resistance levels to watch above include $3997-$4000 (60 EMA), $4020, and $$4040-$4060 (21 EMA).

CLH stock reports earnings after the close today. I will be exiting the remainder of my position in the morning today. I am happy with the gains here and not willing to take the risk of seeing my profit evaporate on earnings. On to the next trade.
