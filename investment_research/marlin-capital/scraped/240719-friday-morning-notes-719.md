# Friday Morning Notes 7.19

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Another day of tough sledding in the market on Thursday with the S&P 500 closing -0.78%, NDX -0.87%, and RTY -1.8%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G-Is!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F56425074-e032-46ba-aed8-d9a2821f1099_971x714.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G-Is!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F56425074-e032-46ba-aed8-d9a2821f1099_971x714.jpeg)

Goldman’s Momentum basket stabilized a bit yesterday, now down -11% off highs (15-20% is where support has been found during the last few sell offs).  De-risking is still apparent in 12M winners (-1.8%) and lower quality pockets of the market with Most Short, 12M losers, Memes, and Non-Profitable Tech all down >3% yesterday.

Mega Cap Tech is down -14% vs Non-Profitable Tech over the past 6 sessions (15-25% is where support is usually found in this pair).

The story of the market the last week - rotation, rotation, rotation.

Biggest Semi outflows in 2+ years.

Biggest Small Cap inflows in 15+ years.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hYZs!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F29aee8d5-d2df-46d4-aa36-edbdf82762d8_1121x374.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hYZs!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F29aee8d5-d2df-46d4-aa36-edbdf82762d8_1121x374.png)

After the bell, NFLX kicked off Big Tech earnings.  NFLX reported a beat (Q2 net adds +8M Q/Q vs consensus ~5M), but guided Q3 revenues below street estimates ($9.73B vs $9.83B consensus and notes “NETFLIX SEES Q3 NET ADDS LOWER VS YEAR AGO” (for reference, Q3 was +8.76M).  Stock is currently trading flat this morning.
