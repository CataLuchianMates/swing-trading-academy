# Friday Morning Notes 11.25

I hope everyone had a great Thanksgiving Holiday! The stock market closes at 1 PM EST today.

---

Technical Update

US stocks have moved higher this week on primarily low volume. The SPX is now approaching the key 200 day moving average, which is around the spot that the previous 2 Bear rallies have failed.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gOaj!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc90b641c-ebc6-4859-9baf-764ada9c1752_958x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gOaj!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc90b641c-ebc6-4859-9baf-764ada9c1752_958x724.jpeg)

On the weekly time frame, we are also nearing key resistance in the 40 week EMA.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zWRX!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff5a40d7b-1a9d-4411-8ae9-4611b5421fa3_973x720.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zWRX!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff5a40d7b-1a9d-4411-8ae9-4611b5421fa3_973x720.jpeg)

Key resistance levels to watch ahead include $4040, $4060, and $4100.

Look for previously strong resistance ($4000) to become support on a pullback. If $4k breaks, $3950 is the next support level I will be watching.
