# Thursday Morning Notes 8.31

Technical Update

A quiet day for the indices yesterday with the SPX closing just below $4500.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $4530-$4550 and $4600.

Key support levels to watch below include $4500 and $4555-$4470.

COUR, ROVR, UPWK - all 3 of these inside day setups I posted in Wednesday’s Morning Notes triggered long yesterday. I’m long all 3.
