# Tuesday Morning Notes 2.27

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Monday was a lackluster session for the indices with the S&P 500 finishing -38 bps, its worst Monday since early December and 2nd worst Monday since last June.

With that said, there were some big moves in select individual names.  We caught a massive move [in CLSK](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/bubble-talk).

CLSK - Wow! Huge move here off my long entry at $17.07.  At Monday's highs, we were up +22.7%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vkXw!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F42038ffb-c708-4c12-b064-167faeccc10d_1200x891.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vkXw!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F42038ffb-c708-4c12-b064-167faeccc10d_1200x891.jpeg)

Our risk on the trade was -6.9%, so this is more than 3x1 on our risk.  This is also where I will look to take a good amount of profits.  I began selling at $20 and scaled out of half at 3x1 ($20.6).  My bigger picture targets on this breakout are $23 and $25.

We are set for a continuation gap up towards $23 this morning, where I will be looking to take more profits for a 34.7% gain. I will look to hold my remaining 1/4th position with a trailing stop ($17.5 for now).

ARM - gapped above my entry point, so I do not enter on these trades. In my trading, I do not like to buy random gap ups with no notable catalyst. These trades can still work, but it is a different trade than the one I outlined in "Bubble Talk".  This is because (1) the risk is higher, (2) the risk/reward is not as good, (3) and random / no catalyst / low volume gaps tend to get filled (so the success rate on these trades certainly drops).  After yesterday's gap and go move, ARM is no longer setup for a quality long entry here, in my view.

With that said, I am seeing no shortage of potential long setups as we head into Tuesday.  My top 2 setups for today are PLTR and LYFT.

PLTR

I traded this one off earnings day for 12%+ gains, exiting my position on the recent consolidation.  I like the way it’s setting up here again with an inside day candle yesterday.  I will look to re-enter long on an inside day break.  My bigger picture target here remains $30.
