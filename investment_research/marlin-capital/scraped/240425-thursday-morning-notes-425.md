# Thursday Morning Notes 4.25

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Choppy session out there yesterday with earnings moves dictating the direction.  The indices finished near flat on the day following the gap up (led by TSLA earnings gap).

TXN was +5% while NVDA -3% and semis flat following the bullish revenue forecast from TXN (poor sentiment going in), which drove broad based strength across analog semis.

META -12% on earnings with an inline print (not good enough for investors).  The guide on Q2 revs was slightly below at the midpoint.  FY capex raised which is a potential positive for semis (early strength there this morning as well).

Other key earnings from yesterday:

ALGN: Q1 ahead of consensus/high end guide, ’24 growth raised from 6% to %

NOW: -4% on solid print, crowded software long

IBM: -6% on headline rev miss.  FY FCF reaffirmed.

LRCX: Beat and guide in line. Stock +2%.

All eyes will be on MSFT and GOOGL prints AH this afternoon.
