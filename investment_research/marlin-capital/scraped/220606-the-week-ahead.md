# The Week Ahead

Technical Analysis

On Friday, the QQQ broke the 15 minute upwards channel I have been discussing since the market temporarily bottomed.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!xnLb!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F02fb6916-eeaf-455a-a2bd-0d2000623d83_1396x669.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!xnLb!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F02fb6916-eeaf-455a-a2bd-0d2000623d83_1396x669.png)

This does not mean that the rally attempt is over, but it did prompt me to close out my TQQQ long as the risk became too elevated to hold.

On the daily chart, the SPY is now at a key point where this rally attempt can either fail, or begin another leg higher.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!N9y0!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7cb4f907-7552-43b9-8ec6-e0dce9a7b307_1396x669.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!N9y0!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7cb4f907-7552-43b9-8ec6-e0dce9a7b307_1396x669.png)

We are set for a gap up on today’s open. A move over $417 increases the odds of another leg higher for the market. A move below $407 would shift the probabilities to this rally being over.
