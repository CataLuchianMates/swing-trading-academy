**2026 Portfolio Update: Re-Buying SNDK**

This morning, I will re-buying SNDK in the 2026 Portfolio at the open.

I [exited SNDK on 2/4](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/2026-portfolio-changes) at $612 (for +153% gains) as part of my broader portfolio de-risking. At the time, the chart looked like a possible Climax Top (after a massive run). With the overall market deteriorating rapidly, I made the decision to exit. But, as I talked about [in yesterday’s report](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/leaders-shine-bright), that decision to exit now looks premature to me.

Re-buying SNDK here at ~$658 means I am paying ~7% more than where I sold. I am not losing any sleep over that. Missing ~7% on a name with this kind of fundamental momentum (+ a name that we made +153% on) is not something I will dwell on.

Here’s why I changed my mind. 👇

SNDK is a fundamental AND technical leader. After watching the chart build out more over the last ~3 weeks, I no longer believe the recent price action looks like a Climax Top. In fact, SNDK has held up incredibly well during a very difficult market environment for most AI names, which is exactly the kind of relative strength you want to see in a leader. When a stock refuses to go down (even after a massive runup) while everything around it is selling off, that tells you something important about the underlying demand.

And the fundamentals here remain among the very best in the entire market. As I covered in detail [in yesterday’s report](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/leaders-shine-bright), SNDK just delivered one of the most dramatic EPS + rev guidance raises we have seen on Wall Street. EPS guidance for next quarter came in roughly +182% above Street consensus. Rev guidance +58% above consensus. The company is now essentially debt-free, operating in full allocation mode, and sitting at the center of the Memory Supercycle I talked about extensively this weekend.

SNDK is a fundamental and technical leader. If the market can stabilize (and see another wave higher), SNDK can continue to lead, and the stock can see $1000+ this year.
