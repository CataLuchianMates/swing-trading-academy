# Wednesday Morning Notes 4.17

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Price action was mixed yesterday with incremental hawkish comments from Fed Chair Powell in the afternoon stealing the show.  The SPX finished -0.21% on the day and closed just below its 60 EMA.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LVeu!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fef9afedb-4ece-4243-9592-998545b9a887_961x729.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LVeu!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fef9afedb-4ece-4243-9592-998545b9a887_961x729.jpeg)

Powell’s comments indicated that the recent data has not given the Fed the confidence to cut rates and instead will take longer to achieve that confidence.  Yields continued to climb and the 10 YR is now north of 4.65%, a level not seen since last November.

The market is not pricing its 1st full cut until November now, quite different than the 3 full cuts the market expected by November just a month ago.  Fund Managers surveyed by BofA are now expecting 2 rate cuts before year end compared to 5+ at the beginning of 2024.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5-F_!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9eca022f-0622-4f03-a69c-a16ad07ed585_843x447.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5-F_!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9eca022f-0622-4f03-a69c-a16ad07ed585_843x447.jpeg)

MU – Hit my stop at $119 yesterday so I am all out on the final 2/3rds.  Taking the gain here and moving to cash as we observe how the major indices react to key support.
