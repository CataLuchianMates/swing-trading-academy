# The End of March Shift

**Daily Market Commentary:**

It’s all about the Fed meeting today.

There is basically a 0% chance of a rate cut today, so Powell’s commentary/any changes to the Fed’s outlook will be the most important thing to focus on.

The market is rallying so far into the event with the SPX up +0.8% as of this writing. I talked about the technicals yesterday, which are concerning on a longer time frame. With that said, if the market can respond well to today’s Fed meeting, we have 2 major catalysts to the upside through the end of March:

(1) CTAs are finished selling and will be forced to buy on rallies

I covered these 2 positioning tailwinds yesterday. Anyone who has been reading Marlin Capital for the last month is aware of my view that positioning was the biggest catalyst for this current market correction. We saw the biggest drop in US equities allocation on record, as investor de-risked from ATH long positioning to now underweight. Hedge Funds, CTAs, and institutional investors all de-levered at an unprecedented pace.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7VYC!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F596cec04-8cd8-4ffa-88a0-4a56a7c4c399_1280x589.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7VYC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F596cec04-8cd8-4ffa-88a0-4a56a7c4c399_1280x589.jpeg)

Bigger picture, it is very possible that positioning can still go lower (and stay low for a prolonged period of time). But, through the end of March, this is shaping up to now be a clear tailwind for US equities. Remember, CTAs are the trend following community. Currently, they are max short US equities and max long European equities.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!t94W!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F66051abc-8273-43cb-895e-d5f8734effef_1125x744.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!t94W!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F66051abc-8273-43cb-895e-d5f8734effef_1125x744.png)

This is the largest spread we have ever seen. For me, this is a clear sign of trend exhaustion. US stocks are -4% YTD while European stocks are +20% YTD. Based off lopsided positioning alone, I expect to see some reversion of this trade into quarter end. I believe we will see some rebalancing back into US equities here.

It is all about the Fed meeting now, but if we see a positive reaction to today’s FOMC, I see potential for a US market rally into quarter end. I am turning bullish into the end of March. I will be looking to play this potential rally to the long side in my active portfolio.
