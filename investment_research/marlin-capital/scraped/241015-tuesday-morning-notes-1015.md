# Tuesday Morning Notes 10.15

**Daily Market Commentary:**

It was a quiet Monday (not unexpected given the Bond market holiday) to kick off the week yesterday with the SPX clinching its 46th ATH of the year.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-xu9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc5bd9748-be03-4f1a-ae4c-e8d87d345807_1198x891.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-xu9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc5bd9748-be03-4f1a-ae4c-e8d87d345807_1198x891.jpeg)

It was the 3rd slowest volume session of the year with 9.5B shares traded and the 8th slowest notional session with $465B traded.  Breadth was relatively strong with nearly every sector in the green except energy.  The Nasdaq is now less than 1% below its July 10th ATH.

NVDA gained +2.4% to close at its highest level on record.  Improving AI sentiment has helped NVDA finish 7 of the last 8 sessions in the green.  Elsewhere, Software continued its green streak (5 days straight) amidst cleaner positioning and a recovery in sentiment.

Active Portfolio –

SN – Came within 25 cents of hitting my stop loss yesterday but rallied into the close.  I am moving my stop on the rest to yesterday’s lows ($104.77), ensuring a +14.7% gain on the final 1/5th of this long.

MU – Good day yesterday and this trade is beginning to play out nicely.  My stop loss is set at $100.84.  First target = post earnings highs at $114-$115
