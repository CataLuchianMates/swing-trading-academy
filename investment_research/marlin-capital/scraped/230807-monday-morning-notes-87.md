# Monday Morning Notes 8.7

Technical Update

The market faded hard on Friday with the SPX closing -0.53% on the day.  The index was rejected by 8 EMA resistance and formed an ugly bearish engulfing candle to close out the week.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Me6d!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faae6f294-abe3-4f74-bbce-ef09cebb00e0_1195x903.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Me6d!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faae6f294-abe3-4f74-bbce-ef09cebb00e0_1195x903.jpeg)

We closed at the lows of the week and below the 21 EMA once again.  Market breadth (% of stocks above their 50d MA) has deteriorated since recent index highs.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!A9xs!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5a66e491-e519-4d38-a640-3e41724a715c_1144x640.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!A9xs!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5a66e491-e519-4d38-a640-3e41724a715c_1144x640.jpeg)

Technically, these are all reasons for caution as we head into this week.  I initiated a small long position in CLS at Friday’s open, but have no plans to add any more long exposure so soon with general market conditions deteriorating.

A move back above the 21 EMA ($4510) is the first step for bulls.  On the other hand, last week’s lows become key support ($4470-$4480) as we head into this week.  A break below and there is not much support until the 60 EMA ($4390-$4400).
