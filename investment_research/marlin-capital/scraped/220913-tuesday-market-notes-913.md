# Tuesday Market Notes 9.13

# **August CPI Release:**

* CPI YoY rises +8.3% vs. 8.1% estimate and vs 8.5% last month.
* Aug consumer price index (CPI) month-over-month was up an unexpected +0.1% vs. est. (-0.1%) and unchanged prior print, while
* The Core CPI reading (ex food & energy) month-over-month rose +0.6%, more than double the +0.3% estimate and prior read of 0.3% and
* Food component of CPI rose by 11.4% y/y in August, up from +10.9% prior and fastest rate since April 1979
* Core year-over-year rose +6.3% vs. estimate +6.1% and +5.9% in July
* 10-yr yield spikes to 3.42%, more than 12 bps jump post CPI data; the 2-yr at 3.67% up 10.7 bps

Technical Analysis

Throw out all your technical analysis from yesterday. As I’ve said before, technicals mean next to nothing in the face of market moving news events. In this case, higher than expected CPI was obviously not priced into the market (in fact, I feel pretty confident in saying a lower than expected CPI print was priced in).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!580I!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2fda083f-7e57-481f-9cd3-3e03ea0bffca_1024x750.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!580I!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2fda083f-7e57-481f-9cd3-3e03ea0bffca_1024x750.jpeg)

This morning, we are set for an immediate gap down in the QQQ to the $303 region. Key support remains at $301-$302. If this breaks we can see $295-$296 and later $290-$291.
