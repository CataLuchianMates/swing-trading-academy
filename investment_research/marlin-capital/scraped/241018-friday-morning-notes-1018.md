# Friday Morning Notes 10.18

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Early rally yesterday led by broader Tech and Semis following Taiwan Semi (+11%) results that came in above even the most bullish expectations. The rally largely fizzled out into the close, though, with the SPX finishing -0.02% and NDX +0.08%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!aFI5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4228cb75-1149-4b68-a982-99de77afc204_1215x888.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!aFI5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4228cb75-1149-4b68-a982-99de77afc204_1215x888.jpeg)

We saw some notable risk reduction over the last several sessions, particularly after ASML’s big miss, which undoubtedly helped to create a better risk/reward setup into TSM’s print.

NFLX is +6% in the pre-market after a solid Q3 report driven by better than expected operating income and revenue, slightly weaker subscriber net adds (largely drive by LatAm) and provided forward commentary that was inline/slightly ahead of Street estimates for Q4 and FY 2025.

Today, we have $3.0 trillion of notional options expiry including $1.9 trillion of SPX options and $515B of notional single stock options (the largest October expiration on record). The rest is across ETFs. This should undoubtedly lead to a more volatile session today with some possible whipsaw action.

The last few weeks have felt like a hesitant grind higher, as everyone’s playbook suggested a sell off into the election followed by a rally into year end. This is, after all, what the historical seasonality playbook of election years suggested. That sell off has yet to materialize, and as we approach the election without the volatility investors are forced to contemplate a scenario of “what if we actually keep rallying into election day and I’m very underweight”.

Active Portfolio -

SN - Hit my stop on the final portion at $104.77 yesterday. Sold the last portion of my long for +14.7% profits. Position closed after nice gains on this trade.

MU - Boosted by TSM results yesterday. Hit my first target of $114, where I took 25% profits +10%. I am moving my stop on the rest to $102.70. If we hit $115, I will lock in some more profits and keep 2/3rds of my original long.
