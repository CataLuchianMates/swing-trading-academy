# Wednesday Afternoon Notes 5.14

**Daily Market Commentary Notes:**

* Market flattish today after the huge 2-day rally to kick off the week.
* The Macro Bearish outlook that was consensus 5-6 weeks ago has been rapidly reset following the 90-day tariff pause in early April and now the US-China Trade deal over the weekend.
* With that, we have seen a massive scramble for long exposure among institutions (both systematic & discretionary).
* I covered the Systematic portion yesterday (now ~3.5/10, CTAs long exposure in 40th percentile vs history)
* Vol Control Funds (which I covered in detail last week) have bought back $35B of US equities over the past 1 week (97th percentile) to get the overall historical exposure back to a less extreme under allocation to US stocks, but still sub 20th percentile vs history
* Monday saw the 2nd largest notional net buying among Hedge Funds in 5+ years (+4.3 z score), driven by short covers and to a lesser extent long buys (1.6 to 1).
