We have a very busy Macro tape this week with CPI + FOMC tomorrow and Initial Jobless Claims on Friday.  Another quiet/consolidation day today ahead of CPI + FOMC tomorrow would be my base case.

Elsewhere, there was a “sell the news” event in AAPL (-1.9% yesterday) on the lack of major AI updates at the WWDC keynote.  Semis led the way yesterday +1.45% with some impressive moves in MU, LRCX, AMAT.  Renewables and solar stocks were hot with FSLR (top options bull bet) and NXT seeing big activity in the options market amid yesterday’s rallies.

Updates on my trades –

ONON – No changes here.  Holding final 20% of long position with a stop at $40.34.

VITL – Liquidity sweep/stop raid hit me out yesterday morning at my $41.35 stop.  Position closed for a +9.1% gain on the final 30% portion.

NVO – No changes here and still holding my full position.  My closing stop is placed below $135.

No new planned trades for me today ahead of tomorrow’s FOMC meeting.

Side note - If you use Charles Schwab brokerage, as I do, you may be having technical issues with not being able to log in to Schwab/Thinkorswim this morning.  Customer support confirmed to me this is a mass issue they are having.
