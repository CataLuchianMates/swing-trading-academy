# Wednesday Morning Notes 7.26

Technical Update

Another quiet session on Tuesday with mixed reactions post-market in MSFT and GOOGL.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wfXU!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F450e9b9c-1d1b-441e-93c1-7d05ba5ae12f_1210x910.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wfXU!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F450e9b9c-1d1b-441e-93c1-7d05ba5ae12f_1210x910.jpeg)

All eyes are on today’s Fed meeting now. The options market is pricing in a 0.76% move today in either direction for this afternoon’s FOMC decision. The market has been underpricing vol thus far through earnings season, so this could certainly be a similar setup into today’s FOMC.

There is a 99% probability the Fed will hike 25 basis points, according to Fed funds futures. More important, in my view, will be the committee’s commentary on future meetings and Fed Chair Powell’s tone in the press conference.
