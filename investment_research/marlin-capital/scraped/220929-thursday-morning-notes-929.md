# Thursday Morning Notes 9.29

Technical Analysis

The market had a strong bounce today, with the SPY and QQQ each closing up around 2%. The QQQ temporarily broke out of the 15 minute range I have been following, and ended up closing right at the $280 resistance level. In my view, the setup is suggesting a continuation of this bounce that will take the QQQ to the $285-$290 region.

We are set for a gap down this morning towards the middle of our range. Look for price support to come in the $275 region. If this level breaks, the last line of support is the bottom of the range ($270-$272). Key resistance remains at the $278-$280 level, which the QQQ to breakout from.
