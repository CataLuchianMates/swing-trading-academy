# Thursday Morning Notes 8.17

Technical Update

The grind down in the market accelerated on Wednesday with the S&P 500 finishing -0.76% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bC0o!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F98a3531b-b0cc-4b45-a026-b6127a10e1b4_1150x863.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bC0o!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F98a3531b-b0cc-4b45-a026-b6127a10e1b4_1150x863.jpeg)

We closed right at the 60 EMA and $4400 support. A decisive break below the 60 EMA ($4400) opens the door for a further decline to $4300 and $4250 support levels.

We are set for a small gap up this morning. Key resistance levels to watch above include $4440-$4450 and $4460.
