# Thursday Morning Notes 12.12

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Risk on session yesterday following the in-line CPI report (Core CPI came in at +31bps vs +30bps consensus…odds of Fed cutting by 25bps at the 12/18 meeting jumped to 98%).

The S&P 500 finished just a hair shy of making its 58th record high close this year. For those keeping score at home, the only years with more all-time-highs: 1995 (77), 2021 (70), 1964 (65), and 2017 (62).

Strength was largely driven by Mega Caps and Semis (note - equal weight SPX finished flattish). Moves in the factor complex normalized with Momentum Longs vs Shorts finishing +2% (largely driven by momentum longs bouncing after a rough start to the week).

The big micro story of the day was the divergence in ride sharing with GOOGL +5.5% and TSLA +6% vs UBER -6% and LYFT -5% following a 3rd party report of increased share for GOOGL’s Waymo. In Semis, AVGO finished +6% on reports of AAPL chip collaboration.

Active Portfolio -

LRN - No changes here. My trailing stop is set to $105 on the rest, ensuring +23% gains on the final portion here.

TWLO - Moving my trailing stop to $108 on the rest, ensuring +30% gains on the final portion here.

SNOW - Bounced yesterday but we are seeing software weak again this morning following ADBE earnings yesterday (ADBE now -12%). My stop will remain at post-earnings lows at $161.

GLBE - Moving my trailing stop to $52.9 here.

Trump Trades: TSLA, COIN, PLTR, Small Cap 6 - long term holds

My top trade setup for today -

ASAN - Huge volume following earnings and this stock has remained super strong with software weak on some earnings moves lower (MDB, ADBE). I think we are setting up for higher prices here.
