# Tuesday Morning Notes 3.28

Technical Update

The S&P 500 continued its rangebound action yesterday to kick off the week. The index hit a high of $4000 before pulling back to finish the day at $3977. Mega cap tech continues to lead the market while small caps lag.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ejgG!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fca44ef27-bfdc-4c8d-afb1-9aa49ad15e01_1203x909.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ejgG!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fca44ef27-bfdc-4c8d-afb1-9aa49ad15e01_1203x909.jpeg)

With price now above all key EMAs, this setup leans bullish into the end of the quarter. Key resistance levels to watch above include $4000-$4005, $4035-$4040, and $4070-$4080.

Key support levels to watch below include $3965, $3930-$3940, and $3900-$3910.
