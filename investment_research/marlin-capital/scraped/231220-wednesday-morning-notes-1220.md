# Wednesday Morning Notes 12.20

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks continued their rally yesterday with the S&P 500 finishing +0.59% on the day.  10 YR Yields plummeted to new 4.5 month lows at 3.88%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!x5HD!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd0b80eea-9baa-4d28-80ad-c9e8ef4877d0_987x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!x5HD!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd0b80eea-9baa-4d28-80ad-c9e8ef4877d0_987x723.jpeg)

In the options market, it was another muted day ahead of the holiday weekend with 35M options traded across all exchanges (vs 42.5M YTD avg).  Calls made up 62% of the activity as the chase for upside beta continues.

Russell 2000 stocks continued their outperformance yesterday, and are have now outperformed the SPX over +5% since Powell’s pivot.  I am still holding 20% of my original TNA long (now up ~38% since entry).  With yesterday’s break higher, I will be moving my stop loss to a close below $37.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gYOG!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5f1c2d00-ca79-4897-b025-09662eb4a165_1204x895.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gYOG!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5f1c2d00-ca79-4897-b025-09662eb4a165_1204x895.jpeg)

PATH continued higher yesterday, closing over $26 for the first time this run (+2.26%).  I am still holding my full long position in PATH (with entries from $23.93 & $24.67).  I will be moving my stop loss to a close below $24.50.
