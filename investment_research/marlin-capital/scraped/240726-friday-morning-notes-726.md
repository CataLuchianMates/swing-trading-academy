# Friday Morning Notes 7.26

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks fell in volatile trade yesterday with the S&P 500 declining -0.51% and closing right at its 60 EMA / $5400 support level I highlighted.

We are set for a big gap up this morning as buyers have stepped in at support thus far.  Today’s close will be key as we head into a loaded slate of Big Tech earnings next week.

There was a solid [earnings gappers](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-earnings-gapper-trade-setup?utm_source=publication-search) opportunity out there yesterday with NOW.  The 2nd 5 minute candle for NOW was an inside 5 with a long entry at $817 and a stop at LOD at $783 for -4.2% risk.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!R9hs!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb3e8c046-d958-40b7-a1e0-1f149cb5fe0e_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!R9hs!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb3e8c046-d958-40b7-a1e0-1f149cb5fe0e_1396x653.png)

I’m long NOW heading into Friday.  These trades will always be worth taking for me at around -4% risk or lower (I often take at -7% or lower, depending on the name).

VKTX did not give the day 1 entry I was looking for yesterday and I will wait for it to hopefully set up on the daily chart.

AEYE – From my [top small caps picks](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-small-caps-with-massive-upside), announced earnings yesterday and it was a nice beat all the way around.  Its set to open +9% higher this morning.

My top trade setup for today –

PRG – Simple long setup here on this earnings gapper.  We saw the highest volume of the year on earnings day and relative strength on day 2 (yesterday) with a strong close.  As the market faded into the bell, PRG turned green and ended up closing near highs.
