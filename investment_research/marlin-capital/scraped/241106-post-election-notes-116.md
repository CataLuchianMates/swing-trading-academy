# Post-Election Notes 11.6

BREAKING: Donald J. Trump has been elected the 47th President of the United States.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OqKX!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb23b3683-b0f6-45db-8a75-b66c28487847_1200x592.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OqKX!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb23b3683-b0f6-45db-8a75-b66c28487847_1200x592.jpeg)

The Senate race has already been called in favor of Republicans with a minimum of 51 seats. The House of Representatives is a (closer) call, however, but latest odds from betting markets give Republicans ~80% odds of keeping control.

The big surprise here is Trump becoming the first Republican to win the popular vote in 20 years + Republicans have a real shot of getting up to 53 seats in the Senate.

So, what does Trump’s victory mean for markets?

Starting with the obvious, US equity futures are reacting kindly to the news this morning with SPX Futures +2.2%, NDX Futures +1.7%, and RTY Futures up a staggering +5.6%. Bitcoin is trading above $74k for the first time in its history.

In the near term, the uncertainty of who will take office is now behind us, and that has typically meant a year end rally for US stocks.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Niqq!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc426fa2d-e971-4ea8-b5b3-4504e37c5b5a_1284x739.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Niqq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc426fa2d-e971-4ea8-b5b3-4504e37c5b5a_1284x739.jpeg)

If history is any indication, volatility has likely peaked for 2024 (VIX futures down -22% this morning).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!omvC!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5f4c91a4-e07b-491b-a663-02ab4ed18571_776x521.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!omvC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5f4c91a4-e07b-491b-a663-02ab4ed18571_776x521.jpeg)

Bigger picture, a Republican mandate likely means:

(1) De-regulation

(2) Lower corporate taxes

(3) Tariffs

(4) Immigration crackdown

BofA projects a +4% benefit to S&P 500 EPS from lower Corporate taxes (21% to 15%).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!r2uj!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb3e0b02a-2feb-4d7c-b5fc-91ecb98edbaa_1284x920.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!r2uj!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb3e0b02a-2feb-4d7c-b5fc-91ecb98edbaa_1284x920.jpeg)

This is great for US domestic/value/banks but bad for international. Crypto is seen as a beneficiary of Trump’s victory as he has embraced the asset class (+ his campaign promise to fire anti-crypto SEC Chair Gary Gensler).

We are seeing a huge rally in Small Caps this morning (RTY Futures +6%) following Trump’s victory. Small Caps are more sensitive to a lower Corporate Tax Rate and are seen as a big beneficiary of the Red Wave.

Be sure you’ve reviewed my [Top 6 Small Caps Picks](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-small-caps-with-massive-upside). The IWM Index of Small Caps has been rangebound since July, but is setting up for a major breakout this morning (set to open near $238).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pw3b!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6373bd28-092b-4482-831f-7a845a135762_1197x887.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pw3b!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6373bd28-092b-4482-831f-7a845a135762_1197x887.jpeg)
