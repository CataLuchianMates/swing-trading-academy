# Tuesday Morning Notes 7.26

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday was a boring start to the week for US stocks, as the indices chopped around most of the day before finishing in the red. With such an important week ahead, this should come as no surprise. Everyone is awaiting tomorrow’s Fed decision, as well as big tech earnings, which will start post-market today (Google and Microsoft).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!heVd!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9d9597cc-2b83-4ec1-b578-ff3ce88e90f4_962x714.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!heVd!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9d9597cc-2b83-4ec1-b578-ff3ce88e90f4_962x714.png)

It is difficult to tell a whole lot about the market based off yesterday’s low volume action. I am potentially anticipating some similar action today before the news catalysts begin to take over the rest of the week. Key support levels to watch below include $296-$297 and $290. Key resistance levels to watch above include $306-$308.
