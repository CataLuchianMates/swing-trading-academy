# Friday Morning Notes 4.21

Technical Update

The market pulled back -0.6% yesterday, closing right at the $4130 support level I have talked about.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pVte!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7bdb837d-a9c4-47d0-affc-3311ac9b89a4_937x707.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pVte!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7bdb837d-a9c4-47d0-affc-3311ac9b89a4_937x707.jpeg)

The choppy market action continues with Options Expiration today and a slew of big tech earnings coming in next week.  We have seen many instances of the SPX closing flat to slightly negative on OpEx weeks.

Key support levels to watch below include $4130-$4135, $4100, and $4075-$4080.

Key resistance level to watch above include $4160, and $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).
