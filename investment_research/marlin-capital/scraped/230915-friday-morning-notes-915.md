# Friday Morning Notes 9.15

Technical Update

The S&P 500 staged a rally on Thursday, closing +0.84% on the day and breaking above the recent chop/consolidation.

The SPX closed just below the $4500 resistance level, but we are set for a small gap down back below $4500 this morning.

Key support levels to watch below include $4475, $4460, $4420-$4430, $4400, and $4330-$4350.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $4500 and $4540-$4550.

I remain holding COUR and CLS into Friday’s session with no changes.

COUR - stop on final 2/3rds at $17.32

CLS - stop placed below $22.4
