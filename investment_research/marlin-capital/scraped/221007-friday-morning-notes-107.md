# Friday Morning Notes 10.7

Technical Analysis

The indices experienced more choppy action yesterday before a late afternoon reversal that saw the S&P 500 and QQQ close near the lows of the day. This type of choppy/rangebound action is easy for me to sit out from (and easy for many traders to lose money).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!1SZc!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0c0e376e-3de0-402e-ac2e-58a1e7e875e7_1396x669.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!1SZc!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0c0e376e-3de0-402e-ac2e-58a1e7e875e7_1396x669.png)

On the daily chart, the QQQ has bounced to the 21 EMA (purple line). This is a logical spot for the rally to stall out, as we saw often on bounces during the first 2 waves down.

Now, we have a potential daily rejection at the 21 EMA. A continuation move to the downside below the $274/275 region would spell trouble for this bounce.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!aNdK!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6042eb3f-2965-45f2-9832-0646879066a6_1033x655.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!aNdK!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6042eb3f-2965-45f2-9832-0646879066a6_1033x655.png)

We are set for a gap down this morning after September job numbers were released (see below). Support levels below include $274-$275 and $270-$272. Resistance levels ahead include $278-$280 and $284-$285.

Full report and analysis coming this weekend.
