# Potential Stock Setups & Market Update - 11.18

Below is a list of potential stock setups, an update on my Active Portfolio names, and an update of the broader market.

First, let’s take a look at the general market.

The QQQ closed at new all time highs today as it was a strong day for the market.

It was a strong day for the market, but you wouldn’t know it looking at most growth stocks.  Let’s take a look at ARKK, a good proxy for growth stocks.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2z5D!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9031726f-655c-49fd-829d-4ea268f477ab_1244x945.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2z5D!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9031726f-655c-49fd-829d-4ea268f477ab_1244x945.jpeg)

This is an ugly chart right now, which speaks to the divergence between a lot of growth stocks and the general market.  Speaking to the ARKK chart specifically, price broke through the 200 EMA decisively today and the ETF is now clearly in a downtrend.

ARKK is a proxy for growth stocks, so this tells me to be cautious with high valuation stocks.  The market looking ahead to the end of QE and the Fed tightening could certainly cause valuation compressions across the market as it is will be the end of “easy money policy”.  The question is: Will there be a flight to companies that already have strong earnings?

If recent price action is any indication, the answer is yes.  AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, and NVDA each hit new all time highs today.  NFLX hit new all time highs just days ago, while AMZN is now nearing an all time high.

This tells me to add exposure to Megacap tech.  These are the stocks showing the most relative strength in the market.  AAPL is my favorite of these charts, as I will describe below.

First, let’s take a look at the stocks in my Active Portfolio.
