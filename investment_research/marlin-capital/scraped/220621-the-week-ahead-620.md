# The Week Ahead 6.20

I hope everyone had a nice long weekend! Here is a preview of the week ahead.

Technical Analysis:

Since this Bear Market began, we have seen a pattern developing on breakdowns (in the indices). The pattern has been playing out, in this order:

1. h sell breakdown
2. Bounce to 21 EMA that fails
3. Re-test of lows

Below is the daily chart of the QQQ.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YMS_!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7d06ca1a-19e4-404b-a819-1eba42782583_1172x563.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YMS_!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7d06ca1a-19e4-404b-a819-1eba42782583_1172x563.png)

As of Thursday’s lows, we were extended 6.2% below our latest h sell breakdown level. This is beyond the extension point where “Stage 2” has started in the last 2 “h sell” breakdowns, meaning an oversold bounce to the declining 21 EMA (currently $293) would be the logical next step here. This oversold bounce may have already begun with Friday’s rally.

If our breakdown pattern holds, this bounce should be followed by a retest of Thursday’s lows ($269). Whether or not the re-test holds will ultimately determine if we will see a bigger rally like March (successful re-test) or an immediate break lower.

If the indices can not even muster up the strength to rally to the declining 21 EMA, with sentiment and oversold readings hitting such extremes, we have likely entered a a more serious liquidation phase (like wallstjesus references below).

My take:

History shows us that a 15.3x P/E Ratio is not yet cheap enough to have discounted the worst.  Recent Bear Markets have bottomed with a forward P/E Ratio below 14x.  Even the 2018 rate shock correction bottomed near 13.5x forward earnings, and financial conditions are **already** just as tight now as they were back then.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2rIh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F634bfddb-8d69-4f47-8bbb-a9b48f6f52f3_419x145.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2rIh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F634bfddb-8d69-4f47-8bbb-a9b48f6f52f3_419x145.png)
