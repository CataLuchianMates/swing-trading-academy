# Tuesday Morning Notes 1.7

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks were strong to kick off the week yesterday with Tech leading the charge (NDX +1.1%) on (1) Sam Altman blog post discussing AGI and “superintelligence”; (2) upside Foxconn revenue numbers; (3) Microsoft’s Friday announcement about its plans to spend $80B+ on AI-linked data center in 2025; (4) anticipation of the Consumer Electronic Show
