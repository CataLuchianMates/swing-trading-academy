# Friday Morning Notes 9.20

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500 closed Thursday at an ATH of $5713, marking the 39th ATH of the year and the first since July 16th.  The NDX was up +2.5% (and now up +8% since last Mon), its best day in 18+ months (since 2/23).  Market volumes were up 20% vs 10-dma and there was a clear tilt towards Cyclicals > Defensives (2nd best day for Cyc > Defs pair in 2+ yrs).

Today is the 9th option expiry of 2024, and the lad quad witching before the US election.  $4.5 trillion notional will expire between now and 4PM.  $2.6T of this is SPX Sep regular / the remaining is split across ETF and single stock at end of day.  This will be the largest September expiry of all time, so expect heightened volatility during today’s sessions (especially towards the close).

Active Portfolio –

SN – Hit a high of $108.68 yesterday before pulling back.  I’m moving my trailing stop up to $99.50.

CAVA – No changes here.  Stop loss at $118.78.

AVO – New 52 week high yesterday and new closing high.  Volume has remained elevated.  My first target = $14

NMM – Bounced back yesterday with a new 52 week high and new closing high.  My first target = $60.
