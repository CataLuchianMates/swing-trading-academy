# Tuesday Morning Notes 11.22

Technical Update

The major indices staged another choppy session to start off the week yesterday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zBlE!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2211b70a-c41c-4e93-95d7-96060c058570_977x718.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zBlE!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2211b70a-c41c-4e93-95d7-96060c058570_977x718.jpeg)

For the SPX, the consolidation between $3900-$4000 continues. Short/medium term, the technicals now lean more bullish with the 21 EMA crossing over the 60 EMA. Long term, we are still below the 200 day moving average, and as I mentioned in yesterday’s morning notes, the rally is getting long in the tooth (based off weekly chart & fib retracement levels).

Notice the very low volume in yesterday’s session, as we are in a holiday shortened trading week. With lighter trading volumes, more chop should ultimately be expected.
