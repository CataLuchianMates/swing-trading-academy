# Wednesday Morning Notes 6.22

Technical Analysis (from yesterday):

We are seeing QQQ attempting to bounce now off of very oversold conditions, but we ran into some resistance at the daily 8 EMA today. If we can get over this resistance along with todays high ($283.50), this bounce can certainly continue into the 21 EMA ($292 region).

I entered TQQQ long today as the oversold bounce to 21 EMA has been the breakdown pattern (as highlighted in this week’s preview). If the pattern continues, this should equate to an 8-12% gain in TQQQ versus 3.3% risk on the trade. When the math checks out, it’s worth the risk. That said, the rug can and will be pulled at any time, so I will not be overstaying my welcome here.

Morning Update:

Futures are set for a big gap down this morning as the rally has already started to fizzle. The TQQQ looks to gap below yesterday’s low, which is my stop loss. I will look to exit this position in the first 15-30 minutes of the trading day, unless this dip is immediately bought up.
