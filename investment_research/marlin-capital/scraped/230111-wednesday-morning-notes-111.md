# Wednesday Morning Notes 1.11

Technical Update

The market rallied back above the $3900 level yesterday as traders anticipate tomorrow’s CPI report. We closed right at the $3920 resistance level, and the market is set for a small gap up this morning.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5KHJ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F826b2996-8899-4288-99ca-083a9107f6cd_1122x822.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5KHJ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F826b2996-8899-4288-99ca-083a9107f6cd_1122x822.jpeg)

Further resistance levels to watch include $3950-$3960 and $3980-$4000.

On a green to red downside reversal, look for key support at $3900 (previous resistance) 3870-$3880, $3840-$3845, and the key Bear flag low $3800 level.
