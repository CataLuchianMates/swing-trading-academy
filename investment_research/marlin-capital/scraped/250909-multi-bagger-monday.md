Can we stay hot?

Updates on my other 2 biggest holdings below. I have a feeling you don’t want to miss these…

**APLD**

This recent APLD dip will prove to be a gift, in my opinion.

There has been so much positive news on this company in the past couple months.

First off, CoreWeave exercised their option for an additional 150MW, and is now paying Applied Digital $11B over 15 years ($733M per year) for 400 MW (previously $7B over 15 yrs for 250MW).

Secondly, Applied Digital just announced that they have broken ground on Polaris Forge 2. This is huge news because the CEO confirmed a deal is in the works.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ElgR!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F68cb5659-f1c9-4abb-aa9f-4ce0e29dfedf_1191x739.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ElgR!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F68cb5659-f1c9-4abb-aa9f-4ce0e29dfedf_1191x739.jpeg)

What could this mean for APLD?

**I crunched the numbers on Polaris Forge 2.**

Polaris Forge 2 is a 280 MW Data Center campus (compared to ~400 MW for Polaris Forge 1). For Polaris Forge 1, CoreWeave is paying $11B over 15 years ($733M per year) for 400 MW.

**That’s $1.83M in revenue per MW per year.**

Using the $1.83M per MW per year from the CoreWeave deal as a baseline…280 MW × $1.83M per MW per year = **$512M annually.**

**If Applied Digital can secure a deal for Polaris Forge 2 with similar terms to the CoreWeave deal, it would be worth $450M-$550M in revenue per year.**

Combined with Polaris Forge 1’s $733M (from CRWV), this would total $1.24B+ per year, exceeding the $1B+ threshold I talked about in my [SMID Cap 6 report](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-smid-caps-with-massive-upside-potential).

My target remains $30+ here.
