# The Week Ahead

Technical Update

Weekly chart below. As you can see, the S&P 500 found support at the 200 week moving average, a level of key long term significance.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!QSx6!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb5483896-246a-4311-9794-a17099ad6364_1179x901.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!QSx6!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb5483896-246a-4311-9794-a17099ad6364_1179x901.jpeg)

After a 5 week bounce, the index has caught up to its 40 week moving average. This is around the point at which the previous 2 Bear market rallies have failed.

Further, we have reached the 61.8% Fib Retracement level on this rally. Once again, this is the point at which the previous 2 Bear market rallies failed.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!grlx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F65150a17-6e61-419b-9eee-7002ac305001_2035x1168.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!grlx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F65150a17-6e61-419b-9eee-7002ac305001_2035x1168.jpeg)

We enter this week with the SPX within 2% of its 40 week moving average. In my view, the upside at the index level is limited from here. The risks remain skewed to the downside after a large 5 week rally off lows. With that said, I find it best to remain patient as a swing trader and wait for the correct reversal signals to enter short.

Keep in mind this is a holiday shortened week with the market closed Thursday for Thanksgiving. The market also closes at 1 PM EST on Black Friday. Typically, we would expect to see significantly lighter trading volumes during the week of Thanksgiving.
