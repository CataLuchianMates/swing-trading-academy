# The Week Ahead

Technical Update

The S&P 500 pulled back small on Friday, finishing the day -0.14%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HSEJ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0ce6062f-adff-445b-ad61-d9df1d1a06c0_1142x860.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HSEJ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0ce6062f-adff-445b-ad61-d9df1d1a06c0_1142x860.jpeg)

Technically, this action remains healthy despite the index struggling at $4200 resistance.  As I mentioned last week, a technical breakout above $4200 points to a potential continuation rally towards $4320-$4350.

With that said, a further pullback towards the 21 EMA ($4130-$4140) would be healthy as short term indicators are flashing overbought signals.

In terms of individual stocks, breadth remains weak with big tech and large caps leading the way.  Semis have been strong, and we were able to capture a big run by LRCX last week from the $532 breakout to $600.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tDdS!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Feaa565bf-3f7b-4eb9-a785-4e76a3b47d63_974x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tDdS!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Feaa565bf-3f7b-4eb9-a785-4e76a3b47d63_974x723.jpeg)

I am entering the week still holding half my LRCX long position, but I plan to fully exit this trade at today’s open.  The chart still looks healthy, but the stock is quite extended up here and I’d prefer to exit and take the 11% gains rather than hold through a likely pullback towards $560 (in my opinion).  Additionally, NVDA earnings this week is likely to move the semiconductor sector, and I expect anything short of stellar numbers and guidance to be a “sell the news event”.

With the LRCX breakout leading to quality gains, I am more interested in searching for additional long opportunities.  Below are my top 3 coming into the week.

V

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!K35b!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc581a59d-cb7e-42e5-a781-cb14e0b977e9_1064x791.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!K35b!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc581a59d-cb7e-42e5-a781-cb14e0b977e9_1064x791.jpeg)

The setup: Long V above $234.50

Stop below $229 (2.3% risk)

V is setting up nicely to breakout of its recent 2-month base.  This stock is not a high flyer, but I am targeting $250 on this breakout move.  The risk/reward here is solid in my view.

SYK

No specific setup yet as I want to see how the stock acts at support.

SYK is sandwiched between its 21 and 60 EMAs.  I am watching to see how the stock reacts today/to start off this week, but a move back above the 21 EMA would have me interested in a potential long as the week unfolds.

WING

No specific setup yet as I want to see how the stock acts at support.
