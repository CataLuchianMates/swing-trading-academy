# Friday Morning Notes 9.30

Technical Analysis

The market broke to the downside yesterday, clearing the bottom of the range and the key $270-$272 support level.  A late day bounce allowed the QQQ to close right at the bottom of the range and previous support ($272).  $270 and yesterday’s low ($269) remain the key support levels to watch on the downside.  Resistance levels to watch ahead include $274-$275 and $278-$280.
