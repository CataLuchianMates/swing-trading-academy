Technical Update

The S&P 500 staged a big rally Friday, closing the week just below key $3900 resistance ($3895).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!TePx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5c9cd73f-298e-4419-aece-7906763c628d_868x648.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!TePx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5c9cd73f-298e-4419-aece-7906763c628d_868x648.jpeg)

We are set for a gap above the $3900 level this morning to kick off the week. This is is a big week of potential news catalysts with Fed Chair Powell speaking tomorrow morning and December CPI to be released Thursday pre-market.

If the market can stabilize above $3900, the next key resistance levels to watch include $3920, $3960, and $3980-$4000.

Support levels below include $3870-$3880 (previous resistance), $3840-$3845, and the key Bear flag low $3800 level.
