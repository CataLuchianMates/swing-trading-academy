# The Week Ahead 7.25

We have a huge week for US stocks ahead of us. First off, 50% of the S&P 500 market cap reports earnings this week. Even more importantly, the Federal Reserve will announce their decision on interest rates this Wednesday. On Thursday, we will get our first release of Q2 GDP, which will officially tell us if we are already in a recession or not.

Technical Analysis:

The market fell for the first time in 3 days on Friday. The QQQ did not break any keep support levels yet, however, as we closed above the key 60 EMA $300-$301 level & 8 EMA.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WkD3!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6c3451fb-1d94-40d3-844a-8634d515261d_1284x968.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WkD3!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6c3451fb-1d94-40d3-844a-8634d515261d_1284x968.jpeg)

This week, we have an aforementioned blizzard of potential market moving events - the FOMC decision, with a likely second 75 BP rate hike, and a slew of big tech earnings - Google, Microsoft, etc. There will be plenty of risks to the downside holding overnight, so I plan to be sticking with more short term trades (2-5% risk).
