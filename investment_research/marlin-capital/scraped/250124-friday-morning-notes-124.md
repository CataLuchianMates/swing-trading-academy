# Friday Morning Notes 1.24

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500 closed at a fresh ATH yesterday ($6118) in a very quiet session. Market volumes were muted running -16% vs the 20 day average.

Earnings are starting to dominate trading activity, and next week is the big one with 41% of the SPX set to report. The bar for earnings is indeed higher this quarter (analysts are expecting +8-10% EPS growth vs +3% last qtr), but so far so good from Banks/NFLX/other select names.

We also have some very favorable seasonals at play for the remainder of the month and beginning of February.
