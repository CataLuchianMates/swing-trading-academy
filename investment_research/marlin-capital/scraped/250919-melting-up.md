**My Thoughts on the FOMC + Big Picture Thoughts**

This is the dovish cut we were looking for with the DOTS aligned at the consensus view of 2 or more cuts this year. The Fed essentially confirmed it is in full on “easing mode” now.

Most important, though, is the market reaction. I usually like to wait a day post-Fed to gauge the market’s true response, and today was clear confirmation. All 3 major indices (SPX, NDX, RTY) broke out to new highs following yesterday’s FOMC.

The key for me, as I have been highlighting, is the Russell 2000 breakout. Small Caps are the most rate sensitive group, and tend to perform better when the Fed is easing.

Small Caps are attempting to break out of a 4 year base.
