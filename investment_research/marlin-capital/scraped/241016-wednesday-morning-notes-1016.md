# Wednesday Morning Notes 10.16

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500 sold off -0.76% yesterday, while the NDX declined -1.37%.  Small Caps were the top performers on the day +0.23%.

Yesterday’s sell off was very micro driven, led by ASML earnings.  ASML finished -16% on a BIG miss in quarterly net bookings and dragged Semis (NVDA/AMD/INTC/MU) and Tech down along with it.

This puts an even greater emphasis on TSM earnings pre-market tomorrow with some renewed investor anxiety seeping into Tech/Semis.
