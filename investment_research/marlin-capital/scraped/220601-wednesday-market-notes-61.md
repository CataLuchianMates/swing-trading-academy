# Wednesday Market Notes 6.1

Technical Analysis

Yesterday was an interesting day to kick off the week for US stocks. The indices fared okay, closing nearly flat on the day. A little bit of digestion after the big gains we had last week is to be expected.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vYb3!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4c2558ab-d0e0-43ad-89d8-facc67e2b3d2_1396x669.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vYb3!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4c2558ab-d0e0-43ad-89d8-facc67e2b3d2_1396x669.jpeg)

However, it was not a good day for the leading sector (energy). The XLE gave a high volume reversal on OPEC headline. Leaders like OXY, VLO, DVN, etc. each gave sell signals on the daily. This is a red flag to note. Small caps and ARKK showed more relative weakness today.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!kIq9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe7479086-a546-48d7-b14f-07dd748145b9_1396x669.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!kIq9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe7479086-a546-48d7-b14f-07dd748145b9_1396x669.jpeg)

Overall, the setups in individual names are not there for me. No need to force it in a market like this, but I would consider adding some long index exposure if the charts setup.
