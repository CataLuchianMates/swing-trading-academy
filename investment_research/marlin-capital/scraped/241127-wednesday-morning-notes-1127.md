# Wednesday Morning Notes 11.27

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Stocks finished marginally higher yesterday (SPX & NDX +0.57%) as investors digested an update on tariffs (Trump vowed an additional 10% tariff on goods from China and 25% on all products from Canada and Mexico, sharply countering expectations that he would be taking a more measured stance with Scott Bessent as Treasury Secretary).

Price action reflected a reversion of the recent Small Caps outperformance trade with Quality/Large Cap Tech back at the top of the leader board (think AMZN, META, NFLX, AAPL, MSFT, etc.) vs Small Caps/Non-Profitable Tech resting. In my view, this definitely felt like some natural reversion with 14d RSIs in these groups well above >70.

Expect today to be very quiet/low volume/low liquidity ahead of tomorrow’s Thanksgiving holiday.

Active Portfolio -

No changes from yesterday.

Stops in place on LRN, TWLO, APP, RKLB, SNOW, and GLBE.

Otherwise, no actions for me today as I am off the desk visiting family.
