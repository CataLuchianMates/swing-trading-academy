**Applied Digital (APLD)**

In terms of price action, APLD is 1 of the **elite standouts** from Q2 earnings season so far.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wbzO!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2ef834bf-65e7-4e1c-bd86-220999751f9f_1132x652.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wbzO!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2ef834bf-65e7-4e1c-bd86-220999751f9f_1132x652.png)

We own shares of Applied Digital for the long haul. It’s 1 of my [Top 6 AI SMID Cap Long Term Investments](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-smid-caps-with-massive-upside-potential).

**I see Applied Digital as a future $10B company** (at $3.9B now), and it could get there sooner rather than later.

This is NOT a Bitcoin mining company anymore.

This is an AI Data Center company.

**Two MASSIVE things from the conference call have APLD absolutely soaring:**

1. CoreWeave exercised the additional 150 MW (now 400 MW total), which boosts the contract revenue to $11B over 15 years (or $733M per year, up from $467M per year). Again, this is a business that did $160B in annual revenue last year as a bitcoin mining company. The CoreWeave deal alone is set to 5x their revenue.
2. Applied Digital’s CEO revealed that they are currently in advanced negotiations with another investment-grade hyperscaler for a large-scale data center campus beyond Polaris Forge One. If this deal closes, revenue could further skyrocket to $1B+ per year.

In [“End of July Fireworks”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/end-of-july-fireworks), I highlighted the clear path to multi-bagger gains in APLD.
