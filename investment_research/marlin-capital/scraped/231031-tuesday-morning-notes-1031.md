# Tuesday Morning Notes 10.31

**Daily Market Commentary**

US stocks bounced to kick off the week yesterday with the S&P 500 finishing +1.2% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WKNI!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F77dabff7-747c-4a32-8ef2-d044343612b9_999x735.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WKNI!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F77dabff7-747c-4a32-8ef2-d044343612b9_999x735.jpeg)

The QQQ finished +1.13% on the day and is now up 17 straight Mondays (SPX is up 16 out of the last 17 Mondays).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KRkY!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9bd040ad-2930-417a-a6db-604417d9b562_1200x903.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KRkY!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9bd040ad-2930-417a-a6db-604417d9b562_1200x903.jpeg)

Price action was back in favor of quality/safety with Big Tech +218 bps.  After some positioning adjustments following earnings last week, the market is not yet finding a better alternative to highly liquid, strong balance sheet names.

Mega Cap tech led yesterday’s move higher with MSFT, AMZN, and AAPL accounting for just under 50% of the move in the Nasdaq.  Rate sensitive sectors also saw a boost with liquid regional banks rising for only the 2nd time in 9 sessions and financials posting their best 1 day move since early June.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4BLx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F09fac01f-3fb4-4005-989a-41394d1cff70_549x770.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4BLx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F09fac01f-3fb4-4005-989a-41394d1cff70_549x770.jpeg)

Contrary to the typical squeeze action, we did not see much of a bid from the liquid most short/meme baskets.  The lack of participation from these names tells me that the market believes pain in certain pockets is not over yet.  Even after 12 straight weeks of increased short flow, non-quality businesses struggled to catch a bid yesterday.  To that point, Non-Profitable Tech is -35% from summer highs, giving back all its 1H gains as the interest to hold such stocks in the current point in the cycle is non-existent.

Buybacks – something to watch as potential bullish catalyst here as we come out of the buyback blackout period.  GS estimates that ~50% of the SPX is in open window with 70% in open window by the end of the week.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XtYu!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F24c26348-f5ea-440b-b8e3-f5695c310dc7_570x234.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XtYu!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F24c26348-f5ea-440b-b8e3-f5695c310dc7_570x234.jpeg)

Market breadth is currently as narrow as it gets, suggesting a further relief rally could be near.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!STVA!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F77c192d3-277a-4583-8d9e-9d26c571ebdf_613x447.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!STVA!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F77c192d3-277a-4583-8d9e-9d26c571ebdf_613x447.jpeg)

 Positioning – US single stock shorting has increased for 12 straight weeks, the longest shorting streak ever recorded by GS Prime.  Such aggressively bearish positioning is often the catalyst for a short covering/squeeze rally (as we have seen many times throughout this Bear Market).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BlZJ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5f458fb8-20a7-46fd-82c5-96a6792d8372_1202x1486.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BlZJ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5f458fb8-20a7-46fd-82c5-96a6792d8372_1202x1486.jpeg)
