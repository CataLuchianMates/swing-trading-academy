# Friday Morning Notes 3.3 

Technical Update

The market staged a major reversal in yesterday’s session, recapturing the 200 day MA after gapping below. The SPX closed +0.76% on the day at $3981.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ujtJ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F310f72e4-c56d-4652-8d69-63e8104fd5dd_1108x847.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ujtJ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F310f72e4-c56d-4652-8d69-63e8104fd5dd_1108x847.jpeg)

We are set for a continuation gap up this morning towards the $4000 resistance level. Key resistance levels to watch in today’s session include $3995-$4000, $4020-$4025 (21 EMA), and $4045-$4050.

On a bearish reversal, the SPX has support at $3970-$3975 and the key level at $3940.

The big question is, does yesterday’s reversal change the greater market trajectory? Full detailed analysis coming this weekend.
