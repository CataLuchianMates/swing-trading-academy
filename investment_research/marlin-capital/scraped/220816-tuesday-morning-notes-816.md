# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.16

Technical Analysis:

The market resumed higher yesterday to start off the week, with the QQQ breaking and closing above Friday’s highs. We are now just 2-3% below the 200 day MA ($342), which is the next key resistance level I will be watching.
