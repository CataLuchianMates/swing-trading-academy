# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.1

Technical Update

A quiet day for the indices to kick off the week with the SPX finishing slightly positive

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_sum!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F321620d6-e0a0-42da-a81f-d78d8f740be5_1187x881.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_sum!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F321620d6-e0a0-42da-a81f-d78d8f740be5_1187x881.jpeg)

We saw some big moves in highs shorted manes (+10% last 2 days - see below), which often means at least a slight pullback/ cool off is near.

CLS

A 3rd straight day of huge volume in this name post-earnings. These are now 3 of the 4 highest volume days in the stock in the last 10 years.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!3F9T!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff9e88203-b29e-41fd-a6dd-6224c9b25f66_1105x651.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!3F9T!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff9e88203-b29e-41fd-a6dd-6224c9b25f66_1105x651.png)

I’ll be looking to get involved on the first pullback to the adaptive 8 EMA (currently $19.93 and rising). I believe this stock is likely to hit $28-$30.

SYM

This stock provided a textbook double inside 5 earnings gapper setup.

This is why you study - to be prepared for these types of moves.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_vcI!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fed5f3f31-eee2-472c-b1e7-4108d3629340_1107x651.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_vcI!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fed5f3f31-eee2-472c-b1e7-4108d3629340_1107x651.png)

The stock made a +39% intraday move out of the double inside 5 break to the upside. Congrats to those who caught it.
