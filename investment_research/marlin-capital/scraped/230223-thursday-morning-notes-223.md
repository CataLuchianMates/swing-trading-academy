# Thursday Morning Notes 2.23

Technical Update

Yesterday was a choppy session for the indices, with the S&P 500 closing near flat on the day

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Rbkf!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F20b89707-dfc0-4b16-9e2a-c5b8b6b77995_959x729.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Rbkf!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F20b89707-dfc0-4b16-9e2a-c5b8b6b77995_959x729.jpeg)

We are set for a sizable gap up this morning on the back of NVDA earnings results. For the S&P 500, this gap will take the index back above the key $4000 support level and 60 EMA.

Key resistance levels ahead include $4045-$4055, $4060 (21 EMA), and $4090-$4100.

If the gap cannot hold, look for key support to come in at $3990-$4000.
