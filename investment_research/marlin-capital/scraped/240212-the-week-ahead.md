# The Week Ahead

Last week, the S&P 500 marked its 14th positive week out of the last 15 and now heads into the week of 2/12 above $5000.

Friday was a squeezy day of price action with Bitcoin Sensitive / Liquidity Most Short and Expensive Software leading the way.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BdM7!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F739322b2-7a6d-4f04-acac-2fe2672d47d2_1076x1041.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BdM7!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F739322b2-7a6d-4f04-acac-2fe2672d47d2_1076x1041.jpeg)

The 2 main charts underpinning this market rally:

1.       Financial Conditions are as loose as they have been in the last 2 years with no signs of getting any tighter.  This is allowing the market to continue to run higher.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5QVJ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8f889596-4d68-4449-be42-a5d75a47d30e_1212x1182.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5QVJ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8f889596-4d68-4449-be42-a5d75a47d30e_1212x1182.jpeg)

2.       The GS Economic Surprise Index has signaled that the recent economic numbers are increasingly surprising to the upside.  The data continues to show a stronger and more resilient economy than consensus has anticipated.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!a-d4!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F17c86636-016b-4531-80af-1a3c47578524_1161x1186.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!a-d4!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F17c86636-016b-4531-80af-1a3c47578524_1161x1186.jpeg)

It’s been all about quality of a big portion of the rally in early 2024.  Profitable companies showing growth have been rewarded handsomely, as seen in the outperformance of Mega Cap Tech.  In the same vein, Goldman’s High Beta Momentum Basket is up almost 20% YTD.  14d RSI is at 80, now the most overbought since March of 2020.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RKD5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F150864f0-77ba-4e06-af7a-970dacba61f7_1066x570.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RKD5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F150864f0-77ba-4e06-af7a-970dacba61f7_1066x570.jpeg)

As a result, the equity options market is pricing in almost no panic – the GS US Panic Index – a 2 year rolling percentile across 4 equity vol metric is approaching decade lows.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EnIS!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7fa6e7a9-94ca-4cd2-a657-f82c7d8476e5_1149x715.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EnIS!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7fa6e7a9-94ca-4cd2-a657-f82c7d8476e5_1149x715.jpeg)

This week, we head into a monthly February options OPEX.  Once concluded, this will help to loosen up some of those powerful forces from the options market which have helped to keep volatility suppressed.  We also have another loaded slate of earnings with ANET, ABNB, UPST, HOOD, LYFT, CSCO, HUBS, UPWK, DKNG, COIN, ROKU, DASH, TOST, and more all set to report.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!uQVv!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F65166886-6a75-4d89-ad65-c0c7c7619872_1920x1080.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!uQVv!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F65166886-6a75-4d89-ad65-c0c7c7619872_1920x1080.jpeg)

For my current positions, I am heading into this week holding half my PLTR position as well as NXT.

PLTR wisely pulled back on Friday after a huge 3-day run post-earnings.  Technically, I believe we can see a further pull back towards $22-$23.  However, I remain bullish on the bigger picture after this high volume earnings gapper.  My bigger picture target remains $30 and my stop loss will be moved to breakeven on the remainder of my position ($20.51).

2 stock to watch this week:

Along with potential earnings gappers, which will be my focus this week, I am watching 2 stocks for potential long setups this week.

AFRM : -10.9% on earnings last week but I’m still bullish here.  Technically, key support levels held and we closed well off the earnings day lows.  I am interested to see if AFRM starts to turn up and forms a bullish inside day today.  This would have me interested in a potential long entry tomorrow.
