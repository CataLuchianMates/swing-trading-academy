# Friday Morning Notes 8.5

Technical Analysis:

Choppy action yesterday with the indices closing near flat on the day. These consolidation/rest days are a bullish sign as they allow the extended moving averages to catch up to price. For the QQQ, which has been the stronger index, the $325 level provided strong resistance in the pre-market, and twice in the regular session. A break above this level could trigger the next move higher towards the 200 day MA ($343).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WM9n!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7a750aa3-43b4-46ef-a9bf-a880da80b22d_1037x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WM9n!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7a750aa3-43b4-46ef-a9bf-a880da80b22d_1037x653.png)

On the flip side, the formerly $320 resistance level turned into support, as $320.77 marked the low of the day. The action here looks constructive for at least 1 more thrust higher tomorrow/early next week.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!QCvD!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fad3f8c2e-8013-4bd5-9b58-bf9eaf420d51_961x731.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!QCvD!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fad3f8c2e-8013-4bd5-9b58-bf9eaf420d51_961x731.jpeg)

We are set for a sizable gap down this morning after a stronger jobs report than expected. Let’s see if buyers continue to step in to buy the dip, or if this the catalyst for a temporary peak in the market.
