# Tuesday Morning Notes 3.7

Technical Update

The S&P 500 closed slightly green yesterday (+0.07%) after reversing off Morning highs. This formed a potential bearish reversal candle on the daily chart.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-nfi!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F51d3e792-3829-4d3c-97f3-5b6ef6e6906a_936x706.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-nfi!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F51d3e792-3829-4d3c-97f3-5b6ef6e6906a_936x706.jpeg)

Despite the potentially bearish candle, there is 0 reason to be bearish here on a technical basis. As long as we remain above all key moving averages in the chat above, I will be focusing on trading to the long side.

Jerome Powell will testify in front of the senate today at 10 AM EST. This is likely to increase volatility in today’s session. In my view, the markets are set up for further upside on a technical basis as we head into the Fed Chair’s testimony.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $4050, $4075-$4080, and $4095-$4100.

Key support levels below include $4025-$4030 and 3990-$4000.
