# Thursday Morning Notes 2.2

Technical Analysis

With a more dovish Fed than anticipated, the market broke through key Nov/Dec highs yesterday ($4100).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!t13z!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd9747a77-2ede-4e8b-b29e-df22b0b90ea6_1042x795.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!t13z!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd9747a77-2ede-4e8b-b29e-df22b0b90ea6_1042x795.jpeg)

Now that we have broken above this key level, in my view, the market can start to make a run towards August highs ($4300).
