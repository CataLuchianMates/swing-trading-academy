# Wednesday Market Thread 1.26

**Wednesday Morning Thoughts:**

I will be closely watching the FOMC meeting results and more importantly, the market’s reaction. We are set to gap up in a big way this morning, but everything can change quickly this afternoon.  As I said before last month’s FOMC meeting, with big news events like this, the first move is often a fakeout. Expect a lot of volatility regardless. This is exactly what happened last month, as we had a big rally the day of the FOMC meeting, followed by more downside in the coming weeks.

I will be posting my detailed thoughts today post Fed meeting in a Substack article.  Keep an eye out this afternoon.  I am not planning to make any portfolio moves prior to the event, as I am expecting lots of volatility, but no clear direction, as everyone awaits the FOMC outcome.
