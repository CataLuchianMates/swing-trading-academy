# Wednesday Morning Notes 7.27

Technical Analysis:

The market pulled back again on Tuesday, breaking and closing below the key 60 EMA medium term trend level. Now, we are immediately set for a gap up this morning after Google and Microsoft earnings last night.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jIo6!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F49c8896f-5c4e-40bb-a593-4be53d063cf6_905x657.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jIo6!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F49c8896f-5c4e-40bb-a593-4be53d063cf6_905x657.jpeg)

It has been a choppy several days leading up to today’s Fed decision, but I expect the market to gain a larger sense of direction towards the end of this week. It all starts with today’s Fed decision, statement, and Jerome Powell’s press conference. A 75 BP hike is currently priced into the market, and as previously stated, the market is pricing in numerous rate cuts in 2023 despite the Fed’s plans for continued tightening. Powell will be tasked with the difficult job of walking the line between inflationary and recessionary risks. I am expecting a hawkish tone from today’s meeting, as the market has already called the Fed’s bluff and does not believe the central bank will be able to follow through.

Key levels to watch today -

Resistance : $300-$301, $305-$307

Support : $293-$294, $288-$290, $280
