# Thursday Morning Notes 4.27

Technical Update

The S&P 500 failed to hold its early morning gains yesterday, closing -0.38% on the day. Meanwhile, the Nasdaq closed +0.47% as MSFT earnings helped to boost the tech sector.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!w8Lf!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fda98487f-8ab5-4df1-898d-014c98b23d2a_1184x887.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!w8Lf!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fda98487f-8ab5-4df1-898d-014c98b23d2a_1184x887.jpeg)

Notice (in the chart above) that the SPX has just experienced its first bearish MACD cross since mid-February. Back then, we saw the SPX break below all key moving averages before the recent snap back (which began just as the Fed stepped in to bail out the banks & undid 6 months of QT with a massive liquidity injection).

We are set for a 2nd straight gap up this morning after META results.

Key support levels to watch below include $4040-$4030 and $4000.

Key resistance level to watch above include $4100, $4130-4135, $4160, and $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).
