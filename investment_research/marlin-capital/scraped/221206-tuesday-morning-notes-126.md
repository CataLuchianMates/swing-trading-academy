# Tuesday Morning Notes 12.6

Technical Analysis

The SPX kicked off the week with a pullback and break below the 200 day moving average.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!TCWO!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5dc57a4a-5bfe-4f75-a799-04f9b94a14d9_1194x884.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!TCWO!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5dc57a4a-5bfe-4f75-a799-04f9b94a14d9_1194x884.jpeg)

This is a good start to the week for bears. Still, the index remains above the key short and medium term moving averages. Further, there has still been no bearish MACD across on the daily chart. It is possible to see that bearish cross by the end of today’s session.

It will be key to see if the index can retake the $4000 level early in todays session. Other resistance levels above include $4040-$4050 and $4100.

Support levels below include $3980, $3950-$3960, $3920, and $3900.
