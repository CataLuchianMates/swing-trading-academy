# Wednesday Morning Notes 5.17

Technical Update

Mostly choppy action continued for the SPX on Tuesday with the index closing -0.64%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!aOJ4!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F10666d31-192e-4230-ae64-5223a282ab27_980x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!aOJ4!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F10666d31-192e-4230-ae64-5223a282ab27_980x723.jpeg)

This was a weaker close than bulls would have liked, as the index closed below 21 EMA for first time since May 4th.

The 60 EMA ($4070-$4080) is the next key support level to watch below. If this support level breaks, I believe we are likely to see the selling accelerate with the 200 SMA support at $3975 looming below.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $4120 (21 EMA), $4150, and $4200.

LRCX - Nice continuation off of Monday’s breakout, but the stock faded a bit into the close. My stop will remain at breakeven ($532).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WSGh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff0dbd331-5edf-42ca-b6e3-611df06e6d85_977x721.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!WSGh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff0dbd331-5edf-42ca-b6e3-611df06e6d85_977x721.jpeg)

Many breakouts in individual names continue to fail/chop around rather than continue higher, so I believe a more cautious stance is warranted on new trades.
