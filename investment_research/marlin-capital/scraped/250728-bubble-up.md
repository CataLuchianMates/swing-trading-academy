**Welcome to the AI Bubble.**

One telltale sign of an equity market bubble is excessive risk taking.

Goldman’s Speculative Trading Indicator now sits at at highest levels on record outside of the late 90s Dot Com Bubble and the 2020-21 Covid-QE Bubble (although it remains well below the highs reached during those episodes).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nZX3!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F54b46b21-719d-4b33-ac60-16239315e9fc_1016x685.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nZX3!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F54b46b21-719d-4b33-ac60-16239315e9fc_1016x685.jpeg)

Some MTD moves that stand out (throwback to the Covid Bubble with highly shorted/meme stocks soaring): OPEN +354%; KSS +61%; DNUT +48%; LCID +41%

Each component of Goldman’s Speculative Trading Indicator is elevated vs. history.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YaRR!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5ff0bcb2-dd15-43a0-96a2-204c729352a9_950x679.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YaRR!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5ff0bcb2-dd15-43a0-96a2-204c729352a9_950x679.jpeg)

The good news for bulls is that sharp short-term increases in speculative trading have typically signaled above average equity returns on a 3M, 6M, and 12M basis (but have faltered on a 2+ year horizon, as all bubbles eventually pop).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vIZ5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F07f269a1-4f4c-4068-858f-be08884e3d56_344x236.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vIZ5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F07f269a1-4f4c-4068-858f-be08884e3d56_344x236.jpeg)

In other words, equity market bubbles usually get bigger/last longer than you think (until they eventually do pop).

Enthusiasm for the AI Trade, the driving force of this Bubble (“The AI Bubble”), feels like it has never been higher. So far through Q2 earnings, the industry data points are adding fuel to the fire.

Last week, Google reported YoY CapEx growth of +70% in the quarter (up from +43%), raised its FY25 CapEx guide by $10B, and said they expect a further increase in CapEx in 2026.
