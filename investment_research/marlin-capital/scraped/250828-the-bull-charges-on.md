This was supposed to be their moment.

**My Big Picture Thoughts Following NVDA Earnings:**

Nvidia’s earnings yesterday were solid, but unspectacular. Data Center revenue came in a bit light, which was the big negative (but still grew +56% YoY and 5% QoQ). However, NVDA’s Q3 guide was above consensus, mostly driven by Data Center. The Q3 guide also did not include China/H20, but management noted the potential for an extra $2B-$5B from that.

The market reaction tells the real story today. It was very possible for the market to sell off due to the NVDA DC miss (slight miss), but the S&P 500 hit new ATHs instead. The QQQ is now just a stones throw away from ATHs. And the IWM looks [set up for a big breakout on the weekly](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/small-cap-september).

Overall, I remain bullish the market here following NVDA earnings. The [AI Bubble](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-big-picture) is not finished yet.
