# Thursday Morning Note 5.5

Fed Decision Outcome:

The QQQ finished up over 3.3% in yrsterday’s session. The catalyst for the sharp move higher was Powell’s comment that the Fed is not considering a future 75 BP hike. The market had a 75 BP hike priced in for June, so yesterday’s outcome is seen as dovish (or at least, more dovish than anticipated). Futures markets priced out roughly one rate hike (see chart below). More detailed report about the Fed meeting coming tomorrow.

The first move post-Fed decision is often a fakeout , but this rally makes sense given expectation vs reality. The key moving averages pictured below are potential resistance levels to watch if we start to get a bigger rally.
