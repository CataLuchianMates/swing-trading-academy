The Fed minutes were mixed to hawkish with various participants willing to tighten more if needed.

NVDA +9% on earnings this morning with an EPS beat and raise with Data Center revs up +23% QoQ (keeping pace with +27% QoQ last qtr).  NVDA also announced a 10 for 1 stock split effective June 7th and raised its quarterly dividend 150%.  Recall the summer of 2021 when another retail favorite, TSLA, announced a stock split.  The stock ended up outperforming the S&P 500 by 20% from announcement day (May ’21) to the effective date (July ’21).

After yesterday’s earnings report and this morning’s gap up, NVDA is currently trading at 38x NTM P/E, below its 3-year median on NTM P/E.
