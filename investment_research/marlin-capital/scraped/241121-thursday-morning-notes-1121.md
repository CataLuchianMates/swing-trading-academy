# Thursday Morning Notes 11.21

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Choppy session yesterday into NVDA earnings with the SPX closing flat on the day.

TGT was in focus with a -22% decline, weighing on the consumer space. In my view, we are clearly in a market environment where winners can continue to outperform but are not in an environment where a rising tide lifts all boats. It is important to be in the right names/themes as this is very much what I would call a “stockpickers market”.

After the bell:

NVDA - Up small this morning following a solid beat. Reported Oct qtr revs of $35B vs cons. $33.3B and prev guidance $32.5B (note that NVDA beat expectation by ~$2B last several quarters, so this was a slightly smaller beat).

NVDA beat FYQ3 rev and EPS street expectation by +6% and +8%, respectively. Strength in data center compute (+22% QoQ vs +17% QoQ last qtr) more than offset what was a surprising sequential decline in data center networking.

Looking ahead, NVDA guided Jan qtr revs to $37.5B vs street estimate $37.1B. The most bullish street expectations were looking for $38B.

My thoughts - Jan Qtr rev guidance was a touch light of bullish expectations, hence the initial sell off following the results. With that said, these results should be “good enough” for the market to continue its uptrend as we enter a bullish seasonal part of the year. These are not the type of results to provide a major catalyst in either direction, so I do not see the trend reversing here and remain bullish the market into year end.

SNOW - Up +24% this morning following a big FQ3 product revenue beat ($900 vs cons $856M) and guided FQ4 product revs well above estimates ($909M vs cons $890M). Setup skewed short/under owned into the print. This is a potential earnings gapper to watch today.
