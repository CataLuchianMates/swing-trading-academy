# Friday Morning Notes 8.25

Technical Update

The S&P 500 formed an ugly bearish engulfing reversal candle following yesterday’s gap up.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c8_v!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F30c1557d-f653-4db1-91f3-4b22455f2f9c_971x727.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c8_v!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F30c1557d-f653-4db1-91f3-4b22455f2f9c_971x727.jpeg)

Right when it looked like we might be in the clear following blowout results from NVDA, the indices sliced through key support in an ugly day of selling.

Today, we have Fed Chair Powell speaking at Jackson Hole. While likely not as important as last year, this is still a catalyst to watch for the market.

Only 1 of my inside day setups triggered yesterday in ALRM, and stopped me out on the break of the inside day low for a -1.2% loss.

Back to 100% cash and will be watching to see how the 1st day of Jackson hold shapes out today as well as the weekly candle in the indices. No planned moves today for me.
