#### Speculatives are Breaking Down

I have been stressing to watch the most speculative groups very closely, because I view them as the best indicator of risk tolerance.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yGyJ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faca9cbc4-f828-4069-bd5d-b8aa43af78b3_1205x824.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yGyJ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faca9cbc4-f828-4069-bd5d-b8aa43af78b3_1205x824.jpeg)

And for that reason, they are the best indicator of where we are in the market cycle.

Historically, they are the first bubbles to pop.

**Dot Com Bust example**

During the topping of the Dot Com Bubble, the most speculative stocks who had nothing more than a business plan (i.e. Pets.com) peaked first. From that point on, the weakness continued to spread. Individual names got taken out one by one. **By the time the S&P 500 topped in March 2000, over 40% of the SPX was already at 52 week lows.**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EB2z!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F15efdd40-bf86-4e96-b25a-9002d69a849a_1558x353.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EB2z!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F15efdd40-bf86-4e96-b25a-9002d69a849a_1558x353.png)

The same thing happened in 2021-22 with SPACs, ZM, ARKK, etc. peaking many months before the S&P 500.Pre-revenueSPACs (most now bankrupt) began peaking 14+ months before the SPX did. ZM peaked 14 months before the SPX. ARKK peaked 10 months before the SPX. **By the time the S&P 500 topped in January 2022, 66% of the Nasdaq was already in a Bear Market (and 35% of the NDX was down over -50%).** I wrote about this rapidly deteriorating breadth way back in December 2021 in [“Wild Times in the Market”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/is-this-market-crazy-or-is-it-just?utm_source=publication-search). This report was written about 1 week before the SPX topped and we entered the 2022 Bear Market. Most every stock had already topped for the cycle at that point.

My research shows that Bubbles throughout history have peaked this way (with the most speculative groups/names topping first).

#### **Bear Flags and “h sells” Everywhere.**

First, I must talk a bit about Stan Weinstein’s “Stage Analysis”. Stan Weinstein is a legendary trader who became famous for his stage analysis trading strategy, which he talks about in his book “[Secrets for Profiting in Bull and Bear Markets](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1556236832?ie=UTF8&tag=nextbigtrade-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=1556236832)”.

Stage Analysis centers around the premise that assets move in cycles. These cycles contain boom and bust phases. No asset moves in one direction forever. These cycles also contain periods of consolidation.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!IzQU!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8a140162-701e-465a-b546-f5899e44ebcd_490x232.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!IzQU!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8a140162-701e-465a-b546-f5899e44ebcd_490x232.png)

**Stage 1: Basing Stage**

In this stage, the stock is consolidating or basing with no defined trend or direction. The stock is chopping around in a general price range.

**Stage 2: Advance Stage**

This is the phase where the stock breaks out of its consolidation/basing pattern and starts to advance. The trend flips to a defined uptrend. Aka the Bull Phase.

**Stage 3: Topping Stage**

This is the phase where the stock begins to top out after its advance. This can occur very quickly, or over a longer period of time. In any case, the uptrend will be under pressure at this point.

**Stage 4: Decline Stage**

In this stage, the stock begins to decline after topping out (stage 3). The trend flips to a defined downtrend. Aka the Bear Phase.

**On Short Entries**

Timing is everything on short trades. Your timing must be immaculate on shorts because you will get squeezed violently otherwise.

Shorting is not nearly as easy as trading to the long side in a low volatility, bullish environment. This is why I am very selective on short trades.

I do not like to enter short on the breakdown, because there are often U&R’s/lots of volatility at the breakdown level. My preferred method of shorting is to enter on bounces to key moving averages (which should act as firm resistance). Often, this best plays out as 3 straight green days (big bounce) into declining EMAs/resistance.

This was the setup for our [APP short trade on 4/2/25](https://substack.com/chat/408010/post/3d65a58d-fce3-4f48-a8ed-ff9cc56f669e) (our top trade idea of Q2, which we made >21% on):

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!FYYm!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2ee986bf-982b-4390-9539-e6ef7976a990_1012x675.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!FYYm!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2ee986bf-982b-4390-9539-e6ef7976a990_1012x675.png)

In the APP chart above, notice the 3 day bounce into the declining 21 EMA (resistance). The ideal short entry is on Day 3, because that allows you to enter short at resistance (following a multi-day bounce) and puts the risk/reward in your favor. In my view, you do NOT want to be entering short on big red candles because that puts you at much higher risk of being caught in the eventual dead cat bounce/short squeeze. Instead, you want to be entering short **during** these dead cat bounces, so that you can be covering your shorts on big red candles/gap downs.
