# Thursday Morning Notes 6.15

Technical Update

As expected, the Fed paused its interest rate hiking cycle at yesterday’s meeting. In response, the S&P 500 was volatile and finished the day near flat (+0.08%).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vPv1!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6d71ce17-8ac7-41cb-96a6-05350175f072_1195x889.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vPv1!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6d71ce17-8ac7-41cb-96a6-05350175f072_1195x889.jpeg)

The Fed hinted at 2 more rate hikes before year end, but the market fully believes they are done hiking. Only time will tell which side wins this game of “chicken”.

Technically, the market continues to act strong, riding its 8 EMA to higher prices. With that said, I would be hesitant to add more exposure at this point with the indices so extended above their 21 and 60 EMAs.

The QQQ is now 20% above its 150 SMA, which has historically meant a subsequent pullback is near (since 2009).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!45o2!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7038efe3-b9a5-44a9-8208-5f6d15aeb07f_1362x1304.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!45o2!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7038efe3-b9a5-44a9-8208-5f6d15aeb07f_1362x1304.jpeg)

It is possible to see the indices get even more extended, but in my view, the risk/reward is not favorable for any new entries up here.

I am still holding 3/4ths long on both my ARKK and IWO positions with stops at breakeven for each ($41.60 for ARKK & $233.1 for IWO). I will continue to let these trades play out as long as they do not break trend.
