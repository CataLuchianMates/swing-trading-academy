# Marlin Capital Live Portfolio Dashboards

Marlin Capital Readers,

Today, I’m excited to launch our new **Live Portfolio Dashboards** feature.

One of the most frequent requests I’ve received is for a better way to track our current positions without waiting for the next report/going through the old links.

This is why I am so thrilled to roll out this major upgrade for our community: **The Marlin Capital Live Portfolio Dashboards.**

### What’s New?

I have built 3 separate, interactive trackers (1 for each of our current portfolios) that pull live market data directly into this post. With this new feature, you can now check our entry prices, current values, and total P/L (Profit/Loss) at any time.

### How to Use the Dashboard

Below, you will find 3 distinct tabs for our **Trump Trades, 2026 Top Picks, and Active Trading** portfolios.

* **Real-Time Data:** Prices and P/L reflect live market data (with a standard 15-20 min delay).
* **Sortable:** You can click the column headers (like “PnL”) to sort the stocks and see what’s moving.
* **Searchable:** Search any ticker for more details.

**When I buy or sell a position, it will update here automatically.** If you’ve been waiting for a reason to go Paid, this is it.
