# Wednesday Morning Notes 8.31

Technical Analysis

We saw another follow through to the downside for US stocks yesterday. The QQQ bounced right at the $298 support level I talked about in Tuesday’s morning notes, and closed above $300 at $301. $298-$300 remains key support to watch today.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wFaW!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc61ac09b-2687-4fe5-9176-2fe687a4159b_1396x669.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wFaW!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc61ac09b-2687-4fe5-9176-2fe687a4159b_1396x669.jpeg)

We are set to open higher today, with the QQQ treading around $303-304 pre-market. We have had a heavy wave of selling the last 3 days, so it is possible for this bounce to continue to $304 and potentially $307. This is where I would look for the bounce to top out, and where I will look to potentially enter SQQQ. A breakdown of $298 support looks imminent. My short term target on the breakdown is $288-$290.
