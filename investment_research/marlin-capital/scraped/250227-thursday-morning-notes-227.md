# Thursday Morning Notes 2.27

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks closed flattish yesterday as the momentum unwind we have been battling with for the last week reverted a bit ahead of NVDA’s earnings print. NVDA is up slightly this morning on a skinnier beat for the quarter but a healthy q/q guide. Q4 revs $39.3B vs cons $38.3B and guided Q1 revs $43B vs cons $42.3B. Sentiment and positioning were about as friendly as they have been into an NVDA print in the last 2 years. The numbers and guidance look good enough, although it’s certainly not the blowout report we’ve seen from them before.

For the QQQ, yesterday’s candle was not technically an inside day but I would treat it like one. Above $519.66 and we can test the 50d MA at $523. If today’s gap up fades and we break yesterday’s lows at $511.36, a further decline to the 200d MA at $490.05 will come into play.

CORZ - Another gap up from our [Small Cap 6 Portfolio.](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-small-caps-with-massive-upside) That’s now 5/5 this earnings season! AMSC, WGS, INOD, SEZL, and now CORZ have all gapped up over +10% following Q4 results. The market/momentum pullback has weighed on these names a bit but I am very happy with the earnings from all 5 of my Small Cap 6 names that have reported. One left to go (AEYE), and this is the one on the chopping block for me. We will need to see a good report out of AEYE (ideally beat and raise).
