# Thursday Morning Notes 10.26

Daily Market Commentary

Wednesday was a big risk off session for equities with the S&P 500 breaking through recent lows and finishing -1.43% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qelL!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd03b8b30-203f-4af5-a271-942a419c86dc_1290x2096.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qelL!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd03b8b30-203f-4af5-a271-942a419c86dc_1290x2096.jpeg)

The h-sell setup I covered in [“The Key Story”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-key-story-19e) is fully playing out now with a decisive break below the $4200 level and 200-day moving average.  My next price targets for the SPX on this drawdown are $3800-$4000.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ts-q!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd6a183c5-7549-49c3-b776-a62720406a6a_1190x901.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ts-q!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd6a183c5-7549-49c3-b776-a62720406a6a_1190x901.jpeg)

The NDX experienced its worst day of 2023 yesterday, with 4 Mega Cap stocks (GOOGL, AMZN, META, NVDA) contributing over 50% of the index’s decline.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!kfT7!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fad0a4bcc-154c-4dec-a4d7-5cc6e7e29372_978x733.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!kfT7!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fad0a4bcc-154c-4dec-a4d7-5cc6e7e29372_978x733.jpeg)

GOOGL finished the day -9.5%, matching its worst earnings move in more than 10 years (and biggest single day decline since March 2020).  [I discussed](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-key-story-19e) the challenging backdrop for Mega Cap tech heading into Q3 earnings, as many of these stocks were priced to perfection ahead of time.  We are now seeing what happens when their results are less than perfect (GOOGL, TSLA).

After the bell, META reported solid quarterly results.  Total ad revs +21% YoY vs 13% YoY last quarter.  Guidance of $36.5B-$40B in Q4 revs versus consensus $38.76B.  The stock is trading slightly negative (-2%) this morning.
