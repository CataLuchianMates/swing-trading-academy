**Watchlist:**

None. There are times to get aggressive in the market, and there are times to do nothing. The last thing I want to do is chase performance into the end of the year and eat into my 70%+ YTD gains.

My biggest lesson from 2021, as I will write about this week, is patience. There are times to be aggressive and get heavy with position sizing. There are also times to be conservative and get light with position sizing. There are also times like now, where I simply see no edge in trading this very difficult and uncertain market. These are the times to be on the sidelines completely.

As I’ve said, there are basically 2 scenarios until/if the Fed pivots. One involves ARKK and growth stocks bouncing 20-30% from here, but with high volatility, it would not be easy to capture these gains. It would coming in the context of a larger downtrend, which is not my preferred setup. The other is that we simply break down without any large bounce.

If ARKK takes out Friday’s highs, I will close my short and be 100% cash in my Active Portfolio.
