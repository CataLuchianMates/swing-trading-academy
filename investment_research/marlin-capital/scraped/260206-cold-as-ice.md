# Cold As Ice

#### The Plunge Continues

Thursday was yet another rough day for US stocks, particularly momentum/high beta names. To me, this market action is very reminiscent of 2022. 2022 was a nasty Bear Cycle. This one could be too.

Bubbles are popping all over the market. Many AI names are exhibiting signs of topping behavior, while others have already entered their [Stage 4 downtrends.](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/grinch-selloff?utm_source=publication-search)

It all started with the most speculative names back in October.

This is how it [always starts](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/mixed-market-a68).

Many stocks have likely already seen their [AI Bubble](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-big-picture?utm_source=publication-search) peaks.

The charts tell the real story right now 👇👇

**NBIS**

NBIS was 1 of our biggest winners of 2025. We [gained +112% in the stock.](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/portfolio-change-and-performance)

Unfortunately, NBIS now appears to be in its Stage 4 Bear phase.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2BR6!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F72dbb637-7b1f-488a-b43d-dbf0afa48614_1266x844.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2BR6!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F72dbb637-7b1f-488a-b43d-dbf0afa48614_1266x844.png)

One thing that’s important to remember is that Fundamental analysis does not matter in Bear Markets. Stocks are going to decline heavily in their Bear phases (50-80% usually). There is no escaping the selling.

Every stock has a -50% drawdown at some point. That includes NVDA, AAPL, GOOGL, META, etc. The most valuable companies in the world have all suffered these 50-80% drawdowns before (and they will again). Small and Mid Caps (the more speculative names) typically decline -80%+.

As [I talked about on Tuesday (referencing Jeff Bezos)](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/mixed-market-a68?utm_source=publication-search), the company is NOT the stock. The fundamentals might be improving while the stock is going down. This is normal. In fact, it’s exactly what happens to good companies during Bear cycles. Eventually, the best fundamental names recover during the next Bull cycle (after a -50-80%+ decline).

The current NBIS price action is particularly alarming to me because (1) it’s the strongest fundamental play in the Data Center space and (2) it’s much further along in it’s decline than peers like APLD, IREN, WULF, etc. This tells me those peers are likely to catch down soon.
