# Monday Morning Notes 10.3

Technical Analysis

The indices broke down to new 52 week lows on Friday, putting a fork in the extended summer Bear Market rally as we head into October. In my view, the indices are now in the [3rd Wave Down](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-worst-third), [as I wrote about in the linked report](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-worst-third). As outlined in [the report](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-worst-third), the final declines tend to be the worst part of the Bear Market. For the QQQ, my target remains $220-$240 on the 3rd wave down. For the SPY, my target remains $2900-$3300.

With the VIX near its highest levels of the year (>30), traders can expect plenty of volatility moving forward. Counter-trend bounces are even more violent when the VIX is elevated. During the initial 2 waves down, counter-trend bounces typically stalled out between the daily 8 and 21 EMAs. These counter-trend bounces are shorter in nature and typically only last 1-5 days (when the market is in “selling mode” and in the midst of a large wave down). The bounces occur from extended overselling in the short term, so when price is >5% extended below the 8 EMA, a bounce is likely not far behind.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!VaWq!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc3a9eb93-ccbe-48ed-97bc-183b04acd01d_959x725.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!VaWq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc3a9eb93-ccbe-48ed-97bc-183b04acd01d_959x725.jpeg)

We currently come into the week with the QQQ >3% extended below its 8 EMA along with various other oversold readings pinging.

On a bounce, resistance levels ahead include $269-$270, $273-$275, and $278-$280. The next support level I am watching below is the $260-$263 region.
