# Wednesday Morning Notes 7.20

Technical Analysis:

We had a bullish day yesterday following Monday’s reversal, as the QQQ finally broke through $297.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ifgo!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F08276378-2972-42dc-ae59-df7416e459e2_1396x669.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ifgo!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F08276378-2972-42dc-ae59-df7416e459e2_1396x669.png)

I decided against taking a TQQQ long trade yesterday afternoon, as I was not willing to hold overnight with the risk of NFLX taking down the market post-earnings. The company declined over 20% in each of its previous 2 earnings reports, but the Street appears much happier with NFLX’s Q2 report.

On the daily, the QQQ finally broke through its 50 day MA. Next up appears to be a test of the 60 EMA, which I often use to assess the medium term price trend. Potential resistance levels to watch ahead include $300, $306-$307, and $312.
