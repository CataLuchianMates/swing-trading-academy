# Friday Morning Notes 6.9

Technical Update

The S&P 500 rallied on Thursday, finishing +0.62% on the day, and just 7 points below key $4300 resistance.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!fuAX!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0c9dd0a0-5aeb-4678-9529-cea9c5655346_958x729.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!fuAX!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0c9dd0a0-5aeb-4678-9529-cea9c5655346_958x729.jpeg)

$4300 remains the key resistance level to watch during today’s session. Thus far, the SPX has not been able to decisively break through this level. With that said, this recent consolidation is forming a bull flag like pattern that points to a break higher in the near future.

The Nasdaq led the way yesterday while small caps took a much needed rest.

I remain long IWO, as I see small caps testing YTD highs in the near future.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0xfk!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9abd8834-fc13-4d67-8eab-3aa67d399288_1191x895.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0xfk!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9abd8834-fc13-4d67-8eab-3aa67d399288_1191x895.jpeg)

I also remain long ARKK here with an initial price target of $44-$45.
