# Friday Morning Notes 2.3

Technical Analysis

The market rally continued yesterday with a gap and close above Nov/Dec highs ($4100). The S&P 500 closed at $4179, its highest close since late August. August highs of $4300 are well within reach now.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!rXN4!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F40b9812c-cb1e-4c0b-abad-5c22bec31f81_955x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!rXN4!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F40b9812c-cb1e-4c0b-abad-5c22bec31f81_955x726.jpeg)

Mega cap tech earnings (AAPL, AMZN, GOOGL) after the bell yesterday have thrown some water on this explosive rally. The market is set for a gap down between of around 1.4%. This is a healthy pullback (so far) after such a big move to the upside, and the technicals remain bullish.

Key support levels to watch below include $4100, $4050, 4020, $4000, and the key moving averages above. A successful re-test of the $4100 breakout level is what bulls want to see on this pullback.

Resistance wise, $4150, $4200, and $4300 are the key levels to watch above.
