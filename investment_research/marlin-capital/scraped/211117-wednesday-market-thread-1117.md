**Morgan Stanley on RBLX:**

Morgan Stanley Sees Roblox as Early in Evolution:

Management commented that “In the Next 3-5 years All Brands Will Have a Roblox Strategy”, implying that one day advertising agencies will have Roblox teams as they normally have Social teams today.

The $24bn+ Music oppor. is just beginning to be tapped. RBLX has already delivered multiple music experiences such as virtual concerts, launch parties, listening parties and virtual merchandise but the analyst expects there to be more with more artists from around the globe.

RBLX is investing to capitalize on the $252bn education spend opportunity with 240+ organizations teaching with Roblox Studio, 74 countries with educations programs that include Roblox Studio and 7mn+ monthly users in educational experiences…

RBLX is investing to improve developer tools and the quality of Avatars and Experiences

We are in the early days of RBLX’s optimization efforts. Management talked about the search experience, content recommendations, and improvements to the on-platform experience promotion products, which have increased user conversion 40%/60% on PC/mobile this year.

The analyst sees continued focus on optimization leading to more durable multi-year user, engagement and monetization growth.
