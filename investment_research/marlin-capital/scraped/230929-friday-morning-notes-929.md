# Friday Morning Notes 9.29

Technical Update

Today marks the final day of Q3 and final trading day of September. Historically, Q4 is the best performing quarter dating back to 1928. The numbers are even better when Q1-Q3 is up >10% heading into Q4, as we are currently.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!NzhP!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff3d8d438-e5bf-4af1-b295-713c1a2d7a3f_666x405.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!NzhP!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff3d8d438-e5bf-4af1-b295-713c1a2d7a3f_666x405.png)

The S&P 500 staged an oversold bounce on Thursday, closing the day +0.59%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!rB41!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F72034906-932c-4bc8-8a12-e66e60dbdb89_968x727.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!rB41!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F72034906-932c-4bc8-8a12-e66e60dbdb89_968x727.jpeg)

We are set for a continuation gap higher this morning. Key resistance levels to watch above include $4340-$4350, $4370-$4380, and $4400.

Important to watch today will be how the weekly charts develop and close. For the SPX, a strong close will be very constructive on the weekly chart after a tumultuous beginning to the week.
