# A Thank You and a Heads Up

**Marlin Capital Paid Subscribers,**

I want to start with a genuine thank you.

Your support is what allows me to do what I do every day. Wake up, study the market, find the best opportunities, and share them with all of you in real-time. None of this happens without you, and I do not take that for granted.

2026 has been an incredible year for us so far. We are up +32% YTD in the 2026 Portfolio. We have hit 14 multibaggers in the last 17 months.

And we are just getting started.

In the next ~2 weeks, I will be unveiling the **2026 SMID Cap 6 Portfolio**.

For those of you who are newer to Marlin Capital, the SMID Cap 6 has been one of our most successful concentrated portfolios. Our 2024 SMID Cap 6 returned +99% in ~7 months. Our 2025 SMID Cap 6 returned +64% in ~7 months. The 2026 SMID Cap 6 will be my 6 highest conviction SMID Cap ideas for the year ahead.

Now, on to the heads up. **As a current paid subscriber, the following change will NOT affect you.**

Starting with the launch of the 2026 SMID Cap 6 Portfolio, the Marlin Capital subscription rate for **new subscribers** will be moving from $39.99/month ($399.99/year) to $49.99/month ($499.99/year).

I want to be very clear about what this means for you.

## **This price increase does NOT affect you.**

**As a current paid subscriber, your existing rate (the rate you pay now) is locked in FOR LIFE.**

As long as you remain subscribed, you will continue to pay the same rate you pay today, forever.

#### **You will never see a price increase as long as your subscription stays active.**

The only way you would hypothetically lose your locked-in rate (the rate you pay today) is if you cancel and later re-subscribe. At that point, the new rate of $49.99/month ($499.99/year) would apply.

So consider this a small loyalty reward for being part of the Marlin Capital community early. You are now paying $120/year less than every new subscriber going forward, for the exact same product (and a product that, in my view, is only getting better).

Thank you again for your trust and your support. The 2026 SMID Cap 6 launch is right around the corner. Stay tuned!

Sincerely,

David Marlin
