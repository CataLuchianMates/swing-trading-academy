# Thursday Morning Notes 12.26

Daily Market Commentary:

I hope everyone had a very Merry Christmas!

The market finished higher on Tuesday’s half day with the SPX +1.1%.

That’s now 3 days in a row higher since last week’s Fed induced sell off. We’re back above all key moving averages and already recuperated the majority of Thursday’s market decline. This is a fitting way to end 2024 as we saw any / all market dips promptly bought up all throughout the year.

Big picture - Including 2024 (currently +28%), the NDX has finished higher in 19 of the last 22 years. So, what’s in store for the year ahead?

I am currently working on my 2025 preview report which will be released later this week. Keep an eye out for this report in the coming days.

We will also be adding real-time trade alerts (active portfolio buys and sells) and a monthly webinar series to the Marlin Capital service in 2025. I am very much looking forward to another great year ahead.

Thank you for all your support this year! Merry Christmas and Happy Holidays from my family to yours
