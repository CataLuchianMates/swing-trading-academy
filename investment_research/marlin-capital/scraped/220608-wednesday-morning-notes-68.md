# Wednesday Morning Notes 6.8

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday was a big day for the market after the morning gap down into support. Buyers showed up right off the open, and the indices found support at the bottom of their recent price ranges. We now come into Wednesday with the S&P 500 and QQQ near the top of their recent ranges.

With the indices stabilized and the VIX suppressed (for the time being), we are seeing some great action in individual names. It’s “take your pick” in the energy/oil & gas sector as we saw many breakouts yesterday.
