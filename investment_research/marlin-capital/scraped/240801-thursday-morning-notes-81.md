# Thursday Morning Notes 8.1

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Huge rally in the market yesterday with the NDX +3%, matching its best day in 18 months.  The SPX finished +1.58% on the day and closed back above its 8 and 21 EMAs.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bnH_!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F40e18117-c29d-47b0-8d6a-0595fda11c71_1191x888.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bnH_!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F40e18117-c29d-47b0-8d6a-0595fda11c71_1191x888.jpeg)

We saw explosive moves higher across Mag 7, Momentum Winners, and Semi bellwethers (Semis were -18% from July peak coming into the day).  NVDA finished +12.9%, adding $329B in market cap (most for a stock in history).  ANET was +11% on a beat and raise and AMD +4.5% (faded throughout the day).

FOMC – The Fed held rates in the 5.25-5.5% target range, as expected.  The statement was dovish on net.  The main revision is that they acknowledge the risks to “both sides of its dual mandate” (inflation + max employment) rather than just inflation – in line with recent comments from Fed speakers in speeches.  Overall, it was mostly a non-event.

Updates on my trades –

PRG – No changes here.  Still holding the final 2/3rds of my position with a stop at breakeven ($43).

NOW – No changes here.  Still holding my full position with a stop at $783.

Top trade setup for today –

SFM – Earnings were great here and I like the volume we’ve seen on day 1 and day 2.  Yesterday was an inside day.
