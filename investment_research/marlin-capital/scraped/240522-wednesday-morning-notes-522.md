# Wednesday Morning Notes 5.22

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Choppy day for the market yesterday with the S&P 500 closing +0.25% and the QQQ +0.20%.

Investors remain frozen as the indices continue to trade in a vacuum ahead of NVDA’s earnings post-close today, which will direct sentiment and price action for the rest of the week.  NVDA registered a new record high close yesterday at $953 and just ~2% away from its ATH tick ($974).

Breadth was narrow yesterday with NVDA, MSFT (Copilot updates), AAPL, LLY (+2.6% on China approval of popular weight loss drug), and TSLA +6.6% contributing to ~98% of today’s S&P move higher.

China ADRs struggled (BABA and BIDU weak) as well as US internet names (SQ -3%, SNAP -5%, UBER/DASH -1%).

Updates on trade setups:

BIDU – Inside day long setup didn’t trigger.  No position here.

SG – Still holding with stop in place.

JMIA – Stopped out here for a loss.

VITL – Still holding up +8% on this trade so far.

ENVX – Still holding with stop in place.

RMD – Still holding with stop in place.
