# Tuesday Morning Notes 5.7

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Positive start to the week for the major market indices with the S&P 500 finishing +1.03% and the NDX +1.13%.  It was a quiet tape with only 9.8B shares trading (-14% below YTD avg and 5th lowest on the year) as yields held on to Friday’s post payrolls pullback.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!GbT0!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe4f04780-683e-4fee-a9f4-7f034ec0e2ef_980x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!GbT0!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe4f04780-683e-4fee-a9f4-7f034ec0e2ef_980x726.jpeg)

Buybacks – Mentioned yesterday but a little more color here.  The buyback window is getting very active again as we are now through ~80% of SPX earnings.  The open buyback window is estimated to run through 6/14.  GS estimates that ~86% of companies are now in open window with ~92% in open window by the end of the week.  There continues to be a lot of positive sentiment around buybacks, led by AAPL announcing a $110B buyback program last week.  This will continue to be a big tailwind for the market for the next month.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!v7xr!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2542ad05-fb5c-4e5a-b235-9d2d41e01bbb_1032x444.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!v7xr!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2542ad05-fb5c-4e5a-b235-9d2d41e01bbb_1032x444.jpeg)

CTAs – GS estimates that CTAs bought $9B of global equities last week, taking positioning to $119B (82nd percentile), including $23.5B length in S&P.  CTAs are modeled to be significant buyers of US equities this week in every scenario (so long as $5100 holds on the SPX).

Seasonality – Seasonality is strong.  This mid-may to June period during election years is very strong.
