# Wednesday Morning Notes 9.14

Technical Analysis

US stocks got slammed yesterday following the higher than expected CPI reading for August. The market was pricing in the opposite, as certain indicators of inflation (used car prices) were collapsing. However, many other indicators of inflation continue to soar, as the August CPI report highlights.

Technical buyers were suckered into the market ahead of this CPI report, as we had a false breakout on the QQQ 15 minute (and daily move above the 60 EMA). This type of action is typical in a Bear market, where every move should be questioned.

I entered short S&P 500 Dec Futures (/ES) at $4026. I will be looking to cover the majority of my contracts in the low $3900 area, which would be a re-test of recent lows.
