# Tuesday Morning Notes 2.13

CPI Report -

January CPI inflation rate falls to 3.1%, above expectations of 2.9%.

Core CPI inflation was unchanged at 3.9%, above expectations of 3.7%.

This is the 34th consecutive month with inflation above 3% and first jump since September.

May rate cut odds have fallen from 52% to 36% following the January CPI print, while the odds that there won't be any rate cuts through the July meeting are up to 22.3% from 7.8%.

The market is set for a big gap down this morning following today’s CPI report.

Here is how I’m handling my current positions:

On PLTR, I will be moving my stop loss to $21.38 on the final half of my position.

On NXT, I will be exiting this position this morning.  It is time to play defense here and take some risk off the table.
