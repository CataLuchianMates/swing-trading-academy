# Activate the Trump Trades

Last week, Donald Trump was elected the 47th President of the United States. The stock market responded with a rip roaring rally, as the S&P 500 broke $6,000 for the first time in its history.

The post-election market rally was broad based. Tech, Consumer Discretionary, Financials, and Industrials all broke out to new record highs following Trump’s victory, marking the first coordinated breakout for these 4 sectors since 2021.

**Tesla (TSLA)**

This is an obvious pick. Doesn’t matter. I’m betting on Elon Musk 100 out of 100 times, especially when he has the full support of the Trump administration behind him.

Elon Musk very publicly (and financially) supported Trump throughout his campaign. Trump's acknowledgment of Musk’s influence during his victory speech (“Elon…a star is born”) and promise of roles like a government efficiency chair for Musk suggest a positive inclination towards Elon’s business interests.

There is also an expectation that the Trump admin will reduce bureaucratic hurdles, which could expedite Tesla's various projects, including those in Full Self-Driving (FSD), AI, space, and energy sectors.

Further, Trump's policies very much emphasize domestic production, which aligns well with Tesla's significant investment in US manufacturing. Tesla's Gigafactory in Nevada and the company's plans for expansion in the US are likely to see benefits from policies favoring American made products. Also, Tesla's competitors are likely to be much more disadvantaged from tariffs on imported goods (particularly from China), given Tesla's established US production capabilities.

We are coming off a massive week for TSLA (+31%) following the election, yet the stock still sits -22.5% from all-time-highs. I would not be surprised to see TSLA trade all the way back to ATHs before Trump formally takes office in January.

I fully expect TSLA to be the top performing Mag 7 name during Trump’s presidency.

**Coinbase (COIN)**

We are already seeing Crypto breakout to new highs following Trump’s victory, with Bitcoin trading above $80k for the first time in its history. During Trump’s first term, Bitcoin rocketed a staggering +1890%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!j7Lm!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F23f0b397-0c13-4a0d-92e3-3fc133a1043d_1663x897.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!j7Lm!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F23f0b397-0c13-4a0d-92e3-3fc133a1043d_1663x897.jpeg)

Trump has fully embraced Crypto as an asset class, promising to make the US the "Crypto capital of the world." This could lead to regulatory changes that benefit Crypto exchanges like Coinbase.

One of Trump’s campaign promises was to fire anti-crypto SEC Chair Gary Gensler, who has been a thorn in the side of the Crypto community. With Trump's administration, we are likely to see an attempt to provide clearer regulations for Crypto’s, potentially shifting oversight from the SEC to the CFTC.

Further, **Vice President Elect JD Vance reported owning between $250k-$500k in Bitcoin through Coinbase**, according to federal disclosure forms filed in August.

COIN is an obvious beneficiary of a more Crypto-friendly environment, and we saw the stock advance +50% last week following Trump’s victory. Still, the stock remains -37% off ATHs. I fully expect the stock to trade back to ATHs and beyond in the near future.

**Palantir (PLTR)**

Trump's 1st term showed a strong inclination towards boosting military and national security budgets. Given Palantir's deep ties with the US government, particularly with agencies like the Department of Defense, an increase in defense spending could lead to more contracts for Palantir. Their software is used extensively for analytics in security contexts, which aligns with Trump's focus on strengthening national defense.

Peter Thiel, one of Palantir's co-founders, has significant political influence, especially within conservative circles. His relationship with VP Elect JD Vance runs deep. The two met in 2019 when Vance was a student at Yale Law School. After law school, Thiel hired Vance to join his Venture Capital Tech Fund Mithril Capital.

Vance later moved back to his native Ohio where he started his own VC firm, backed heavily by Peter Thiel. In 2022, Thiel encouraged Vance’s entry into politics and provided Vance with $15M in donations for his Ohio Senate race.

Thiel’s relationship with JD Vance and support for Trump could potentially streamline government dealings and provide more opportunities for Palantir. These connections could facilitate a smoother path for Palantir to secure government contracts.

This sums up my top 4 Trump trades (Top 6 Small Caps, TSLA, COIN/Crypto, and PLTR). I believe these Trump plays are poised to outperform due to anticipated regulatory rollbacks, corporate tax cuts, pro growth policies, and tight-knit relationships with the Trump admin.
