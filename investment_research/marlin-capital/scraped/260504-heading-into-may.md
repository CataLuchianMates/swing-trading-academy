**My Top Setups Heading Into This Week**

Here are the 3 setups I am watching most closely heading into this week. 👇

**BE**

This is my top setup for the week.

It all started with the Oracle deal. On 4/19, Bloom Energy announced a massive expansion of its Oracle partnership with a master services agreement for up to 2.8 GW of fuel cell systems for AI and cloud data centers (1.2 GW already contracted). The stock surged +22% on the news.

Then came the blowout Q1 earnings I covered in full detail last week in [Follow The Money](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/follow-the-money).

Revenue of $751.1M grew +130% YoY (the first +100% YoY growth quarter in BE’s history as a public company). Product revenue +208% YoY. Gross margin expanded +280bps. EPS crushed consensus by +155%. And BE generated positive operating cash flow of +$73.6M for the first time in a Q1 ever.

BE also raised FY26 revenue guidance to $3.4-3.8B (up from $3.1-3.3B), implying +80% YoY growth. EPS guide was raised to $1.85-2.25 from $1.33-1.48, another significant boost.

For BE, these are 2 major fundamental catalysts in the last month (the Oracle deal + a blowout earnings report & guidance). And the price action is exactly what you want to see in response to these major fundamental catalysts. Consecutive unfilled gaps and a high tight bull flag heading into this week.

**NXPI**

NXPI has emerged as a clear standout from this Q1 earnings season so far. As I talked about last week, NXPI’s report was all about the upbeat guidance and data center disclosure.

NXPI guided Q2 revenue to $3.45B (+18% YoY, +8% QoQ), beating consensus of $3.27B by +5.3%. EPS guide of $3.50 blew away the $3.20 estimate. Gross margin guide of 58% implied +150bps YoY expansion.

But the bigger story was the data center disclosure. Management disclosed that data center revenue is going from $200M in 2025 to >$500M in 2026. This is a 2.5x ramp.

That is massive news, and the stock reacted accordingly (with a huge gap up and continuation move higher so far).
