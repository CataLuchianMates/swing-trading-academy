# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.9

Technical Analysis

The market reversed yesterday after a morning rally that saw us break Friday’s highs. The QQQ put in a shooting star candle on the daily chart. The 3 names I was most interested in (SOFI - which I tried for a loss, CRSP, BMBL) each put in ugly reversal candles.

For the QQQ, a move below $318-$319 would open the door for a bigger drop here. **Remember, CPI data will be released Wednesday pre-market.** The consensus is calling for 8.7%. In my view, the risk is significantly skewed to the downside after such a big rally off the lows.
