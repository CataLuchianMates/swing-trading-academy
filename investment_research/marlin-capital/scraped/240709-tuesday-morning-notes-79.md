# Tuesday Morning Notes 7.9

**Daily Market Commentary:**

No fireworks yesterday as the market kicked off the week of 7/8 on a quiet note (SPX +0.10%; NDX +0.23%).

We are sandwiched in between last week’s shortened holiday week and CPI Thursday/earnings kicking off on Friday.  Consensus expects +9% YoY EPS growth for the S&P 500 in Q2, which would mark the strongest quarterly growth rate since Q4 2021.  I’m expecting today/tomorrow to continue to be quiet with activity picking up on Thursday following CPI and bank earnings Friday.

We saw a notable dispersion under the hood yesterday with Semis > Software.  GLW led the way, +12% on positive pre-announcement with the AI tailwind mention).

GLW is now my top watch for the rest of this week.
