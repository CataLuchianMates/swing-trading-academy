# Friday Morning Notes 7.7

Technical Update

The S&P 500 pulled back -0.79% yesterday, closing right at its 8 EMA support level ($4410).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vDUG!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ffc0ab739-5c3b-4b5b-82fc-81e5dd8f0c5e_1259x944.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vDUG!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ffc0ab739-5c3b-4b5b-82fc-81e5dd8f0c5e_1259x944.jpeg)

RSI has potentially made a lower high (not yet confirmed) for the SPX while MACD appears to be crossing over negative.

Meanwhile, the Nasdaq swung back to net lows for the first time in almost a month.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jx7x!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F90a1fe99-b832-4423-8271-dd0efc01c9e5_919x613.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jx7x!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F90a1fe99-b832-4423-8271-dd0efc01c9e5_919x613.png)

While there are more technical reasons for caution, it is too early to get bearish in my view until some key support levels to the downside start to break. Key support levels I am watching include $4410 (8 EMA), $4400, and $4355-$4360 (21 EMA).

No changes to my current holdings and no plans to add on this dip until I see how price reacts at key support.
