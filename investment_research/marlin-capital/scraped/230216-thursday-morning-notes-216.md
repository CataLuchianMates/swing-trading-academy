# Thursday Morning Notes 2.16

Technical Update

The S&P 500 rallied again yesterday, closing +0.28% and at its highs of the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HjMm!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F18899b85-3a22-4739-936a-e8f1b0ac1fd8_886x658.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!HjMm!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F18899b85-3a22-4739-936a-e8f1b0ac1fd8_886x658.jpeg)

Still, no key upside or downside levels were broken in yesterday’s session. We are now set for a sizable gap down this morning after higher than expected PPI. The market has responded well to gap downs and bad news throughout this rally, so we will see if that continues today.

Key support levels below include $4090-$4100 and $4060.

In the case this gap down is bought up once again, key resistance levels ahead include $4147, $4155-$4160, and $4176.
