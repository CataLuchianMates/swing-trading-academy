# Thursday Morning Notes 3.21

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks rallied higher on the FOMC continuing to project 3 rate cuts for 2024 and leaving the language unchanged.  The S&P 500 crossed above $5,200 for the first time, closing at $5224.  It took 757 days to go from 4800 to 4900 and just 5 days to go from 4900 to 5200.

Overall, the majority of Powell’s answers suggest a relatively dovish Fed Chair as the committee is not making any adjustments for the stronger data than they assumed in December.  3 cuts are still on the table for 2024 and the Fed is looking ahead to easing mode despite some recent stronger data points.  Powell and the Fed are more concerned about the overall trend than 1 month of data points.

Bitcoin Sensitive Equities, Non-Profitable Tech, and Liquid Most Short rallied the most (highest beta plays).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!a8py!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F88bc16c6-78f7-438d-bfd7-507c6ff9679d_1125x1095.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!a8py!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F88bc16c6-78f7-438d-bfd7-507c6ff9679d_1125x1095.jpeg)

The QQQ launched off 1-HR support ($433-$434), closing just below $444.  This is a very bullish technical setup for Tech and in my view, it appears headed to new highs.

After the bell – MU +17% this morning on a nice guide above (new ATH pre-market),  “We believe Micron is one of the biggest beneficiaries in the semiconductor industry of the multi-year opportunity enabled by AI”.

I will be watching MU for a potential earnings gapper entry today.

My 2 top trade ideas for today –

COIN.  I’ve talked about how I expect COIN to have a huge year as long as the Bitcoin bull run continues.  With the Fed out of the way, Bitcoin bounced big and COIN looks set up for a move higher.  I will be looking to get long off the open with a stop below $230.
