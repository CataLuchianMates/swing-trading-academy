### **APLD**

We are now up over +125% in ~4 months on APLD!

Applied Digital just announced Q3 Earnings, and absolutely knocked it out of the park! APLD is up +17% in the overnight session and up over +22% on the day.

[Immediately following](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/big-tech-blowouts?utm_source=publication-search) APLD’s Q2 earnings on 7/31, I said that I would not be surprised to see the stock double from here.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!PvWh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9242fc2c-27a3-4bf2-a046-39e45e7dfb56_1192x679.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!PvWh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9242fc2c-27a3-4bf2-a046-39e45e7dfb56_1192x679.jpeg)

And it did, from $13 to $29+.

Now, I am saying that **AGAIN** following Q3 earnings (Fiscal Q1 ‘26).

**This was an incredibly bullish conference call for APLD.**

Applied Digital’s CEO announced that the company has a 4 GW pipeline and that they are currently negotiating on 2 new Data Center campuses. This type of huge expansion was not built in to my projections for APLD.

My projections included 1-2 more Hyperscaler deals (Polaris Forge 2 +). But based off what management said during today’s call, we should be looking at 3+ more future deals. Applied Digital management said that we can expect the company to constantly be negotiating new Hyperscaler deals for the foreseeable future.

In [“Multi-Bagger Monday”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/multi-bagger-monday?utm_source=publication-search), I projected $1.24B+ in annual revenue for APLD with 1 more deal (on Polaris Forge 2). I used the $1.83M per MW per year from the existing CoreWeave deal (on Polaris Forge 1) as a baseline in my projections.

Using that same baseline of $1.83M per MW per year, let’s run the math for 4 GW of power:

$1.83M per MW per year x 4000 MW = $7.32B annually

**If Applied Digital can secure 4 GW of power, we would likely be looking at $7B+ in ARR.**

This is a company that did $180M in annual revenue last year as a Bitcoin Mining company. **That would be a 40x in annual revenue.**

It should be noted that the $7B in ARR projection on 4 GW of power is a “far out into the future” projection. It is a Bull Case projection. The 4 GW is not secured yet, and there is no guarantee they will get there + sign deals at each location. There is execution risk involved.

Still, APLD continues to be 1 of the very best growth stories in the market right now.

And during this AI Bubble, we are seeing the market start to price in “far out into the future”/ bull case projections. Because of this super bullish market environment, I can certainly see APLD running to a $20B+ valuation based off their 4 GW pipeline.

Prior to today’s earnings call, the highest future projections I had seen for APLD were at ~2 GW (operational + pipeline). The 4 GW pipeline is a completely new number, and rivals IREN. IREN has a ~3.4+ GW pipeline, and no major Hyperscaler deals. IREN is valued at over $17B+ market cap. APLD is trading at half IREN’s valuation, currently at ~$9B market cap including AH gains.

And APLD is **just now** starting to break out of its 4+ year base.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!K-s1!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F48063853-4761-4bd8-8227-ef3f2b38baad_1132x652.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!K-s1!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F48063853-4761-4bd8-8227-ef3f2b38baad_1132x652.png)

Somehow, APLD hit $34 back in 2021 when Bitcoin Miners were going nuts (if you wanted to know where we currently are in relation to the 2021 Covid Bubble). Because now, Applied Digital is a legitimate AI Data Center business backed by Nvidia with a near-term path to $1.24B+ ARR and a future pipeline to $7B+ in ARR. This move (unlike the ‘21 Covid Bubble) is backed by real financials, and it hasn’t even gotten crazy yet. Crazy would be pricing in the full $7B ARR pipeline x 6 P/S = $42B+ valuation (which would take APLD to $120+).

My target for APLD on this 4+ year base breakout is $65+, which would be a $20B+ market cap.

APLD continues to be 1 of my favorite growth stories in the market, and the stock has grown into 1 of my Top 2 biggest positions overall.
