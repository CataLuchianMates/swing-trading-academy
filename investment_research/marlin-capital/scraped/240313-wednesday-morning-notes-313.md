# Wednesday Morning Notes 3.13

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Markets rose yesterday following the CPI release for February with the S&P 500 rallying +1.12% and NDX +1.49%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pICQ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5ef78f83-f151-4677-938b-c1d7f8e06c8b_1207x887.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pICQ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5ef78f83-f151-4677-938b-c1d7f8e06c8b_1207x887.jpeg)

The number was a touch higher than expected on core at 36bps vs consensus 30bps MoM, but clear disinflation in supercore (step down to 47bps from 85bps) and OER (step down to 44bps from 56bps and back to the Q4 avg).  The upside surprise came from  used cars and communications but neither categories are persistent.  Overall, there is nothing in the data alerting the inflation trend and yesterday’s print appeared to be priced in by the market ahead of time.

The QQQ bounced once again at key support (21 EMA level) as it continues to hold the trend line from last October lows.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lHad!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6d486df0-5974-45db-a553-5f2a4af76967_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lHad!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6d486df0-5974-45db-a553-5f2a4af76967_1396x653.png)

As mentioned yesterday, the trends remains up until this support definitively breaks (and this has not happened in over 4 months).

Super Cap Tech and long duration assets led the tape higher yesterday despite upward pressure on long end yields.  High Beta Momentum closed +3.51%, NDX +1.49% (NVDA, MSFT, and META leading the way), but the R2K dragged -2bps.

My TNA long stopped me out at $39.48 in yesterday’s session. Position closed for a -4.2% loss as this breakout just could not gain enough traction over $42/$43.

My top trade idea for today –

BAND

I like the way this stock is setting up on the daily and weekly for more upside.  We have a post-earnings Bull Flag on the daily that looks primed for a break higher.
