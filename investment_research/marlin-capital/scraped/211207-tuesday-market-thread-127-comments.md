**Q:** And one last thing: do you agree that we should have another ~2 more years of a bull market before we see the top?

**David Marlin:** Historically, the end of bull markets are caused by the inversion of the yield curve and market discounting a potential recession.  That’s not what this is right now.  I believe that is ultimately when the indices will peak.  However, many growth stocks like ROKU, ZM, TDOC, FVRR, PINS, etc. will not recover for years in my opinion (if they do).  2022 will very much be a stock picker’s market.  The type of year we saw in 2020 post Covid is not going to be happening again.
