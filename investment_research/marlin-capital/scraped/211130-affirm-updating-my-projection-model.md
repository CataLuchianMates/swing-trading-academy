# Affirm: Updating My Projection Model

The consensus BNPL projections modeled a 5% penetration rate for North America in 2024.  Truth be told, I was expecting faster BNPL adoption in North America than these consensus projections indicated.

---

**Updating Model**

Based off my research, I am adjusting by Base Case to 10% BNPL Penetration of E-Comm volumes in 2024.  This is in line with the Afterpay funded projections above.

I am adjusting my Bull Case to 13% BNPL Penetration of E-Comm volumes in 2024.   This would assume that the US BNPL Industry largely catches up to Europe by 2024.

I am adjusting my Bear Case to 5% BNPL Penetration of E-Comm volumes in 2024.  This was my previous Base Case scenario, but it has become evident that we are on pace to see faster adoption over the next 3 years.

**What are the analysts expecting?**

Consensus future projections for AFRM show that analysts are expecting a scenario slightly better than my current Bear Case to play out.  In Fiscal 2024, analysts are expecting AFRM to generate $2.56B in revenue.  These numbers have been on the rise over the last month, as analysts have raised their FY ’24 estimates from $2.2B to $2.6B.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pn1K!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe78a56e0-3467-4b6f-a884-48398a35e340_429x171.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pn1K!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe78a56e0-3467-4b6f-a884-48398a35e340_429x171.png)

It’s also important to note that Affirm’s Fiscal 2024 includes the last 2 calendar quarter of 2023.  With that said, we can estimate that analysts are expecting Affirm to generate roughly $3.1B-$3.5B in the Calendar year 2024.  This is roughly in line with the previous Bull Case I laid out in early October, where I assumed a 7% BNPL penetration rate in Calendar Year 2024.

To recap, analysts previously modeled a 5% BNPL penetration rate in CY 2024 with expectations for AFRM to generate $2.2B in revenue in FY ‘24.  Over the last month, they have increased their estimates.  Now, analysts are modeling a 7% BNPL penetration rate in CY 2024 with expectations for AFRM to generate $2.6B in revenue in FY ‘24.

I believe analysts will continue to raise their estimates until they meet my current Base Case of 10% BNPL Penetration in 2024.  BNPL is a nascent payment method in the US where analysts were forced to make best guesses as to future adoption rates.  They continue to show they are behind on the data, as BNPL is already tracking at 8% exposure during the 2021 Holiday season.

---

**Conclusion**

My Base Case scenario has AFRM generating roughly $4.2B in revenue in CY 2024.  Based off consensus projections, we can assume analysts are expecting AFRM to generate roughly $3.3B in revenue in CY 2024.

I see potential for a >20% upside surprise on the consensus 2024 revenue estimate in my Base Case scenario.  In my Bull Case scenario, I believe there is potential for a >60% upside surprise on Affirm’s 2024 revenue estimates.
