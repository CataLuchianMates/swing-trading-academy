# 6 Small Caps With Massive Upside Potential

As we enter a potentially bullish period for Small Cap stocks, I'm excited to share my top 6 Small Cap stock picks for the next 12 months.

Historically, Small Caps tend to outperform when the Fed eases monetary policy.

If this historical pattern holds true and we are indeed at the cusp of a Small Cap Bull Run, these 6 picks offer huge, multi-bagger potential.

Here are my top 6 Small Cap (<$2B market cap) picks with 10x potential:

(These picks are in no particular order).

**Audioeye (AEYE)**

Market Cap: $262M

Why it’s a top pick…

Audioeye (AEYE) is 1 of my top picks due to its positioning in the rapidly growing digital accessibility market.  As businesses increasingly prioritize compliance with web accessibility standards, Audioeye's AI-powered solutions are well positioned to capitalize on this growing market.

The digital accessibility software market is expected to reach $959M in the next 5 years, offering significant room for growth.

In Q1, AEYE reported record revenues, EPS profitability, and an +18% increase in customer count.  The company also raised its FY revenue guidance to $34.5M, alongside an adjusted EBITDA forecast of $5M.

The stock responded very positively to these results, with record trading volumes and a number of unusual, long term bull bets in the options market.  In my view, the stock has shown signs of institutional accumulation, and there is plenty of upside left if Small Caps can continue their recent Bull trend

**Innodata (INOD)**

Market Cap: $508M

Why it’s a top pick…

Innodata (INOD) stands out among Small Caps primarily due to its positioning in the AI sector.  Innodata's AI and machine learning solutions are helping businesses handle their abundance of data.  As a data engineering company, INOD helps companies implement AI models and directs them to perform specific tasks.  With increasing demand for efficient data management, INOD is well positioned to capitalize in this market.

Last quarter, INOD reported a +41% YoY increase in revenue and strong EBITDA growth of $3.8M.

The stock responded very positively to these results, with ATH volumes (over 20x avg daily volume), and has continued to run since.  In my view, this Small Cap AI play has huge potential upside from here.

**American Superconductor (AMSC)**

Market Cap: $1.07B

Why it’s a top pick…

With the increasing focus on renewable energy, American Superconductor's advanced power systems and superconductor wire technology are positioned to see significant demand.  Its solutions for wind energy and power grid stability align well with global energy trends.

Last quarter, AMSC reported +65% YoY revenue growth, driven primarily by its power grid unit, Gridtec.  AMSC's Gridtec Solutions are targeting a nearly $9B total addressable market.  The technology allows for the transmission of up to 10x more power while occupying less space, making it essential for modernizing energy infrastructure.

The company also reported a positive cash flow of $1.3M and a notable turnaround in net income, from a loss to a profit of $900K.

Following these results, we saw the stock gap up on big volume and has continued to run to ATHs since.  For those following the “Top Bull Bets/Unusual Call Activity” in the daily morning notes, AMSC stock has continued to show up here (many LEAPS) since its 5/29 EPS report.  Institutional investors appear to be betting on more upside for AMSC, and I am right there with them.

**Core Scientific (CORZ)**

Market Cap: $1.95B

Why it’s a top pick…

The Bitcoin Miner, Core Scientific (CORZ), has seen a major stock rally following its entry into the AI sector.  The company secured a $3.5B partnership with CoreWeave to provide 200 megawatts of infrastructure, repurposing its (Bitcoin Mining) facilities for high-performance computing and AI applications

This strategic shift allows CORZ to not only diversify its revenues beyond just Bitcoin mining, but also positions the company to capitalize on the growing demand for AI infrastructure.

Following the deal announcement with CoreWeave (on 6/4), CORZ stock gapped up on ATH volume (more than 8x avg daily volume), and the stock has nearly doubled since.  This AI catalyst has attracted a much broader investor base to CORZ (beyond crypto/bitcoin mining investors), and we have routinely seen this stock show up on the “Top Bull Bets/Unusual Call Activity” page.

The ongoing demand for AI infrastructure and CoreWeave deal positions CORZ favorably in this market, and I believe there is plenty of upside left here as the company ramps up operations by mid-2025.

**Sezzle (SEZL)**

Market Cap: $487<

Why it’s a top pick…

Sezzle (SEZL) stands out for me due to its strong growth and profitability in the FinTech/BNPL space, as well as its reasonably low valuation.  In Q1, the company reported a +36% YoY sales increase and net income of $8M, a +364% YoY increase.  For the fiscal year, SEZL is projecting a record total net income of $30M.

With a market cap of $487M and a forward P/E of ~17x, Sezzle appears undervalued compared to its growth potential.

I am bullish on future of the BNPL industry, and believe there will be plenty of growth in this industry in the years to come.  I see SEZL as a potential multi-bagger play to capitalize on the growth of the BNPL industry.

**Genedx Holdings (WGS)**

Market Cap: $893M

Why it’s a top pick…

Genedx (WGS) is at the forefront of genomic testing and analysis.  As personalized medicine gains traction, Genedx's comprehensive genetic testing services could see exponential growth in the healthcare industry.

WGS has shown a positive financial trajectory, as evidenced by the company's gross profit turnaround from a loss of -$27M in 2022 to a profit of +$90M in 2023.  Last quarter (Q1 ’24), the company announced record results across a myriad of different metrics.

Revenue surged +51% YoY to $62M, driven by a +96% increase in exome and genome test revenue.  Gross margins expanded significantly from 34% to 61%, while the company also raised FY revenue guidance to $240M, with expectations of achieving profitability by 2025.

The stock responded to these results with a huge gap up on ATH volumes (over 8x avg daily volume), and has continued to run to ATHs since.  In my view, this rally in WGS is far from over so long as the company can continue to execute towards its goal of profitability by 2025.
