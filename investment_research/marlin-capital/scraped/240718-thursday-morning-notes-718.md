# Thursday Morning Notes 7.18

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Big sell off yesterday (SPX -1.4%, NDX -2.9%, RTY -1%) with semis under max pressure on a combination of underwhelming semi prints (ASML guided down; stock -12%) + tariff concerns + ongoing rotational pressure.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c_5u!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0efd5d0d-8445-4bfa-8c6c-67d6843c5869_1194x889.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!c_5u!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0efd5d0d-8445-4bfa-8c6c-67d6843c5869_1194x889.jpeg)

The weakness bled over to the Mag 7 with AMZN, META, MSFT all meaningfully lower on the day.  Mega Cap finished -3.51%, among the worst performing groups on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ZGg6!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F429ebadf-460d-493e-bc1a-ac4cd8ca4ed8_548x474.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ZGg6!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F429ebadf-460d-493e-bc1a-ac4cd8ca4ed8_548x474.jpeg)

There was nowhere to hide in Tech, as the major AI baskets got crushed while the NDX posted its worst day since December 2022.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!AfNi!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F16b4c041-ac4e-4857-9a90-2984d5556f3b_581x548.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!AfNi!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F16b4c041-ac4e-4857-9a90-2984d5556f3b_581x548.jpeg)

Hedge Funds have been actively unwinding risk amid the recent market rotation.  In cumulative notional terms, the de-grossing activity over the past 5 sessions is the largest since November 2022 and ranks in the 99th percentile over the past 5 yrs.  10 of 11 US sectors saw de-grossing activity over the last week, led by Tech (long sales > short covers).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lJDJ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa02df4ff-6ced-4ca3-a63f-56a4b090baf6_571x277.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lJDJ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa02df4ff-6ced-4ca3-a63f-56a4b090baf6_571x277.jpeg)

Goldman’s Momentum Basket is now -8.9% over the last 5 trading sessions.  The last 2 unwinds were -15%, so that would leave us halfway through this episode using recent history as our gauge.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XL95!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F20cba5f1-489d-4df0-9dc6-f297d8962782_587x346.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XL95!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F20cba5f1-489d-4df0-9dc6-f297d8962782_587x346.jpeg)

In my view, this de-risking into Mega Cap EPS prints presents a much better trading setup for these names post earnings.  The first 2 weeks of July played out as planned, and it appeared like we may continue the Mag 7 melt up into earnings.  This pull back is welcome and should end up giving us better trading opportunities post-EPS.

Updates on my trades –

NVO – Stopped out for around breakeven on my shares.  Position closed.

AVGO – Big sell off in Semis yesterday and my stop loss was triggered here for a ~4% loss.  Position closed.

AMD – Ugly day for AMD along with the rest of the chip sector yesterday.  I took the loss on my remaining 2/3rd shares.  Position closed.
