# Friday Morning Notes 7.1

Welcome to July, and the 2nd half of 2022!  The 1st half of the year was one for the record books, as the S&P 500 capped off its worst first half plunge (-21%) since 1970.

Here are just a few of the headlines from the first 6 months of 2022:

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday was an eventful day for the market.  In the morning, QQQ broke the bottom of this 15 range ($278-$279) and our last support levels.  This was followed by a big reversal back into the range, and gap fill to yesterday’s close.  The reversal eventually began to fail around previous resistance levels, ($283-284).  The volume shelves indicate a lot of price congestion in that area.

Heading into today, we are at the bottom of the range.  This setup remains bearish on all time frames.  Bulls will want to break through the top of the range soon ($285-$286) to start feeling a little more comfortable.
