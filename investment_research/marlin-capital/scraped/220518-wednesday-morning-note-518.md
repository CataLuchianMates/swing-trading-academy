# Wednesday Morning Note 5.18

The S&P 500 had a solid day yesterday after some morning chop, closing above the 8 EMA. The next level to watch is the 21 EMA at $414. We have not closed above the 21 EMA since Mid-April. A close above this key level would open the door for a bigger and longer duration Bear rally, like the one we saw in March.
