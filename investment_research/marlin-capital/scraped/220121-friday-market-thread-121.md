# Friday Market Thread 1.21

**Marlin Capital Friday Plan:**

We are within my first “Buying Tranche” range for my IRA, so I intend to potentially put some cash to work today.  Ultimately, I believe we are inching closer to a bottom, but I still do not see the necessary capitulation/panic selling yet.  As I have mentioned, the probabilities are pointing towards this correction ending closer to the 20% variety.

With that said, I am seeing some attractive R/R investment opportunities at these prices, for those that have a 5+ year outlook.  I’m coming into the day 55% cash in my IRA, which is by far the largest cash position I have ever held in my retirement account.

At this first tranche, I intend to use 20-25% of my current cash position.  This would still leave me with a 44% cash position, minimum.  The larger buying for me would come in tranche 2 and 3, as I do believe we will see the Nasdaq reach my lower price targets at some point in 2022.

In simpler terms, I believe you can START to buy your highest conviction names today, provided you are investing for the long term (5+ yrs).  This does not mean to buy heavy with all your cash as if today will certainly be the ultimate bottom.

If you are following along on my investments, I am targeting potential buys today in RBLX, COIN, AFRM, and ENPH.  I will post any final buys in real time in the Discord Group.
