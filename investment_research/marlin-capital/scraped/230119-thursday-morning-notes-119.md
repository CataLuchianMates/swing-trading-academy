# Thursday Morning Notes 1.19

Technical Update

A big reversal for the market yesterday as we broke back below the 200 day MA and broke several key support levels to the downside.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nMxk!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0423d01a-11af-4da7-9e55-d40083a2dd96_1036x780.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nMxk!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0423d01a-11af-4da7-9e55-d40083a2dd96_1036x780.jpeg)

Now, we are set for a continuation gap down below $3900 this morning. In my view, a decisive move below the $3890-$3900 range is potentially shortable for those looking to play further downside on this reversal. The stop should be above yesterday’s highs ($4015), ensuring no more than a 3-3.3% loss on the trade.

Key support levels to watch below include $3890, $3840-$3850, and $3800-$3810.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $3900, $3940-$3950, $3980, and $4000.
