Technical Analysis

The market made a decisive move lower Friday following Powell’s speech. The Fed chair’s hawkish tone should not have come as a surprise, but for some market participants, it was a dose of reality (as I’ve highlighted in write ups, the market rallied due to FCI easing because of a perceived July dovish pivot that never happened.)

The QQQ broke and closed below all my key support levels, including the 60 EMA (medium term trend line). The volume that was absent from the market the last month or so finally came back on Friday, and it was in the form of selling + put volume.
