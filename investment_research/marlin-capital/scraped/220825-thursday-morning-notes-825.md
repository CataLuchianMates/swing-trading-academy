# Thursday Morning Notes 8.25

Technical Analysis

The indices closed higher for the first time in 3 days yesterday. For the QQQ, the $311-$313 region marked the low of the day once again, acting as support for the 3rd straight day. Now, we are heading into Thursday with the market set to gap higher.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!PxJQ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcc7ec86e-0b73-4ddc-84eb-c12417eb1ac0_880x662.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!PxJQ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcc7ec86e-0b73-4ddc-84eb-c12417eb1ac0_880x662.jpeg)

Key resistance levels to watch ahead include the $317-$319 region, where the QQQ has struggled to stay above. A close above the 21 EMA ($318) level would be ideal for bulls. Keep in mind Fed Chair Powell speaks at Jackson Hole tomorrow, so we may be in for another choppy day as investors & traders await tomorrow’s news.
