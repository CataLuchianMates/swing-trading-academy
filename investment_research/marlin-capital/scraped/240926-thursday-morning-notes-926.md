# Thursday Morning Notes 9.26

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Rangebound action again yesterday with the S&P 500 finishing -0.19% at closing.

Tech showed modest outperformance (NDX +0.14%) with semis (NVDA/AMD/INTC) leading the way higher.  We are poised for a big gap up today in Tech/Semis on the backs of the MU earnings print.  MU is +17% pre-market after a strong beat/guide above print with F1Q revs guided +12% QoQ.

MU is my top watch for today for an [earnings gapper setup](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-earnings-gapper-trade-setup?utm_source=publication-search).  This stock is back in play after a terrific quarter and has plenty of room to run, in my view, to $130+.  I’ll be looking for a day 1 entry today and depending on how it acts, a possible entry in the coming days.

NMM – Another big day here yesterday and we are now up over +15% from our $55.5-$55 entries. The increasing volume on green days is extremely bullish.  I am moving my trailing stop to $57.46.
