# Tech Wreck

It was an ugly day for US stocks with Tech leading the way to the downside. The QQQ closed -1.36%.

Growth and AI names took the biggest beating. Goldman’s High Beta Momentum Basket (GRPRHIMO) is now down -13% since August 11th (and down over -10% in the last 5 sessions).

**META**

Another former leader. We exited our META trade for a -2.6% loss as the stock closed below its 21 EMA.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!eHT_!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff0253780-6fcc-4ab3-8f80-950c8772d800_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!eHT_!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff0253780-6fcc-4ab3-8f80-950c8772d800_1396x653.png)

This ended up being a false breakout for META. Not a good sign.

In [“Market of Stocks”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/market-of-stocks), I wrote this about the META trade setup:

> “it’s one the leading Mega Caps, so if this setup fails it would likely come alongside a greater market correction.”

This setup failed, and it looks like it’s coming alongside a greater market correction.

**AMD**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!P671!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fca73f42f-6cd5-4d8a-ae5d-c5cf253eb3c6_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!P671!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fca73f42f-6cd5-4d8a-ae5d-c5cf253eb3c6_1396x653.png)

We exited our trade for a -6.6% loss.

**ALAB**

This was a particularly painful trade for us because ALAB came within 53 cents of hitting my first target at $200 (hitting $199.47). Typically, I look to take 1/3rd (or 1/4th) profits at my first target. We missed my first sell by mere cents before the stock reversed hard.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lbwU!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fee524ad9-808e-41ea-9297-43a6b814f950_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lbwU!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fee524ad9-808e-41ea-9297-43a6b814f950_1396x653.png)

Hindsight is 20/20. Definitely a frustrating way for this to play out. Today was a sell signal candle for me. In hindsight, I still should have a gotten a 1/3rd sell in at $197-$199 after the stock began reversing off $199.47. It is not worth obsessing over 1-2%. Complacency is a killer. The market is always humbling/teaching us.

**Big Picture Thoughts**

So far, we have a false breakout in the QQQ and concerning action in leading names. In my view, we could see more selling pressure ahead. We are now 100% cash in the Active Trading Portfolio.

For now, I believe caution is warranted here. I see the potential for a deeper drop if Powell’s Jackson Hole speech fails to calm the markets on Friday.
