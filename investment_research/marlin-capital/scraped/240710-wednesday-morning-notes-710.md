# Wednesday Morning Notes 7.10

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Another choppy session for the market indices on Tuesday with the S&P 500 and NDX both closing +0.07% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Oz7G!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F556502b1-e631-404d-a87e-94e3508ca4a4_1206x891.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Oz7G!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F556502b1-e631-404d-a87e-94e3508ca4a4_1206x891.jpeg)

9.5B shares traded across all US equity exchanges yesterday vs 11.5B YTD daily avg (down -17%).  Most traders seem to be awaiting tomorrow’s CPI and the first tranche of Q2 earnings about to begin.

Fed Chair Powell repeated a similar message to the recent Sintra remarks (disinflation process in underway, employment remains strong, etc.). The market is currently pricing in a 75% probability of a September rate cut and now just over 2 cuts before year end.  Tomorrow’s CPI number may shift those probabilities.

Updates on my trades –

NVO – Chopping around the $140 region now after the Biden/Sander OpEd deflated the breakout momentum.  I’m letting this play out with my shares and hoping this proves to be just a shakeout, but my closing stop is set at $138.

MSFT – Reversal day for MSFT yesterday and with earnings coming up in ~2 weeks, it is possible this pre-earnings run could be running out of steam.  I am moving my closing stop to $455 to ensure at least a small gain on this trade.

ORCL – Reversal day for ORCL yesterday and I sold my position near breakeven with the close below $141.  Position closed.

AVGO – Small pullback in AVGO yesterday, but everything still looks good here.

Why sell when Pelosi is buying?

AMD – Healthy pullback/consolidation day yesterday after a big 2 day rally.  Overall yesterday’s action is bullish to me and my first targets remain $185-$187
