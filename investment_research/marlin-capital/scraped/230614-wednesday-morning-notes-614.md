# Wednesday Morning Notes 6.14

Technical Update

The S&P 500 rally continued yesterday with the index finishing +0.69%.

We head into today’s FOMC Interest Rate Decision with a very extended and overbought setup on the daily chart. The Fed Funds Futures market is pricing in a 93% probability of a Fed pause today.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Fga7!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa55ab9c8-3ca5-4738-9883-3564c7c91dae_1167x486.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Fga7!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa55ab9c8-3ca5-4738-9883-3564c7c91dae_1167x486.png)

In every meeting since 2009, the Fed has done exactly what the market was expecting it to do. There were no surprises. I do not expect Powell to surprise the markets today, either, as this is not the way he has operated as Fed Chair.

ARKK and IWO - I remain long into today’s FOMC decision, now holding 3/4ths of each position after both ETFs hit my initial price targets.

For ARKK, my next target and profit taking point is $45.

For IWO, my next target and profit taking point is $245.

Stop losses on both positions remain at breakeven and I will let these trades play out from here.
