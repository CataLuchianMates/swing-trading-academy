# Thursday Morning Notes 6.29

Technical Update

The S&P 500 finished flat yesterday (-0.04%) after rallying back above its 8 EMA on Tuesday.  Technically, this action remains constructive as we head into the final 2 days of Q2.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!su_a!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdc4499b6-61d7-4f78-ae72-88c20d612f7f_959x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!su_a!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdc4499b6-61d7-4f78-ae72-88c20d612f7f_959x726.jpeg)

The SPX found support just above its 21 EMA and is now back above all key moving averages.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $4390-$4400 and YTD highs ($4440-$4450).

Key support levels to watch below include $4360 and $4320-$4330.

MDB – this long setup triggered yesterday on the move above $397.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!SBDb!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F513af31b-020b-4aaf-8202-718c79b7ecc8_1181x891.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!SBDb!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F513af31b-020b-4aaf-8202-718c79b7ecc8_1181x891.jpeg)

Not an ideal close for MDB after rallying above $400, but I will let this trade work.  Stop is placed below $380 (-4.3% risk) and it is out of my hands now.  My first target is $430-$435.

NU – I like this name fundamentally and the chart is setting up nicely for a new long trade.  The stock found support at the 21 EMA and bounced right back to make new highs.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yvwS!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb9a79c27-1828-4a57-b049-5a861f2b6eb7_958x730.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yvwS!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb9a79c27-1828-4a57-b049-5a861f2b6eb7_958x730.jpeg)

Similar close to MDB yesterday, but I am okay with that as it is re-testing the breakout area and giving us a better entry opportunity today.  I plan to enter NU at today’s open.  Stop will be placed below $7.33.  First target = $8.5, second target = $10.

GTLB – strong action in this software name after a big earnings report and guidance.  Great action here with the stock holding its 8 EMA on this pullback while volatility contracts.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!i0Cu!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fba022dcb-9618-4ae7-8fc8-511bb5ea3cd5_971x729.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!i0Cu!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fba022dcb-9618-4ae7-8fc8-511bb5ea3cd5_971x729.jpeg)

Long entry will be on a break above $50.  Stop will be placed below $48 (-4% risk).  I’m targeting a move back to recent highs ($54) and later, YTD highs ($58).
