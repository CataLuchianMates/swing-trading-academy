# Friday Morning Notes 12.15

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks continued their rally yesterday with the S&P 500 hitting new highs for the year ($4738).  Under the surface, the squeeze continued in the most short pockets of the market.  Yesterday’s biggest outperformers included Most Short Rolling, Liquid Most Short, and Non-Profitable Tech.

Positioning wise – I took off more of my TNA long exposure at yesterday’s open (+30%) and am now holding the remaining 20% of my original position.  My stop loss on the final portion will remain a close below $31.50.  As long as this uptrend continues, I will continue to hold my final 20% portion of TNA while simultaneously raising stops when appropriate.

PATH broke through recent flag highs yesterday and continues to act constructively.  I am long from 2 separate entries ($23.93 & $24.67).  My closing stop will remain below $22.94 and I will continue to let this stock work.  My initial target is $27.

Now plans for me to add any additional exposure today.
