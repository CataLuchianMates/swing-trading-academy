# My Top 4 Stars of Q2 Earnings

The market rollercoaster continued last week with the SPX +0.9%, NDX +0.8%, and RTY +2.9%. This recent market bounceback has now fully erased the early August dip, pushing the SPX back towards ATHs.

### What’s Driving The Bounceback?

**Looming September Rate Cut + WAY Stronger Than Expected Q2 Earnings**

Last week was a whirlwind. First, we had a super bullish reaction to CPI, which triggered a massive 2-day breadth expansion. As I highlighted in [“Everything Rally”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/everything-rally), this was the best 2-day breadth for the market since April

Historically, this is a very bullish signal for forward returns.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0usE!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F036d7a8a-a199-48aa-ab59-ff488fd6b4c2_896x678.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0usE!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F036d7a8a-a199-48aa-ab59-ff488fd6b4c2_896x678.png)

Small and Mid Caps (the most rate sensitive names) led the charge as September rate cut odds spiked above 80% (per [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com/event/fed-decision-in-september)).

Later in the week, a hotter than expected PPI report on Thursday sparked a selloff, with Sept. rate cut dipping to now 72%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jT6i!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3793b4a3-a431-42c3-a587-8b30ed6c5651_1284x1624.jpeg)](https://polymarket.com/event/fed-decision-in-september)

**Powell’s Jackson Hole speech is this Friday.**

The market is currently pricing in a September rate cut. As mentioned, Small and Mid Caps (the most rate sensitive pockets), rallied hard post-CPI and Sept rate cut odds are now at 72%%.

This is why I view Powell’s speech on Friday as the biggest risk to our current uptrend. If Powell’s speech is hawkish and September rate cut odds fall significantly (below 50%), the market (and particularly Rate Sensitive pockets) will face pressure.

#### **Q2 Earnings Have CRUSHED Estimates**

With 84% of the S&P 500 market cap having reported, Q2 has been marked by one of the highest frequency of earnings beats EVER.

60% of companies have beaten consensus EPS forecasts by >1 Std dev, the highest rate in 25 years of data (outside of 2009 and the Covid re-opening).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OtXN!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F49f0028a-d919-47a1-9c56-0c54ca333c30_1260x894.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OtXN!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F49f0028a-d919-47a1-9c56-0c54ca333c30_1260x894.jpeg)

EPS is currently growing +11% YoY vs consensus of +4%. This is a **HUGE** overall beat. Analysts were expecting a big deceleration from +12% YoY in Q1 to +4% YoY in Q2.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vBK_!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5e62ed48-9458-4696-8339-643ea447d388_1270x1029.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vBK_!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5e62ed48-9458-4696-8339-643ea447d388_1270x1029.jpeg)

As you can see, no real deceleration played out.

Forward looking guidance has also been stronger than average. 58% of SPX companies this quarter raised their FY EPS guidance, twice the 29% rate we saw in Q1.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gjJt!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3037d85d-e74f-4b7f-b0c1-b194fc4bd3e2_1289x889.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!gjJt!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3037d85d-e74f-4b7f-b0c1-b194fc4bd3e2_1289x889.jpeg)

Q2 has been no different. The market is rewarding leadership. **Below are my Core 4 stocks to own from Q2 earnings season:**

**These are the 4 names I believe are the strongest coming out of Q2 earnings.** Their combination of fundamental strength, technical breakouts, and high demand make them each core holdings for the weeks/months ahead.

We are currently positioned in all 4 across our [SMID Cap 6](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-smid-caps-with-massive-upside-potential), Active Trading Portfolio, and Top ‘25 AI Picks.

**NBIS**

We are long NBIS, a standout in our [SMID Cap 6 portfolio](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-smid-caps-with-massive-upside-potential). NBIS is proving why it’s 1 of the top stocks to own in the entire market. The stock has surged to new ATHs after Q2 results showcased +625% YoY revenue growth.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!3lHe!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb82c35c1-c38e-4a1e-9936-8144eb1cf82b_1132x652.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!3lHe!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb82c35c1-c38e-4a1e-9936-8144eb1cf82b_1132x652.png)

Most importantly, Nebius is in the process of securing more than 1 GW of power by the end of 2026 (from est ~220 MW by the end of 2025). Currently, they are guiding for $1B in ARR at 220 MW. **Crunching the numbers, at 1 GW, ARR would likely be $4B+** (conservatively). This would be a +4x in revenue.

With continued strong execution, we are looking at a sure-fire multi-bagger in the making here. This is still great long-term value at a $17B market cap.

APLD, another [SMID Cap 6](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-smid-caps-with-massive-upside-potential) name, has been a clear standout post-Q2 earnings.

2. Applied Digital’s CEO revealed that they are currently in advanced negotiations with another investment-grade hyperscaler for a large-scale data center campus beyond Polaris Forge One. If this deal closes, revenue could further skyrocket to $1B+ per year.

On the weekly chart, APLD has plenty of room to $20+. ATHs are at $34+.

The company announced Q2 revenue grew +150% YoY(vs +125% YoY consensus, an 11% rev beat), and an acceleration from last quarter’s 144% growth. Non-GAAP Gross Margins came in at 76% vs cons 74%. For Q3, they guided for $203-$210M in rev vs $180M cons (a +15% guidance raise) and GMs guided 75% vs cons 73.6%.

On the weekly chart, ALAB has broken out of an 7 month base. I see room to $240+ here.

**My Top 2 Setups For This Week:**

As mentioned, we are already positioned in my top 4 + META (my top Mega Cap) and AMD. I’m still seeing some great setups, but there is more risk present this week with Powell’s Jackson Hole speech on Friday. In any case, below are my top 2 setups to watch this week:

**SOFI**

SOFI was an early leader from Q2 earnings but has since pulled back following its secondary offering. A secondary offering will often kill a stock’s momentum, but when it doesn’t (and the post-offering dip is bought right up), it can be even more bullish as it shows strong demand even in the face of bad news.

This is what we are seeing here with this SOFI setup. The stock filled its earnings gap following the offering, but this dip has been bought right up. Friday was SOFI’s highest close post-Q2 earnings.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!V0RG!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F07d7c948-57fe-4f61-8e31-190869c6e2e6_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!V0RG!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F07d7c948-57fe-4f61-8e31-190869c6e2e6_1396x653.png)

SOFI is my top setup for this week. I see $30+ coming here if the market can stay stable.

**FTAI**

FTAI Aviation is a leading provider of aircraft leasing and engine services. Q2 revenue grew +52% YoY, net income grew +175% YoY, and the company raised FY guidance.

Technically, this is shaping up beautifully for FTAI stock. We had a strong Day 1 earnings move, which has been followed by volume contracting and price getting tight.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!viHu!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F331d1c1a-71db-4275-aa41-12c855997919_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!viHu!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F331d1c1a-71db-4275-aa41-12c855997919_1396x653.png)
