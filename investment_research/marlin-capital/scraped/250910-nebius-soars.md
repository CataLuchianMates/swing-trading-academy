From a MW monetization standpoint, the Microsoft deal was significantly better than our projections. We are therefore raising our projections on Nebius and expect the stock to see $200+ in 2026.

Here’s why 👇👇👇

[Just over a month ago](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/slugfest?utm_source=publication-search), I outlined a conservative revenue projection for NBIS ($4B+) based on their existing MW monetization rate ($4.55M per MW at 1 GW).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Q5HR!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc1bbb8ef-4f21-4255-97b5-ec3acc203e08_470x290.webp)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Q5HR!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc1bbb8ef-4f21-4255-97b5-ec3acc203e08_470x290.webp)

The deal with Microsoft implies a much higher MW monetization rate.

The official press release does not provide a specific MW capacity associated with the NBIS + MSFT deal. But, we can estimate based off public info. Multiple sources published that Nebius was developing a **300 MW** Data Center in Vineland, NJ. This suggests the Data Center’s total planned capacity, but it does not specify how much of this capacity is allocated to the Microsoft deal.

Being that this MSFT deal is so massive ($17.4B), it is fair to assume that Microsoft will utilize most (if not all) of this 300 MW Data Center capacity. For our projections, let’s say all of it.

If the 300 MW Vineland Data Center is used fulfill the Microsoft contract, it will generate $3.48B annually.

**That’s $11.6M per MW per year.**

This is significantly higher than my pre-deal estimate of $4.55M per MW, indicating a much more lucrative monetization rate.

**Following the Microsoft deal, Nebius will have achieved ~$4.5B in ARR with only 520 MW, instead of the projected 1,000 MW.**

This means that NBIS would need ~480 MW more to reach their 1,000 MW target. The deal’s momentum suggests they are on pace to secure this much earlier than expected. And if they do so at a similar rate to this deal, we could have at least 1 more MSFT-like deal ($3.5B annually) to add. This would take us to $7B+ in annual revenue.

Again, my original price target for NBIS was $100+ ($20b+ mkt cap) at $2-3B in annual revenue.
