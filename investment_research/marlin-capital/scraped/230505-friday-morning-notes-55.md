# Friday Morning Notes 5.5

Technical Update

The rangebound market continued yesterday with the S&P 500 pulling in to the 60 EMA support level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!SCpq!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0a491a9b-8eef-4bfd-a80c-4e27d7afb0dd_959x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!SCpq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0a491a9b-8eef-4bfd-a80c-4e27d7afb0dd_959x723.jpeg)

We are set for a small gap up this morning with AAPL (7% of SPX) beating on top and bottom line estimates.

We have spent the last month trading in this 100 point range in the SPX, frustrating both sides. As I wrote in yesterday’s morning notes, the index remains in “no man’s land” until some key support/resistance levels start to break decisively.

Key resistance level to watch above include $4100-$4110 (21 EMA), $4140-$4150 and $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).

Key support levels to watch below include $4056-$4060 (60 EMA), $4040-$4030 and $4000.
