# Thursday Market Thread 6.2

Technical Analysis

This rally attempt is seeing its first real test as the QQQ has reached the bottom of its 15 minute channel.

Key support levels below include the 21 EMA at $304 (held today), the $301-$300 region, $296, and the $293-$293 region.

If we break upwards through $309, I believe we can see $313, $319, and the 50 day at $326. I would likely look to play this by entering TQQQ shares.
