**GLW**

It was a massive day for Corning today on HUGE news.

Nvidia and Corning announced a multi-year commercial and technology partnership to dramatically expand US-based manufacturing of advanced optical connectivity for AI data centers. The deal is massive in scope. 👇

* Under the deal, Nvidia is investing 500M dollars via an equity‑linked package.
* 10x increase in US optical connectivity manufacturing capacity.
* 50% expansion in fiber production capacity.

GLW soared to new ATHs off this news, surging as much as +21% intraday before closing +12% on the day. This is exactly the type of price action you see when a company gets fundamentally re-rated by Wall Street in real-time.
