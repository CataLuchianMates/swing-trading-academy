**Another Day of Near Record Call Buying for AAPL:**

AAPL - Apple options on track for another 3M contract day as shares hit new highs. Shares of the world's most valuable company (2.6T mktcap vs MSFT 2.57T) are up $1.17 this morning near $159 following a $4 climb on Thursday and are up 6% this week. Over a million contracts have traded in the first 75 minutes, suggesting another 3M contract day is likely after the 3.9M contracts traded yesterday, a one-year record (all time record 5.1M traded in Sep 2020). More than half today's flow are Nov contracts that will expire in 5 hours, including 200k Nov 160 calls.
