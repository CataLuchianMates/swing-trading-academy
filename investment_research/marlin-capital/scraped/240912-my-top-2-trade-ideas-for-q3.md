**My Top 2 Trade Setups for Q3**

Long AVO –

This is along the same lines of SN and CAVA with this trade idea.  We saw a huge revenue beat for Mission Produce in Q3 ($324M vs cons $231M, a 40% beat!).  The stock responded with a +21.6% gain on Earnings Day 1 on the highest volume day the stock has traded in the last year (11x avg daily volume).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9BmZ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F98502bb9-f697-4027-99e1-ea5044bf9d8a_1112x650.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9BmZ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F98502bb9-f697-4027-99e1-ea5044bf9d8a_1112x650.png)

Day 2 followed it up with the 2nd highest volume day of the last year (behind only Day 1).  Volume was also elevated yesterday (Day 3), with the stock having its 4th highest volume day of the last year.

On the Weekly chart, this week is already the highest volume week in the stock’s history (IPOed 3 years ago), and we still have 1 more trading day left in the week.  I see room to $16 here on the weekly.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!C64A!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc7fd6699-949b-4ea6-843e-17b9b839e6fd_1107x651.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!C64A!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc7fd6699-949b-4ea6-843e-17b9b839e6fd_1107x651.png)

$16 would be a +20% gain from here.  I will be entering long AVO tomorrow, but I am mindful of similar action occurring in AVO here as we saw in SN and CAVA (a sideways consolidation period before the next move up).

With that said, I don’t want to miss out on this trade if we just continue higher from here and don’t consolidate/pull in (which also happens plenty with high quality earnings gappers).  That is why I am planning to enter long tomorrow and will leave room to add more if we pullback smartly (below $13 but above $12, earnings day lows).

NMM –

I’m sticking with the Earnings trade theme here with this one, which are always my favorite long setups during earnings season.  In the past, [I have highlighted how I liked to trade these setups on Day 1](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-earnings-gapper-trade-setup?utm_source=publication-search), but these trades can often given even better trade setups in the following days (especially when the market is volatile/pulling back like in much of Q3).  Entering on later days allows you to fully analyze volume, which is what ultimately separates the best of the best on these setups.  When you have successive days of ATH/12M High volume, this is where I become very interested, as this is typically institutional buying after a big earnings beat/guidance raise.

With NMM, we saw the highest volume day of the last year following the company’s earnings beat and Buyback raise.  Volume remained elevated in the following 2 days with Top 10 volume days in the last 12M.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!usSY!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F827e28df-fc78-4ec2-8aab-fc991bb9c8ca_1106x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!usSY!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F827e28df-fc78-4ec2-8aab-fc991bb9c8ca_1106x653.png)

Then, we pulled back/consolidated while the market retreated.  Yesterday began the breakout to new highs (above >$55), coming on huge volume (3rd highest volume day of the last year, 2nd highest excluding Day 1 of Earnings).

It’s hard to see this breakout failing and NMM not continuing higher to $60+ from here (barring a broad market pullback post-FOMC next week).  I will be planning to enter NMM long at tomorrow’s open, with room to add if we re-test the $55 breakout level and $53.7 resistance level.  My stop loss will be below $50.86.
