Futures are surging this morning following the lighter than expected Core CPI number. As I talked about in “The Week Ahead”, this was the most important CPI report in a long time as investors had become increasingly anxious about re-accelerating inflation. The Bond market had become especially anxious with 10 YR yields rising to 52 week highs, which appeared to be the main culprit behind the recent selloff in the stock market.

Today’s lighter than expected Core CPI number is a huge relief for the stock market and the bond market, and we are seeing US index futures surging as a result. After the recent selloff, a number of potential long setups are catching my eye now that we have a positive catalyst and sentiment rebounding.
