With the QQQ breaking down from its Bear Flag yesterday, I have added some short exposure in my Active Portfolio. As I have stressed, this is a treacherous environment for equities, and all traders should keep that in mind when sizing into positions (especially shorts). I only plan to potentially add to my DOCU and XPEV shorts on bounces.

Big picture, I do not plan on buying in my IRA until the QQQ comes down to its 200 EMA. All major corrections since 2009 have come back to this area, and I do not expect this time to be any exception. For long term investors, I believe patience will continue to pay off here.
