## David Marlin Q&A (Comments)

**Q:** is it because you think its a low volume days

**David Marlin:** Basically, I am keeping an open mind for what may come next.  That means I’m just following price and my trend indicators.  Thus far, today’s bounce is encouraging, but we are still far from out of the woods.  If you look at ARKK, it is still clearly in a downtrend, and today could simply be an oversold bounce as the RSI slipped below 30.  I will still need to see a follow through and confirmation for me to be confident enough to take the hedge off & add long exposure. Again, I’d rather be late than too early here. When the market is in a correction, violent bounces from oversold conditions are the norm.  It’s too early to say a bottom is in place, in my opinion.
