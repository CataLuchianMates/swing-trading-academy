## David Marlin Q&A (Comments)

**Q:** Hello David. Thanks for another great note on the markets. Could you explain how you identify your "in the money" stops? Is there any particular reason why you put it at 42.43 for ARKK? Perhaps securing a % of gains?

**David Marlin:** Thank you.  For me it is a balancing act between analyzing the chart and key support levels as well as deciding how much profits I would be willing to give back on. 

In this case, ARKK made a low of $42.44 on June 9th and a low of $42.53 the next trading day (June 12th).  June 9th’s low was key as the level held before the next day’s inside candle and break higher. 

I considered June 8th lows as a stop ($41.90) as it is also a key technical level, but in my view, this would be giving too much back on the trade and would essentially stop me out at breakeven (+0.7%) if it were to hit.
