# Wednesday Morning Notes 7.6

Technical Analysis

Huge turnaround for the market yesterday after the morning gap down. For the QQQ, the key support levels ($276-$277) I discussed in yesterday’s morning notes held up. The LOD was $276.75. The QQQ was unable to meaningfully break down from the bottom of its 15 minute price range despite several attempts.

With yesterday’s action, we broke some key resistance levels (including the 8 EMA) and closed above the top of our range. The next key resistance level to watch is the 21 EMA in the $287-$288 region. So far, instead of the full retest of lows, we got a slightly higher low. If we can break through the 21 EMA resistance, the evidence supports a temporary low being in place for the indices. I would consider adding swing longs for a trade at that point.
