We are set for a gap down this morning after the weekend break. It has been a big run for the market off a slightly higher low, and now we have CPI data coming out on Wednesday. It is not the time to press long positions ahead of that report, in my view.

I am still holding the remaining half of my long position in TQQQ. I will be moving my stop loss on the rest of this position to $26.71, below Friday’s lows. This will ensure a gain if the market reverses early in the week.
