**APH**

Every stock has its own personality. APH is a calm, steady climber.

Below is the YTD chart of APH.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!IfT9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7df11014-faa6-4576-b4c3-563d0d59e809_1266x844.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!IfT9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7df11014-faa6-4576-b4c3-563d0d59e809_1266x844.png)

This APH uptrend all started on April 23rd following a tremendous earnings report. This is no surprise, as most of the top uptrends start with an earnings/news catalyst. This is why I always emphasize how critical earnings/news catalysts are for a stock.

We originally traded APH back in April, buying it on Day 3 post-earnings (4/25). It was one of the cleanest trends in the entire market, as the stock surfed its 21 EMA higher for months. It was a stress-free hold for us. We essentially held quarter to quarter and closed out right before Q2 earnings for +39%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6UKl!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbe28b618-d220-40c4-b877-fa971655f48d_1137x752.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6UKl!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbe28b618-d220-40c4-b877-fa971655f48d_1137x752.png)

Since our exit back in July, APH never looked back.

Then came the November SPX correction. With the market weak, APH finally had its first real shakeout since the April uptrend began.

The stock pulled back in an orderly fashion to its 60 EMA, where it formed a daily hammer/bullish reversal candle. This has since been followed up by a quick recovery back above the 21 EMA (and all key EMAs including 8 EMA & 5 EMA).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OnP3!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa8226d09-f514-4598-9cf5-f08c817f4443_1266x844.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OnP3!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa8226d09-f514-4598-9cf5-f08c817f4443_1266x844.png)

For me, this is textbook leading stock behavior. Yesterday, we rebought APH at $138.81.

My target on this move is $160+.
