# Breaking Out

Huge day for the market today with the S&P 500 finally breaking out of its recent 1+ month trading range.

Today was a textbook gap and go breakout with a strong close. As I mentioned this morning in [“Clearing Event”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/clearing-event), the catalyst for the move higher was the de-escalation of the Middle East conflicts. The price action in the equity and oil markets told this story yesterday and this morning.

But make no mistake, **this Bull Market is all about Artificial Intelligence.** As I highlighted in Monday’s report [“Wall of Worry”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/wall-of-worry), Goldman’s AI Basket has already broken out to new all-time highs ahead of the indices.

In my view, **this Bull Market will continue to be all about AI.**

Bigger picture, as I have recently highlighted, **my target for the $SPX is $7300+** during this AI led Bull Run.
