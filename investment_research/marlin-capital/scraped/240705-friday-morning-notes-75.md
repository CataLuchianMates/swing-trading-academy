# Friday Morning Notes 7.5

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500 set another ATH on Wednesday’s half day with Tech leading the way higher once again.

As I’ve highlighted in recent newsletters, the first 2 weeks of July are the best 2 weeks of the calendar year for US stocks historically (despite lower volumes).  We are seeing this phenomenon play out once again here in 2024.

Updates on my trades –

NVO – The Bernie Sanders/Biden OpEd on Weight Loss drugs killed the momentum here as we saw a big 2-day drawdown in NVO.  With that said, we didn’t hit my closing stop as we closed at $138.87 on Wednesday.  I will be selling my Sep $140 calls this morning however as it’s still a ~+20% gain from my entry and the momentum has faded thanks to the news flow.  I will be holding my shares with the same $138 stop and letting it play out.

AVGO – Sometimes you get bad luck with the news flow and sometimes you get good luck.  In NVO, we got bad luck with the Bernie/Biden OpEd.  In AVGO, we got good luck with Nancy Pelosi disclosing a nearly $5M buy in AVGO Jun ’25 $800 calls.

It’s always great news when Nancy Pelosi buys one of your stocks.  The stock responded to the news with a >4% rally on Wednesday.  We can see $1800-$1850 on this runup for AVGO.

MSFT – Hitting new ATHs this morning and no reason to do anything but hold here.  We have earnings coming up in ~2 weeks in the name but it looks like we are setting up for a rally into the EPS report.

ORCL – Hit new ATHs this morning and we look headed to $150 here.  No changes to my long position.
