# The Week Ahead

Daily Market Commentary – Mid-Afternoon Monday Update

We are starting off April with a choppy day so far in the major indices, which is a continuation of last week’s action.  This morning’s gap up was promptly filled and then some, and the major indices are all negative as of 1:35 PM EST.  Small Caps are the weakest of the major indices with RTY currently down around ~1.1% for the day.

Historically, April is one of the strongest months for the S&P 500.  With that said, we have traditionally seen a mid-April dip during tax day/week.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EXEV!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F03e1c918-2be4-47de-80a2-4fb0e0000ef5_962x751.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EXEV!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F03e1c918-2be4-47de-80a2-4fb0e0000ef5_962x751.jpeg)

We are entering the corporate blackout period for most of the S&P 500, which will be a potential headwind for large cap stocks this month.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yhQ5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8a1b1df8-10ec-47d8-ad27-40c91ef6a027_964x418.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!yhQ5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8a1b1df8-10ec-47d8-ad27-40c91ef6a027_964x418.jpeg)

It may be a bigger headwind than you think. Since the year 2000, US corporations have, on net, bought back $5.5T of stock.  This has amounted to more demand than any other market participant, and it’s not even close.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zB0l!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F96db16f9-d7f6-443b-ad14-9eb73205a3c8_1134x672.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zB0l!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F96db16f9-d7f6-443b-ad14-9eb73205a3c8_1134x672.jpeg)

With Large Caps in their buyback blackout period, this would be an opportune time for Small Caps to outperform.  We saw RTY have its highest weekly close since January of 2022 last week.  Small Caps are not off to a strong start this week, though, as RTY is forming a bearish reversal candle on the daily chart to kick off this week.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qoWh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F73c39fa8-706d-4e1b-8678-88f2addfcbff_1107x645.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qoWh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F73c39fa8-706d-4e1b-8678-88f2addfcbff_1107x645.png)

With that said, I am still bullish on Small Caps for April and will look for this dip to be bought up.  The weekly chart for RTY still looks strong with support towards $2100.  I will be re-assessing the technicals at today’s close, but I am most likely planning to go long RTY Futures at some point during/after this dip.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!D4LY!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F290cdfe8-d95a-414a-ad9a-0f69f3e0ab70_1109x644.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!D4LY!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F290cdfe8-d95a-414a-ad9a-0f69f3e0ab70_1109x644.png)

COIN – Pulling back to the 21 EMA on the daily.  I am planning to hold this position as long as the stock and Bitcoin (as well as the major indices) are in an uptrend.  Bitcoin broke $71k last night, but is now trading below $68.5k this afternoon.  We may see a larger pullback in COIN because off this, but I am still bullish on COIN and Bitcoin for 2024 and expect that this dip will be bought up.  Technically, I believe Bitcoin can see $85k in Q2.

CAVA – Ugly daily candle already today with a big reversal off $71.6.  This is a sell signal for me and I will take the small loss.

MU – Big day so far to kick off this week.  Currently up +6% on the day and traded as high as $127.38 this morning.  Today’s move has come on big volume so far, and I believe this stock is showing all the characteristics of a larger move.  My first target is $130, bigger picture target $150.

One more interesting note - everyone is expecting a market correction (-10%+) over the next 6 months.

According to JPMs Investor Survey, 82% of market participants view the risk of an equity market correction over the next 6 month’s as “high”. When everyone is expecting the same thing to happen, it very often does not play out that way.
