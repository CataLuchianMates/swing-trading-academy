**The Small Picture**

Short-term, there is definitely some risk that this could turn into a multi-day pullback. As mentioned above, we are coming off the best 2-week stretch for High Beta Momentum in 4+ years. The retracement of these massive upside moves (particularly higher beta) are often multi-day ordeals. They can be fast and painful.

With that in mind, I think it is a time to play defense instead of offense. When we get the first signs of a potential multi-day retracement after a huge run, I think its best to protect gains instead of pushing the gas looking for more.

We are currently holding MP and CRWV as our 2 longs in the Active Portfolio, with no plans to add any new positions tomorrow. My expectation is that many names on my watchlist will follow scenario 2 highlighted above, and pull in/tighten up along the right side of their bases. If that is the case, we will continue stalking these setups and will be ready to strike when the time comes.

**The Big Picture**

Big picture, nothing changes for me. I view today/yesterday as normal retracement/give back action after a huge rally. Remember, stocks don’t go up in a straight line, even in a Bull Market.

2 steps forward, 1 step back. 2 steps forward, 1 step back. Repeat.
