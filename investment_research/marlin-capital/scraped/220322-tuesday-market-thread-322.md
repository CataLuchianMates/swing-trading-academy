Marlin Capital Strategy & Watchlist:

As my subscribers know, I use technical analysis to try to determine probabilities and the most likely outcomes.  Evidence is mounting that we are currently in a bounce phase, especially in the general market.  The S&P 500 is showing relative strength versus the Nasdaq, while the VIX is now under 25, indicating less volatile daily movements.

A more calm market opens the door for potential swing long opportunities in my Active Portfolio.  I am focused on those leading stocks which showed relative strength during the market sell off.

My watchlist for today, in no particular order:

1. VET
2. FANG
3. CVE
4. UNP
