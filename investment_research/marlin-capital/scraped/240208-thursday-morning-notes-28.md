# Thursday Morning Notes 2.8

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500 closed +0.82% yesterday after briefly eclipsing $5000 earlier in the day.  NDX was +1% (led by Meta +3.3%) alongside some familiar quality leaders within software and semis.

PLTR – I haven’t always liked trading this name in the past, but this trade has worked out perfectly so far.

If you followed this trade idea from Tuesday, I took some profits at +10% ($22.56) and +15% ($23.58) yesterday.  The weekly chart looks great with volume nearing ATHs already (with 2 days left in the week).  A larger move to $30 is my longer-term target.

NXT – Heads up if you followed this inside 30 earnings gapper trade that I took last week.  I am moving my stop to breakeven ($53.81) on this trade.  I am playing some defense here to protect against any loss on this trade.  A break below $53.81 will a lower high and a lower low on the 30 minute chart, breaking the uptrend from earnings day.
