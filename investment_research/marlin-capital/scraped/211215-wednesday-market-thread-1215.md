**Watchlist:**

None. I will be closely watching the FOMC meeting results and more importantly, the market’s reaction. If we get a flush today prior to the meeting, I will cover some more ARKK short as we will be going into the meeting oversold.

I would not be surprised to see a bounce following the FOMC results, but if the outcome is the Fed getting more hawkish, it will likely just be a dead cat bounce before we see more downside in the coming weeks.
