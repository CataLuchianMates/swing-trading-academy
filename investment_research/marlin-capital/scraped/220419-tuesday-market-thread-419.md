Marlin Capital Active Strategy:

They’re not making it easy for traders on either side (l/s) right now.  There remains a high degree of choppiness and intraday volatility across the market.  Continue to keep this in mind when position sizing.  It is not the time to be a hero.

My active plan is to remain short ARKK and potentially add to this position on a larger bounce, as I see the retest of recent lows as highly likely at this point.  I also plan to add 1-2 long positions (in the commodities/energy space) to add some l/s balance to my Active Portfolio.

As a side note- if you’re finding the intraday volatility challenging to hold through, consider reducing your position sizing (until you are comfortable).  Then, step away from the laptop/phone.  As my subscribers know, I am not a day trader/scalper, so I purposely do not watch every tick in the market.  For my strategy, I believe this would make anyone a worse trader and more prone to emotional mistakes.
