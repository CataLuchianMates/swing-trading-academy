# Wednesday Morning Notes 2.28

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Tuesday was another session of choppy price action in the indices with the S&P 500 closing +17bps, NDX +21bps, and R2K leading the way +1.33%.

Non-Profitable Tech rallied +2.3% while Mega Caps were +0.26%.  Bitcoin and the broader crypto complex is becoming increasingly In focus with BTC crossing $60k.

Speaking of crypto plays, I locked in another 25% profits in my CLSK long at yesterday's open (+35%!).  I am now holding the final 1/4th of my position into Wednesday.
