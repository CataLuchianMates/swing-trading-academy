# Tuesday Morning Notes 11.29

Technical Analysis

The market pulled back to start off the week yesterday, with the SPX losing the key $4000 support level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BNVq!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2fc2fd6d-93ce-46d0-855b-8346b486a363_1191x888.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!BNVq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2fc2fd6d-93ce-46d0-855b-8346b486a363_1191x888.jpeg)

Next up in the way of support is $3950, $3910-$3920, and $3880.

Look for previous support ($4000) to turn into resistance if the indices can muster up a turnaround. If the $4000 resistance level breaks, $4020 and $4050-$4060 are the next resistance levels to watch.
