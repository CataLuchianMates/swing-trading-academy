# Friday Morning Notes 6.30 

Technical Update

The S&P 500 finished higher (+0.46%) on Thursday as we head into the final day of June and Q2.

The technical setup here remains bullish, and we are set for a gap up in the indices this morning.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $4400 and YTD highs ($4440-$4450).

Key support levels to watch below include $4360 and $4320-$4330.

Today, like yesterday, is likely to be a low volume/illiquid trading day as traders take off for the long weekend into the July 4th holiday. I am not planning to make any new moves today.
