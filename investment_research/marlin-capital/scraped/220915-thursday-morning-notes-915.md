# Thursday Morning Notes 9.15

Technical Analysis

US stocks finished slightly higher yesterday following Tuesday’s debacle. The technical structure remains highly bearish and points to a potential breakdown before the week ends.

For the QQQ, it is possible for this bounce to continue towards higher resistance levels. First up is the top of the 15 minute trend line ($297-$298).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cWgR!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3f8603ca-3c30-49dd-b6a3-71eeb04506e7_1396x669.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cWgR!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3f8603ca-3c30-49dd-b6a3-71eeb04506e7_1396x669.jpeg)

If this resistance level breaks, look for the QQQ to test the $300-$301 level before reversing. $305-$306 represents another potential resistance level that could come into play on a larger squeeze.

$290-$292 remains the key support level to watch below. A break below this support should open up the flood gates for a waterfall of selling.
