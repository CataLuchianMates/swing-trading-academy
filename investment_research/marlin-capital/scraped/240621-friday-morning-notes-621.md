# Friday Morning Notes 6.21

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks took a slight breather yesterday after making 31 new ATHs already this year. The SPX finished -0.25% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RwP0!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff2722ad5-8b38-4434-8746-74a443b79940_968x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RwP0!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff2722ad5-8b38-4434-8746-74a443b79940_968x724.jpeg)

Stocks faded throughout the session with tech leading the way lower. After 7 straight green closes for the NDX, a little pullback was due.

It felt like there was some broad-based profit taking across recent AI leaders (MU, AVGO, ARM, etc.). These stocks were all a bit extended coming into Thursday and nothing about yesterday’s price action was overly concerning when zooming out.

Updates on my trades -

NVO, MSFT, ORCL - No changes here. Still holding these positions with my closing stops in place.

DKS - Triggered long on the inside day break yesterday. Closing is below $223 for -2.3% risk.

ANF - Didn’t trigger long yesterday. No position here.

No new trades planned for today.
