# Thursday Morning Notes 9.12

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Big reversal day in the market yesterday off some early weakness with the SPX finishing +1.07% and NDX +2.17%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7WkC!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F84fe579d-81fd-4c29-8430-0d4cb8557caa_978x717.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7WkC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F84fe579d-81fd-4c29-8430-0d4cb8557caa_978x717.jpeg)

The NVDA keynote seemed to be the driving force across all of semis (and the market), with the NDX reversing 4% from lows thanks to NVDA’s +7% rally.

NVDA keynote - Despite the hype and buzz coming in, Jensen did not disappoint.  He spoke high level about (1) The trend toward densification and acceleration in Data Center, (2) evidence of instant customer ROI, (3) Nvidia’s competitive moat, (4) the agility of Nvidia’s Asia-centric supply chain, and (5) the ongoing Blackwell ramp.  His comments yesterday + MSFT’s comments Tuesday about demand for their infrastructure materially outpacing their ability to supply it seems to have reinvigorated the genAI trade (which had been dragging Tech/the market lower in recent weeks).

Goldman’s Democratic Policy Pair finished yesterday +3.8% vs. Republican Policy Pair -1.72% (following Tuesday’s debate).  Everything renewables related (which Harris favors) is heavily shorted and ripped causing an unwind.  We will see if this is just a knee jerk reaction to Tuesday’s debate or the start of a larger move.  Most betting markets currently have Harris/Trump odds to win in November around 50/50.

SN – Nice day yesterday hitting new 52 week highs.  I am looking to take another 1/3rd profits (leaving 1/3rd of my original position) at my $100 target.

My top trade setup for today –

CAVA – Acting well here post-earnings, holding the earnings day lows on the recent pullback.  Yesterday was an inside day for CAVA, giving us a nice low risk/high reward entry.
