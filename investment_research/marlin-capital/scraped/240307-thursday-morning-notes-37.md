# Thursday Morning Notes 3.7

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Bounce back session for the indices yesterday after some heavy selling pressure Tuesday.  The S&P 500 finished the day +0.51% while the QQQ rallied +0.63%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jEo6!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe6903a2a-6116-4c58-9bfd-f443bb8729b0_990x744.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!jEo6!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe6903a2a-6116-4c58-9bfd-f443bb8729b0_990x744.jpeg)

Tech was back at the forefront which seemed to fuel risk appetite and a bit of recovery from Tuesday’s losers: Bitcoin Sensitive Equities +9%, Meme Stocks +2.5%, Long Momentum +2.5%, China ADRs +1.9%, and Software +1.9%.

Elsewhere, there is a lot of noise about the Mag 7 dispersion.  Last year, the Mag 7 names essentially moved in unison and was the make break trade of 2023 for US stocks.  2 months into 2024, we are seeing dispersion in the group with only 4 of the 7 names in the green YTD (NVDA, META, MSFT, AMZN).  GOOGL, TSLA, and AAPL continue to fall farther into the negatives YTD.

Updates on my trades –

HIMS – Stopped out near yesterday’s open on the break below Tuesday’s lows at $13.91 for a +3.2% gain.  A little bit of a disappointing end to this trade but I’d always rather protect my gains than give them all back and end up taking a loss.  Position closed.
