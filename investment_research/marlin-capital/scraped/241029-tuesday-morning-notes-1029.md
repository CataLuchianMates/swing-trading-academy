**Daily Market Commentary:**

Calm before the storm type action to kick off the week yesterday with the SPX finishing +0.27%.

As expected, it was a quiet day with NDX volumes down -20% vs 20dma. Small Caps, popular shorts, and non-profitable tech all squeezed +1-3% on the day.

41% of the S&P market cap is set to report this week, headlined by GOOGL & META Tues, META & MSFT Wed, and AAPL & AMZN Thurs.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!utQP!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F06a41dc9-5f19-4325-9f30-d1797b18a8b2_1185x887.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!utQP!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F06a41dc9-5f19-4325-9f30-d1797b18a8b2_1185x887.jpeg)

This is a momentum killer and an unlucky break for a stock that looked primed to run to $100+. I’m moving my stop to $86 and expecting to take the small loss today. Unforeseen news events like this can happen, which is why you always need to be mindful of position sizing.
