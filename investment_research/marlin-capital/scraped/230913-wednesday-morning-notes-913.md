# Wednesday Morning Notes 9.13

August CPI +3.7% year/year vs. +3.6% est. & +3.2% prior

Core CPI +4.3% vs. +4.3% est. & +4.7% prior

US CPI has moved up from a recent low of 3.0% in June 2023 to 3.7% today.

Driving that increase is primarily higher energy prices and smaller YoY declines in the price of Gasoline and Fuel Oil.

Core CPI continues to trend lower, however, with declining YoY increases in Shelter being the biggest contributing factor.

Following this morning’s CPI report, the Fed Funds Futures market is still expecting the Fed to pause when it meets next week (>90% probability of holding rates at 5.25-5.50%).

Technical Update

The SPX struggled into this morning’s CPI report with the index dropping and closing below some key support levels.

Key support levels to watch below include $4460, $4420-$4430, $4400, and $4330-$4350.

I remain holding COUR and CLS into Wednesday’s session.
