# Thursday Morning Notes 9.8

Technical Analysis

The market rallied yesterday, with the QQQ closing right near the $300 resistance level. Now, there is a potential path for a rally to $305-$308.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!08_p!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F62b83bf8-6d46-4a7c-87ad-2b5ca5d33cd9_1013x619.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!08_p!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F62b83bf8-6d46-4a7c-87ad-2b5ca5d33cd9_1013x619.jpeg)

Last week, institutions bought a record amount of hedges. Institutional short covering episodes can last awhile and can be violent to the upside, as we know. If this squeeze starts to gain some momentum, institutions could continue to drive it even higher. Credit default swaps are also indicating more potential upside in stocks.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YFit!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3325e837-1355-4144-af85-d366c53bc690_772x674.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!YFit!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3325e837-1355-4144-af85-d366c53bc690_772x674.jpeg)

I will look to enter this rally via TQQQ if the market shows strength in today’s session. Key resistance levels to watch ahead are $300, $302-$303, and $305-$308. $294-$295 support should hold for the setup to be valid.
