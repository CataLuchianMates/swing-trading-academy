# Wednesday Morning Notes 7.5

Technical Update

I hope everyone had a great 4th of July Holiday!

The stock market is back open for a full trading day today, July 5th. Volumes should continue to be light today.

The S&P 500 finished the half day Monday (7/3) near flat, closing +0.12% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zcIe!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F47f4af82-d688-4a0a-953f-fcba337ef737_1207x889.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!zcIe!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F47f4af82-d688-4a0a-953f-fcba337ef737_1207x889.jpeg)

Technically, the setup remains bullish here with the index sitting right at YTD highs. We are currently in the best seasonal period historically for stocks (the first 2 weeks of July - see last Friday’s morning notes for more detail).

Many names on my watchlist continue to act well. I am currently holding 3 longs into Wednesday, 7/5. I am not planning to add any new longs until these positions start to gain a little more traction.

Current holdings:

Long MDB at $397

Stop at $380

Long NU at $7.88

Stop at $7.33

Long GTLB at $50

Stop at $47.99
