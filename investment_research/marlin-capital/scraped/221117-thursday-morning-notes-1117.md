# Thursday Morning Notes 11.17

Technical Analysis

On a technical basis, we have a possible topping structure forming on the daily chart. After such a large rally off lows, it is typically a process of topping rather than a sharp immediate reversal.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!twcx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F046cad9f-e769-425f-b2e8-f58a21d621dc_980x718.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!twcx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F046cad9f-e769-425f-b2e8-f58a21d621dc_980x718.jpeg)

We are set for a gap down this morning towards the $3900 support level. If this level breaks, it is critical for price to hold the 21 and 60 EMAs below ($3850).

Key Support Levels Below: $3900, $3850, $3800

Key Resistance Levels Ahead: $4000, $4030, $4060-$4080
