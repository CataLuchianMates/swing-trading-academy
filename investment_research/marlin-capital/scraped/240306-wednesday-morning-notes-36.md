# Wednesday Morning Notes 3.6

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Pretty clear “risk off” market yesterday with the SPX -1%, NDX -1.8%, and R2K -0.8%.  The SPX closed below its 8 EMA for the first time since Feb 21st.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2Tyg!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F55a3ab1b-58db-4631-804a-d65d8e16c120_1284x912.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2Tyg!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F55a3ab1b-58db-4631-804a-d65d8e16c120_1284x912.jpeg)

As you can see in the chart above, dips below the 8 EMA have been quickly bought up in the SPX since this uptrend began last November. We are set for a gap up this morning back above the 8 EMA (so far so good).

For the QQQ, we found support yesterday at the 21 EMA.  Any trips to/below the 21 EMA during this uptrend have been quickly bought up since this uptrend began last November.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!90fy!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F07919cc5-8444-4a91-a5d3-8e1779324e09_1212x888.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!90fy!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F07919cc5-8444-4a91-a5d3-8e1779324e09_1212x888.jpeg)

Updates on my trades-

UBER – stopped out here for a -0.6% loss as UBER succumbed to the market weakness.  Position closed.

TNA – Not an ideal start to the week as the breakout has failed thus far.  With that said, this still looks fine bigger picture and I am holding the full position into today’s session.  I am moving my stop loss to yesterday’s lows ($38.86) as I believe today’s gap up should ideally hold.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LVdA!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9432f1ec-a406-4f17-b1b1-e30745938494_990x729.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LVdA!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9432f1ec-a406-4f17-b1b1-e30745938494_990x729.jpeg)

HIMS – Stayed relatively strong yesterday as it gave back only -3% of Monday’s double digit percentage gains.  I trimmed a bit at the open and am holding the rest of my long position into Wednesday’s session.  Tuesday was an inside day for HIMS and we are set to gap near/above yesterday’s highs this morning.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!SuGx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3eaa630a-f056-43cc-a7ba-62009e31be16_1185x897.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!SuGx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3eaa630a-f056-43cc-a7ba-62009e31be16_1185x897.jpeg)

I will be moving my stop loss to yesterday’s lows ($13.91) as this should not break the inside day to the downside, in my opinion.  This will also allow me to protect my gains here in case today’s gap up is faded across the market.

CRWD – Gapping up over +20% this morning after earnings last night.  This is the only name I will be looking to trade today and will only carry overnight/into tomorrow if I get a good entry with a 3%+ profit cushion by EOD.
