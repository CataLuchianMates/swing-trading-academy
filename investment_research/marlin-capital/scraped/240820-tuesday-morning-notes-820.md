# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.20

Daily Market Commentary:

The S&P 500 finished +0.97% to kick off the week yesterday and is now green 8 days in a row / ~1% from ATHs.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!3gWX!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4234fac5-df0a-40f7-8cdc-9eb3812b6f89_1203x888.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!3gWX!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4234fac5-df0a-40f7-8cdc-9eb3812b6f89_1203x888.jpeg)

It was a slow drift higher on low volumes with no real themes standing out. The VIX (14.7) id now below August 1st levels (16.2). Don’t worry about that 65 handle intraday high on the 5th. Nothing to see here.

As I touched on yesterday, the pain trade for equities is still higher for the next few weeks and the bar for being bearish into Labor Day is high with CTA and funds re-leveraging, unwinding of puts, and corporate buybacks opening up. Sellers appear to be out of ammo for now, forcing fundamental investors to keep chasing higher. GS estimates that there is $12B of non-discretionary/unemotional demand per day, which will continue to drive a larger impact when adjusted for August liquidity.

My top trade setup for today -

SPOT - This is too good of a long setup to ignore as it looks to be breaking out of this post-earnings consolidation. This breakout can eventually take us to $400.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vfb5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd05d3101-7d46-4c3c-bc14-b4eebc67102c_1201x882.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!vfb5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd05d3101-7d46-4c3c-bc14-b4eebc67102c_1201x882.jpeg)

My long entry will be above $346 today.

Stop below $332 (-4.0% risk)

Holding SHOP, AXON, SN, and SG into Tuesday with stops in place.
