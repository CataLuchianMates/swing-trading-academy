# Tuesday Market Thread 1.25

**Tuesday Morning Notes:**

On Monday, we finally saw some fear/capitulation, followed by a massive bounce. The Nasdaq was down -4% or worse at its daily low, only to finish the session in the green.  This has happened several other times below:

1/24/2022

11/13/2008

10/16/2008

7/15/2002

6/26/2002

10/19/2001

4/20/2001

2/23/2001

1/8/2001

12/26/2000

10/26/2000

6/2/2000

These dates are not bull friendly, short-term.  As I have mentioned, Bear Market bounces can be quick and violent.

We are set to gap down today.  I am expecting a lot more volatility both pre and post FOMC meeting this week.  I will keep monitoring the price action for clues for what may come next.  It is impossible to call a bottom just before the FOMC decision, which will have an enormous impact on the market, one way or another.
