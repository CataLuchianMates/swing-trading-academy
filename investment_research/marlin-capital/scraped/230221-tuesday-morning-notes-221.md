A Mike Wilson favorite -- EQUITY RISK PREMIUM

Equity Risk Premium shows the earnings yield on the S&P 500 minus the interest rate on 10-yr Treasury notes. The higher the risk premium, the more attractive stocks are relative to bonds.

The SPX risk premium is now the lowest since 2007.
