# Thursday Morning Notes 7.21

Technical Analysis:

Another big squeeze in the market on Wednesday, with the QQQ closing above its 60 EMA for the first time since March 21st. During that rally, we stayed above the 60 EMA for 11 days, extending as much as 5% above the line at the peak. Back then, we also rallied to the 200 Day MA, which now stands at $347 (14% above today’s close). The current rally is the only one outside of March to see a close above the 60 EMA. It has not been a vertical move up like in March, but this rally has been strong, with every dip getting bought. Resistance levels ahead include $306-$307 and $312. Key support levels for the QQQ to hold include $297 and $290. I may consider quick TQQQ long momentum trades if the market continues to act well.

With the medium-term trend flip, I added some long exposure in INMD. This is a great looking technical setup after the company raised guidance last week. We broke out of that consolidation pattern today. The chart is pointing to more upside ahead. The 200 Day MA is at $49 (66% above today’s close), so there is plenty of room for this stock to move higher. Resistance levels ahead include $32, $34, and $38-$40. My stop will be placed below $28.

I will look to continue to add exposure if my initial trade(s) begin to gain traction. It is take your pick in the beaten down tech space, as many stocks look set to bounce even higher. SHOP, SE, BILL are a few high growth bounce names I’ll be watching for the rest of this week.
