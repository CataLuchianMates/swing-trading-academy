# The Week Ahead 12.12

**Analysis**

A critical week for stocks is upon us. Tomorrow 12/13, November CPI Data will be released at 8:30 AM EST. The last 3 CPI days resulted in fireworks, as highlighted in the chart below.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KjxZ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F99cdc0c1-4d6b-408d-a23a-15cfbe3df4a7_1029x651.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KjxZ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F99cdc0c1-4d6b-408d-a23a-15cfbe3df4a7_1029x651.png)

You’ll notice the technical setup heading into the data release means very little once the numbers come out. From a trading strategy standpoint, I see no clear edge in opening any positions prior to the report.

On Wednesday 12/14, the Federal Reserve will conclude its December meeting and announce its decision on interest rates. A 50 BP hike is widely expected to be announced. More important for the market will be Jerome Powell’s press conference at 2:30 EST. His tone will set the stage for how stocks trade the rest of the year.

Expect lots of volatility this week, starting tomorrow. From a technical standpoint, the SPX has been trading in a month-long range. Below is the 30 minute chart of the last month.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lAwm!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd2b88912-d840-436b-a065-5cc0b415f9c8_1365x651.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lAwm!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd2b88912-d840-436b-a065-5cc0b415f9c8_1365x651.png)

We are nearing the low of the range ($3900) as we head into tomorrow’s CPI report. I would expect this range to be fully broken this week, and a big move will likely follow. The direction of said move will depend more upon CPI and the FOMC news events, rather than the technical setup.

As a trader, it is important to be nimble and flexible with the news coming out this week. Support is at $3900 (low of range). Resistance is at $4000-$4020 (high of range). I will be prepared to trade long or short on a break of this range.
