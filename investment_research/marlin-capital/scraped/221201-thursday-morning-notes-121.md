# Thursday Morning Notes 12.1

Technical Analysis

Wow - yesterday’s major squeeze came out of left field. Notably, Fed Chair Powell did NOT push back against the recent loosening of financial conditions and rally in risk assets as everyone was expecting. Instead, Powell finally admitted that the hiking cycle will soon be coming to a pause and the Fed does not necessarily want to purposely break something by over tightening. Powell is setting the market up for a pause so that the committee can reassess the damage that has been done to the economy and the progress they have made on inflation.

The market cheered and applauded the Fed Chair’s speech with a +4% day. On a technical basis, the SPX cleared its 200 day moving average for the first time since April. Back then, the index spent about a week above the 200 day before the reversal.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XxR9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F032e4cff-4c34-4dfb-95de-2ace8d493750_1190x903.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XxR9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F032e4cff-4c34-4dfb-95de-2ace8d493750_1190x903.jpeg)

I would not expect an immediate reversal with this type of momentum. As I have highlighted, lopsided short positioning causes these squeezes to be even more violent.

Watch for resistance at $4150-$4200.

Support at $4040-$4050 and $4000.

As I have highlighted, patience is a key for shorts, as many have gotten violently squeezed by getting short too early. This is precisely why it is key to know your levels and stay disciplined. The SPX held the $3940-$3950 support level I discussed in yesterday’s morning notes, while we never got a bearish MACD cross on the daily to confirm the reversal. These technical rules should’ve kept shorts out of the market until confirmation.
