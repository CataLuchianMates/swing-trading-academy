#### Wednesday’s Fed Meeting

As mentioned, the Fed will be cutting rates this week and Small Caps, the most rate sensitive area of the market, are setting up for a ~4 year base breakout.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!PKMU!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff630561a-3ea7-4102-8f48-af4a7da2c52d_1132x652.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!PKMU!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff630561a-3ea7-4102-8f48-af4a7da2c52d_1132x652.png)

Prior to that, the IWM was last trading this high in November of 2021 (almost 4 years ago). I named that period the “Covid Bubble”.

During the Covid Bubble, the IWM broke out of a ~2 year base in 2020 and ran +41% above the breakout level over the next 12 months.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6ucH!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0f93fbeb-2a32-4514-bf5f-ab71cb74c317_1132x652.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6ucH!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0f93fbeb-2a32-4514-bf5f-ab71cb74c317_1132x652.png)

#### **Watch the Speculatives**

Way back in late 2021, during the [Covid Bubble Bust,](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/were-not-in-kansas-anymore?utm_source=publication-search) I highlighted how the most speculative areas of the market (SPACs) topped first.

In [“Wild Times in the Market”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/is-this-market-crazy-or-is-it-just?utm_source=publication-search), I compared the December 2021 price action to the peaking of the Dot Com Bubble in 2001, because that is exactly what happened back then.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EB2z!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F15efdd40-bf86-4e96-b25a-9002d69a849a_1558x353.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EB2z!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F15efdd40-bf86-4e96-b25a-9002d69a849a_1558x353.png)

My research shows that Bubbles throughout history have peaked this way (with the most speculative groups topping first).

Back in [2021-22](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/were-not-in-kansas-anymore?utm_source=publication-search), the most speculative groups were SPACs and pre-revenue companies.

During this cycle, I believe the most speculative groups are Quantum Computing and Space Travel (which are mostly pre-revenue companies). These most speculative groups are currently breaking out to new highs, which is a strong signal that this Russell 2000 breakout is indeed legitimate

#### On Bubbles and Busts

On that note, one of the accomplishments I’m most proud of since launching Marlin Capital in 2021 is how quickly we flipped bearish in both of the 2 Bear Markets we’ve had since our launch.

**During the [2022 Covid Bust](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/were-not-in-kansas-anymore?utm_source=publication-search), we [got bearish](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/were-not-in-kansas-anymore?utm_source=publication-search) with the S&P 500 STILL at all-time highs.** Many names would go on to decline -70% + after my bearish call, and the SPX ended up declining ~24%.

**During the Q1 2025 Tariff Tank, we [got bearish on Feb 23rd](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/red-flags-emerge?utm_source=publication-search) with the SPX just -2% off ATHs.**  Many names would go on to decline -50% + after my bearish call, and the SPX ended up declining ~20%.

“Only when the tide goes out do you discover who's been swimming naked” - Warren Buffet.

I’m sure those who were around for the Covid Bubble and Bust cycle remember it well.

Many people are able to make significant gains during a Bubble. A large fraction of those same people will not keep their gains. That is because, historically, Bubbles retrace ALL of their parabolic gains during the Bust Phase, as I highlighted in [“History Repeating (1/19/22)”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/history-repeating?utm_source=publication-search) (bookmark this report for when the music stops).

During the [2022 Covid Bust](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/were-not-in-kansas-anymore?utm_source=publication-search), Cathie Wood’s flagship fund ARKK gave back all of their parabolic Bubble gains during [the Bust Phase](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/history-repeating?utm_source=publication-search).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qhht!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F43709855-3d43-402e-a917-c0f14c45a827_1132x652.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qhht!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F43709855-3d43-402e-a917-c0f14c45a827_1132x652.png)

And the same will be true for the AI Bubble. Many people will not keep their gains during this cycle.

At Marlin Capital, we have a history of identifying toppy market behavior early on, as we did during the last 2 major Bear Markets since our launch (the only 2 Bear cycles since our launch). We are confident that when the time comes, we will be able to do the same for the AI Bubble.
