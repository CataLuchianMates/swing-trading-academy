# Thursday Morning Notes 6.8

Technical Update

The S&P 500 pulled back on Wednesday, finishing -0.38% on the day. Big Tech was by far the weakest group on the day, closing -1.7%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EHXM!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe93b9c3c-1037-4471-9d34-6ede0591eb07_958x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EHXM!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe93b9c3c-1037-4471-9d34-6ede0591eb07_958x724.jpeg)

Meanwhile, small caps once again led the way. IWM vs Mega Cap Tech had its best day of 2023 and 1 of its best days over the last 5 years.

I talked about this rotation to small caps beginning to take place last week, and it has continued into this week after smartly consolidating on Monday. We went long IWO on Tuesday’s inside day break for this catch up trade.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!07B0!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbe9bf489-e92c-477c-bba7-f1d7403b364d_1191x888.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!07B0!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbe9bf489-e92c-477c-bba7-f1d7403b364d_1191x888.jpeg)

In my view, there is plenty more gas in the tank for continued small cap outperformance. With that said, this has been a big 2 day move and we are once again getting quite extended from the 21 EMA. I will be looking to take some profits off the table on this trade at my 1st target - $243 (and potentially add back on the upcoming pullback).
