We have seen a couple bounce attempts for the market indices during this downtrend, but this 1 feels different for me due to the CPI catalyst helping to rebound declining sentiment and calm anxieties in the bond market. I [talked about](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/wednesday-morning-notes-115) how rising 10 YR yields have been weighing on equities [this morning](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/wednesday-morning-notes-115). The Inflation # was a bullish development for equities, as we saw the 10 YR down -2.8% today in response to the Core CPI miss.

Small Caps have now given back all their gains and then some from the Trump election night victory gap up. Small Caps are more sensitive to changes in interest rates, so the fast rising 10 YR yield was really weighing on this group. Let’s see if Wednesday’s Core CPI surprise to the downside has put a (temporary?) top in yields, allowing Small Caps to start to bottom here.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!L4g7!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7fbbed38-405c-434f-bf55-c933c4ca8015_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!L4g7!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7fbbed38-405c-434f-bf55-c933c4ca8015_1396x653.png)

On a positioning basis, we have seen shorts in ETFs increase +24% over the last month, which was the fastest pace of shorting since the Feb ‘21 Meme Stock Frenzy.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!S1D9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F94e6f429-833c-4e7e-a3bb-62b9e2b287fa_547x368.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!S1D9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F94e6f429-833c-4e7e-a3bb-62b9e2b287fa_547x368.jpeg)

For me, this is a favorable setup for longs because shorts have already started to cover with the big gap up on CPI today, and this will likely need to be a multi-day short covering rally.

We’re certainly not out of the woods yet on a technical basis, but I see decent odds the market has seen its lows on this mini-correction and some good risk/reward long opportunities.
