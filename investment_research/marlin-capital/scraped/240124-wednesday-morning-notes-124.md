# Wednesday Morning Notes 1.24

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks traded sideways for most of yesterday’s session with the S&P 500 closing +0.29% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oI6k!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F48d77967-4aba-450b-98e7-91fcb680a214_1167x881.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oI6k!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F48d77967-4aba-450b-98e7-91fcb680a214_1167x881.jpeg)

Bond yields pushed higher (US 10 YR now at 4.13%) on a relatively quiet macro news front outside of China looking at a potential $278B equity market rescue package, igniting a squeeze across Chinese ADR names.  The China ADR basket squeeze +4% yesterday but is still down -9% in 2024.

Eleswhere yesterday, large caps outperformed small caps and momentum underperformed for the first time in 2 weeks as investors worked through a slew of mixed earnings.  Homebuilders took a 2 sigma move lower, driven by DHI -9% on weaker earnings, citing margin compression.  MMM also traded -10% on EBIT miss and guide below.

After the bell, NFLX rose +9% on a big subscriber beat, but Q1 revs guide was a little light.  TXN -4% on revenue miss and guide below.

Potential Earnings Gaps to monitor today – NFLX, LRN
