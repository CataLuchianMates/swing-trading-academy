# Thursday Morning Notes 3.30

Technical Update

A big day for the market yesterday as bulls finally gained some traction with a move and close above $4000 in the SPX.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Mlhs!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1f2090d3-75a9-46bd-bda2-7f8ac66156ff_958x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Mlhs!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1f2090d3-75a9-46bd-bda2-7f8ac66156ff_958x724.jpeg)

All of a sudden, the technical setup is looking a lot more bullish here. Yesterday I posted the below chart in the “Goldman’s morning insights” note, showing how hedge fund net Long exposure remains near 5 year lows and has not budged at all in the last 4 months.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8rwy!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1a3ff467-09e1-489c-933a-58ee566d1ebb_1120x594.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8rwy!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1a3ff467-09e1-489c-933a-58ee566d1ebb_1120x594.jpeg)

With the market rallying, hedge funds could be forced to chase this rally higher, in turn bidding up stock prices even higher and exacerbating the rally. When you combine this with the amount of cash on the sidelines (ATH assets in money market funds - see Sunday’s report), we have a couple catalysts for a potential rally higher.

The first step for bulls is moving above $4040-$4050 resistance. Next up would be $4075-$4080. If we can break these resistance levels, the chase could be on, leading to a potential test of $4200 and YTD highs.

A move back below $3990-$4000 support would effectively invalidate the bullish technical setup and put us right back in the chop zone. On any pullback, look for $3990 support to hold for the bullish setup to remain intact.
