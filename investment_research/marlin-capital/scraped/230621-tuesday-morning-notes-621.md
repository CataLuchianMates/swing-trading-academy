# Tuesday Morning Notes 6.21

Technical Update

The S&P 500 pulled back below $4400 to start off the week, closing -0.47% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mY1m!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff3e4eea5-8b25-49e7-b295-f3fffb617a74_1002x758.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mY1m!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff3e4eea5-8b25-49e7-b295-f3fffb617a74_1002x758.jpeg)

Buyers showed up at the 8 EMA as $4367 marked the low of the day. During this uptrend for the SPX, the 8 EMA has marked key support multiple times as we have not closed below the line since late May.

My indicators remain in overbought territory despite the 2 day pullback in the market. I am looking for a further pullback/consolidation into the 21 EMA ($4290-$4300) before adding more long exposure.
