# Friday Morning Notes 1.6

Technical Update

The rangebound action (between $3800-$3890) for the S&P 500 continued yesterday with the index marking a low of $3802 on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MFhW!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F71842fbd-a91e-4c41-a102-8aa3188739b2_1201x897.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MFhW!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F71842fbd-a91e-4c41-a102-8aa3188739b2_1201x897.jpeg)

A weak close yesterday excited some bears about the possibility of a breakdown from this range, but we are set for a gap up this morning on stronger than expected jobs report.

Resistance levels to watch on the upside include $3874, $3889, and $3900.

On the downside, $3800 is the all important key support level to watch.
