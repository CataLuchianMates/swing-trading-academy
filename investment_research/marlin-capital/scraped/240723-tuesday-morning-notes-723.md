# Tuesday Morning Notes 7.23

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US markets finished broadly higher yesterday with Tech leading the way.  The S&P 500 finished +1.08% and NDX +1.54%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!xvbM!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff9479b0e-968d-48eb-ac5a-c2c13e2f2869_980x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!xvbM!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff9479b0e-968d-48eb-ac5a-c2c13e2f2869_980x723.jpeg)

The Hedge Fund community spent the day re-risking after last week’s huge de-grossing (99th percentile selling).  The Biden news has the market believing a republican sweep is now less likely than it was on Friday, but still the most likely scenario.  Goldman’s Democratic Policy pair bounced +1.43% yesterday (now up 0.4% YTD versus Republican Policy pair up +5.7%).

TSLA and GOOGL are set to announce earnings after today’s close with the potential to move the entire market.  In my view, if we start to see these Mega Caps announce solid enough earnings, it could send asset managers back into re-risk mode after last week’s big selling event.

SPOT – Gapping up +14% this morning.  This is my top watch today for an earnings gapper setup.
