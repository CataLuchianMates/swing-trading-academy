# Thursday Morning Notes 10.12

Wednesday was a choppy day for US stocks with the S&P 500 ultimately finishing the session near highs at +0.43%.  The index closed right at 60 EMA resistance ($4380) with the afternoon rally.

The QQQ remains the stronger index with a +0.71% rally on the day.  We closed the session at highs and just below $372 resistance.

Breaking data from this morning – September CPI inflation came in at 3.7%, above expectations of 3.6%.

Core CPI inflation came in at 4.1%, in line with expectations of 4.1%.

Equity futures are largely unchanged after this morning’s CPI release.  Today’s CPI numbers, along with the recent FOMC minutes, seem to further reduce the risk of another rate hike in Q4.  Although the headline CPI number came in slightly higher than expected, I do not see this report changing the current message from the Fed as the trend remains encouraging.  Excluding shelter, the core CPI rose by just 0.1% m/m.  Below is the full breakdown:
