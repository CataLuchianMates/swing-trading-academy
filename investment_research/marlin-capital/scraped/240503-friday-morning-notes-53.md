# Friday Morning Notes 5.3

Daily Market Commentary:

US stocks rallied higher yesterday (RTY +1.81%, NDX +1.29%, SPX +0.91%) as rates settled down (US 10 YR @ 4.58%) following Wednesday’s FOMC meeting.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EVNU!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F99dbdab0-8bda-4450-8c49-8bd25561a3bc_976x717.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!EVNU!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F99dbdab0-8bda-4450-8c49-8bd25561a3bc_976x717.jpeg)

The rally was accompanied by a high beta squeeze with GS Most Short Basket +4.7% (led by CVNA – largest daily move YTD), Bitcoin Sensitives +7%, and China ADRs +7% (3.5 sigma move).  China Internet ETF KWEB +8% now in breakout mode with HSI +2.5% overnight.  I talked about this in The Generals Hold Court and still believe there is plenty of meat on the bone in this KWEB/China ADR breakout rally.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mOIC!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F26411f09-8df7-48d0-911d-68b39b4a2672_1191x887.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mOIC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F26411f09-8df7-48d0-911d-68b39b4a2672_1191x887.jpeg)

After the bell, AAPL reported a solid beat across the board (total revs, iPhone revs, service revs, EPS all beat).  Other EPS movers: BKNG +9% - very nice beat in a highly debated stock. SQ gapping up +9% on a beat + raised outlook. FTNT down as Q1 billings miss.

We are set for a massive gap up in the indices this morning following this morning’s NFP report where unemployment surprised to the upside, rising to 3.9%.  Hiring slowed to 175k jobs in April, well below the forecasted 240k and the lowest figure since October 2023.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!83DJ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3b152b82-60ba-4d59-a7d9-da5967136b5b_2046x569.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!83DJ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3b152b82-60ba-4d59-a7d9-da5967136b5b_2046x569.png)

The weakening labor market is seen as a huge positive (at least initially) for investors, and the Fed Funds futures market is now pricing in 2 rate cuts for 2024 vs 1 prior to the weak jobs data.  September rate cut probabilities jumped to 77% following this morning’s NFP report.
