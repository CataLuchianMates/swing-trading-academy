# Wednesday Morning Notes 12.21

Technical Update

The S&P 500 found support at the $3800 level yesterday, making a low of $3795 for the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!O1RQ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd5d2e2f1-2b00-4319-bdc9-849d5d3c670a_961x727.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!O1RQ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd5d2e2f1-2b00-4319-bdc9-849d5d3c670a_961x727.jpeg)

We are set for a small gap up this morning towards $3850. As long as the $3800 support level holds, the door is still open for a bounce to $3900.

If the $3800 support level breaks, I will be looking for another 50 point drop towards $3745-$3750.
