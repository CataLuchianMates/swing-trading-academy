Active Portfolio:

Short ARKK (half position)

Stop at breakeven ($58)

As mentioned yesterday, I am expecting choppy/rangebound action (at the index level) throughout this earnings season. This is precisely why I decided to cover half my ARKK short at the close yesterday. Today, we are set for a gap up in ARKK and many tech names on the back of strong TSLA earnings. I am planning to place my buy stop (on my remaining ARKK short) at breakeven, as I am not willing to watch profit turn to loss in such a choppy market.

Elsewhere, energy and commodities continue to lead. Choppiness at the index level could prove to be a positive for these stocks, as the market focuses on Big tech earnings. I plan to add 1-2 swing long energy/commodity names in my Active Portfolio, as I believe the lack of volatility at the index level will allow these stocks to trend up “cleaner”.
