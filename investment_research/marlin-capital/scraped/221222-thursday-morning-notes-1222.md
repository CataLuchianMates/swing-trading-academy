# Thursday Morning Notes 12.22

Technical Update

The S&P 500 staged a rally yesterday, making a high of $3889 before closing just below $3880.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!n6Sp!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcd1a78b3-aef4-48c3-822a-99eddd62b482_1095x835.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!n6Sp!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcd1a78b3-aef4-48c3-822a-99eddd62b482_1095x835.jpeg)

We are set up for a gap down this morning. The $3795-$3800 level remains key support on the downside. If this support level fails, I will be looking for a further 50 point drop towards $3745-$3750.

If the market can stabilize after today’s gap down and rallies from red to green, look for resistance at $3900. If this resistance level breaks, we can see a rally to $3920-$3930.
