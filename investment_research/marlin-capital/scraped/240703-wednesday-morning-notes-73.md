# Wednesday Morning Notes 7.3

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500 (+0.62%) and Nasdaq (+1.01%) both set new ATHs yesterday with Big Tech leading the way.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!15dq!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9c059687-59a0-489c-bef3-1d84b5c9f775_987x721.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!15dq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9c059687-59a0-489c-bef3-1d84b5c9f775_987x721.jpeg)

Holiday activity levels were in effect with volumes -9% vs 20d avg.  Seasonality is also in full effect, as this week tends to be the best of the calendar year (and volumes are always low this week).

We saw some pockets of reversion with 12M winners up +0.14% on the day and 12M losers +0.63%.  TSLA was the standout (+9%) on delivery numbers beating expectations.  2.5M calls hit the tape on TSLA yesterday.  Reminder for TSLA – we still have the August 8th robotaxi day as a catalyst ahead, while we will get more details on margins, etc. at earnings in a couple weeks.

We also saw some weakness in Bio-pharma, specifically weight loss drugs, as a Biden/Bernie OpEd in USA Today called for the GLP-1 behemoths (NVO, LLY) to reduce prices for their obesity drugs in the US.  With that said, I will still be holding my NVO long position with my closing stop set at $138.  
  
Today is a half day with the US equity market closing at 1PM EST.

Updates on my trades –

NVO – Still holding my position with my closing stop set at $138.

AVGO – Triggered long at $1638 yesterday.  Closing stop is set below $1570.  My first target here is $1700 and I think we will see a move back to new ATHs (~$1850 region).  We have a stock split coming up in this name.

MSFT, ORCL – Still holding both positions with no changes.
