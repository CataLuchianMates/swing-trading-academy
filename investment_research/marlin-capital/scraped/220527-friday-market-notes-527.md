# Friday Market Notes 5.27

Technical Analysis:

The Market staged a solid rally yesterday, with the S&P 500 closing right at its 21 EMA. If we can break through today’s high ($407), I believe we are likely to see a test of the 50 SMA ($427). This is also where I would consider adding some long exposure in my Active Portfolio. Energy names continue to act the strongest, and certain oversold tech names might be playable for a further bounce and squeeze.

As I stated last week, this relief rally was much needed to reset some of the balance after 7 straight down weeks. I envision this rally lasting longer than just this week. Of course, QT starts next week, and the rug can be pulled at any time. This is to say I would not be looking to get overly aggressive, but money can be made on the long side in Bear markets.
