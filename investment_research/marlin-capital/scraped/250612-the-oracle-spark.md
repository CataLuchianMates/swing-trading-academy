# The Oracle Spark

The major indices are trading slightly higher so far this morning as an extended/overheated market bumps into a complicated night of headlines.

In the short-term, it appears sentiment is starting to get a bit overheated with short-term sentiment reaching near 1yr highs.

Underneath the hood, the action in recent days has been very mixed. Lower quality/high beta pockets of the market are leading while high quality segments (and previous leaders) are lagging.

Big Tech has lagged Non-Profitable Tech for 7 straight days, its longest losing streak in several years.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!AqOo!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1855a4a2-8c47-4392-b509-a3cfa4cb65e7_1032x478.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!AqOo!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1855a4a2-8c47-4392-b509-a3cfa4cb65e7_1032x478.jpeg)

Yesterday’s biggest gainers included pre-revenue Nuclear energy names like OKLO, SMR and pre-revenue Quantum Computing names like QUBT. OKLO rallied +30% on what appeared to be a re-hashing of old news and immediately announced a $400M stock offering after the bell (smart move by management to capitalize). Now, the stock is flirting with a red to green move this morning (only -1% on the day as of this writing). **This should give everyone a good idea of the type of market we are currently in as far as risk appetite/speculation go.**

On the Micro side of things, **Oracle delivered exceptional results after the bell yesterday** (helping to drive this positive sentiment). With the new forecast, annual revenue is expected to grow by +16.7% YoY compared with ORCL’s prior projection of +15% YoY growth. The company guided to accelerating growth in Total Cloud revs >50% (vs +24% in FY25) and Cloud Infrastructure growth >70% (vs 51% in FY25).

**For anyone looking for a bullish AI take, tune in to the Oracle call transcript from last night.** I pulled out a couple quotes from the conference call, but the bigger picture takeaway is that visibility around AI continues to extend out to 2026 as the CEOs of AVGO (last week) and ORCL (last night) offered extremely convicted, upbeat views on demand into 2026.

> “I expect that **FY26 CapEx will be higher at over $25 billion as we work to meet demand from our backlog.** As we bring more capacity online, our revenue and profit growth will further accelerate.”…… Note - **for context, this is up ~4x vs FY24 CapEx levels**
>
> “I am still in a position where **our supply is not meeting our demand.** We actually, currently are still waving off customers from - or scheduling them out into the future so that we have enough supply to meet demand. **This is a situation that we have not seen in our history, and the numbers themselves are so enormous**, and the reason is because our technology is different…We don’t build unless we’ve got ordewrs for our capacity to be built out.”
>
> - Oracle Conference Call, 6/11/25

ORCL stock is trading +14% higher this morning following these upbeat results, its best day in years.

Big picture, it’s hard to listen to that ORCL conference call and not come away extremely bullish on the future of AI. In [“Big Picture”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-big-picture), I compared the Q1 2025 Tariff Tank Bear Market to the 1998 market correction (a big, Macro-induced correction in the middle of the Dot Com Bubble). I continue to believe that when we look back at this time period in history (many years from now), we will look at the Q1 Tariff Tank as similar to the 1998 correction. This is because of the type of technological progress, innovations, and transformations I believe we will see in the next 5-10 years.

The Micro side of things could not be more bullish here. Typically, throughout history, these types of generational technological transformations have culminated with an asset bubble. Big picture, my belief is that we are likely heading for an AI Bubble as well in the next couple years.
