# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.23

Technical Analysis:

The market followed through to the downside to start off this week, with the SPY and QQQ both closing down over 2% yesterday. The QQQ has now fully broken its 21 EMA support level and looks set for a test of the 60 EMA ($311). If this level breaks, new lows can come quicker than many think.

For the SPY, the index also closed below its 21 EMA yesterdat and gave a bearish MACD cross. The 60 EMA at $407 will be key support to watch in the coming days. Meanwhile, high yield bonds (HYG) followed through to the downside yesterday, with an ugly breakdown of the 60 EMA support level. The market seems to believe Powell will be hawkish at Jackson hole this week, so it is possible to see an event clearing bounce if sentiment gets too bearish ahead of time. I believe a bounce and subsequent failure will provide the opportune time to enter shorts in my active portfolio.
