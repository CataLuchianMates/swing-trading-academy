# Wednesday Morning Notes 6.7

Technical Update

The S&P 500 chopped around again on Tuesday, finishing +0.24% on the day. $4300 remains the key resistance level to watch above.

Meanwhile, we saw small caps outperform on Tuesday with a textbook inside day break by the Russell 200 Growth ETF (IWO).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7BW9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdbbc66a3-d12c-4f35-809d-7bc4a3af22ec_967x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!7BW9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdbbc66a3-d12c-4f35-809d-7bc4a3af22ec_967x723.jpeg)

I entered long IWO on the inside day break for this catch up trade from $233.10 with a stop below $230.10 (1.3% risk). First targets are $243-$245 and a test of YTD highs.

ARKK - another catch up trade I entered Friday working well so far. ARKK also outperformed the indices yesterday and is gaining momentum.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!41SP!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff672be58-293d-4dce-8026-c232c04bc40b_971x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!41SP!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff672be58-293d-4dce-8026-c232c04bc40b_971x724.jpeg)
