As Marlin Capital readers know, I use technical analysis as a tool. But, it is my belief that you can never have super high conviction in a purely technical setup.

This is why I believe the biggest winning stocks in a Bull Market are found on the fundamental side first. The technicals (price & volume) are simply a confirmation, playing a supporting role. Deep understanding of the business and industry breeds real conviction.

In this AI driven Bull Market, the biggest winners are coming from fundamental catalysts (like re-ratings sparked by major deals). In [“Re-Rating Season”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/re-rating-season?utm_source=publication-search), I covered some of the major catalysts for these stock-ratings (new subscribers, please read the linked report for more examples).

> **“There has NEVER been a better time to be a stock picker.**
>
> What we are seeing in the market right now is aggressive re-ratings to the upside in AI stocks, driven by major catalysts.”

With Q3 earnings season right around the corner, we should be getting plenty more of these fundamental/re-rating catalysts shortly.

There has truly never been a better time to be a stock picker!
