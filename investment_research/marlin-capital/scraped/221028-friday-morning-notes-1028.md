# Friday Morning Notes 10.28

Technical Analysis

The QQQ broke to the downside yesterday, clearing some key support levels in the process. Bulls were unable to hold the daily 21 EMA support, and the QQQ closed near session lows.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-p48!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcbf33416-f3e4-4833-a268-06eaa43801a8_1024x767.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-p48!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fcbf33416-f3e4-4833-a268-06eaa43801a8_1024x767.jpeg)

We are set for another gap down this morning following disappointing earnings from Amazon.

Key support levels include the $267-$270 region. If this level breaks, a re-test of lows should be on deck.

The $275 (21 EMA) level is now the key resistance level to clear in order to restart this rally.

The Fed Meeting and decision is set for next Wednesday. Following the WSJ report by Nick Timiriaos, the expectation is that the committee will lay out a path for slowing down the pace of future hikes.
