# Tuesday Morning Notes 5.7

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday was another choppy day for US stocks today as the market continues to trade in its price range.

The SPY has traded in a price range between $406 and $417 since May 27th. During the same time frame, the QQQ has traded in a range between $303 and $315. It is not yet a time for me to look to get aggressive until the market breaks this trading range (up or down). We still have some good looking setups if the market can cooperate, but that remains a big if.

This morning, the QQQ is set to gap down to the $303 area, the lows of its recent trading range. If the recent range lows are not able to hold, the next leg lower may be set to begin.
