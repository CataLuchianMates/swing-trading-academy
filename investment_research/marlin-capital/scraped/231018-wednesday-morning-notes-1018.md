# Wednesday Morning Notes 10.18

Daily Market Commentary

US stocks finished Tuesday flat with the S&P 500 closing -0.01% on the day.  The index briefly broke through $4380 resistance before hitting a high of $4393 and reversing.  We closed below the 21 EMA resistance level ($4380) once again despite another attempt to break through .

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OATV!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdf0badcd-7248-4e56-be99-863c3dbaa2a0_964x728.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OATV!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdf0badcd-7248-4e56-be99-863c3dbaa2a0_964x728.jpeg)

Tech was a bit weaker with the QQQ finishing the day in the red (-0.33%).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!GmId!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F34e84928-941f-443e-bdc7-c2c0afb81919_1197x897.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!GmId!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F34e84928-941f-443e-bdc7-c2c0afb81919_1197x897.jpeg)

The big story of Tuesday was the headline regarding NVDA/semis.  NVDA closed -5% on the back of this headline: “US restricts sale of NVDA made for China chips in new rules.”

On the economic front, the retail sales numbers released yesterday provide another bullet point on the resilience of the US economy.  This number is the latest in the string of strong September economic data released (jobs and the headline CPI/PPI.  September retail sales rose 0.7% and core retail sales rose 0.6% - both above expectations – and spending was revised higher in prior months from already strong levels.)

Futures are pointing to a lower open this morning with the 10 YR yield rally back above 4.8% after a brief dip last week.
