In my view, its best to stick with the EPS standouts here. The market is making it crystal clear which stocks those are.

**1 of our [SMID Cap 6 names](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-smid-caps-with-massive-upside-potential) just catapulted to the very top of that standout list, with 1 of the absolute best earnings reports of Q2, soaring +20% today.**

**NBIS**

Nebius Group reported a Blowout quarter with a capital B.

Revenue grew +625% YoY and +106% QoQ! The company also increased ARR guidance to $900M - $1.1B (vs. $875M est, a +14% guidance raise above consensus).

Nebius is in the process of securing more than 1 GW of power by the end of 2026 (from est ~220 MW by the end of 2025). Currently, they are guiding for $1B in ARR at 220 MW. **Crunching the numbers, at 1 GW, ARR would likely be $4B+** (conservatively).

With continued strong execution, we are looking at a sure-fire multi-bagger in the making here. This is STILL undervalued at a $16B market cap.

**ALAB**

ALAB is 1 of the other A+ earnings reports I believe we saw this quarter.

The company announced Q2 revenue grew +150% YoY(vs +125% YoY consensus, an 11% rev beat), and an acceleration from last quarter’s 144% growth. Non-GAAP Gross Margins came in at 76% vs cons 74%. For Q3, they guided for $203-$210M in rev vs $180M cons (a +15% guidance raise) and GMs guided 75% vs cons 73.6%.

We have held ALAB since January in our [Top 2025 AI Picks Bucket](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/third-bounce?utm_source=publication-search).

With this massive earnings catalyst, we just entered for a momentum trade yesterday in the Active Trading Portfolio as well.
