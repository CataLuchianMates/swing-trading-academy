# Friday Morning Notes 8.9

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500 rallied big yesterday in its biggest 1-day gain since November 2022, closing +2.3%.

As I mentioned in yesterday’s morning notes, initial jobless claims provided a sigh of relief regarding recession concerns.  Corporates, asset managers, hedge fund managers, and sovereigns have all bought the dip this week in Tech and higher quality defensives.  Most of the demand yesterday came with buyers stepping in to chase the rally.  The SPX recaptures its 100 SMA of $5310 and held into the bell.

Semis bounced back yesterday with a >6% rally (now ~30% off the highs).  AI/Momentum/GLP-1 themes took back the lead yesterday, propelling the market higher.  GLP-1 is the 2nd most crowded theme (behind AI) and LLY delivered (stock +9%) on a huge EPS beat driven by sales and margins.  The full year guide hiked by $3B on sales and $2.60 on EPS (new EPS range is $16.1-$16.6 vs street est $13.71).

Coming into this earnings season, a lot of fear was that the bar was too high to clear at 9% EPS growth projected.  We are 88% of the way through and currently at +11% EPS growth.

It was a big day for [my top small caps picks](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-small-caps-with-massive-upside) with SEZL rallying +35% in yesterday’s session following a big earnings beat and huge guidance raise.

INOD, another 1 of my top 6, is rallying over +20% this morning on a phenomenal earnings beat and guidance raise.  It has been a huge earnings season for these picks!
