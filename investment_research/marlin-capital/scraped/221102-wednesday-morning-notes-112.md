# Wednesday Morning Notes 11.2

Technical Analysis

The QQQ stayed rangebound yesterday, closing right at the key $275 support level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!NKaX!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F61d4eace-6efd-4ba0-a5bf-1dabfb485338_1119x839.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!NKaX!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F61d4eace-6efd-4ba0-a5bf-1dabfb485338_1119x839.jpeg)

We are setting up for a big move in either direction from this consolidation range. Today’s Fed decision and press conference should provide the catalyst needed for a large directional move.

Key support levels below include $274/$275 and $270

Key resistance levels ahead include $278-280 and $284/285.
