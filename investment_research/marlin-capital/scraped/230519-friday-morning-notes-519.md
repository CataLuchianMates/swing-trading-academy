# Friday Morning Notes 5.19

Technical Update

The squeeze continued yesterday in the S&P 500 with the index finishing +0.94% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XxqC!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0f106b92-4fd5-4804-a841-4918ae16b0a8_974x727.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XxqC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0f106b92-4fd5-4804-a841-4918ae16b0a8_974x727.jpeg)

The SPX broke $4150 resistance and headed straight for the $4200 level I have been discussing. The high of the day was $4202 and we closed just below $4200 resistance.

Technically, this breakout is pointing to the possibility of a further momentum run to $4320-$4350. With that said, we are very overbought on shorter time frames and I believe a pullback next week is looking more likely.

A huge week for LRCX as this has turned into a big trade. With yesterday’s move, LRCX is now up 12.6% in a week from entry.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9Q0T!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F69de89be-f111-44e2-8139-42e4a41cca69_976x725.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9Q0T!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F69de89be-f111-44e2-8139-42e4a41cca69_976x725.jpeg)

Seeing a number of parabolic moves like this across the market suggests a pullback could be near. I would consider taking profits on at least half this position up here.

And a big week for Marlin Quant Portfolio members.

Our TQQQ 30 Min systems, available to all Marlin Quant members, just locked in gains of +11.5%, +11.2%, and +10.9% respectively from 3 unique long entries last week.

Our SOXL 1H system, available for Core Portfolio members, just locked in 16.7% gains from last Wednesday’s long entry.

The SOXL 1H system is now heading into Friday with unrealized gains of +25% and +19% on 2 unique long alerts subscribers received on Monday (5/15).
