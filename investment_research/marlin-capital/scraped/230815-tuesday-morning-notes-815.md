# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.15

Technical Update

The S&P 500 rallied 0.58% on low volume to kick off the week Monday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MmY9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd4bc849a-7e29-4308-9ddc-9d0d4b846c98_1194x894.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MmY9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd4bc849a-7e29-4308-9ddc-9d0d4b846c98_1194x894.jpeg)

We closed just below the key 8 and 21 EMA resistance levels I have discussed. We are set for a gap down this morning as this choppy summer market action continues.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $4490-$4500. I am not interesting in doing much here on the long side until we close back above the 8 and 21 EMA levels.

Key support levels to watch below include $4460, $4440-$4450, and $4400.

My watchlist remains the same (see “The Week Ahead”) and still holding a small long position in CLS.
