# Wednesday Morning Notes 3.29

Technical Update

The low volume chop continued yesterday in the indices as we head into the final days of Q1. The SPX finished off Tuesday -0.16%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6L7V!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1a8e2d09-13ed-423c-a193-3f70d28a600a_962x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6L7V!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1a8e2d09-13ed-423c-a193-3f70d28a600a_962x726.jpeg)

Price continues to hang around the 21 and 60 EMAs without much movement or volume. This is a good time to re-hash the old Wall Street saying “never short a dull market”. As long as we remain above the 200 SMA, it is hard to get overly bearish here on a technical basis.

At the same time, it is hard to get overly bullish here until some key resistance levels start to break. The first step for bulls is to break through and stay above the $4000 level.

We are set for a gap up this morning towards the $4k level in the SPX. Key resistance levels to watch above include $4000-$4005, $4035-$4040, and $4070-$4080.

Key support levels to watch below include $375-$3980, $3965, $3930-$3940, and $3900-$3910.
