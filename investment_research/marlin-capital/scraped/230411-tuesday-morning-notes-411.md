# Tuesday Morning Notes 4.11

Technical Update

The S&P 500 kicked off the week yesterday with another red to green intraday rally off a morning gap down. The index finished +0.1% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9Mjn!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe7006691-651e-4aba-bdff-5f6c7e8c7a46_958x719.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9Mjn!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe7006691-651e-4aba-bdff-5f6c7e8c7a46_958x719.jpeg)

Bulls remain in control here on a technical basis. This chart continues to set up for a rally to $4200 and YTD highs.

Key resistance levels above include $4130-$4135, $4150-$4160 and YTD highs at $4190-$4200.

Key support levels to watch below include $4100, $4075-$4080, $4050, and the last line of technical support at $3990-$4000.
