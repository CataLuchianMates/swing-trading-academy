# Tuesday Morning Notes 6.28

Technical Analysis:

A choppy Monday to start the week for the market. For the QQQ, we overshot the 21 EMA and came right back to it. The low of the day yesterday was $291, right at that 21 EMA support level. If the $291 and the $286 support levels (8 EMA) break, a re-test of 52 week lows ($270) could follow. Getting above yesterday’s highs ($296.5) would be a bullish sign and would point to temporary lows likely being in.
