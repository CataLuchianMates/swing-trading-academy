## David Marlin Q&A (Comments)

**Q:** hi David. fairly new to your substack. wondering about your sell guidelines? thanks alan

**David Marlin:** Hi Alan. For the 2026 Portfolio, I manage each name individually using my Stage Analysis framework. As long as the stock remains firmly in its Stage 2 uptrend, I will hold. If I start seeing signs of topping behavior or a Stage 4 downtrend, that’s my signal to exit. So no hard stop losses on these positions, but I exit when I believe the uptrend is breaking/a downtrend is beginning.
