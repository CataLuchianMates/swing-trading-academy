# Friday Morning Notes 4.12

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Big bounce back day for the market yesterday with the SPX finishing +0.74% at $5199.  The index closed right at its daily 8 EMA.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5s4h!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7d9f2bf6-a4f2-4d71-92bd-cad749ffb604_976x720.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!5s4h!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7d9f2bf6-a4f2-4d71-92bd-cad749ffb604_976x720.jpeg)

The QQQ finished +1.6% and rallied back above its 8 and 21 EMAs.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-wbb!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F02b1b59b-b119-4e9e-acff-5867a6018fe2_1212x889.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-wbb!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F02b1b59b-b119-4e9e-acff-5867a6018fe2_1212x889.jpeg)

There was not a lot of news out there yesterday to spark the rally other than a benign PPI reading that was better than feared.  With that said, the market rallying back towards 2-month highs following Wednesday’s CPI number is a bullish sign for equities.  When stocks rally on/after bad news, this should always be seen as a bullish sign.

The QQQ rallied back towards the top of its trading range yesterday, but remains in this choppy landscape (we have been in for the last ~2 months) heading into Q1 earnings season.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!noTx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F17ddcdc4-296c-49bf-8d2c-3a0f9672a334_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!noTx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F17ddcdc4-296c-49bf-8d2c-3a0f9672a334_1396x653.png)

We are set for a gap down this morning following yesterday’s rally, which is no surprise for those who have been following this choppy market.  The QQQ is set to open right in the middle of the 2-month trading range this morning near $441-$442.

MU – No changes to this long position.  Still holding the final 2/3rds here with a stop loss at $119.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Z-qY!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2d17278a-c723-4f56-af64-94f346a6436c_973x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Z-qY!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F2d17278a-c723-4f56-af64-94f346a6436c_973x724.jpeg)

COIN – I exited here last week but will be looking to re-enter on today’s gap down as I like the technical setup in COIN, Bitcoin, and crypto names.  Stop will be placed below recent lows at $234.
