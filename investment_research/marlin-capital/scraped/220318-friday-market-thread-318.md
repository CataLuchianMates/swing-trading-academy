ARKK Technical Analysis:

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OMuj!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F726069d9-f444-46d0-8d1b-48d136391c7e_1920x1017.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OMuj!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F726069d9-f444-46d0-8d1b-48d136391c7e_1920x1017.png)

Yesterday, ARKK got its first official close above its 21 EMA since November 8th, over 4 months ago. The ETF closed at $62.93, 0.4% above the 21 EMA of $62.66. As mentioned in Wednesday’s report, this could open the door for a larger and longer duration relief rally. A potential test of the 50 EMA (currently $71.29) could be on the horizon now.

As you can see in the above chart, essentially all bounces during this 4 month move down have failed after 3 Green Day’s. Today is the 4th day of this rally attempt, so if ARKK truly wants to bounce higher this time, today is a very important day for bulls. If bears regain control today, I would consider this to be another rejection at the 21 EMA despite a close above by 20 cents. Fake outs like this can be common in volatile markets, which is why I need to see more follow through to consider the short term downtrend broken. For that reason, today is likely just a day of waiting and observing in my Active Portfolio.
