# Tuesday Morning Notes 10.3

Technical Update

Big day for the market yesterday with the SPX pulling in to the $4260 support level I mentioned in “The Week Ahead”.  The market found support there and this was the low of the day with the index closing +0.01%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!UYXv!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F11d6954f-bee6-4032-9785-84defe640640_983x740.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!UYXv!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F11d6954f-bee6-4032-9785-84defe640640_983x740.jpeg)

This is still a bearish looking daily setup for the SPX after a rejection at the 8 EMA.  A break below $4240 support would be significant and is likely to spark another wave of selling to (at least) the 200 day moving average at $4200.  A break below to the 200 day and I see further downside to $3800-$4000 and the 2023 lows.

With that said, the other indices are giving some conflicting signals.  The QQQ was the strongest index yesterday and was able to finish green (+0.85%).  The QQQ was able to close above its 8 EMA after testing the 21 EMA earlier in the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!JlqI!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F55709f7b-c5cc-40e9-b627-9b71028e6dca_970x718.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!JlqI!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F55709f7b-c5cc-40e9-b627-9b71028e6dca_970x718.jpeg)

On a shorter time frame, the 1 HR QQQ chart is showing some signs of bottoming with a strong close yesterday.  $357 is the key support level to watch here.  Resistance wise, a move above $363 would be a bullish sign for more upside to come.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!aBmR!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F758e022c-578c-463d-b8ee-890053db8bea_1396x663.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!aBmR!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F758e022c-578c-463d-b8ee-890053db8bea_1396x663.png)

On the other hand, we have already seen small caps break down below recent September lows with yesterday’s -1.51% decline.  This chart is ugly and looks setup for more downside to YTD lows at $168.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!QX2i!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc6270d4a-b408-4f80-a214-e5d5867e898f_978x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!QX2i!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc6270d4a-b408-4f80-a214-e5d5867e898f_978x723.jpeg)

On the weekly chart, a move below $168 would be a significant breakdown for small caps and would set us up for more potential downside all the way to Bear Market lows at $159.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ptV9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdefbace0-b953-4e5a-86ae-dd5d1b218cb4_1206x889.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ptV9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdefbace0-b953-4e5a-86ae-dd5d1b218cb4_1206x889.jpeg)

I have talked in the past about how small caps did not fully participate in the market wide YTD rally.  This is a concern because small caps tend to outperform early in bull markets, and that is not what we have seen in 2023.

Overall, the market remains in no man’s land here with some contradicting signals but an overall bearish lean to the action.  I am waiting to see which way the indices break before jumping back in on the action.  I do not see any high risk/reward trade opportunities right here just yet.
