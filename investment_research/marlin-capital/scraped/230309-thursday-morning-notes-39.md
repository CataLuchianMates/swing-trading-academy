# Thursday Morning Notes 3.9

Technical Update

The market struggled for direction yesterday during (and after) Powell’s 2nd day of testimony. The S&P 500 finished the day +0.14% in the lowest volume session of March so far.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MJCf!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F373cf09e-1baf-413a-963d-2ffb904ed3b2_863x651.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MJCf!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F373cf09e-1baf-413a-963d-2ffb904ed3b2_863x651.jpeg)

Technically, this setup remains much the same after yesterday’s choppy move. We are currently below the 21 and 60 EMAs, but above the 200 SMA. The market is at a key point now, and appears to be setting up for a big move in the near future. I will be waiting to see which way we break before getting more involved.

On a move to the downside, key support levels below include $3970-$3975, **$3940**, $3925-$3930, and $3900.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $3995-$4000, $4020-$4025, $4050, $4075-$4080, and $4100.
