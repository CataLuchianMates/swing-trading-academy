# Friday Morning Notes 10.6

Daily Market Commentary

Thursday’s price action was mostly choppy and came on lighter volumes (<10B shares traded).  The SPX finished the day -0.13% and formed a 2nd consecutive inside day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ruxf!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4ea596bd-0fcb-4f43-9438-5151988715e7_958x712.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ruxf!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4ea596bd-0fcb-4f43-9438-5151988715e7_958x712.jpeg)

Tech could not catch a bid until the afternoon and the QQQ finished the day -0.29%.  For the time being, the QQQ has remained above recent September lows.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OCyh!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3390a1b9-a76d-4cf3-929f-738683f65e18_968x729.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OCyh!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3390a1b9-a76d-4cf3-929f-738683f65e18_968x729.jpeg)

The more oversold pockets of the market like small caps, non profitable tech, and the most short baskets also failed to catch much of a bid.  Consumer exposed names struggled, despite WTI finishing down another -2% yesterday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_4xr!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd41a85ad-800e-46af-b2bc-f5ca8cde445b_852x774.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!_4xr!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd41a85ad-800e-46af-b2bc-f5ca8cde445b_852x774.jpeg)
