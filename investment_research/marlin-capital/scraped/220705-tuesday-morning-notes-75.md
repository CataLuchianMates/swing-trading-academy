# Tuesday Morning Notes 7.5

Good morning! I hope everyone enjoyed the long weekend and 4th of July holiday!

**Technical Analysis:**

The market is set to gap down this morning amid recessionary fears. As I pointed out in Friday’s morning notes, The Atlanta Fed’s GDPNow gauge now sees Q2 GDP running at -2.1%. Coupled with Q1’s decline of -1.6%, this would fit the technical definition of recession.

We may already “technically” be in a recession, but the Fed’s job is just beginning. The central bank must inflict more pain to solve inflation. Specifically, in my view, the Fed must orchestrate a significant spike in the unemployment rate. I will be covering all this and more in my upcoming report.

Now, back to the charts. Friday was an “inside day”, and we are immediately threatening to break the day’s lows in pre-market. The $276-$277 price points represent key support levels. If these levels break, the next support is at 52 week lows ($270).

On the upside, the first step would be taking out the 8 EMA ($284) and 21 EMA ($288). As long as we remain below these key MAs, the path of least resistance remains down.
