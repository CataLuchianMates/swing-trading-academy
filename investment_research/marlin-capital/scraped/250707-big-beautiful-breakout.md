#### Broad Market Analysis

**A Bullish Time of Year**

Strong July seasonals are at play once again here in 2025. As I highlighted in [“Heading into July”](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/heading-into-july), **the NDX has finished positive in 17 of the last 18 July’s** for an **average return of +4.2%**.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Xoz4!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fae012fd9-2b52-43da-8411-aa5d50a87029_908x568.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Xoz4!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fae012fd9-2b52-43da-8411-aa5d50a87029_908x568.jpeg)

This is the **best month of the year** for US stocks.

These strong returns tend to be frontloaded, as the **first 2 weeks of July are the best 2-week stretch on the calendar.**

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!53_Q!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6a891910-dded-41dc-9e60-d26fb956eecc_988x658.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!53_Q!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6a891910-dded-41dc-9e60-d26fb956eecc_988x658.jpeg)

In fact, the Nasdaq has been positive in **31 of the last 40 years (78% hit rate)** from June ~26th to July ~14th (the final few days of June + the first 2 weeks of July).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OQNy!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7255f488-b851-4219-a1af-d3bce9605845_844x418.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!OQNy!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7255f488-b851-4219-a1af-d3bce9605845_844x418.jpeg)

**Systematic Buying Frenzy**

Mechanical buying is projected to be a **major bullish tailwind** for US stocks in the **next 3-8 weeks**.

Sustained levels of lower volatility bring systematic inflows back to the forefront, particularly from Vol Control Funds. Total systematic positioning is currently only in the 35th percentile versus history.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iA1V!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe8631e74-43c6-4be1-b3f9-4e4172318d10_630x444.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iA1V!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe8631e74-43c6-4be1-b3f9-4e4172318d10_630x444.jpeg)

**Nomura’s Model projects over +$100B of systematic equity buying in the next month,** the **most it has ever predicted** since being deployed in 2004. Nomura’s McElligott said the model’s output was largely driven by the looming drop in 3M realized Vol (which triggers Vol Control Fund buying).

Overall, **Nomura’s Model predicts $114.2B of Vol Control buying from June 26th - July 26th.** Again, this is the l**argest projected 1M buying in the model’s history.**
