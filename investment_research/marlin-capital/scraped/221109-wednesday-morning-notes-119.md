# Wednesday Morning Notes 11.9

Technical Analysis

The market bounce continued with plenty of intraday chop yesterday as investors and traders await Thursdsy’s CPI report.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!X_t8!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff3561172-b30a-4372-9343-091181c20e82_1213x889.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!X_t8!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff3561172-b30a-4372-9343-091181c20e82_1213x889.jpeg)

The SPX briefly broke its 60 EMA, but was unable to close above the key resistance level. $3833 and yesterday’s high ($3860) are the key resistance levels to watch today.

On the downside, yesterday’s low ($3785) is the key support level that should ideally hold in order for this rally to continue.
