# Tuesday Morning Notes 6.27

Technical Update

The S&P 500 pulled back further below its 8 EMA yesterday, with the QQQ leading the way to the downside. The SPX finished the day -0.45% while the QQQ closed down -1.34%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!w1dx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0799e6a1-6da8-4087-b68a-09e5d0de1a1b_970x720.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!w1dx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0799e6a1-6da8-4087-b68a-09e5d0de1a1b_970x720.jpeg)

The 21 EMA, currently sitting at $4300-$4310, remains the key support level to watch below. A break below $4300 would open the door for a further decline to the 60 EMA level at $4200-$4205.

MDB long setup mentioned yesterday is still valid on a break above $397. Otherwise, I am in no hurry to make any moves here with the indices continuing to shake off overbought conditions. This is a time to wait and observe.
