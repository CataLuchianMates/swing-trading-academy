# Thursday Market Notes 5.26

Technical Analysis:

The indices are trying to bounce as they look to break the 7 week losing streak. The S&P 500 continues to act stronger than the Nasdaq, which failed to close above its 8 EMA yesterday.

**Active Portfolio**

Short DOCU (1/4th of original position)

Short GME (1/4th avg. position)

I plan to likely close out my DOCU short today. For GME, my first entry was mistimed, but I anticipated a potential bounce, so I have not yet added more to my short. I will likely look to double my short position (to a half sized position) in the $125-$135 resistance range. I do not expect to size this short bigger than a half sized position with the stock announcing earnings next week.
