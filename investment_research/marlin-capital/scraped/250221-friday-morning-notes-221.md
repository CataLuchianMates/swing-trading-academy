# Friday Morning Notes 2.21

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Yesterday was one of the more active trading sessions we have seen with sharp momentum unwinds, mostly concentrated in Tech and Financials. The SPX closed -0.43%, NDX -0.48%, and RTY -0.91%.

Momentum 12M winners traded off -2.2% while Goldman’s Meme Basket finished -1.3% (and would have been much more without BABA +8% on earnings). I talked a few times about the uptick in retail activity and yesterday was definitely a flash rug pull in a lot of those more popular/crowded names (PLTR, HIMS, TEM, RDDT, HOOD, etc).

Today is Monthly OpEx which always adds a layer of volatility as we approach. GS Derivatives Desk estimates that over $2.7T of notional option exposure will expire today. Dealers are long $9.8B of S&P 500 gamma at current spot, acting as a market buffer and supporting weakness & muting rallies. The market can move freer next week once OpEx clears.

NVDA earnings will be the big catalyst next week. NVDA is the 2nd largest weighting in the SPX (6.6%) and the options market is implying a 9% move on a $3.4T market cap (~$300B+ move in the name). This will obviously be a major market moving event.

No planned actions for me today. INOD from the [Small Cap 6 portfolio](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-small-caps-with-massive-upside) announced a major earnings beat (+11.7% above consensus on revenue, +26.6% above on EPS) and the stock is now trading over $70 (+30% today). We are now up over +320% on INOD!

This is now our third +300%+ gainer from the [Small Cap 6](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-small-caps-with-massive-upside) (SEZL, WGS, INOD)! It has been a great Q4 earnings season for our Small Cap 6 with AMSC, WGS, and INOD all rallying >20%+ following results. Great earnings from all 3 and we have 3 left to announce.

In my Active Trading account, I am just holding my final 2/3rds of LRCX with a trailing stop above breakeven at $86. No planned actions for me today.
