# Thursday Morning Notes 4.6

Technical Update

The S&P 500’s pullback extended a 2nd day yesterday, with the index closing -0.25% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4HwM!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fac46a21a-f1ec-4011-969c-4138fe0de45a_971x727.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4HwM!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fac46a21a-f1ec-4011-969c-4138fe0de45a_971x727.jpeg)

On a technical level, this is an ordinary pullback so far from the index. Beneath the surface, I noted quite a bit of weakness in some of the top performers from this rally. This is a possible warning sign to note.

We are set for a small gap down this morning towards $4075-$4080 support. Other key support levels below include $4050, and the last line of technical support at $3990-$4000.

On a red to green rally, key resistance levels above include $4100, $4150-$4160 and YTD highs at $4190-$4200.
