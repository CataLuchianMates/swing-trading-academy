# Tuesday Morning Notes 7.25

Technical Update

Low volume trading action to start the week on Monday as investors await MSFT & GOOGL earnings and Wednesday’s FOMC decision. The S&P 500 finished +0.4% on the day while the QQQ finished +0.16%.
