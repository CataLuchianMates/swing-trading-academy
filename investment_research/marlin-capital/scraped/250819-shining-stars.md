# Shining Stars

It was a flat open to this week for the market, with the S&P 500 trading in its narrowest intraday range since March 2024.

It was not flat for us at Marlin Capital, though.

2 of my [Top 4 Stars of Q2 Earnings](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/my-top-4-stars-of-q2-earnings) were up over +6% today.

**Starting with APLD, which soared +16% today** ‼️

[Immediately after Q2 earnings](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/big-tech-blowouts), I said this would be the beginning of a multi-month Bull Run for APLD.

**It’s now up an astonishing +40% in <3 weeks since that was written.**

Today’s +16% catalyst was the [announcement that Applied Digital is breaking ground on Polaris Forge 2](https://substack.com/chat/408010/post/21fe3fd3-7ffa-4177-b1ca-9a898c6e7f9c). Polaris Forge 2 is a $3B, 280 MW AI Data Center campus in North Dakota.

Here’s why this is MASSIVE news for Applied Digital, and why it means another major Hyperscaler deal is likely coming soon.

Again, this was 1 of the [2 main reasons](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/earnings-gaps-everywhere) we LOVED APLD’s earnings report.

A deal is in the works.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ElgR!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F68cb5659-f1c9-4abb-aa9f-4ce0e29dfedf_1191x739.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ElgR!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F68cb5659-f1c9-4abb-aa9f-4ce0e29dfedf_1191x739.jpeg)

Including Polaris Forge 2, Applied Digital's evolution from a ~$160M annual revenue Bitcoin mining company to a potential $1B+ AI data center business is nearly complete. During the company’s July conference call, Applied Digital’s CEO revealed that they are currently in advanced negotiations for Polaris Forge 2.

**I crunched the numbers on Polaris Forge 2.**

Polaris Forge 2 is a 280 MW Data Center campus (compared to ~400 MW for Polaris Forge 1). For Polaris Forge 1, CoreWeave is paying $11B over 15 years ($733M per year) for 400 MW.

**That’s $1.83M in revenue per MW per year.**

Using the $1.83M per MW per year from the CoreWeave deal as a baseline…280 MW × $1.83M per MW per year = **$512M annually.**

**If Applied Digital can secure a deal for Polaris Forge 2 with similar terms to the CoreWeave deal, it would be worth $450M-$550M in revenue per year.**

Combined with Polaris Forge 1’s $733M (from CRWV), this would total $1.24B+ per year, exceeding the $1B+ threshold I talked about in my [SMID Cap 6 report](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/6-smid-caps-with-massive-upside-potential).

In my view, APLD stock is a gift below $16. My target is $30+ here.
