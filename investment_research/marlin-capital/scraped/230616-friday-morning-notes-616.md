# Friday Morning Notes 6.16

Technical Update

The S&P 500 continued its melt up rally yesterday with a +1.22% gain and new highs on the year.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ggJj!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8d55e248-bd94-4b8d-b2de-2799667180df_962x724.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ggJj!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F8d55e248-bd94-4b8d-b2de-2799667180df_962x724.jpeg)

This has been a huge week for the index with the Fed hitting the pause button. S&P 500 call volume hit another all time high in yesterday’s session as the chase is on.

Today is OpEx, so be prepared for a potentially more volatile session, particularly in the final couple hours of the day.

ARKK - I will be looking to take more profits at my $45 target, leaving half the position

IWO - Next target level is $245 where I will look to take more profits, leaving half the position
