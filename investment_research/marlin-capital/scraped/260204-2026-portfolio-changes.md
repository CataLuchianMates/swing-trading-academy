# 2026 Portfolio Changes

Marlin Capital Readers,

Today is a brutal day for many names. In fact, it changes what I said yesterday (that many of our stocks were still holding up great). The bearish warning signs have been building up underneath the surface since October. Now, they are too loud to ignore.

Adjustments must be made to my 2026 Portfolio.

I will be sticking to my 2026 plan and managing each name in the portfolio individually. To make it simple, any stock that I believe to have entered/be entering in its [Stage 4 downtrend](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/grinch-selloff?utm_source=publication-search) (its Bear phase) will be a sell. If I believe the stock has ‘more than likely’ topped for the cycle, there is no reason for me to continue to hold it.

With that said, I am exiting 18 different names today.
