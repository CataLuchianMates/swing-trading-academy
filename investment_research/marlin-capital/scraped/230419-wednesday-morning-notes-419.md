# Wednesday Morning Notes 4.19

Technical Update

The market chop continued on Tuesday as the morning gap up was sold into immediately. The SPX closed +0.09% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!3kRx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F27c2417f-841c-4a77-acea-8ed9c02ffca0_1221x915.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!3kRx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F27c2417f-841c-4a77-acea-8ed9c02ffca0_1221x915.jpeg)

Technically, there is no major change to the setup I have been discussing. With that said, we are set for a gap down this morning across all indices.

Key support levels to watch below include $4150, $4130-$4135, $4100, and $4075-$4080.

Key resistance level to watch above include $4160, and $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).
