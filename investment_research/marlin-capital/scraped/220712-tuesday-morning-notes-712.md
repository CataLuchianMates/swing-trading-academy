# Tuesday Morning Notes 7.12

Technical Analysis

The market took a step back yesterday as we broke Friday’s lows in the morning and never looked back. So far, the QQQ has been unable to get above the 50 day MA on this rally attempt.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!xUwp!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1de38dcc-1aa3-4601-9f7e-7c7a776caf11_888x648.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!xUwp!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1de38dcc-1aa3-4601-9f7e-7c7a776caf11_888x648.jpeg)

All eyes on the CPI number to be released tomorrow pre-market. Last month’s CPI surprised to the upside, and we were hitting new lows within 3 days. Nothing in the technicals can prevent that from happening again if inflation has still not peaked yet.
