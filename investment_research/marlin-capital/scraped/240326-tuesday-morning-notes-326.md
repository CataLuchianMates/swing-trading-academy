# Tuesday Morning Notes 3.26

**Daily Market Commentary:**

It was an extremely quiet session to kick off the week yesterday with trading volumes tracking -20% vs 20dma.  The Russell 2000 finished +0.16% while the SPX and QQQ each finished down ~30-40bps.

Mega Caps were weak on news the EU launches probe into Meta, Apple, and Alphabet under sweeping new tech law.  INTC and AMD were also weaker on China seeking to limit the use of US-made chips in government computers.

COIN – Still holding strong on this long term swing.  Great day yesterday and I like the way this stock is setting up for a move through $300.

My stop will remain a close below $230.

CELH – Not a great close/reversal candle yesterday after briefly hitting $95 in the morning.  With that said, I will let this trade play out and my stop will remain a close below $85.

Top trade setup today –

CAVA – Very strong name consolidating nicely at recent highs.  We have a triple inside day after yesterday.

Long above $69.19

Closing Stop below $67.60 (-2.3% risk)
