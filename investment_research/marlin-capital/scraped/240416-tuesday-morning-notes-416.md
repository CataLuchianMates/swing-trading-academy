# Tuesday Morning Notes 4.16

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Big sell off in the market to kick off the week yesterday with the S&P 500 finishing -1.2% and closing right at its 60 EMA.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!eNBm!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F53adbe1d-5946-4432-a794-c850fd1daee7_971x722.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!eNBm!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F53adbe1d-5946-4432-a794-c850fd1daee7_971x722.jpeg)

Israel/Iran headlines continued to weigh on broader risk sentiment while a hot retail sales report in the AM drove rates higher.  The 10 YR broke through 4.6%, and long duration remained under heavy pressure with Low Profitability R2K -3% and Non-Profitable Tech -3.5%.

The QQQ broke down below the bottom of its 7 week trading range yesterday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!weS3!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe0ca46ef-140a-40c8-8742-33b05b7ebf24_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!weS3!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe0ca46ef-140a-40c8-8742-33b05b7ebf24_1396x653.png)

On the daily chart, the QQQ closed right at its 60 EMA.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Df1T!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ffaaa3ced-61c6-400e-9864-65e433a1c000_964x707.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Df1T!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ffaaa3ced-61c6-400e-9864-65e433a1c000_964x707.jpeg)

With both the SPX and QQQ sitting right at 60 EMA support, today (and the rest of this week) suddenly becomes very important.  I will be waiting and watching to see how the indices react to their first major support test since this rally began last October.  No planned trades for me today.

MU – My only current long remaining.  Still holding 2/3rds of my original entry with a stop loss in the money at $119.
