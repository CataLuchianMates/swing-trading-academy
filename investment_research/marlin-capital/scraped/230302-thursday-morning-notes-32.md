# Thursday Morning Notes 3.2

Technical Update

The S&P 500 closed just above its 200 day MA ($3940) yesterday after another test of critical support.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!1wt2!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbd09b645-01bb-406d-ad46-8964ee0fc4d8_962x735.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!1wt2!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbd09b645-01bb-406d-ad46-8964ee0fc4d8_962x735.jpeg)

A breakdown from this Bear flag pattern looks imminent here. We are set to gap below the $3940 level this morning.

There is not much support left for the SPX until $3900. Other key support levels below include $3840-$3850 and $3800. In my view, the selling is likely to accelerate if we cannot recapture the $3940 level in today’s session.

On a bounce, key resistance levels above include previous 200 day support at $3940, $3970-$3975, and $3995-$4000.
