# Monday Market Thread 3.28

I wanted to share this quote from 3x trading National Champion Mark Minervini:

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!AsY9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbd717677-b12b-47e3-a68f-c4fecdcf9abb_1284x1136.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!AsY9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbd717677-b12b-47e3-a68f-c4fecdcf9abb_1284x1136.jpeg)

This is 100% true, as those traders that have a propensity for gambling do not last long in this game. In my experience, the best traders have a well defined strategy with rules, and do not deviate from their proven strategy.

For me, I simply do not trade stocks under their 50 and 200 day moving averages.  As a rule, these stocks are off limits in my Active Portfolio, as I am a trend following trader, not a counter-trend trader.  This excludes most all growth tech stocks in the current market we are in.

Energy would be my focus here from a technical standpoint, but I expect these stocks to be very headline driven based off any new developments in Russia.  This adds a layer of risk that you must account for in position sizing when trading energy stocks.
