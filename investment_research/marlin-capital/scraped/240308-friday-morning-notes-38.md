# Friday Morning Notes 3.8

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Continued strength in the market yesterday as the early week dip was bought right up where it needed to be (key support levels all held).  The S&P 500 closed +1.03%, NDX +1.5%, and R2K +0.69%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cteH!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc2d4fe11-eb91-4014-8a3f-6344f5a2ff49_1197x895.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cteH!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc2d4fe11-eb91-4014-8a3f-6344f5a2ff49_1197x895.jpeg)

395 members of the S&P 500 participated in the rally yesterday as breadth continues to improve.  NVDA accounted for 19% of yesterday's rally, but after that it dropped off to a more healthy distribution.  Traders upped bets for rate cuts, pricing in 100bps of reduction this year.

We are set for a continuation gap higher this morning following the NFP report.  The Russell 2000 is the strongest index pre-market, which bodes well for my TNA (3x Small Caps) long position.

TNA – has struggled to close strong this week but we are set for a gap above $42 this morning.  The weekly and daily charts align and I am still holding my full position (from $41.25) for this breakout.  Moving my stop loss to $38.85 and otherwise no changes.  First target = $45.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!J5KX!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fea435ce2-6c7e-4bbe-a27a-96af7915a39f_1204x885.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!J5KX!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fea435ce2-6c7e-4bbe-a27a-96af7915a39f_1204x885.jpeg)

IOT – Gapping up >10% this morning after a solid Q4 print that saw them guide both Q1 and FY revs & EPS higher.  This is my top watch for an earnings gapper setup today.

DOCU, GRND 2 other potential earnings gapper setups to watch today. Not looking to buy into today’s gap otherwise and just holding TNA with my stop in place.
