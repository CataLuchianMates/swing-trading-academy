# Thursday Morning Notes 5.11

Technical Update

We saw an overall bullish day post-CPI yesterday, with the S&P 500 closing +0.46% on the day. Mega Cap tech and the Nasdaq continued to lead.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!d372!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff31a42b0-34e4-4049-9ed4-5448d3d04664_964x723.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!d372!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff31a42b0-34e4-4049-9ed4-5448d3d04664_964x723.jpeg)

Intraday, the SPX filled its morning gap with buyers stepping in at key $4100 support.

$4150 remains the key resistance level to watch ahead. A break above $4150 opens the door for a test of $4200 and YTD highs.

Key support levels to watch below include $4100-$4110 (21 EMA), $4056-$4060 (60 EMA), $4040-$4030 and $4000.
