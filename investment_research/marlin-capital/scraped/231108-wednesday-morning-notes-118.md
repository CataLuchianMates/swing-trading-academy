# Wednesday Morning Notes 11.8

Daily Market Commentary

The S&P 500 finished positive (+0.28%) for the 7th consecutive session in a quiet/low volume day for the market.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oVwj!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb0865d23-761b-414a-9b84-8863e2102ed3_987x721.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oVwj!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb0865d23-761b-414a-9b84-8863e2102ed3_987x721.jpeg)

The elements of a short squeeze continue to be present with expensive software names leading yesterday’s rally (+4.5% and led by DDOG +28%).

Yields have remained stable throughout the early part of this week with the 10Y currently sitting at 4.58%.

Fed Chair Jerome Powell is speaking today as well as 5 other Fed speakers throughout Wednesday.
