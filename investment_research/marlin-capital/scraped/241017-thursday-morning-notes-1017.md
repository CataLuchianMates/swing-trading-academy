# Thursday Morning Notes 10.17

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Choppy day yesterday with the SPX +0.47%, NDX +0.07%, and RTY outperforming +1.67%.

We saw the notable dispersion across the market with the Nasdaq unchanged and Small Caps outperforming +1.7%. There was built up anxiety in Tech/Semis following ASML’s big miss, but that appears to have flipped this morning following TSM’s report.

The numbers from Taiwan Semi were solid, and TSM is +9.6% in the pre after rev guidance better than expected/said 2025 CapEx likely to be higher than 2024/said AI demand extremely robust and just beginning. This is unquestionably helping sentiment in Semis and Tech with the QQQ set to open this morning >1% higher.

SN - No changes here. Stop at $104.77 on the rest.

MU - Getting boosted by the TSM numbers this morning. My stop is set at $102.70. First target = $114-$115
