# Thursday Morning Notes 10.31

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US stocks are selling off this morning following MSFT and META earnings last night.

MSFT - Solid FY1Q revenue and EPS beat with Azure in-line; FYQ2 Azure growth guide **disappointed** at +31-32%

The slight deceleration (down ~2%) in Azure growth is to blame for the stock’s weakness today, in my view. With that said, this issue may be short lived, as Goldman projects Azure growth to re-accelerate in 2H ‘25 (to +34-35%). I would also expect the stock’s weakness to be short lived.

META - Reported solid Q3 EPS beat with Q4 revenue guidance above consensus at the midpoint. Expectations were elevated into the print & positioning skewed long.

The stock is -3% this morning. The perceived negative, in my view - Management continues to guide for sizable CapEx growth into a multi-year investment cycle to support the company’s AI efforts, including a raising of the FY24 CapEx guide and reiterating guidance for significant CapEx growth in ‘25.
