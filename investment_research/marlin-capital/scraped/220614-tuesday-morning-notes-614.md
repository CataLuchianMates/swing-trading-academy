# Tuesday Morning Notes 6.14

Technical Analysis:

The troubling technical setup I talked about over the weekend played out for the indices yesterday as both the SPY and QQQ broke down and made new 52 week lows.

Now, we find ourselves very short term oversold heading into Tuesday. We are $20 extended below the 8 EMA heading into the day, indicating that we may be due for a short term bounce.

With that said, there are a couple important things to note. (1) The FOMC meeting is tomorrow; and (2) Technicals matter less in the face of real panic.

It appears like we may be entering a period of panicked liquidation. However, it would not be surprising to see some choppy action today as we head into tomorrow’s FOMC meeting.
