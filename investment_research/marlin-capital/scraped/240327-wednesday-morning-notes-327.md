# Wednesday Morning Notes 3.27

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Tuesday was another choppy, low volume session for the indices with the SPX finishing -0.28% and the QQQ finishing -0.32%.

Participation was light as we drag through the shortened holiday week (market is closed on Friday).  CTAs are in a holding pattern, corporates are in a blackout period (we usually see a 30% drop off in flows), and tax season is upon us.  We had a sell off into the bell with a very large MOC, likely an indication of pension rebalance selling/quarter end rebalancing.

Obesity drugs, expensive software, and infrastructure stocks led the pack while China ADRs, HY Debt Sensitivity, and Liquid Regional Banks were the weakest groups.
