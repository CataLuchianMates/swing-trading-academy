# Tuesday Morning Notes 10.25

Technical Analysis

The market rally continued to start off the week, with the indices each closing up above 1%. Buyers showed up on the Morning dip under the 21 EMA.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-swG!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F59194e90-dfa7-4d8d-acd0-f5a95e4216b8_1033x783.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-swG!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F59194e90-dfa7-4d8d-acd0-f5a95e4216b8_1033x783.jpeg)

For the QQQ, we have now cleared the key $275 resistance level (21 EMA). This points to the possibility a further rally up to the 60 EMA level ($287). It would be unsurprising to see some backtesting of this recent rally off the lows before the next push higher.

Key support levels below include $274-$275 and $270-$271.

Key resistance levels ahead include $279-$280, $284-285, and $287-$290.

Microsoft and Google report earnings after the bell today.
