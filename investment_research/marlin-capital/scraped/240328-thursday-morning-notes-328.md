# Thursday Morning Notes 3.28

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Today is the final day of Q1 (market is closed tomorrow for Good Friday).

Yesterday was reflective of a catch up trade (sell YTD winners, buy YTD losers) with the RTY outperforming the SPX by 146 bps.  10Y yields edged 4bps lower.  The tape remained squeezey with GS most short basket popping +2.9%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pY8W!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F35e7e29b-b512-4e79-9a06-1291a847e8f7_1210x885.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pY8W!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F35e7e29b-b512-4e79-9a06-1291a847e8f7_1210x885.jpeg)

There were a ton of outsized moves to the upside screening > 2 sigma.  To name a few – expensive defensives +1.5%, long value +2.8%, renewables +4%, CRE +2.6%, regional banks +3.7%.  The market exploded higher during the last 20 minutes of the session (reminder – the last few days have had large sell MOC’s which were likely Q-end/pension rebalance driven).

The big news from yesterday was the move in Small Caps (IWM), which had its highest daily close in over 2 years.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oMw9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb4bc10f6-1f2d-4a9e-a685-79bcd2618408_971x719.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oMw9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb4bc10f6-1f2d-4a9e-a685-79bcd2618408_971x719.jpeg)

Today is the end of the weekly/monthly/quarterly candles, and IWM looks to close on highs (which would bode well for a continuation rally into Q2).

CELH – Sold off on news yesterday and broke through my key support levels.  Took the loss and position closed.

COIN – Still holding this longer term swing with a closing stop below $230.

CAVA – Still holding with a closing stop below $67.6.

MU – Pulled in to $117 support yesterday allowing me to initiate a starter long position, but the dip was bought up into the close.  Still looking for a pull back to $114-$116 to add more to my position.
