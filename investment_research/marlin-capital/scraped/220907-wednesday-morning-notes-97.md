# Wednesday Morning Notes 9.7

Technical Analysis

Stocks fell again yesterday in what was an overall choppy session for the market.  The QQQ broke the key $292-$293 support level I discussed in yesterday’s morning notes, but did not follow through to the downside, and closed just above the level at $293.05.

The technical structure remains bearish, but a further oversold bounce is still not out of the question.  $295-$296 and $300 are the key resistance levels to watch ahead on any rally attempt, On the 15 minute, the $290-$293 level represents a crucial multi-day support level.

I will be monitoring SQQQ for a potential entry.

Another potential short setting up is the Biotech sector. XBI broke down yesterday and looks primed for a future re-test of 52 week lows.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Fs50!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1fd9436b-3342-428c-b308-75571abe6bc0_1031x651.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Fs50!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1fd9436b-3342-428c-b308-75571abe6bc0_1031x651.jpeg)

LABD is a 3x Short Biotech ETF that will be my preferred way to play this move. The short term technical setup (15 minute) on LABD is confirming the larger biotech breakdown.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nuoY!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe850e3d6-ad50-4ff7-add6-2149182bc678_1013x619.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nuoY!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe850e3d6-ad50-4ff7-add6-2149182bc678_1013x619.jpeg)

Our quant systems believe biotech has a very high probability of re-testing new lows, which would result in a 60-80% move in LABD. The key will be to try to catch the move while keeping a tight stop (within 10%).
