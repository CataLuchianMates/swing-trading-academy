Technical Update

Another day of low volume chop in the S&P 500 yesterday with the index closing -0.46%.

More importantly, this morning’s CPI number has the market set for a +0.7% gap up.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mkim!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd4fa2235-46dd-49fe-9a2f-d64bf82c8651_965x722.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mkim!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd4fa2235-46dd-49fe-9a2f-d64bf82c8651_965x722.jpeg)

Technically, this is a bullish looking setup with this morning’s pre-market move. If the index can break and stay above $4150 today, we are setting up nicely for a test of YTD highs ($4200).

Key resistance level to watch above include $4150 and $4190-$4200 (YTD highs).

Key support levels to watch below include $4100-$4110 (21 EMA), $4056-$4060 (60 EMA), $4040-$4030 and $4000.
