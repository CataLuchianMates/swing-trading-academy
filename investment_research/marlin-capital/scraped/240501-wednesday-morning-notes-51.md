# Wednesday Morning Notes 5.1

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The major indices all finished lower on Tuesday with RTY -2.1%, NDX -1.9%, and SPX -1.6%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bKaX!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F444715c6-e492-4028-9b84-f1d5f4edb2c2_964x729.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bKaX!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F444715c6-e492-4028-9b84-f1d5f4edb2c2_964x729.jpeg)

Another upside surprise on wage inflation with ECI rising 1.2% in Q1, +4.5% on a quarterly annualized basis, well above the 3.5% pace that we hoped to see to get inflation back to normal levels.  We also had unfavorable commentary from Yellen – “concerned about where we’re going with US deficit” as well as some month end dynamics at play.

With rates continuing to push higher, more speculative pockets of the market were under pressure (Bitcoin Sensitives -9.5%, Memes -3%, Most Short -3%).  The market melted down into the bell with investors skittish ahead of today’s FOMC and AMZN EPS, which ultimately beat and guided above with AWS re-accel to +17% YoY (cons. +13%).  Other key reports – PINS +17% on big beat + raise (watch for earnings gapper setup), AMD largely inline but stock down, SMCI reversed after the numbers failed to clear the high bar, SBUX -10% on a miss, POWL and ROOT both up >20% (potential earnings gappers to watch today).

The big event today is the FOMC meeting conclusion.  While there is a near 0% chance of a rate or a cut at this meeting, Powell’s commentary on the future outlook will be very important for the market.
