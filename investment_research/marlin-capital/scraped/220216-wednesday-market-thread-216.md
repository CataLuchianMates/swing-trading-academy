Active Portfolio:

100% Cash.

Avoiding the chop is key to my strategy in my Active Portfolio. As legendary trader Jesse Livermore said, “Wait for the trend to develop, then jump on.” Currently, we are in a range/chop phase, so I want to remain patient until there are more opportunities. The biggest moves come after a consolidation phase, but it can be costly to bet which way we will break out of the range. As a trader, I strictly follow what the tape tells me.
