# Wednesday Morning Notes 9.21

Technical Analysis

It was all chop again for the market yesterday as traders await tomorrow’s FOMC decision and presser. The QQQ has traded in a tight 5-6 point range since Friday’s gap down. $285-$286 has become the key support level to watch, while $290-$291 has become the key resistance level.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!DILB!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd6dc205a-a892-4669-a9b1-cdb6135313ee_1013x619.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!DILB!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd6dc205a-a892-4669-a9b1-cdb6135313ee_1013x619.jpeg)

We are setting up for a break in either direction in today’s session. Technically, our quant systems are suggesting a possible “event clearing squeeze” to $295-$300. But, given that real rates continue to skyrocket higher (and most seem to be “expecting” a post-Fed squeeze), one must also stay open to the possibility of an immediate breakdown and move lower today.
