# Wednesday Morning Notes 1.25

Technical Analysis

The market staged a choppy session yesterday on lower volume. No key resistance or support levels were broken during the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iZCo!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F23289d16-1021-4d60-a044-6e4e3886a7d3_1036x779.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iZCo!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F23289d16-1021-4d60-a044-6e4e3886a7d3_1036x779.jpeg)

Now, we are set for a sizable gap down (>1%) this morning after Microsoft earnings/guidance failed to impress.

Key support levels to watch below include $3965-$3970 (200 SMA), $3950, $3920-$3930 and $3890-$3900.

A close back below the 200 SMA ($3960) would be a bad sign for bulls and indicate that this rally may finally be over.

Bulls would like to see the index push back above the $4000 level. Further resistance levels above include $4020-$4030, $4050, and $4100.
