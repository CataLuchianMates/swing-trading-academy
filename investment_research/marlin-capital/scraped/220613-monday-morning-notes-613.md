Technical Analysis (from Friday):

On a technical basis, we saw a continuation from Thursday’s move to the downside. Support levels to watch on the SPY are $386, and 52 week lows at $380. If/when these levels break, I would look out below, because this is where we may start to see some real panic/capitulatory selling. My longer term target on the SPY is $2900-$3300. On the QQQ, we have another “h-sell” pattern. The charts are saying major caution is warranted here.
