# Friday Morning Notes 9.9

Technical Analysis

Yesterday was a choppy day for US stocks, with the indices flipping between red and green. In the final hour of the session, stocks rallied into the bell and the QQQ closed just above the $300 resistance level at $300.51.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!u3kH!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbb93b60b-1745-497a-b45e-43421d695403_1013x619.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!u3kH!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbb93b60b-1745-497a-b45e-43421d695403_1013x619.png)

Now, we are set for a gap up this morning to the $304 level. The chart is suggesting a possible move to the $308-$310 level, but there is resistance at $305 to get above first. On the downside, key support levels to watch include the $300 level and $295-$296.
