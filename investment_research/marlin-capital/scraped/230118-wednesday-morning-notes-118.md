# Wednesday Morning Notes 1.18

Technical Update

A slow start to the week yesterday as the battle for $4000 continues. The S&P 500 closed the day at $3990 after breaking $4k in the morning.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6Es1!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe72d2b8a-a6f8-475a-8417-f61cdbc561b6_1071x814.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!6Es1!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe72d2b8a-a6f8-475a-8417-f61cdbc561b6_1071x814.jpeg)

We are set for a gap up above the $4000 level this morning. Key resistance levels to watch above include $4020-$4030, $4060, and $4100.

If the market reverses and the $4000 level cannot hold as support, further support levels below include $3980, $3940-$3950, and $3900-$3910.
