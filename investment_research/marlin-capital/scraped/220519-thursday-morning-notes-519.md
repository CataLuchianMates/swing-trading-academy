# Thursday Morning Notes 5.19

The counter-trend rally fizzled out yesterday, giving way to another beatdown for US stocks. Technically, this is an ugly picture and the chart is pointing to new lows.

One thing to keep in mind is options expiration this week. Put/call ratio rose substantially yesterday, so it seems the max pain trade would be premium burn for the rest of the week. Something to keep in mind for traders as the market doesn’t like to make it too easy for anyone.
