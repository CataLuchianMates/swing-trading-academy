For me, risk management is the name of the game. In my experience, when I’ve been on the fence about holding or selling, a sell has more often been the right choice. This means you might get shaken out easier, but as Mark Minervini says, “If you’re not feeling like an idiot OFTEN, you’re not managing risk. Get used to it!”

Discipline over hope.
