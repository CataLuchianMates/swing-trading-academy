# Tuesday Morning Notes 7.19

Technical Analysis:

The market saw a big reversal yesterday following the morning gap up.  The QQQ was rejected (for a third straight time) at the $296-$297 resistance level I talked about in yesterday’s morning notes.  The top of this range has been very strong resistance on the QQQ for the last month.  This remains a market to sell into strength, not buy.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nyYE!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F57eaedac-b59f-44d2-b721-192c4817db9a_1013x619.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!nyYE!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F57eaedac-b59f-44d2-b721-192c4817db9a_1013x619.jpeg)

For the QQQ, we are still rangebound and heading into some big earnings this week that have the potential to swing the indices (NFLX, TSLA, SNAP).  This could mean more chop for the indices, unless they are universally (much?) worse than expected.  On the QQQ, $287-$288 and $280 are the key support levels to watch below.  If the market can hold support, earnings season has the potential to bring some trading opportunities in individual standout earnings names.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!b2Mb!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1e8da36c-60bd-475c-b3e7-b5f2680fa206_891x653.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!b2Mb!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1e8da36c-60bd-475c-b3e7-b5f2680fa206_891x653.jpeg)

We are set for another gap up this morning after yesterday’s reversal. $296-$297 remains the key resistance level for bulls to get through. Netflix will be the first of the FAANG stocks to announce earnings after hours this afternoon.
