# Monday Morning Notes 11.28

Technical Update:

In my view, this rally off lows is in its final stages (if not already complete). For the S&P 500, price has bounced up to (near) the 200 day moving average ($4057).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8A6n!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F82dee355-2250-41b7-aa7f-65b074b85806_1206x898.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8A6n!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F82dee355-2250-41b7-aa7f-65b074b85806_1206x898.jpeg)

On the weekly chart, the index has also rallied up to resistance in the 40 week moving average ($4043).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Nm9i!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F429f674b-3586-49d4-8764-3b412e6f6f97_1126x863.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Nm9i!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F429f674b-3586-49d4-8764-3b412e6f6f97_1126x863.jpeg)

In my view, the technicals are pointing to a reversal beginning this week (or at latest, next week),

We are set for a gap down this morning to start off the week. For the SPX, the key resistance levels to watch are those mentioned above ($4040-$4060).

Key support levels include $4000, $3950, and $3900-$3920. In my view, a break back below the 60 EMA ($3900) would suggest a high probability the rally is over.

My target on the next wave down remains $2900-$3300. This is a downside move I will look to capture short (via ES futures & potentially certain individual tech names).

Patience remains the key for shorts here, as many have gotten violently squeezed by entering short too early.
