Technical Analysis

Once again, inflation has come in hotter than expected. So far, the result has been a big move down in equity futures and spike in yields.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bpCu!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6a173851-fa89-4d03-95c8-39e4e8a0f20e_977x718.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bpCu!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6a173851-fa89-4d03-95c8-39e4e8a0f20e_977x718.jpeg)

Bulls needed a lower than expected reading to help turn things around, especially given how oversold we already are. With today’s report, there is nothing stopping the equity indices from hitting my wave 3 targets (SPY - $2900-$3300, QQQ - $210-$230).
