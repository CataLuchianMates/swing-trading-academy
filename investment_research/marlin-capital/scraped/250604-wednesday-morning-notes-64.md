# Wednesday Morning Notes 6.4

The market rallied yesterday with the SPX finishing +0.58%, NDX +0.79%, and RTY +1.59%.

The AI complex continues to propel stocks higher following a big beat and raise quarter from CRDO, nuclear power deal between META/CEG (despite CEG fade), Data Center deal with CRWV and APLD, and recent story that Google’s driverless service Waymo now does more ride in SF than Lyft, etc. A better JOLTs print eased some Macro fears heading into NFP.

I talked on Monday about how Hedge Funds are fully chasing this rally now. New data out from Goldman Prime yesterday shows that May was a largest monthly buying from Hedge Funds on record.

The top 4 buying days in May together made up 81% of this month’s notional net buying, which point to signs of forced buying from funds.

Yet even after the record buying in May, discretionary Hedge Funds still remain underweight US equities.
