# Marlin Capital 2022 UPDATE

Happy New Year to all my subscribers!

I would like to announce some exciting changes coming to Marlin Capital in 2022.

All subscribers will be invited to join the Marlin Capital Trading Room, which is a Discord group where I will be posting REAL TIME trade alerts, daily news updates, market commentary, and options flow.  The Discord group will greatly add to the user experience by introducing real time alerts and making updates easily accessible.

The Marlin Capital Substack will continue to be used for reports and articles, as Substack designs it to be used for.  I will also be adding a monthly Podcast where I will be reviewing the markets and important topics once per month.  All subscribers will get access to the Substack and Discord.

Marlin Capital is introducing a new pricing plan that better reflects the needs of my subscribers, while allowing the service to keep improving.  The pricing will be increased as follows:

$39.99 for Monthly Plan

$399.99 for Yearly Plan

The price increase will NOT affect current yearly subscribers.  I am giving ALL current monthly subscribers the option to upgrade their plans with the current pricing ($230 per year).  The price increase will officially take place tomorrow for new subscribers (1/3), but all current monthly subscribers will be given the option to upgrade for $230 per year until their subscription expires.

I would like to thank all my founding members for staying patient through the growing pains of starting a new service.  It has not been easy, but I am certain the Discord trading room and monthly podcast will greatly improve Marlin Capital.

The Discord group is a must for any serious trader.  Don’t miss your chance to receive real time alerts, invaluable data points, resources, new ideas, etc. from a Pro Trader and former Asset Manager who beat the market by 40% in 2021.
