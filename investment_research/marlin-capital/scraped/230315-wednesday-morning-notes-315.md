# Wednesday Morning Notes 3.15

Technical Update

The S&P 500 rallied following in-line CPI data yesterday, finishing +1.68% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!h2is!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3ca5aca5-023a-473f-9a52-4efb21e7c692_1191x895.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!h2is!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F3ca5aca5-023a-473f-9a52-4efb21e7c692_1191x895.jpeg)

The index made a daily high of $3937, just 2 points below the 200 SMA before pulling back a bit at the end of the day.

In my view, as long as the SPX remains below the 200 SMA resistance level (and 21 and 60 EMAs), the technical setup remains bearish here.

We are set for a sizable gap down this morning. Key support levels below include $3900-$3905, $3870-$3875, $3850-$3855 and $3800-$3810.

Key resistance levels to watch above include $3935-$3940, $3970-$3975, and $3990-$4000.
