# Tuesday Morning Notes 11.12

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The market kicked off the week on a high note yesterday with the SPX closing the day at $6001, marking its 51st ATH of the 2024 calendar year.

We saw a continuation of the “Trump trade” (my top Trump trades - TSLA, COIN, PLTR, Small Caps - specifically my top 6). Despite the Bond Market being closed for Veteran’s Day, volumes were surprisingly busy, with volumes exceeding 15B shares for the 4th straight session.

Retail was clearly involved with Bitcoin Sensitive names finishing +19.7%, Meme Stocks +7%, and Retail Favorites +3.7%. Flows were reflective of this behavior, with significant buying interest in COIN, SOFI, and HOOD.

Semis were the laggard on the day, finishing -2.8% on TSM China restrictions, which spotlights expected increased US China trade tensions. MPWR did not help the group, closing -15% off an Edgewater note discussing Monolithic’s allocation to Nvidia’s Blackwell GPUs is in jeopardy due to performance problems with its power management ICs.

Active Portfolio -

It has been a huge rally since the election, and while I remain bullish into year end, some consolidation/a pullback has become much needed here. With that said, I expect any real pullback to be bought up and we may just see consolidation in the indices given how strong the market is acting post-election. I would not be adding new long positions up here without a couple consolidation days first, as the risk/reward is skewed the downside after such a furious upside move.

LRN - Now trading above $103. I’m out 2/3rds and +21% on the rest of this long position. For new traders, please review my [Earnings Gapper Trade Setup post](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-earnings-gapper-trade-setup?utm_source=publication-search). LRN was an inside 1 HR setup off earnings day.
