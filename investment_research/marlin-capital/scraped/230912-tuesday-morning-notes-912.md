# Tuesday Morning Notes 9.12

Technical Update

The market rallied to kick off the week yesterday with the S&P 500 finishing +0.67% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MxY4!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F080d1da5-421e-4a66-a33b-cefeff64983f_962x721.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MxY4!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F080d1da5-421e-4a66-a33b-cefeff64983f_962x721.jpeg)

We closed just above the 8 EMA resistance level at $4475. Next up in the way of resistance for the SPX is $4500 and $4540-$4550.

We are set for a small gap down this morning. Key support levels to watch below include $4460, $4420-$4430, $4400, and $4330-$4350.

I remain holding COUR and CLS into Tuesday’s session. CLS gapped above my inside day entry setup level yesterday (and did not fill the gap/revisit the level), so I did not add on.
