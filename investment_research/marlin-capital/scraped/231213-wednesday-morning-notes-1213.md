# Wednesday Morning Notes 12.13

**Daily Market Commentary:**

After a benign CPI reading yesterday morning, we saw extremely tight trading ranges with small outperformance by NDX and a further drop in implied volatility.  The S&P 500 finished +0.46% on the day while the VIX closed at multi-year lows (pre Covid 2020 numbers).

Mega Cap Tech led the charge yesterday while commodity sensitive and renewables underperformed the most.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LoSB!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe88e91a6-e626-460a-bf83-315a9095bd10_1102x1295.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!LoSB!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe88e91a6-e626-460a-bf83-315a9095bd10_1102x1295.jpeg)

Yesterday saw the most inflows into the S&P Tech Sector (XLK) in the last 5 years.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!318b!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6ea0722b-9859-42ae-9547-70b9c7bd8d19_1179x807.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!318b!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F6ea0722b-9859-42ae-9547-70b9c7bd8d19_1179x807.jpeg)

Fed expectations were little changed into today’s meeting, which is largely expected to be a non-event with the FOMC leaving rates unchanged once again.  More important than the decision itself will be Fed Chair Powell’s tone and commentary regarding the Fed’s future path (the market is pricing in 4-6 rates cuts in 2024, will Powell push back on that consensus view?).

Positioning wise, I am currently holding 1/3rd long TNA and long PATH.  TNA bounced at its 8 EMA support level yesterday and finished the day near flat (-0.22%).  In my view, this looks to be gearing up for its next move higher (targeting $33-35).  My stop loss on the final 1/3rd portion is a close below recent pivot lows ($30.45).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MdUU!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5a2cee37-4a04-43ec-b59c-1827d981de98_1101x829.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!MdUU!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5a2cee37-4a04-43ec-b59c-1827d981de98_1101x829.jpeg)

PATH broke its inside day higher yesterday but struggled to break through $25 resistance.  My stop loss will remain a close below recent pivot lows at $22.5.
