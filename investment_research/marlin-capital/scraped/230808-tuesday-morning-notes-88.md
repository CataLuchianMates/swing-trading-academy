# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.8

Technical Update

The market bounced to start off the week yesterday, closing right between the 21 and 8 EMAs.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lY-g!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdfa1f4be-d9e5-4561-b5e4-28d8ab05b1df_1156x867.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!lY-g!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fdfa1f4be-d9e5-4561-b5e4-28d8ab05b1df_1156x867.jpeg)

The SPX formed an inside day, giving us key defined levels to watch for Tuesday. A break above Monday’s high ($4520) = bullish. A break below Monday’s lows ($4490) = bearish.

We are set for a gap down this morning. I am still holding a starter long position in CLS, which I believe is well setup for a move to $25 and later, $30.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!idY3!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4fcb1a60-a120-43f7-bed3-cc8f4cca1d54_1210x895.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!idY3!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4fcb1a60-a120-43f7-bed3-cc8f4cca1d54_1210x895.jpeg)

With that said, weak general market conditions eventually pressure all stocks, so I am keeping this positions small until we get more clarity from the indices.
