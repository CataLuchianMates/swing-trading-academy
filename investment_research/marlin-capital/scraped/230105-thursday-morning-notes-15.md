# Thursday Morning Notes 1.5

Technical Update

More consolidation yesterday for the S&P 500 as the choppy action between $3800-$3890 continues. We are coming up on 3 weeks in this range now for the SPX.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qZ9l!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc6a8c1d1-5424-47f1-9c29-1cbb34a864c9_1221x925.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qZ9l!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc6a8c1d1-5424-47f1-9c29-1cbb34a864c9_1221x925.jpeg)

We have clear levels to watch on both sides.

On the upside, $3874, $3889, and $3900 are the key resistance levels.

On the downside, yesterday’s low ($3815), and the all important $3800 level are the key support levels.
