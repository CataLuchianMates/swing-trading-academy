# Friday Morning Notes 6.23

Technical Update

The SPX bounced back above its 8 EMA in yesterday’s session, closing +0.37% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Ff4K!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe66ae5f8-a66a-4d93-b4c1-486adceec58e_970x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Ff4K!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe66ae5f8-a66a-4d93-b4c1-486adceec58e_970x726.jpeg)

Large cap tech led the way for the market once again, while we shifted back to net lows for the SPX.

We are set for a gap down this morning. Key support levels to watch below include $4350-$4360, $4325-$4330, and $4300-$4305 (21 EMA).

ARK and IWO - my trailing stop losses hit on both these long trades. Positions closed.
