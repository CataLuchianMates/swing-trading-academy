# Wednesday Morning Notes 10.30

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The NDX finished +1% higher yesterday with strength across mega caps META, MSFT, and AMZN (into earnings) + AVGO / AMD (OpenAI Reuters article) accounted for ~60% of the NDX move. Post bell, we had a number of big earnings reports:

GOOGL came through with a big Cloud beat and slight beat on YouTube / Search. Stock +6% this morning

RDDT Q3 revs beat by ~11% coming in at $3.48M or an acceleration to +68% YoY (vs 54% YoY last qtr) with Ad revs accelerating to +56% YoY (vs 41% YoY last qtr). Q4 Guidance was also above consensus ($385-$400M vs cons $356M, a +10% raise)

SNAP small beat on qtr at $1.37B (+15% YoY) vs cons $1.36B with EBITDA nicely ahead at $132M vs cons $92M…Also announced a $500M share repurchase…DAUs 443M in qtr (NoAm DAUs flat QoQ at 100M).

AMD -9% this morning (stock was +6% during regular session yesterday) after a slight beat on the quarter + inline guide at midpoint (technically ~$50M below at midpoint, the catalyst for the initial sell off today).
