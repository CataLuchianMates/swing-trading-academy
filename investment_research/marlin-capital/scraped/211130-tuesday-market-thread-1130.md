**Fed Comments:**

". a significant change of tone from Powell. . it looks like the Fed wants to create some space to give them the option to raise rates well before the end of next year if they feel they need to. Ending asset purchases earlier than June 2022 would create this space."

My Thoughts:

This is certainly a bearish update for the market, especially growth stocks. The end of QE Cycles has historically correlated with a 10-20% correction in the S&P 500. I was expecting this correction to come next year, but with Powell shifting his tone, we may already be in the midst of this correction. I am getting more defensive to protect my capital.

We prepared for this potential scenario coming into the week. Be sure to keep an eye on the support levels I mentioned in my “Long Term Portfolio: Charts Analysis” article.
