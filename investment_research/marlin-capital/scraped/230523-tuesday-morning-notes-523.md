# Tuesday Morning Notes 5.23

Technical Update

The S&P 500 closed flat to kick off the week yesterday while tech continues to outperform.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hd7r!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc32b25fd-e5d9-4e24-893b-b427841af710_1243x923.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hd7r!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc32b25fd-e5d9-4e24-893b-b427841af710_1243x923.jpeg)

Technically, the setup in the SPX remains bullish as we continue to pressure key $4200 resistance. Another day or two of consolidation/rest would be healthy for the index to shake off short term overbought readings.
