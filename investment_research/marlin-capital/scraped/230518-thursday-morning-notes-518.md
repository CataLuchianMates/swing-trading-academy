# Thursday Morning Notes 5.18

Technical Update

The S&P 500 staged a bullish move yesterday, breaking back above the 21 EMA and recent highs while closing +1.19% on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!S6-j!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4d4bd5e8-7b33-47ed-9451-60c8083df74e_962x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!S6-j!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4d4bd5e8-7b33-47ed-9451-60c8083df74e_962x726.jpeg)

Technically, we are now above the key $4150 resistance level that the index had previously struggled with for much of May. Next up in the way of resistance is $4200 (YTD highs). After yesterday’s move, the technical picture is looking strong for the S&P 500.

Key support levels to watch below include $4150 (previous resistance), $4110-$4120, and $4070-$4080.

LRCX - now up 7.9% from entry. I will be looking to lock in at least 1/3rd profits on a move above $575 today.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tOHB!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe4b66600-b336-4678-8de7-a9309ebb07b8_1169x882.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tOHB!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe4b66600-b336-4678-8de7-a9309ebb07b8_1169x882.jpeg)

My stop will be moved to $540 on the rest of the position.
