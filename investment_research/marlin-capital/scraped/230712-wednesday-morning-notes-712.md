Make it 12 consecutive monthly declines in US CPI, with June (3.0%) the lowest monthly reading since December 2021. Following today’s report, Fed swaps are now pricing in lower odds of another rate hike this year.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pry5!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F59d20505-a7a8-459b-91ab-ae4352a5c724_961x1109.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!pry5!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F59d20505-a7a8-459b-91ab-ae4352a5c724_961x1109.png)

The market is set for a gap up this morning on the lower than expected CPI number.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hMz3!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F091bcc9e-e021-4a4f-9479-f637dda318d9_981x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hMz3!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F091bcc9e-e021-4a4f-9479-f637dda318d9_981x726.jpeg)
