# Wednesday Morning Notes 10.23

**Daily Market Commentary:**

US equities were little change yesterday with the SPX closing -0.05%.

It was a quiet session with market volumes down -9% vs 20dma despite an active earnings slate, which is unusual. Top of book liquidity was -13% vs YTD average (liquidity remains low).

Post market, SBUX disappointed on earnings with prelim Q4 comp sales missing estimates. The stock is currently down -4% in the pre-market.

All eyes on TSLA reporting earnings after the bell today.

Active Portfolio -

MU is my only currently position (2/3rds long) heading into Wednsday’’s session. My stop is set at breakeven ($103.65) and I am letting the rest of this trade play out.

LRN - Big earnings beat and guidance raise and gapping up over +25% this morning. This is my top watch today for a potential [earnings gapper setup](https://marlincapital.substack.com/p/the-earnings-gapper-trade-setup?utm_source=publication-search).
