## SMID Cap 6: Taking Some Profits

Marlin Capital Readers,

Today, I am officially starting to take profits in the SMID Cap 6.

Why?

Because there are cracks forming in the overall market. We are seeing “Risk Off” signals across the board.

We were targeting multi-bagger gains on these positions. We’ve gotten exactly that in 3 out of our 6 stocks (APLD, NBIS, and GRAL; and INOD close behind).

Overall, our SMID Cap 6 Portfolio is up +74% in 4 months.

And now, we are seeing risk off price action in the market. It’s not a time to be greedy. It’s a time to protect our hard-earned gains.

I hoped to wait for these weekly candles to finish, but save for a miraculous rally into the closing bell today, we are seeing many weekly topping candles out there. Are they short-term, intermediate, or long-term tops? That’s unknowable at this point, but I will be dissecting it all in this weekend’s analysis.

In the meantime, I am reducing all 6 of these positions today and protecting gains.

Thank you!
