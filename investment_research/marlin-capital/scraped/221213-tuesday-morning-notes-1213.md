Technical Analysis

With CPI coming in lower than expected, we are set for a major gap up. As we saw following last month’s CPI report, this type of momentum can last awhile. The one key difference to note this time around is the FOMC meeting tomorrow.

In any case, we are set for a gap up towards the $4100 region. This is a bullish breakout move technically out of the recent month long range (on volume and news).
