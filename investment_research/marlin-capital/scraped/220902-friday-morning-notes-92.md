# Friday Morning Notes 9.2

Technical Analysis

US stocks bounced from oversold conditions yesterday afternoon, staging a strong reversal and closing in the green for the session. For the QQQ, yesterday’s reversal snapped a 4 day losing streak.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9t9-!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fad4996c1-10d3-4e6d-90e5-cd38d4208d80_1234x910.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!9t9-!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fad4996c1-10d3-4e6d-90e5-cd38d4208d80_1234x910.jpeg)

Now, the QQQ looks set for a potential continuation move higher above the $300 level. Resistance levels ahead include $304, $307, and the key moving averages in the $309-$310 range. Support levels below include the key $300 level, yesterday’s low ($292-$293), and $288-$290.

---

* August nonfarm payrolls +315k vs. +298k est. & +526k in prior month (rev from +528k) … softest monthly gain since April 2021
* Unemployment rate rose to 3.7% vs. 3.5% est. & 3.5% in prior month
