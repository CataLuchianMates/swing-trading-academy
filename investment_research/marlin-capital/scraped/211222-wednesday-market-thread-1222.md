Watchlist:

Evidence is mounting that ARKK may follow scenario 1 I laid out, which would include a bounce to the $110-$120 area. Many of the short setup ideas I gave in early December, with price targets, have already reached my target levels (NET blew past $137, LC hit $22.5). I gave these price targets because they were support levels, so it is only logical that we get a bounce in growth here after a big drawdown to support.

Ultimately though, I am still not confident in this market as (1) we are in a larger downtrend and (2) even the rallies are indicating unhealthy breadth. The volume was also very light yesterday in the Nasdaq, indicating a lack of sellers versus a big wave of buyers. With that said, this is a challenging market to trade and I still personally need more evidence that this bounce is legitimate to consider getting long.

I will be watching AMD, MU, and LYV today. These should be considered 3 of the strongest charts right now. I am unlikely to make any trades today, but I will be monitoring these 3 to give me clues as to the strength of the market.
