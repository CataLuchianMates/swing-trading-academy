# Thursday Morning Notes 7.7

Technical Analysis:

Yesterday we got some follow through from Tuesday’s upside move in what was overall a choppy session for the market. The QQQ broke and closed below the key $288 resistance level (21 EMA) I talked about. The S&P 500 remains the weaker index on this bounce, as it was unable to break the 21 EMA in today’s session.

If we can hold above yesterday’s lows ($285), a temporary bottom looks to be in place for the indices. I bought some TQQQ in my Active Portfolio as we closed above the 21 EMA. This has been my preferred trading vehicle on bounces as the individual setups are still not there for me. My stop loss will be placed under today’s low, so I will be taking around 4% risk on this position. Resistance levels ahead to watch are $290-$291 (today’s highs) and $295-$297.
