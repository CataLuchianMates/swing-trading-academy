# Wednesday Morning Notes 3.20

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The major indices bounced and closed green following yesterday’s gap down, with the SPX +0.56% and QQQ +0.25%.

For the QQQ, the low of the day was $433.33 as key 1 HR support ($433-$434) held once again.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iptN!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa3a0ff55-5894-468b-99ca-4784b9ab2753_1396x653.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iptN!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa3a0ff55-5894-468b-99ca-4784b9ab2753_1396x653.png)

Technically, it is a bullish sign to see multiple successful tests of supports. Bears have had multiple chances to take price below $433, but have been unable to do so ahead of today’s FOMC meeting.

The Fed is expected to leave rates unchanged in today’s meeting. More important will be Powell/the Fed’s commentary on rate cuts for the rest of 2024 (both the trajectory and when the first cut could come). The most natural outcome for the median dot plot is to remain unchanged at 3 cuts or 4.625% for 2024.

Technically, the market is looking more bullish heading into today’s event as buyers have continued to show up big at support. With that said, today is likely to be a volatile and unpredictable day.
