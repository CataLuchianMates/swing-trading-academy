# Wednesday Morning Notes 2.7

**Daily Market Commentary:**

The S&P 500 closed in the green (+0.23%) yesterday but price action was very choppy under the hood.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oZ4S!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fab55e02e-b44b-4912-8652-e93c9e7b1163_1194x887.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oZ4S!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fab55e02e-b44b-4912-8652-e93c9e7b1163_1194x887.jpeg)

China remained topical after a slew of overnight market stimulus headlines.  Overnight, HIS rallied 4% (biggest move since July ’23) with President Xi taking a personal interest in the latest developments.  US investors have remained hesitant to re-engage in the region until definitive progress is made on the stabilization front.

The rally across China ADRs ignited a cover bid in some of the biggest laggard in the market.  Price action suggested unwinds happening in the Hedge Fund community, with shorts under/owner pockets of the market leading the move higher.  Most Short +3.1%, High Beta +2.5%, Non-Proft Tech +2.9%, while recent winners were also sold: Mega Cap Tech -40bps and AI winners -56bps.

PLTR (mentioned in yesterday’s morning notes) gave a textbook inside 5 earnings gapper setup with a long entry on the 3rd candle of the day (at $20.51).  Stop should be placed at lows of day ($19.75 – for -3.7% risk).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0S72!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5f2bd6bf-87f2-410c-94af-af03848e55e3_1102x648.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0S72!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5f2bd6bf-87f2-410c-94af-af03848e55e3_1102x648.png)

I entered this trade light as PLTR has not been one of my favorite names to trade in the past, but this long is working well so far.  Consider taking some profits at +10% ($22.60) if you took this trade.
