# The Week Ahead

US stocks rallied to new all-time highs last week with the S&P 500 +2.0% and NDX +3.5% on the week.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oej2!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe64dcc45-a9d9-4a33-bb0a-edd9312d825d_1217x720.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!oej2!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe64dcc45-a9d9-4a33-bb0a-edd9312d825d_1217x720.jpeg)

The strength was propelled by Mag 7 +3.3%, TSLA +20% on its delivery beat + increasingly positive sentiment, and a big bounce in Software (now up 8 consecutive sessions).

Strong July seasonals are at play out once again here in 2024.  As I have highlighted in previous newsletters, the NDX has finished positive for 16 consecutive July’s for an average return of +4.6%.  These returns tend to be frontloaded, as the first 2 weeks of July are the best 2-week stretch on the calendar.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-XQ8!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1e469f6b-bf14-4ba0-8e36-940bdebc0424_988x658.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-XQ8!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F1e469f6b-bf14-4ba0-8e36-940bdebc0424_988x658.jpeg)

We are well on our way to 17 straight here in 2024.

In the first week of July, we saw the traditional low volume/holiday volume grind higher.  We are continuing to see the Large Cap/Small Cap divergence as the RTY finished the week -0.22%.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-_QG!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe347a927-dca9-49b2-a219-a7d0fa0852e6_1073x661.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!-_QG!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe347a927-dca9-49b2-a219-a7d0fa0852e6_1073x661.jpeg)

Also notable is the weakness we are seeing in Bitcoin, which is down ~10% so far in July.  The Nasdaq and Bitcoin have largely moved in tandem for the last 4+ years, but we are now seeing the largest divergence between the two over this time frame.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Yj80!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F857426d7-f5d5-491b-9f58-e35a016a1825_796x799.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Yj80!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F857426d7-f5d5-491b-9f58-e35a016a1825_796x799.jpeg)

Investor expectations for lower interest rates, boosted by signs of softer Macro data (both ISM Manuf & Services came in light w/ Manuf posting its lowest reading since Feb + ADP & NFP signaled a gradually slowing labor market) and easing inflation, came to the forefront last week.  The odds of a September rate cut rose to 75% (versus 56% last Friday).

This week, Earnings season officially begins with a slate of Bank earnings kicking us off on Friday.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Sggz!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa36dec76-1949-496a-bf4f-24c5a9730c60_1920x1080.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Sggz!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa36dec76-1949-496a-bf4f-24c5a9730c60_1920x1080.jpeg)

The Financials Sector has been in a choppy consolidation period since March (for ~3-4 months).  The overall outcome of this earnings season in Financials should determine if we break higher or lower from this basing period.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Iutq!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa4e8854f-5923-4ca6-86c5-ade6f36df0e0_1200x897.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Iutq!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa4e8854f-5923-4ca6-86c5-ade6f36df0e0_1200x897.jpeg)

 We will also get US CPI, PPI, and UofM Sentiment on Thursday, while SMCI has their New Ideas Summit on Tuesday.

In my view, Earnings season is likely to lead to an even more bifurcated market than we have seen.  The indices are running hot into the beginning of the season, which means expectations will be heightened (with the market at ATHs).  The bar for Big Tech (beginning reporting in ~2 weeks) has never been higher.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ss1K!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4e8256c9-a84b-4c1c-968b-99d53d054bc2_1290x726.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ss1K!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4e8256c9-a84b-4c1c-968b-99d53d054bc2_1290x726.jpeg)

While questions have continued to circulate about the sustainability of Mega Cap Tech to carry the market higher, past EPS reports have shown that these companies continue to fire on all cylinders while also announcing share repurchase plans that make it nearly impossible to be bearish against.
