# Wednesday Morning Notes 1.4

Technical Update

Fairly volatile action in the indices to kick off 2023. The S&P 500 made a high of $3878 in the morning before reversing and finishing red on the day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cSVE!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff739918e-716b-4b49-a783-095ab05cbd8b_967x720.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!cSVE!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff739918e-716b-4b49-a783-095ab05cbd8b_967x720.jpeg)

The SPX closed off lows of the day ($3794) as the $3800 support level once again held.

We are set for a small gap up this morning. $3800 support remains the key level on the downside. On the upside, $3880-$3900 are the key resistance levels to watch. The FOMC minutes will be released at 2 PM EST today, serving as a potential catalyst for more volatile afternoon action.
