# Tuesday Morning Notes 8.29

Technical Update

The S&P 500 kicked off the week with a rally up to the 21 EMA resistance level ($4440-$4450).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!y2_t!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe7e3cc2b-e300-4193-ae07-93eabb747efd_1284x972.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!y2_t!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe7e3cc2b-e300-4193-ae07-93eabb747efd_1284x972.jpeg)

This 2 day rally has come on declining volume, which is unsurprising for late August trading. I would not expect volume to pick up majorly until after Labor Day (next Monday).

The index is now right between its 8 and 21 EMAs as we head into Tuesday. Key resistance levels to watch include $4440-$4450 and $4500.

Key support levels to watch include $4410-$4415, $4400, and $4350-$4360.
