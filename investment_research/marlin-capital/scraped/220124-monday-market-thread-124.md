# Monday Market Thread 1.24

**Marlin Capital Monday Plan:**

The Nasdaq is set to gap down this morning.  On Friday, I began buying the dip in my IRA, adding to AFRM, COIN, and RBLX.

Coming into today, I remain 46% cash in my IRA.  My 2nd buying tranche will take place when the Nasdaq Composite nears $13,400 (17% off ATHs).  This could certainly occur today.

I believe we are the point where long term investors can look to buy their favorite names.  Net Bullish Sentiment is currently at a 41.86.  The last 3 major market corrections bottomed in the 30-40 range.  Notice the 1 year returns when we get to the 30-40 range, above average and positive 89% of the time.
