Technical Analysis:

We are set for a second straight gap up this morning after a strong day and close for the market on Friday. On the QQQ 15 minute, we are primed to open near the top of our month long trading range.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!JBB9!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F11765922-0e7c-4cae-abd5-17420c4dd84f_1013x619.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!JBB9!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F11765922-0e7c-4cae-abd5-17420c4dd84f_1013x619.png)

$295-$297 are the key resistance levels to watch here, as both of the previous 2 rally attempts faltered right around this spot. We also have a big week of earnings ahead and the FOMC decision at the end of this month. I will not be chasing these gap ups, as price will very likely return to fill these gaps once we start to reverse. This remains a market to sell into strength, not buy.
