# Wednesday Morning Notes 8.3

Technical Analysis:

The market rally has halted and chopped around for the first two days of August. Some consolidation is healthy after last week’s big upside move (we came into the week overbought). The fact that these intraday dips continue to find support is potentially a bullish sign for further upside in the near future.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XwOC!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4d8b75cb-fe06-4b6a-ae83-b78723506246_1284x951.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!XwOC!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F4d8b75cb-fe06-4b6a-ae83-b78723506246_1284x951.jpeg)

Yesterday, we briefly broke Monday’s lows after a morning gap down. Just when it looked like the market might start to finally roll over, buyers showed up and the QQQ bounced back to near Monday’s highs. The intraday rally attempt ended up fading in the late afternoon, however, and the induces closed slightly lower on the day. This morning, we are set for a gap up open.

The $319-$320 level has been strong resistance during the last 2 trading sessions. A break above this level would be a bullish move, signaling that we may be heading for the 200 Day MA next. $310-$312 support will be key to watch, as these levels have held up throughout the start of this week. I plan to continue to stay patient until we break free from this consolidation range (up or down). Price is telling me that we are more likely to break to the upside here, as buyers continue to show up on every intraday dip and put a floor in the market. Things can change quickly in this market, so it is important to stay patient, flexible, and nimble.
