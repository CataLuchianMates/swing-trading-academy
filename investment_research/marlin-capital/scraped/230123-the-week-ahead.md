# The Week Ahead

Technical Update

What a turnaround.  The S&P 500 held key $3890 support last week, failing to decisively break below the level.  After a strong day and even stronger afternoon Friday, the charts are looking a lot more bullish.  Let’s start off with the S&P 500.

On the weekly time frame, the SPX closed above its 40 week MA despite breaking below the line mid-week.  The index also successfully held last week’s lows and formed a bullish looking hammer candle.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wz9M!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F64ddead5-ff7d-409f-873f-8372447f59ab_1029x655.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!wz9M!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F64ddead5-ff7d-409f-873f-8372447f59ab_1029x655.png)

On the daily time frame, the SPX closed just above its 200-day moving average after the early week rejection.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!s5HF!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd30f926b-42ea-4c70-8c8e-6098a6b4acab_1031x649.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!s5HF!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fd30f926b-42ea-4c70-8c8e-6098a6b4acab_1031x649.png)

The 1 hour time frame shows a strong bounce off $3890 support to close out last week.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ujSL!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F66438768-4027-436b-a986-3381df9263ef_1365x649.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ujSL!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F66438768-4027-436b-a986-3381df9263ef_1365x649.png)

A break above 2023 highs ($4015) sets up the SPX for a potential test of November/December highs ($4100).  Resistance levels above include $4000, $4015, $4050, $4080, and $4100.

Key support levels below include $3920-$3930 and $3890-$3900.

Stocks to Watch

NVDA

Inverse head and shoulders pattern on the weekly.  One of the best looking charts in the tech space as NVDA never saw a bearish weekly MA cross, and the daily is now approaching a bullish golden cross.  If the market can stay supported, NVDA can see $190, $200.  Key levels above include $187, $192, $196, $200, and $204.

Another strong semiconductor stock with a potential inverted head and shoulders pattern on the weekly.  AMAT has resistance at $110-$111, but can rally to $120 on a break above.
