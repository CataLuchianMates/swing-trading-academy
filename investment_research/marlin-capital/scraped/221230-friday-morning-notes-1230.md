# Friday Morning Notes 12.30

Technical Update

The SPX made a move back above the key $3800 level yesterday as the low volume chop continues.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ilJc!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc12138d2-39b9-4e0b-9a35-fbc092ecb5d9_1206x904.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ilJc!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc12138d2-39b9-4e0b-9a35-fbc092ecb5d9_1206x904.jpeg)

Now, we are set for a gap down this morning. I will be watching the $3800 support level on the downside.

On a red to green reversal to the upside, look for resistance to come at $3850-$3860 and $3890-$3900.
