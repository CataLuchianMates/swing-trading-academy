# Tuesday Morning Notes 10.1

**Daily Market Commentary:**

Quiet trading session to close out September (and Q3) yesterday with the SPX +0.42% at closing.  Activity levels were muted with volumes down -10% on the session.

The indices have been somewhat ‘stuck’ over the last week+ (last 7 sessions = SPX move less than +/- 41bps daily move).  That has changed so far this morning with the SPX currently down -1% a little over an hour into Tuesday’s trading day.  Friday’s NFP report is likely to be an important data point for the market as we begin Q4.

Further out, the election is 6 now weeks away.  Historically, regardless of the winning party, the election has been a risk clearing event and November/December returns following an election are above average for the SPX.
